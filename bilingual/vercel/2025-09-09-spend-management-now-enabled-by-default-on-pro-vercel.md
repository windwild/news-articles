2025 年 9 月 9 日

![](images/spend-management-now-enabled-by-default-on-pro-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/spend-management-now-enabled-by-default-on-pro-vercel/img_002.jpg)

Spend Management is now enabled for new Pro teams, and will be enabled by default for existing teams when they switch to the new pricing model.

支出管理（Spend Management）现已对新创建的 Pro 团队默认启用；对于现有团队，当其切换至新的定价模式时，该功能也将默认启用。

All Pro teams will have a budget set by default based on any previous usage, if any, and any teams with existing budgets will be unaffected. This can be changed at any time in [spend management settings](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%7E%2Fsettings%2Fbilling&title=Go+to+Spend+Management).

所有 Pro 团队将根据过往使用情况（如有）自动设置默认预算；已配置预算的团队不受影响。您可随时在[支出管理设置](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%7E%2Fsettings%2Fbilling&title=Go+to+Spend+Management)中调整该设置。

Email alerts will be sent when nearing the spend threshold based on your on-demand spend. This can be changed at any time here.

当您的按需支出（on-demand spend）接近设定的支出阈值时，系统将自动发送电子邮件提醒。您可随时在此处修改相关设置。

This new default ensures you receive proactive cost signals to manage your spend. Your deployments will continue without interruption unless a hard limit is manually configured.

此项新默认设置旨在为您提供主动的成本预警信号，助您更有效地管控支出。除非您手动配置了硬性上限（hard limit），否则您的部署将持续正常运行，不会中断。

Read more about the [updates to Pro](https://vercel.com/blog/new-pro-pricing-plan) and [switch to the new Pro pricing](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%7E%2Fsettings%2Fbilling&title=Switch+to+new+Pro+pricing).

了解更多关于[Pro 计划的更新](https://vercel.com/blog/new-pro-pricing-plan)以及[如何切换至新版 Pro 定价](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%7E%2Fsettings%2Fbilling&title=Switch+to+new+Pro+pricing)。
