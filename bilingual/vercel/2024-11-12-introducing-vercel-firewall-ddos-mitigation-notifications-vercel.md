Nov 12, 2024

2024 年 11 月 12 日

You can now receive alerts when the Vercel Firewall detects and automatically mitigates a DDoS attack on your Vercel project. With these alerts, you can get notified immediately when traffic to your application is blocked or challenged, so that you can review attack traffic and take any further action in a timely manner.

现在，当 Vercel 防火墙检测到并自动缓解针对您 Vercel 项目的 DDoS 攻击时，您即可收到告警通知。借助此类告警，您可在应用流量被拦截或面临挑战验证时立即获知，从而及时审查攻击流量并采取进一步应对措施。

To get started, you can either [set up a webhook](https://vercel.com/docs/observability/webhooks-overview) to get notified through a defined HTTP endpoint, or use the [Vercel Slack app](https://vercel.com/integrations/slack) to receive notifications in your Slack workspace.

入门方式有两种：您可[配置 Webhook](https://vercel.com/docs/observability/webhooks-overview)，通过指定的 HTTP 端点接收通知；也可安装并使用 [Vercel Slack 应用](https://vercel.com/integrations/slack)，在您的 Slack 工作区中接收通知。

These alerts are available on Pro and Enterprise plans.

该告警功能适用于 Pro 和 Enterprise 计划。

Learn more about [Vercel Firewall](https://vercel.com/docs/security/vercel-firewall).

了解更多关于 [Vercel 防火墙](https://vercel.com/docs/security/vercel-firewall) 的信息。
