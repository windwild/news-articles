![](images/inngest-joins-the-vercel-marketplace-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/inngest-joins-the-vercel-marketplace-vercel/img_002.jpg)

You can now install [Inngest](https://vercel.com/marketplace/inngest) directly from the Vercel Marketplace to quickly build reliable background jobs and AI workflows for your Next.js app.

您现在可直接从 Vercel Marketplace 安装 [Inngest](https://vercel.com/marketplace/inngest)，从而快速为您的 Next.js 应用构建可靠的后台任务和 AI 工作流。

Inngest is a great fit to add AI features or emerging agentic patterns to your Vercel projects:

Inngest 非常适合为您的 Vercel 项目添加 AI 功能或新兴的智能体（agentic）模式：

- Write background jobs directly in your \`app/\` directory  
- 直接在 \`app/\` 目录中编写后台任务  

- Full support for preview environments and branching  
- 全面支持预览环境与分支功能  

- One-click install and integrated billing with a generous free tier (100K executions/month)  
- 一键安装，账单与 Vercel 深度集成，并提供丰厚的免费额度（每月 10 万次执行）
