You can now easily connect your [Hasura](https://hasura.io/blog/announcing-vercel-integration-with-hasura-cloud/) GraphQL and REST APIs to your Vercel projects for streamlined fullstack development. Deploy faster data-rich applications at global scale on Vercel with a Hasura Cloud backend.

您现在可以轻松将您的 [Hasura](https://hasura.io/blog/announcing-vercel-integration-with-hasura-cloud/) GraphQL 和 REST API 连接到 Vercel 项目中，从而实现更顺畅的全栈开发流程。借助 Hasura Cloud 后端，在 Vercel 上以全球规模更快地部署数据驱动型应用。

[Try out the integration](https://vercel.com/integrations/hasura) and connect your APIs.

[立即试用该集成](https://vercel.com/integrations/hasura) 并连接您的 API。
