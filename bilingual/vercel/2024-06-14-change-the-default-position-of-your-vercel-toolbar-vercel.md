Jun 14, 2024

2024 年 6 月 14 日

You can now reposition the [Vercel Toolbar](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/vercel-toolbar) by dragging it to any corner of your page. It will [snap into place](https://x.com/witsdev/status/1801329495703490869) and persist across deployments until you move it again.

现在，您可以通过拖拽将 [Vercel 工具栏](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/vercel-toolbar) 重新定位到页面的任意角落。它会[自动吸附到位](https://x.com/witsdev/status/1801329495703490869)，并在后续各次部署中保持该位置，直至您再次手动移动它。
