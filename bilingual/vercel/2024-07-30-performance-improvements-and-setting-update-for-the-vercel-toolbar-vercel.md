2024 年 7 月 30 日

![](images/performance-improvements-and-setting-update-for-the-vercel-toolbar-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/performance-improvements-and-setting-update-for-the-vercel-toolbar-vercel/img_002.jpg)

The Vercel Toolbar now loads **up to 10x faster** and uses hardware acceleration for smoother interactions.

Vercel 工具栏现可**最快提速达 10 倍**，并利用硬件加速实现更流畅的交互体验。

The toolbar has features like [comments](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/comments) and [feature flags](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/feature-flags/using-vercel-toolbar), and developer tools like the [interaction timing tool](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/interaction-timing-tool) for optimizing INP.

该工具栏集成了多项功能，例如 [评论（comments）](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/comments) 和 [功能开关（feature flags）](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/feature-flags/using-vercel-toolbar)，以及面向开发者的工具，例如用于优化 INP 的 [交互计时工具（interaction timing tool）](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/interaction-timing-tool)。

You can also now toggle toolbar visibility based on the environment (preview or production) for your team or project from the dashboard. This is under the "Vercel Toolbar" section in general settings.

您现在还可通过控制台，按环境（预览环境或生产环境）统一开启或关闭团队或项目的工具栏显示。该设置位于「通用设置（General Settings）」下的「Vercel 工具栏（Vercel Toolbar）」部分。

When the toolbar is on, individual users can still hide and unhide it using the keyboard shortcut `⌘ + .`(Mac) or `Ctrl + .`(Windows), or disable it for their session with the option in the toolbar menu (☰).

当工具栏启用时，每位用户仍可通过键盘快捷键 `⌘ + .`（Mac）或 `Ctrl + .`（Windows）手动隐藏或显示它；也可在工具栏菜单（☰）中选择相应选项，临时禁用其当前会话中的工具栏。

Learn more about [toolbar settings and functionality](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/vercel-toolbar).

了解更多关于 [工具栏设置与功能](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/vercel-toolbar) 的信息。
