Sign up or sign in with email using one time password.  
使用一次性密码（OTP）通过电子邮件注册或登录。

This update offers three key benefits:  
本次更新带来三大核心优势：

- **Enhanced security:** 6-digit OTPs are single-use and short-lived, significantly reducing the risk of unauthorized access through intercepted or stolen credentials.  
- **安全性提升：** 6位OTP为一次性且有效期极短，可大幅降低因凭证被截获或盗用而导致的未授权访问风险。

- **Flexible sign-up/sign-in**: Start on one device or window, finish on another. No need to keep the original tab open.  
- **灵活的注册/登录流程：** 可在一台设备或一个浏览器窗口中开始操作，在另一台设备或窗口中完成。无需保持原始标签页开启。

- **No magic links:** Avoid the hassle of clicking email links — enter the OTP to complete the process.  
- **无需魔法链接（Magic Links）：** 免去点击邮件链接的繁琐步骤——直接输入OTP即可完成整个流程。

[Sign up](https://vercel.com/signup) today to get started.  
[立即注册](https://vercel.com/signup)，即刻开始使用。
