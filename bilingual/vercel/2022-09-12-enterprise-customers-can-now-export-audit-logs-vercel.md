Customers on the Enterprise plan can now export up to 90 days of Audit Logs to a CSV file.

企业版客户现在可将最多90天的审计日志导出为 CSV 文件。

Audit Logs allow team owners to track important events that occurred on their team including who performed an action, what action was taken, and when it was performed.

审计日志使团队所有者能够追踪团队内发生的重要事件，包括执行操作的人员、所执行的操作类型，以及操作发生的时间。

Check out the [documentation](https://www.vercel.com/docs/concepts/teams/security-and-compliance#audit-logs) to learn more.

请查阅[文档](https://www.vercel.com/docs/concepts/teams/security-and-compliance#audit-logs)了解更多信息。
