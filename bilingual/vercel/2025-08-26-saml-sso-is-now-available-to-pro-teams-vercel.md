[SAML-based Single Sign-On (SAML SSO)](https://vercel.com/docs/saml) is now available as an add-on to all Pro teams and can be configured directly in the dashboard. This includes support for major identity providers like Okta, Azure AD, and Google Workspace.

基于 SAML 的单点登录（SAML SSO）现已作为附加功能向所有 Pro 团队开放，并可直接在控制台中进行配置。该功能支持 Okta、Azure AD 和 Google Workspace 等主流身份提供商。

Previously limited to Enterprise plans, SAML SSO on Pro enables secure, centralized access control without requiring a contract.

此前仅面向企业版（Enterprise）计划提供，如今 Pro 计划也支持 SAML SSO，无需签订合同即可实现安全、集中的访问权限管控。
