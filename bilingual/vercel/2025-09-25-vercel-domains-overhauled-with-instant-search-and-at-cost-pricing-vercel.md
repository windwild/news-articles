2025 年 9 月 25 日

![](images/vercel-domains-overhauled-with-instant-search-and-at-cost-pricing-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/vercel-domains-overhauled-with-instant-search-and-at-cost-pricing-vercel/img_002.jpg)

We’ve rebuilt [Vercel Domains](https://vercel.com/domains) end to end, making it faster, simpler, and more affordable to find and buy the right domain for your project.

我们已对 [Vercel 域名服务](https://vercel.com/domains) 进行了端到端的全面重构，使其在为您的项目查找并购买合适域名时，速度更快、操作更简、价格更优。

- **Search without login**: Look up domains instantly, even when you’re not signed in.

- **免登录搜索**：即使未登录，也能即时查询域名。

- **At-cost pricing**: Domains are offered at registrar cost, with savings up to 50% on popular TLDs.

- **成本价定价**：域名以注册商成本价提供，在热门顶级域（TLD）上最高可节省 50%。

- **Transparent results:** Availability and pricing surface instantly, with no upsells or unnecessary add-ons.

- **结果完全透明**：域名可用性与价格即时呈现，无捆绑销售，亦无冗余附加服务。

- **Fastest search on the web**: Real-time, streaming results show availability and premium status instantly.

- **全网最快搜索**：实时流式响应，即时显示域名可用性及是否为溢价域名。

- **Expanded TLD coverage**: Support for more registries so every project can find the right home.

- **更广的顶级域覆盖范围**：支持更多注册局，确保每个项目都能找到最契合的“数字家园”。

- **Bulk checkout**: Purchase multiple domains in a single streamlined transaction.

- **批量结算**：一次简洁流畅的交易即可完成多个域名的购买。

This update makes Vercel Domains the fastest way to claim a name and get to production. As part of the overhaul, we’ve partnered with [name.com](https://name.com/) as our upstream registrar, delivering better pricing and reliability.

本次更新使 Vercel 域名服务成为抢占心仪域名并快速投入生产的最快途径。作为此次重构的重要一环，我们已与上游域名注册商 [name.com](https://name.com/) 建立合作，从而提供更具竞争力的价格与更高可靠性。

An upcoming blog will share how we built this speed using structured concurrency, layered caching, Bloom filters, and partitioned batching.

一篇即将发布的博客将分享我们如何通过结构化并发（structured concurrency）、分层缓存（layered caching）、布隆过滤器（Bloom filters）和分区批处理（partitioned batching）实现这一速度。

Try it now at [vercel.com/domains](https://vercel.com/domains).

立即试用：[vercel.com/domains](https://vercel.com/domains)。
