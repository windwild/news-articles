Oct 2, 2019

2019 年 10 月 2 日

If you are a business working with [Vercel](https://zeit.co/home), you've probably found yourself in a situation where the **invoices** you've received from us are **missing information** required by your accounting department.

如果您是一家与 [Vercel](https://zeit.co/home) 合作的企业，很可能曾遇到过我们向您开具的**发票**中**缺少贵公司财务部门所要求的信息**的情况。

Today, we are changing this by providing you with ways to configure those missing fields.

今天，我们对此作出改进，为您提供配置这些缺失字段的方式。

## New Settings Fields

## 新增设置字段

To find the new billing settings, navigate to the [Dashboard](https://zeit.co/dashboard) and select the newly designed **Settings** page of a team or personal account of your choice:

要查找新的账单设置，请访问 [控制台（Dashboard）](https://zeit.co/dashboard)，然后选择您任意一个团队或个人账户的全新设计的**设置（Settings）**页面：

![In this case, we are looking at a team named "ACME".](images/advanced-invoice-settings-on-vercel-vercel/img_001.jpg)  
![本例中，我们查看的是名为 “ACME” 的团队。](images/advanced-invoice-settings-on-vercel-vercel/img_001.jpg)  
In this case, we are looking at a team named "ACME".

本例中，我们查看的是名为 “ACME” 的团队。

Next, select the **Billing** tab in the sidebar.

接下来，在侧边栏中选择**账单（Billing）**标签页。

You will be presented with a revamped UI that combines both the old "Plans" page and the new "Billing" settings into a single, cohesive flow with several new fields:

您将看到一个全面重构的用户界面——它将原有的“套餐（Plans）”页面与全新的“账单（Billing）”设置整合为一个统一、连贯的操作流程，并新增了多个字段：

A quick overview of the new billing settings.

新账单设置概览。

As you can see, important invoice fields are now being offered:

如您所见，关键的发票字段现已开放配置：

- **Billing Email**: A custom destination for your invoices, to prevent sending it to the first owner.  
- **账单邮箱**：为您的账单指定一个自定义收件地址，避免发送至组织的首位所有者。

- **Billing Address:** If you'd like to add a postal address to every invoice, enter it here.  
- **账单地址**：如需在每张账单中添加邮政地址，请在此处填写。

- **Billing Language:** Allows you to change the language of your invoices.  
- **账单语言**：允许您更改账单所使用的语言。

- **Billing Tax ID:** If you'd like your invoice to render a specific tax ID, enter it here.  
- **账单税号**：如需在账单中显示特定的税务登记号（Tax ID），请在此处填写。

Only future invoices will be affected by your changes, not existing ones.  
您的修改仅影响今后生成的账单，不会更改已开具的账单。

## Conclusion  
## 结语

With these new billing settings, you can easily make the invoices you receive from [Vercel](https://zeit.co/home) comply with the requirements of your accounting department or the legal requirements of your country's tax office.  
借助这些全新的账单设置，您可以轻松确保从 [Vercel](https://zeit.co/home) 收到的账单符合贵公司财务部门的要求，或满足您所在国家税务机关的法定要求。
