Dec 19, 2022

2022年12月19日

Last month, Vercel had the privilege of sponsoring [AfroTech](https://afrotech.com/) Conference 2022—the place for all things Black in tech and Web3. Our team was joined by the likes of Google, Meta, and Tesla in the expo hall—so we knew that we needed to find ways to stand out, engage with the community, and attract top talent.

上个月，Vercel 很荣幸成为 [AfroTech](https://afrotech.com/) 大会 2022 的赞助商——这是聚焦非裔科技从业者与 Web3 领域一切相关议题的盛会。在展会大厅中，我们的团队与 Google、Meta 和 Tesla 等企业并肩而立；因此我们深知，必须另辟蹊径，方能脱颖而出、深度联结社区，并吸引顶尖人才。

This was our approach.

这便是我们的策略。

## **An immersive experience with \_OFCOLOR**

## **携手 \_OFCOLOR 打造沉浸式体验**

Vercel’s mission is to give every developer the power to create at the moment of inspiration. From creatives to technologists, we envision a world where everyone can contribute to the web development process. And you’ve probably noticed that art and [design plays a major role](https://vercel.com/design) in everything that we do.

Vercel 的使命是赋予每一位开发者“在灵感迸发的瞬间即刻创造”的能力。无论创意人士还是技术专家，我们都构想一个世界：每个人都能参与网页开发的全过程。你或许早已注意到，艺术与[设计](https://vercel.com/design)在我们所做的一切中都扮演着至关重要的角色。

Because of this mission, we brought leaders from Black At Vercel, our new Employee Resource Group (ERG) to partner with local Austin arts alliance [\_OFCOLOR](https://www.ofcolor.org/). We produced an immersive experience called All Kinds of Black In Tech, featuring a photo exhibit of Black tech workers, interactive product demos, and a live DJ. It wasn’t your average networking event.

正因这一使命，我们邀请了 Vercel 全新成立的员工资源小组（Employee Resource Group, ERG）——“Black At Vercel”的核心成员，携手奥斯汀本地艺术联盟 [\__OFCOLOR](https://www.ofcolor.org/) 展开合作。我们共同打造了一场名为《科技界千姿百态的黑人面孔》（All Kinds of Black In Tech）的沉浸式体验活动，内容包括黑人科技从业者的摄影展、互动式产品演示以及现场 DJ 表演。这绝非一场普通的社交活动。

## **One voice, all roles, same team**

## **一个声音，多种角色，同一支团队**

Representation matters—across our whole business. “When I joined Vercel last year, I was amazed to see how many people of color there were on the All Hands company call. It absolutely blew me away. From software engineers to product directors, there were people of color all over Vercel,” said Jeremy Jefferson, Black at Vercel Founder and Leader.

代表性至关重要——贯穿我们业务的方方面面。“去年我加入 Vercel 时，在公司全员大会（All Hands）上看到如此众多的有色人种同事，令我倍感震撼。从软件工程师到产品总监，Vercel 各个岗位上都有有色人种的身影。” “Black At Vercel” 创始人兼负责人杰里米·杰斐逊（Jeremy Jefferson）表示。

With this in mind, we ensured that our AfroTech crew on the ground included folks from all areas of the business, including sales, data, engineering, marketing, and product.

考虑到这一点，我们确保亲临 AfroTech 现场的 Vercel 团队成员覆盖公司各个业务领域，包括销售、数据、工程、市场营销和产品。

![The Vercel team at AfroTech 2022](images/vercel-at-afrotech-2022-an-immersive-experience-vercel/img_003.jpg)  
![2022 年 AfroTech 大会上的 Vercel 团队](images/vercel-at-afrotech-2022-an-immersive-experience-vercel/img_003.jpg)

This was also the perfect opportunity for Black At Vercel to meet and strategize. “While we strive to never be siloed, it’s not every day that you get to work face-to-face with folks from all areas of the business. Our number one goal is to help build our diversity network across the organization. Networking is vital for people of color, especially for those of us working remotely,” says Jeremy.

这同时也是“Vercel 黑人员工社群（Black At Vercel）”成员线下会面与共同制定战略的绝佳契机。“我们始终努力避免各自为政，但并非每天都有机会与来自公司各个部门的同事面对面协作。我们的首要目标，是助力在整个组织内构建多元包容的人才网络。对有色人种而言，建立人脉至关重要——尤其对我们这些远程办公的成员来说更是如此。”Jeremy 表示。

## **Telling our story, attracting top talent**

## **讲述我们的故事，吸引顶尖人才**

At the end of the day, we went to AfroTech to connect with top talent. We spent three days showcasing product demos, reviewing resumes, and performing interviews. We talked to over 1,500 attendees at our booth, conducted interviews, and led a workshop with computer science and MBA students at Huston-Tillotson University, a local historically Black college (HBCU).

归根结底，我们参加 AfroTech 是为了接触并连接顶尖人才。我们在现场连续三天开展产品演示、简历筛选与面试工作；在展台与逾 1,500 名参会者交流，组织了多场面试，并携手当地历史悠久的黑人大学——休斯顿-蒂洛森大学（Huston-Tillotson University），为该校计算机科学与工商管理硕士（MBA）专业的学生举办了一场专题工作坊。

But most critically, we got to tell the Vercel story to an important audience.

但最为关键的是，我们得以向一个至关重要的受众群体讲述 Vercel 的故事。

Maybe someday soon, one of our future engineers or data scientists will come up with an algorithm on how to measure those seemingly intangible metrics. For now, we can say with assurance that we aim to be a place where anyone, inclusive of identity, can contribute to our mission of making the Web faster.

或许在不久的将来，我们某位未来的工程师或数据科学家将开发出一种算法，用以量化那些看似难以衡量的指标。而当下，我们可以坚定地表示：Vercel 致力于成为这样一个地方——无论身份背景如何，任何人都能投身于我们“让 Web 更快”的使命之中。
