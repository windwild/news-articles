# Deepening our collaboration with the U.S. Department of Energy

# 深化我们与美国能源部的合作

OpenAI and the U.S. Department of Energy sign memorandum of understanding to accelerate science with AI.

OpenAI 与美国能源部签署谅解备忘录，携手利用人工智能加速科学进步。

Scientific progress shapes everything from health and energy to national security and our understanding of the universe. If AI can help researchers explore more ideas, test hypotheses faster, and move from insight to validated results more quickly, the benefits compound—across disciplines and across society.

科学进步深刻影响着从人类健康、能源开发到国家安全乃至我们对宇宙的认知等方方面面。倘若人工智能能够助力科研人员探索更多新思路、更快验证科学假设，并更迅速地将洞见转化为经实证支持的研究成果，其带来的益处将在各学科领域及整个社会层面持续叠加放大。

OpenAI and the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) have signed a memorandum of understanding (MOU) to explore opportunities for further collaborations on AI and advanced computing in support of DOE initiatives, including the Genesis Mission. This work is part of [OpenAI for Science⁠](https://openai.com/science/), our effort to help scientists accelerate discovery by pairing frontier AI models with the tools, workflows, and expertise of real research environments.

OpenAI 与美国能源部（DOE）已签署一份谅解备忘录（MOU），旨在探索双方在人工智能与先进计算领域的进一步合作机会，以支持包括“创生使命”（Genesis Mission）在内的 DOE 各项倡议。此项工作隶属于 [OpenAI for Science⁠](https://openai.com/science/) 计划——我们致力于通过将前沿人工智能模型与真实科研环境中的工具、工作流程及专业经验深度融合，助力科学家加速科学发现。

This MOU builds on OpenAI’s existing work with DOE’s national laboratories, where we’ve already deployed frontier models in real research environments and worked directly with scientists on high-impact problems.

本谅解备忘录建立在 OpenAI 与美国能源部下属国家实验室既有合作基础之上：我们此前已在真实的科研环境中部署前沿模型，并直接与科学家协作攻关具有重大影响的科学难题。

The [Genesis Mission⁠(opens in a new window)](https://genesis.energy.gov/) brings together government, national labs, and industry to apply advanced AI and computing to accelerate scientific discovery. The MOU establishes a framework for information sharing and coordination, and creates a path for the parties to discuss and develop potential follow-on agreements as specific projects take shape. Today, OpenAI also [submitted detailed recommendations⁠(opens in a new window)](https://cdn.openai.com/pdf/openai-ostp-accelerating-science-rfi.pdf) to the White House Office of Science and Technology Policy on how the United States can strengthen science and technology leadership through AI. That filing outlines why we see 2026 as a “Year of Science” and why access to frontier AI models, compute, and real research environments is essential to accelerating discovery. The agreement with the Department of Energy reflects that vision moving into practice.

[“创生使命”（Genesis Mission）⁠(在新窗口中打开)](https://genesis.energy.gov/) 汇聚联邦政府、国家实验室与产业界力量，共同运用先进人工智能与计算技术加速科学发现进程。本谅解备忘录确立了信息共享与协同合作的框架，并为各方在具体项目逐步成形后开展磋商、制定后续合作协议铺平道路。今日，OpenAI 还向白宫科学技术政策办公室（OSTP）[提交了详尽的政策建议⁠(在新窗口中打开)](https://cdn.openai.com/pdf/openai-ostp-accelerating-science-rfi.pdf)，阐述美国如何借助人工智能强化其科技领导力。该文件阐明了为何我们将2026年视为“科学之年”，并强调：获取前沿人工智能模型、算力资源以及真实科研环境，是加速科学发现不可或缺的关键要素。此次与美国能源部达成的协议，正是这一愿景迈向实践的重要体现。

This announcement follows today’s Genesis Mission event at the White House, where Kevin Weil, Vice President of OpenAI for Science, joined DOE and other collaborators.

本公告发布于今日在白宫举行的“创生使命”活动之后。OpenAI 科学事务副总裁凯文·韦尔（Kevin Weil）出席活动，并与美国能源部及其他合作方共同参与。

> “We’re excited to collaborate with the Department of Energy and contribute to the Genesis Mission. When frontier AI meets the expertise of the national labs, it opens up new ways to explore ideas, test them faster, and accelerate scientific progress.”

> 我们非常期待与美国能源部（DOE）开展合作，并为“创生计划”（Genesis Mission）贡献力量。当前沿人工智能与国家实验室的专业知识相遇，便催生出探索新思想、更快验证假设、加速科学进步的全新路径。

Kevin Weil, Vice President of OpenAI for Science  
凯文·韦尔（Kevin Weil），OpenAI 科学事务副总裁  

## A shared commitment to accelerate discovery  
## 共同致力于加速科学发现  

OpenAI and the Department of Energy share a focus on advancing basic and applied research and strengthening U.S. leadership in AI and advanced computing. This memorandum of understanding provides a structured way for OpenAI and DOE to exchange technical expertise, coordinate activities, and explore areas of collaboration—such as fusion energy, where DOE labs bring world-leading facilities, modeling tools, and data—while ensuring that future project work is clearly scoped and governed through follow-on agreements.  

OpenAI 与美国能源部均致力于推动基础研究与应用研究，并强化美国在人工智能及先进计算领域的全球领导地位。本谅解备忘录（MOU）为 OpenAI 与能源部建立了一套结构化协作机制，支持双方开展技术专长交流、活动协同，并探索具体合作领域——例如聚变能源：在此领域，能源部下属国家实验室拥有世界一流的实验设施、建模工具与科学数据资源；同时，该备忘录亦确保后续具体项目的工作范围清晰明确，并通过补充协议予以规范治理。

For OpenAI, this reflects how we think about building OpenAI for Science. Progress comes from working side-by-side with researchers to understand where AI meaningfully helps, where it falls short, and how it can be safely integrated into real scientific workflows. That work depends on close engagement with domain experts, access to world-class scientific infrastructure, and rigorous evaluation in the environments where science actually happens. The MOU creates space for that kind of collaboration, while preserving the rigor and accountability required for responsible scientific use.  

对 OpenAI 而言，此举体现了我们构建“OpenAI 科学版”（OpenAI for Science）的核心理念：真正的进步源于与科研人员并肩协作——共同厘清人工智能在哪些环节真正赋能科研、在哪些方面尚存不足，以及如何将其安全、有效地融入真实的科研工作流。这一过程高度依赖于与领域专家的深度互动、对世界一流科研基础设施的可及性，以及在真实科研场景中开展的严格评估。本谅解备忘录为此类协作提供了制度化空间，同时坚守负责任科研应用所必需的严谨性与问责制。

## Building on a growing partnership with DOE’s national laboratories  
## 深化与美国能源部国家实验室日益紧密的合作关系  

Over the past year, we’ve been working closely with scientists across the DOE national lab system to understand where frontier models help, where they fall short, and what it takes to integrate them into real research settings.  

过去一年间，我们与美国能源部所属各国家实验室的科学家开展了密切合作，深入探究前沿大模型在哪些科研场景中切实有效、在哪些方面仍显不足，以及将其成功嵌入真实科研环境所需的关键条件。

#### Putting frontier models in the hands of scientists  
#### 让前沿模型真正触达一线科学家  

Together with DOE’s national labs, we convened the [1,000 Scientist AI Jam Session⁠](https://openai.com/global-affairs/1000-scientist-ai-jam-session/)—a first-of-its-kind event across nine labs where more than 1,000 scientists used frontier AI models to test domain-specific problems, evaluate model responses, and provide structured feedback to inform future system development. This model of collaboration allows researchers to stress-test AI on the problems that matter in their work, and help shape how these tools evolve.  

我们联合美国能源部国家实验室共同举办了[“千名科学家 AI 创意工作坊”（1,000 Scientist AI Jam Session）](https://openai.com/global-affairs/1000-scientist-ai-jam-session/)——这是横跨九家国家实验室的首创性活动，逾千名科学家现场使用前沿人工智能模型，针对各自专业领域的具体问题开展实测、评估模型输出结果，并提供结构化反馈，以指导未来系统迭代优化。此类协作模式使研究人员得以在自身工作中真正关切的核心问题上对 AI 进行压力测试，并实质性参与塑造这些工具的演进方向。

#### Deploying frontier models on national lab supercomputers  
#### 在国家实验室超算平台上部署前沿大模型

We partner with the [National Nuclear Security Administration (NNSA) laboratories⁠](https://openai.com/index/strengthening-americas-ai-leadership-with-the-us-national-laboratories/), including Los Alamos National Laboratory, Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory, and Sandia National Laboratories, to support scientific and technical research. As part of this work, OpenAI has deployed advanced reasoning models on the Venado supercomputer at Los Alamos National Laboratory, where it serves as a shared resource for researchers across the NNSA labs. This collaboration focuses on applying frontier AI models in high-performance computing environments to support complex scientific and national research challenges.

我们与[美国国家核安全管理局（NNSA）下属国家实验室](https://openai.com/index/strengthening-americas-ai-leadership-with-the-us-national-laboratories/)开展合作，包括洛斯阿拉莫斯国家实验室（Los Alamos National Laboratory）、劳伦斯利弗莫尔国家实验室（Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory）和桑迪亚国家实验室（Sandia National Laboratories），以支持科学与技术研究。作为此项合作的一部分，OpenAI 已在洛斯阿拉莫斯国家实验室的 Venado 超级计算机上部署了先进的推理模型，该模型作为一项共享资源，服务于 NNSA 各实验室的研究人员。本合作聚焦于将前沿 AI 模型应用于高性能计算环境，以应对复杂的科学研究与国家战略研究挑战。

#### Measuring bioscience capabilities in realistic settings

#### 在真实场景中评估生物科学能力

We also partnered with [Los Alamos National Laboratory⁠](https://openai.com/index/openai-and-los-alamos-national-laboratory-work-together/) to develop evaluations that study how multimodal AI systems can be used safely by scientists in laboratory settings. That work is designed to move beyond purely text-based assessments and toward more realistic measurements of how models may influence outcomes in high-consequence domains, grounded in expert oversight, careful study design, and a clear commitment to risk reduction.

我们还与[洛斯阿拉莫斯国家实验室](https://openai.com/index/openai-and-los-alamos-national-laboratory-work-together/)合作，开发了一系列评估方法，用以研究多模态 AI 系统如何在实验室环境中被科学家安全使用。该工作旨在突破纯文本评估的局限，转向更贴近现实的衡量方式——即在高风险领域中，切实评估模型可能对最终结果产生的影响；其设计以专家监督为依托、以严谨的研究方案为基础，并始终秉持明确的风险降低承诺。

## How OpenAI for Science fits into this partnership

## OpenAI for Science 如何融入这一合作

Our goal with OpenAI for Science is to accelerate science: to help researchers explore more ideas, test hypotheses faster, and uncover insights that would otherwise take significant time. We approach that goal with two complementary beliefs:

OpenAI for Science 的目标是加速科学发展：助力研究人员探索更多想法、更快验证假设，并发现那些原本需耗费大量时间才能获得的洞见。我们基于两项相辅相成的理念推进这一目标：

- **Scientific tools matter.** Simulation engines, analysis pipelines, domain databases, and high-performance computing are essential for precision and throughput.  
- **科学工具至关重要。** 仿真引擎、分析流水线、领域专用数据库以及高性能计算，是保障科研精度与效率的关键基础设施。

- **Frontier reasoning matters.** As models scale and improve, they can increasingly support conceptual work, connecting ideas across fields, navigating large literatures beyond keyword search, proposing mechanisms, and stress-testing hypotheses.  
- **前沿推理能力至关重要。** 随着模型规模扩大与性能提升，它们日益能够支撑概念性工作：跨学科联结思想、超越关键词检索而高效浏览海量文献、提出潜在作用机制，并对科学假设进行压力测试。

DOE’s national labs are uniquely positioned at the intersection of these two beliefs: they operate some of the world’s most sophisticated scientific infrastructure and convene experts working on problems where better reasoning and better computing can translate directly into scientific advancement and societal benefit.

美国能源部（DOE）下属国家实验室恰好处于上述两大理念的交汇点：它们运行着全球最尖端的科研基础设施，并汇聚了一批致力于解决关键问题的顶尖专家——在这些领域中，“更优的推理能力”与“更强的计算能力”可直接转化为科学进步与社会福祉。

## What’s next

## 下一步计划

Scientific discovery has always advanced fastest when great tools meet great scientists. We believe frontier AI—developed in close partnership with the scientific community—can become a new kind of scientific instrument: one that expands what researchers can explore, improves the speed of iteration, and helps translate insight into impact. We’re proud to work alongside DOE and the national labs as we build toward that future.

科学发现历来在卓越工具与杰出科学家相遇时进展最快。我们相信，前沿 AI——在与科学界紧密协作中共同研发——有望成为一种新型科学仪器：它能拓展研究人员的探索边界、加快迭代速度，并助力将科学洞见切实转化为实际影响。我们深感荣幸能与美国能源部（DOE）及各国家实验室携手并进，共同迈向这一未来。
