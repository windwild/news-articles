# Advancing independent research on AI alignment

# 推进人工智能对齐领域的独立研究

We’re committing $7.5M to The Alignment Project to fund independent research developing mitigations to safety and security risks from misaligned AI.

我们将投入750万美元资助“The Alignment Project”（对齐项目），以支持独立研究人员开展工作，开发应对人工智能错位（misalignment）所引发的安全与安全风险的缓解措施。

As AI systems become more capable and more autonomous, alignment research needs to both keep pace and scale diversity. At OpenAI, we invest heavily in frontier alignment and safety research as it is critical to our mission. We also believe that ensuring that AGI is safe and beneficial to everyone cannot be achieved by any single organization and want to support independent research and conceptual approaches that can be pursued outside of frontier labs.

随着人工智能系统能力日益增强、自主性不断提高，对齐研究既需持续提速，也需拓展研究视角的多样性。OpenAI 高度重视前沿对齐与安全研究，因为这直接关系到我们的使命。同时我们也坚信：确保通用人工智能（AGI）对所有人安全且有益，绝非任何单一组织所能独自完成；因此，我们希望支持那些可在前沿实验室之外开展的独立研究及概念性探索路径。

Today, we’re announcing a $7.5 million grant to [The Alignment Project⁠(opens in a new window)](https://alignmentproject.aisi.gov.uk/), a global fund for independent alignment research created by the UK AI Security Institute (UK AISI). Renaissance Philanthropy is supporting the grant’s administration. This contribution helps make The Alignment Project one of the largest dedicated funding efforts for independent alignment research to date and strengthens the broader, independent ecosystem.

今日，我们宣布向[“The Alignment Project”（对齐项目）⁠（在新窗口中打开）](https://alignmentproject.aisi.gov.uk/)提供750万美元资助。该项目由英国人工智能安全研究所（UK AISI）发起，是一个面向全球的、专为支持独立对齐研究而设立的基金。文艺复兴慈善基金会（Renaissance Philanthropy）将协助本笔资助的管理工作。此次捐助使“The Alignment Project”成为迄今规模最大的、专门面向独立对齐研究的资助计划之一，亦将进一步强化更广泛、更具活力的独立研究生态体系。

Frontier labs like OpenAI are in a unique position to pursue alignment research that depends on access to frontier models and significant compute—work that is often difficult for independent researchers to explore. We devote much of our internal alignment effort to developing scalable methods so that alignment progress keeps pace with capability progress. [We believe iterative deployment](https://openai.com/safety/how-we-think-about-safety-alignment/)—gradually increasing capabilities while strengthening safeguards—helps surface problems early and gives us concrete evidence about what works in practice, and that responsible development requires significant alignment and safety work that is tightly integrated with model building and deployment.

像 OpenAI 这样的前沿实验室具备独特优势，可开展依赖前沿模型与大规模算力的对齐研究——而这类工作往往难以被独立研究人员触及。我们将大量内部对齐研究资源投入到可扩展方法的开发中，以确保对齐进展能与能力进展同步。[我们主张采用迭代式部署策略](https://openai.com/safety/how-we-think-about-safety-alignment/)——即在逐步提升模型能力的同时，同步加强安全保障措施。这一策略有助于尽早暴露问题，并为我们提供关于哪些方法在实践中真正有效的实证依据；同时，负责任的人工智能发展，必然要求将大量对齐与安全工作深度融入模型研发与部署全过程。

In parallel, the field benefits from sustained investment in independent, exploratory research—which can expand the space of ideas and uncover new directions. Independent research remains essential; in many kinds of useful inquiry, labs do not retain a comparative advantage. A healthy alignment ecosystem depends on independent teams testing diverse assumptions, developing alternative frameworks, and exploring conceptual, theoretical, and blue-sky ideas that may not align neatly with any one organization’s roadmap.

与此同时，该领域亦将持续受益于对独立、探索性研究的长期投入——此类研究能够拓展思想疆域、催生全新方向。独立研究依然不可或缺；在许多富有价值的研究类型中，实验室并不具备比较优势。一个健康的对齐生态系统，有赖于独立团队检验多元假设、构建替代性理论框架，并探索概念性、理论性乃至“蓝天式”（blue-sky）的前沿构想——这些构想未必契合任一组织当前的技术路线图。

And because progress toward AGI may ultimately depend on fundamental breakthroughs that change the shape of the alignment problem and which approaches are most useful, it’s important to support research that would matter even if today’s dominant methods turn out not to scale in the way we expect. In those worlds, it becomes especially important to have a strong external ecosystem doing foundational, conceptual, and uncorrelated work. The problem of AI alignment and safety is of unprecedented importance, and we need all hands on deck as we do not yet know which approaches will prove most durable as capabilities continue to advance.

此外，通往通用人工智能（AGI）的最终突破，或将取决于那些从根本上重塑对齐问题格局、并重新定义何种方法最为有效的基础性进展。因此，我们必须支持那些即便在当前主流方法未能如预期般规模化时，依然具有深远意义的研究。在那样的未来图景中，构建一个强大、活跃的外部研究生态，开展基础性、概念性以及彼此正交（uncorrelated）的研究工作，将变得尤为关键。人工智能对齐与安全问题的重要性史无前例；面对能力持续跃升的现实，我们尚无法预判哪一类方法将最具韧性与持久力——唯有汇聚各方力量，方能共克此艰。

Our grant—approximately £5.6 million at current exchange rates—will [co-fund The Alignment Project⁠(opens in a new window)](https://www.gov.uk/government/news/openai-and-microsoft-join-uks-international-coalition-to-safeguard-ai-development) alongside other public, philanthropic, and industry backers.  

我们此次资助——按当前汇率计算约为 560 万英镑——将与其他公共部门、慈善机构及产业界资助方共同支持[“对齐项目”（The Alignment Project）⁠(在新窗口中打开)](https://www.gov.uk/government/news/openai-and-microsoft-join-uks-international-coalition-to-safeguard-ai-development)。

The total fund exceeds £27 million and is designed to support a broad portfolio of alignment research projects worldwide, spanning topics as diverse as computational complexity theory, economic theory and game theory, cognitive science, and information theory and cryptography.  

该基金总额逾 2700 万英镑，旨在支持全球范围内一系列广泛的 AI 对齐研究项目，涵盖计算复杂性理论、经济理论与博弈论、认知科学、信息论与密码学等多元主题。

Individual projects are typically funded at £50,000 to £1 million, and may also receive optional access to compute resources and expert support.  

单个项目资助额通常为 5 万至 100 万英镑，并可酌情获得算力资源及专家支持。

Our funding does not create a new program or selection process, nor influence the existing process; it increases the number of already-vetted, high-quality [projects⁠(opens in a new window)](http://www.aisi.gov.uk/blog/funding-60-projects-to-advance-ai-alignment-research) that can be funded in the current round.  

我们的资助既不设立新项目或新评审流程，也不干预现有流程；它仅扩大本轮资助范围内已通过严格筛选、具备高质量水准的[项目数量⁠(在新窗口中打开)](http://www.aisi.gov.uk/blog/funding-60-projects-to-advance-ai-alignment-research)。

UK AISI is well positioned to direct alignment funding at this scale and range. It brings an established cross-sector coalition spanning government, academia, philanthropy, and industry, along with a grantmaking pipeline already in motion and a large pool of proposals that have undergone expert review.  

英国人工智能安全研究所（UK AISI）具备充分条件，以如此规模与广度统筹对齐领域资助工作。它汇聚了覆盖政府、学术界、慈善界与产业界的成熟跨部门合作联盟；其资助流程已全面启动，并拥有大量经专家评审的项目提案储备。

As a UK government research organization within the Department for Science, Innovation and Technology (DSIT), it also has a mandate focused on serious AI risks and is experienced with running research funding programs.  

作为英国科学、创新与技术部（DSIT）下属的政府研究机构，UK AISI 的法定职责聚焦于重大 AI 风险，并在运行科研资助项目方面经验丰富。

Because the future of AI won’t unfold exactly as anyone predicts—and may advance very quickly—we believe democratization, “AI resilience,” and iterative deployment are essential.  

鉴于 AI 的未来走向无法被任何人精准预测——且其发展速度可能极快——我们认为，推动民主化、“AI 韧性”（AI resilience）以及渐进式部署至关重要。

While we continue advancing our frontier alignment and safety research at OpenAI, progress will benefit from a robust, diverse, independent ecosystem pursuing complementary approaches as capabilities advance.  

尽管我们在 OpenAI 持续推进前沿对齐与安全研究，但随着 AI 能力不断提升，一个强健、多元且独立的生态系统——致力于探索互补性路径——将极大助力整体进步。

This grant is one step toward that goal. We look forward to continuing to collaborate with the broader research community as the field advances.  

此项资助正是迈向这一目标的重要一步。随着该领域持续演进，我们期待继续与更广泛的研究界通力协作。
