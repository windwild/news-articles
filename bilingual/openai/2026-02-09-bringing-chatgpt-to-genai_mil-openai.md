# Bringing ChatGPT to GenAI.mil

# 将 ChatGPT 引入 GenAI.mil

Today, [OpenAI for Government⁠](https://openai.com/global-affairs/introducing-openai-for-government/) is announcing the next phase of our national security work: bringing ChatGPT to GenAI.mil, the Department of War’s secure enterprise AI platform used by 3 million civilian and military personnel.

今日，[OpenAI for Government⁠](https://openai.com/global-affairs/introducing-openai-for-government/) 宣布其国家安全工作的下一阶段：将 ChatGPT 接入 GenAI.mil——美国国防部（U.S. Department of War）面向300万文职与军事人员的、安全的企业级人工智能平台。

By joining the other frontier AI labs on GenAI.mil, we are building on our existing work with the Pentagon—including our [collaboration with DARPA⁠(opens in a new window)](https://www.darpa.mil/news/2023/ai-cyber-challenge-software) to help cyber defenders and the [pilot program we announced earlier this year⁠](https://openai.com/global-affairs/introducing-openai-for-government/) with the Department’s Chief Digital and Artificial Intelligence Office (CDAO) focused on how frontier AI can transform the Pentagon’s operations.

通过加入 GenAI.mil 平台上的其他前沿人工智能实验室，我们正深化与美国国防部（Pentagon）已有的合作——包括我们与美国国防高级研究计划局（DARPA）开展的[合作项目⁠(在新窗口中打开)](https://www.darpa.mil/news/2023/ai-cyber-challenge-software)，以支持网络防御人员；以及今年早些时候我们与国防部首席数字与人工智能办公室（CDAO）联合宣布的[试点项目⁠](https://openai.com/global-affairs/introducing-openai-for-government/)，聚焦于前沿人工智能如何重塑五角大楼的业务运作。

We believe the people responsible for defending the country should have access to the best tools available, and it is important for the United States and other democratic countries to understand how, with the proper safeguards, AI can help protect people, deter adversaries, and prevent future conflict. That’s why we’re bringing ChatGPT to GenAI.mil—to empower American service members with AI capabilities built to the highest standards, delivering reliable performance and mission advantage. Our goal is to help governments use AI effectively and safely. We believe we can best achieve that by participating in efforts like [GenAI.mil⁠(opens in a new window)](http://genai.mil/), where we help shape the technical norms for how AI is deployed across government.

我们坚信，肩负国家防卫重任的人员理应获得当下最先进、最可靠的工具；同时，对美国及其他民主国家而言，理解如何在恰当保障措施下，让人工智能助力人民保护、威慑对手并预防未来冲突，至关重要。正因如此，我们将 ChatGPT 引入 GenAI.mil——旨在为美军官兵赋能，提供符合最高标准的人工智能能力，确保稳定可靠的表现，并带来任务执行优势。我们的目标是帮助各国政府高效、安全地应用人工智能。我们认为，参与诸如 [GenAI.mil⁠(在新窗口中打开)](http://genai.mil/) 这类倡议，正是实现这一目标的最佳路径——在其中，我们共同塑造人工智能在政府各领域部署所应遵循的技术规范。

**Deploying a custom ChatGPT product on GenAI.mil**

**在 GenAI.mil 上部署定制版 ChatGPT 产品**

OpenAI will deploy a custom version of ChatGPT on GenAI.mil, approved for the Department’s unclassified work. The system runs in authorized government cloud infrastructure with built-in safety controls and protections for the Department’s highly sensitive data, giving teams secure access to generative AI.

OpenAI 将在 GenAI.mil 上部署一款定制版 ChatGPT，该版本已获准用于国防部的非密级工作。该系统运行于经授权的政府云基础设施之上，内置多重安全控制机制，并针对国防部高度敏感的数据提供专门防护，从而为各部门团队提供安全、可信的生成式人工智能访问能力。

ChatGPT is designed to support day‑to‑day tasks that strengthen readiness and mission execution for American service members, including:

ChatGPT 专为支持美军官兵日常任务而设计，旨在提升战备水平与任务执行效能，具体应用场景包括：

- Summarizing and analyzing policy and guidance documents  
- 汇总与分析政策及指导性文件  

- Drafting and reviewing procurement and contracting materials  
- 起草与审核采购及合同相关材料  

- Generating internal reports and compliance checklists  
- 生成内部报告与合规性检查清单  

- Supporting a wide variety of research, planning, mission support, and administrative workflows  
- 支持各类研究、规划、任务保障及行政工作流程  

**Securely built for the Department of War:**  
**专为战争部安全构建：**  

- The models deployed on GenAI.mil incorporate safeguards at the model and platform level to promote robustness and reliability within the embedded system itself, supporting all lawful uses.  
- 部署于 GenAI.mil 的模型在模型层与平台层均集成了多重安全防护机制，以增强嵌入式系统自身的鲁棒性与可靠性，全面支持所有合法用途。  

- Data processed on GenAI.mil remains isolated to the government environment and is not used to train or improve OpenAI’s public or commercial models, ensuring separation from external systems and protecting mission data.  
- 在 GenAI.mil 上处理的数据严格限定于政府环境内，不会用于训练或优化 OpenAI 的公开或商用模型，从而确保与外部系统的物理隔离，并切实保护任务数据。  

This effort reflects how we approach government work more broadly: focused, practical, safety-forward, and grounded in real-world use.  
此项工作体现了我们开展政府相关工作的整体理念：目标明确、注重实效、安全优先、立足实际应用。
