2026年1月2日

# Apply to OpenAI Grove

# 申请加入 OpenAI Grove

A program for individuals early in their company building journey.

面向正处于创业初期的个人所设立的项目。

_**Update on January 12, 2026**_ _: Applications are now closed._

_**2026年1月12日更新**_：申请现已截止。

Today, we’re opening the applications for the next cohort of OpenAI Grove, a program for technical talent at the very start of their company-building journey. The Grove is not a startup accelerator or traditional program: it offers pre-idea individuals deeply curious about building in AI a dense talent network, co-building with OpenAI researchers, and resources designed to accelerate your journey. As participants explore early concepts, they will receive counsel from the OpenAI team and community with peers in OpenAI Grove.

今天，我们正式启动下一届 OpenAI Grove 的申请通道。该项目专为刚刚踏上创业之路的技术人才而设。Grove 并非传统意义上的初创企业加速器或常规培训项目：它面向尚无具体创业构想、但对在人工智能领域创业抱有强烈好奇心的个体，提供一个高度凝聚的人才网络、与 OpenAI 研究人员协同共建的机会，以及一系列旨在加速你创业进程的资源支持。在参与者探索早期创意的过程中，OpenAI 团队、Grove 社区及同侪将为其提供持续指导与建议。

This program is the starting point of a long-term network. It will begin with five weeks of content and programming hosted in the OpenAI San Francisco HQ, including in-person workshops, weekly office hours, and mentoring from OpenAI technical leaders. In addition to technical support and community, participants will also have the opportunity to get hands-on with new OpenAI tools and models prior to general availability. Following the program, participants will be able to explore raising capital or pursue another avenue, internally or externally to OpenAI.

该项目是构建长期协作网络的起点。项目首阶段为期五周，将在 OpenAI 旧金山总部举办，内容包括线下工作坊、每周办公时间（Office Hours）以及来自 OpenAI 技术负责人的导师指导。除技术支援与社群联结外，参与者还将有机会在相关工具与模型正式向公众发布前，率先上手体验全新的 OpenAI 工具与模型。项目结束后，参与者可自主选择启动融资进程，或以其他方式继续推进——无论是在 OpenAI 内部，还是外部独立发展。

The Grove cohort will consist of approximately fifteen participants. We recommend individuals from all backgrounds, disciplines, and experience levels apply. Please apply by January 12th, 2026.

每届 Grove 将遴选约十五名参与者。我们诚挚欢迎来自不同背景、学科领域及经验水平的申请者踊跃报名。请务必于 2026 年 1 月 12 日前完成申请。

## FAQ

## 常见问题（FAQ）

### When is the application deadline?

### 申请截止日期是什么时候？

Applications are due by January 12th, 2026.

申请截止日期为2026年1月12日。

### What are the program dates?

### 项目时间安排是怎样的？

The program runs Thursday, January 22nd, 2026 to Friday, February 27th, 2026.

项目时间为2026年1月22日（星期四）至2026年2月27日（星期五）。

Participants should plan for required in-person sessions during the first and last week, with an additional 4–6 hours of asynchronous work each week in between.

参与者需计划在第一周和最后一周参加强制性的线下活动，中间每周还需投入额外4–6小时进行异步学习与工作。

### I’m working with someone, can we apply as a team?

### 我正与他人合作，我们可以以团队形式申请吗？

Yes, we welcome groups to apply. Please let us know of your group entry via the form below.

可以，我们欢迎团队申请。请通过下方表格告知我们您的团队信息。

### Does my product need to be built on top of OpenAI’s APIs?

### 我的产品是否必须基于 OpenAI 的 API 构建？

No. The program is for anyone building or aspiring to build with AI.

不需要。本项目面向所有正在构建或有志于构建 AI 应用的个人或团队。

### What is the time commitment?

### 时间投入要求是多少？

Approximately 4–6 hours a week.

每周约需投入4–6小时。

### What are the program benefits?

### 项目有哪些优势？

The Grove is a community-based program designed to connect you to the mentors, resources, and peers that can accelerate your journey. As you build, we will offer additional benefits such as credits and introductions to raise capital.

“Grove”是一项以社区为基础的项目，旨在将您与导师、资源及同行连接起来，从而加速您的成长进程。在您开展构建工作的同时，我们还将提供额外支持，例如积分额度（credits）以及融资对接机会。

### Is the program in person?

### 该项目是否为线下形式？

The first and last week of the program take place in San Francisco. We will cover travel costs for these weeks. Other weeks will be completed asynchronously.

项目的第一周和最后一周将在旧金山举行，相关差旅费用由我们承担；其余时间则以异步方式进行。

### How is this program different from other startup programs?

### 本项目与其他创业扶持项目有何不同？

OpenAI Grove is designed for founders at the very earliest stages—whether you’re pre-idea, just starting out, or preparing to launch a company. [Pioneers](https://openai.com/index/openai-pioneers-program/) and [OpenAI for Startups](https://openai.com/startups/) are for established companies building in a specific domain.

OpenAI Grove 面向处于最早期阶段的创始人——无论您尚处于“想法萌芽期”、刚刚起步，还是正准备正式创立公司。而 [Pioneers](https://openai.com/index/openai-pioneers-program/) 和 [OpenAI for Startups](https://openai.com/startups/) 则面向已在特定领域开展业务的成熟企业。
