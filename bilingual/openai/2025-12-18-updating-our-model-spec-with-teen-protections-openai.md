# Updating our Model Spec with teen protections

# 在模型规范中新增面向青少年的保护措施

Informed by expert guidance, the U18 Principles outline how ChatGPT should support teens with safe, age-appropriate behavior.

在专家指导基础上制定的《18岁以下（U18）原则》明确了ChatGPT应如何以安全、适龄的方式支持青少年。

We’re sharing an update to our Model Spec, the written set of rules, values, and behavioral expectations that guides how we want our AI models to behave, especially in difficult or high stakes situations, with [Under-18 (U18) Principles⁠(opens in a new window)](http://model-spec.openai.com/2025-12-18.html). Model behavior is critical to how people interact with AI, and teens have different developmental needs than adults.

我们现发布《模型规范》（Model Spec）的更新版本——这是一套成文的规则、价值观与行为准则，旨在指导我们的AI模型（尤其在复杂或高风险情境下）应如何表现。本次更新特别纳入了[《18岁以下（U18）原则》（在新窗口中打开）](http://model-spec.openai.com/2025-12-18.html)。模型的行为表现深刻影响着用户与AI的互动方式；而青少年在身心发展方面的需求，与成年人存在显著差异。

The U18 Principles guide how ChatGPT should provide a safe, age-appropriate experience for teens aged 13 to 17. Grounded in developmental science, this approach prioritizes prevention, transparency, and early intervention. In developing these principles, we previewed them with external experts, including the American Psychological Association, as part of our ongoing work to seek input to strengthen our approach.

《U18原则》为ChatGPT面向13至17岁青少年提供安全、适龄体验提供了明确指引。该框架以发展科学为基础，强调预防为先、透明公开与早期干预。在制定这些原则过程中，我们邀请外部专家（包括美国心理学会APA）进行了预审，这也是我们持续广泛征询专业意见、不断优化实践的重要一环。

While the principles of the Model Spec continue to apply to both adult and teen users, this update clarifies how it should be applied in teen contexts, especially where safety considerations for minors may be more pronounced.

尽管《模型规范》所确立的各项原则对成年用户与青少年用户均具适用性，但本次更新进一步明确了其在青少年场景下的具体应用方式——尤其在涉及未成年人安全等关键议题时，相关考量需更为审慎和突出。

The U18 Principles are anchored in four guiding commitments:

《U18原则》植根于以下四项核心承诺：

- **Put teen safety first**, even when it may conflict with other goals  
- **将青少年安全置于首位**，即使此举可能与其他目标相冲突  

- **Promote real-world support** by encouraging offline relationships and trusted resources  
- **促进现实世界的支持**，鼓励建立线下人际关系，并引导使用可信赖的资源  

- **Treat teens like teens**, neither condescending to them nor treating them as adults  
- **以对待青少年的方式对待青少年**，既不居高临下、也不将其视作成人  

- **Be transparent** by setting clear expectations  
- **保持透明度**，通过设定清晰预期来实现

Consistent with our [Teen Safety Blueprint⁠](https://openai.com/index/introducing-the-teen-safety-blueprint/), these principles have guided our teen safety work to date, including the content protections we apply to users who tell us they are under 18 at sign up, and through parental controls. In these contexts, we’ve implemented safeguards to guide the model to take extra care when discussing higher-risk areas, including self-harm and suicide, romantic or sexualized roleplay, graphic or explicit content, dangerous activities and substances, body image and disordered eating, and requests to keep secrets about unsafe behavior.

与我们的[《青少年安全蓝图》⁠](https://openai.com/index/introducing-the-teen-safety-blueprint/)一脉相承，这些原则迄今一直指导着我们在青少年安全领域的工作，包括针对注册时告知我们其年龄未满18周岁的用户所实施的内容保护措施，以及通过家长控制功能所提供的保护。在上述场景中，我们已部署多重防护机制，引导模型在讨论高风险话题时格外审慎，这些话题涵盖：自残与自杀、浪漫化或性化角色扮演、露骨或色情内容、危险活动与物质、身体形象与进食障碍，以及要求对不安全行为保密的请求。

The American Psychological Association, which reviewed an early draft of the U18 Model Spec and offered important insights for the long term, is clear about the importance of protecting teens:

美国心理学会（APA）审阅了《U18模型规范》的早期草案，并就长远发展提供了重要洞见；该学会明确指出保护青少年至关重要：

“ _APA encourages AI developers to offer developmentally appropriate precautions for youth users of their products and to take a more protective approach for younger users.  Children and adolescents might benefit from AI tools if they are balanced with human interactions that science shows are critical for social, psychological, behavioral, and even biological development. Youth experiences with AI should be thoroughly supervised and discussed with trusted adults to encourage critical review of what AI bots offer, and to encourage young people’s development of independent thinking and skills._”— **Dr. Arthur C. Evans Jr, CEO, American Psychological Association**

“**美国心理学会鼓励人工智能开发者为其青少年用户提供符合其发展阶段的安全防护措施，并对更年幼的用户采取更具保护性的策略。如果人工智能工具能与人类互动相互平衡——而科学研究已证实，此类人际互动对青少年的社会性、心理、行为乃至生理发育均至关重要——那么儿童与青少年便可能从中获益。青少年使用人工智能的经历应得到充分监督，并与值得信赖的成年人展开深入探讨，以帮助他们批判性地审视AI聊天机器人所提供的内容，并促进其独立思考能力与技能的发展。**”  
——**小亚瑟·C·埃文斯博士（Dr. Arthur C. Evans Jr），美国心理学会首席执行官**

This update also clarifies how the assistant should respond when safety concerns arise for teens. This means teens should encounter stronger guardrails, safer alternatives, and encouragement to seek trusted offline support when conversations move into higher-risk territory. Where there is imminent risk, teens are urged to contact emergency services or crisis resources.

本次更新还进一步明确了当青少年面临安全风险时，助手应如何响应。这意味着：当对话进入高风险领域时，青少年将遇到更严格的防护限制、更安全的替代方案，并被积极鼓励寻求可信赖的线下支持。若存在即刻风险，青少年应立即联系紧急服务或危机援助资源。

As with the rest of the Model Spec, the U18 Principles reflect our intended model behavior. We will continue to refine them as we incorporate new research, expert input, and real-world use.

与《模型规范》其余部分一致，《U18原则》体现的是我们对模型预期行为的设计目标。我们将持续优化这些原则，不断融入最新研究成果、专家建议及真实世界中的应用反馈。

## Building on our work to strengthen teen safety

## 深化青少年安全建设工作

Alongside updating the Model Spec, we’ve taken a multi-layered approach to [strengthening teen safety⁠](https://openai.com/index/building-more-helpful-chatgpt-experiences-for-everyone/) across ChatGPT, spanning product safeguards, family support, and expert guidance.

在更新《模型规范》的同时，我们还采取了多层次策略，全面[强化ChatGPT平台上的青少年安全保护⁠](https://openai.com/index/building-more-helpful-chatgpt-experiences-for-everyone/)，覆盖产品级防护、家庭支持体系及专家专业指导。

Since rolling out [parental controls⁠(opens in a new window)](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/12315553-parental-controls-on-chatgpt-faq), we’ve extended protections across new products including group chats, the ChatGPT Atlas browser, and the Sora app. These updates help parents tailor their teen’s ChatGPT experience as we introduce new products and features.

自推出[家长控制功能⁠(在新窗口中打开)](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/12315553-parental-controls-on-chatgpt-faq)以来，我们已将相关保护措施拓展至多项新产品，包括群组聊天、ChatGPT Atlas浏览器以及Sora应用。这些更新有助于家长在我们陆续推出新产品与功能的过程中，灵活定制其子女使用ChatGPT的体验。

Consistent with expert guidance, we encourage ongoing conversations between parents and teens about healthy and responsible AI use in their family. To support these conversations, we’ve added new expert-vetted resources to the [parents resource hub⁠(opens in a new window)](https://chatgpt.com/parent-resources?openaicom-did=23f8f302-4f28-4e51-b276-f698e56e328f&openaicom_referred=true), including a [Family Guide to Help Teens Use AI Responsibly⁠(opens in a new window)](https://cdn.openai.com/pdf/a-family-guide-to-help-teens-use-ai-responsibly.pdf) and [tips for parents⁠(opens in a new window)](https://cdn.openai.com/pdf/tips-for-talking-to-your-teen-about-ai.pdf) on how to talk with their kids about AI, that were reviewed by ConnectSafely and members of our [Expert Council on Well-Being and AI⁠](https://openai.com/index/expert-council-on-well-being-and-ai/). We’ll continue adding more resources over time. We also support healthy use directly in the product, with [built-in break reminders⁠](https://openai.com/index/optimizing-chatgpt/) during long sessions to help keep time spent with ChatGPT intentional and balanced.

我们遵循专家建议，鼓励家长与青少年围绕家庭中健康、负责任地使用人工智能持续开展对话。为支持此类对话，我们已在[家长资源中心⁠(在新窗口中打开)](https://chatgpt.com/parent-resources?openaicom-did=23f8f302-4f28-4e51-b276-f698e56e328f&openaicom_referred=true)新增一批经专家审核的资源，其中包括：[《助力青少年负责任使用AI的家庭指南》⁠(在新窗口中打开)](https://cdn.openai.com/pdf/a-family-guide-to-help-teens-use-ai-responsibly.pdf)和[《与孩子谈论AI的家长提示》⁠(在新窗口中打开)](https://cdn.openai.com/pdf/tips-for-talking-to-your-teen-about-ai.pdf)。上述材料已由ConnectSafely组织及我们的[“福祉与人工智能”专家委员会⁠](https://openai.com/index/expert-council-on-well-being-and-ai/)成员审阅。未来我们将持续补充更多资源。此外，我们还在产品中直接嵌入健康使用支持功能，例如在长时间使用期间提供[内置休息提醒⁠](https://openai.com/index/optimizing-chatgpt/)，以帮助用户更有意识、更均衡地安排与ChatGPT的互动时间。

## Working with experts

## 与专家协同合作

Our work in teen safety is guided by close engagement with experts across disciplines and expertise.  

我们在青少年安全方面的工作，始终以与跨学科、多领域专家的深度协作与交流为指导。

In October, we established an [Expert Council on Well-Being and AI⁠](https://openai.com/index/expert-council-on-well-being-and-ai/) to help advise and define what healthy interactions with AI should look like for all ages.  

今年10月，我们成立了[“福祉与人工智能”专家委员会（Expert Council on Well-Being and AI）](https://openai.com/index/expert-council-on-well-being-and-ai/)，旨在为各年龄段用户构建健康、有益的人工智能交互模式提供专业建议与定义。

That work has informed guidance on parental controls and parent notifications.  

该项工作已直接推动了家长控制功能与家长通知机制的设计与优化。

We also incorporate clinical expertise through our [Global Physician Network⁠](https://openai.com/index/strengthening-chatgpt-responses-in-sensitive-conversations/) to inform safety research and evaluate model behavior, including improving how ChatGPT recognizes distress and guides people toward professional care when appropriate.  

此外，我们通过[全球医师网络（Global Physician Network）](https://openai.com/index/strengthening-chatgpt-responses-in-sensitive-conversations/)引入临床医学专业知识，以支撑安全研究、评估模型行为——例如提升ChatGPT识别用户心理困扰的能力，并在适当时机引导用户寻求专业医疗帮助。

We built on these foundations with [GPT‑5.2⁠](https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-5-2/), and we’ve also expanded access to real-world support by surfacing localized helplines in ChatGPT and Sora through our partnership with [ThroughLine⁠(opens in a new window)](https://www.throughlinecare.com/).  

我们在上述基础之上推出了[GPT‑5.2](https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-5-2/)；同时，依托与[ThroughLine（新窗口打开）](https://www.throughlinecare.com/)的合作，进一步拓展现实世界支持渠道——在ChatGPT和Sora中主动呈现本地化心理援助热线信息。

## What’s next

## 下一步计划

We’re in the early stages of rolling out an [age prediction model⁠(opens in a new window)](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/12652064-age-prediction-in-chatgpt) on ChatGPT consumer plans.  

目前，我们正处在面向ChatGPT个人用户计划推出[年龄预测模型（新窗口打开）](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/12652064-age-prediction-in-chatgpt)的初期阶段。

This will help us automatically apply teen safeguards when we believe an account belongs to a minor.  

该模型将帮助我们在判定某账户属于未成年人时，自动启用青少年保护机制。

If we are not confident about someone’s age or have incomplete information, we’ll default to an U18 experience and give adults ways to verify their age.  

若我们对用户年龄缺乏足够信心，或所掌握信息不完整，系统将默认提供面向18岁以下用户的体验（U18 experience），并为成年用户提供便捷的年龄验证途径。

Strengthening teen safety is ongoing work and we’ll continue to improve parental controls and model capabilities, expand resources for parents, work with organizations, researchers, and expert partners including the Well-Being Council and Global Physician Network.  

强化青少年安全是一项持续进行的工作。我们将持续优化家长控制功能与模型能力，扩充面向家长的支持资源，并携手各类组织、研究人员及专家合作伙伴——包括“福祉与人工智能”专家委员会与全球医师网络。

We’re committed to making strong teen protections and improving them over time to better support teens and families.  

我们坚定承诺：构建坚实可靠的青少年保护体系，并不断迭代升级，以更有效地支持青少年及其家庭。
