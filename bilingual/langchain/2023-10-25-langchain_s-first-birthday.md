It’s LangChain’s first birthday! It’s been a really exciting year!

LangChain 庆祝成立一周年！这是令人无比振奋的一年！

We worked with thousands of developers building LLM applications and tooling. We shipped and learned _a lot_. We grew our team. And best of all, we met so many people from all over the world exploring the possibilities of Generative AI.

我们与成千上万的开发者携手，共同构建大语言模型（LLM）应用与工具生态；我们发布了许多成果，也积累了大量经验；我们的团队不断壮大；而最令我们欣喜的是，结识了来自全球各地、一同探索生成式人工智能（Generative AI）无限可能的众多伙伴。

Today, we’re feeling especially grateful for our community. Here are some reflections and highlights from our first year building together.

今天，我们尤其感恩社区的支持。以下是我们携手共建第一年的思考与高光时刻。

Thank you.

谢谢！

## **In numbers**

## **数据速览**

- Over 2,000 developers contributed to LangChain across Python and JS libraries. Full list below.

- 超过 2,000 名开发者为 LangChain 的 Python 和 JavaScript 版本库贡献了代码。完整名单见下文。

- [Over 20M developers](https://www.pepy.tech/projects/langchain?ref=blog.langchain.com) downloaded the Open Source Package to power over [30k apps](https://github.com/langchain-ai/langchain?ref=blog.langchain.com)

- [超 2,000 万名开发者](https://www.pepy.tech/projects/langchain?ref=blog.langchain.com)下载了 LangChain 开源包，支撑了 [超过 3 万个应用](https://github.com/langchain-ai/langchain?ref=blog.langchain.com)的开发。

- With your help, we [connected LangChain with](https://integrations.langchain.com/?ref=blog.langchain.com) more than 150 document loaders, 70 LLMs, 100 tools, 50 vector stores, 40 embedding models, and 20 chat models.

- 在各位的支持下，我们[成功将 LangChain 接入](https://integrations.langchain.com/?ref=blog.langchain.com) 150 多种文档加载器、70 种大语言模型（LLM）、100 种工具、50 种向量数据库、40 种嵌入模型（embedding model）以及 20 种聊天模型。

- Hundreds of prompts were uploaded to [LangChain Prompt Hub](https://smith.langchain.com/hub?ref=blog.langchain.com)

- 数百个提示词（prompt）已上传至 [LangChain 提示词中心（Prompt Hub）](https://smith.langchain.com/hub?ref=blog.langchain.com)

## 一些备受推崇的研究论文

Thanks for advancing our thinking about Generative AI.

- [ReAct](https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.03629?ref=blog.langchain.com)：作为最早系统性探讨“如何提升大语言模型（LLM）行动决策能力”的论文之一

- [RestGPT](https://arxiv.org/abs/2306.06624?ref=blog.langchain.com)：探索“如何将大语言模型（LLM）接入任意 REST API”这一关键问题  
  - 作者：[Yifan Song](https://arxiv.org/search/cs?searchtype=author&query=Song,+Y&ref=blog.langchain.com)、[Weimin Xiong](https://arxiv.org/search/cs?searchtype=author&query=Xiong,+W&ref=blog.langchain.com)、[Dawei Zhu](https://arxiv.org/search/cs?searchtype=author&query=Zhu,+D&ref=blog.langchain.com)、[Wenhao Wu](https://arxiv.org/search/cs?searchtype=author&query=Wu,+W&ref=blog.langchain.com)、[Han Qian](https://arxiv.org/search/cs?searchtype=author&query=Qian,+H&ref=blog.langchain.com)、[Mingbo Song](https://arxiv.org/search/cs?searchtype=author&query=Song,+M&ref=blog.langchain.com)、[Hailiang Huang](https://arxiv.org/search/cs?searchtype=author&query=Huang,+H&ref=blog.langchain.com)、[Cheng Li](https://arxiv.org/search/cs?searchtype=author&query=Li,+C&ref=blog.langchain.com)、[Ke Wang](https://arxiv.org/search/cs?searchtype=author&query=Wang,+K&ref=blog.langchain.com)、[Rong Yao](https://arxiv.org/search/cs?searchtype=author&query=Yao,+R&ref=blog.langchain.com)、[Ye Tian](https://arxiv.org/search/cs?searchtype=author&query=Tian,+Y&ref=blog.langchain.com)、[Sujian Li](https://arxiv.org/search/cs?searchtype=author&query=Li,+S&ref=blog.langchain.com)
