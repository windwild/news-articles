Today, we’re thrilled to announce a collaboration between LangChain and Microsoft.

今天，我们非常激动地宣布 LangChain 与微软达成合作。

LangChain helps developers build context-aware reasoning applications and powers some of the most exciting products in AI. And Microsoft has repeatedly pushed the frontier of innovation and safety in technology broadly.

LangChain 旨在帮助开发者构建具备上下文感知能力的推理型应用，并为当前 AI 领域最具吸引力的若干产品提供核心支撑。而微软则长期在技术创新与技术安全性等广泛领域持续突破边界。

By collaborating more closely with Microsoft, we’re thrilled to offer our joint customers deeper product integrations that live up to our company’s commitment to AI with the enterprise assurances the Azure ecosystem provides. We’re also announcing LangChain’s inclusion in the [Microsoft for Startups Pegasus Program](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/startups/pegasus?ref=blog.langchain.com).

通过与微软开展更紧密的合作，我们非常高兴能为双方共同客户带来更深层次的产品集成——这些集成不仅践行了我们公司对人工智能的坚定承诺，也依托 Azure 生态系统所提供的企业级保障能力。同时，我们正式宣布 LangChain 已加入 [Microsoft for Startups Pegasus 计划](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/startups/pegasus?ref=blog.langchain.com)。

\_\_

\_\_

The AI industry is moving at a breakneck pace, and we’ve been impressed with how quickly large enterprises have mobilized to embrace this new wave. They look to LangChain because the framework provides speed of iteration, choice and flexibility, and the latest cognitive architectures and interaction patterns out-of-the-box.

AI 行业正以惊人的速度演进，而大型企业迅速响应、积极拥抱这一新浪潮的行动令我们印象深刻。他们选择 LangChain，是因为该框架开箱即用地提供了快速迭代能力、丰富的技术选型与高度灵活性，以及最新的认知架构与交互范式。

Startups to Fortune 500 companies already rely on LangChain to:

从初创公司到《财富》500 强企业，均已依托 LangChain 实现以下目标：

- build support assistants and co-pilots,
- 构建智能客服助手与 AI 协同助手（co-pilots），
- power natural language search and discovery experiences, and
- 打造自然语言驱动的搜索与发现体验，以及
- augment existing offerings with premium features to create new revenue streams.
- 为现有产品叠加高端功能，开辟全新收入来源。

While the promise of large language model (LLM) applications is business acceleration and productivity savings, the path to realizing those is hard and comes with new considerations and risks. That’s why we built [LangSmith](https://www.langchain.com/langsmith?ref=blog.langchain.com). LangSmith is a complementary SaaS product that helps manage the entire LLM-powered application lifecycle — all while improving developer productivity, decreasing time to production, and hardening applications to be more reliable at scale. For many enterprises, the combination of LangChain / LangSmith + Azure services (for example, [Azure Machine Learning](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/blog/building-for-the-future-the-enterprise-generative-ai-application-lifecycle-with-azure-ai/?ref=blog.langchain.com), [Azure AI Search](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/blog/building-for-the-future-the-enterprise-generative-ai-application-lifecycle-with-azure-ai/?ref=blog.langchain.com), and [Microsoft Fabric](https://ignite.microsoft.com/en-US/sessions/4980b805-a465-4624-a966-b1b67f32c900?source=sessions)) is the right toolkit to meet the opportunities and challenges of building applications for the GenAI world.

尽管大语言模型（LLM）应用有望显著加速业务发展并提升生产效率，但通往这一目标的道路却充满挑战，并伴随诸多新出现的考量因素与潜在风险。正因如此，我们推出了 [LangSmith](https://www.langchain.com/langsmith?ref=blog.langchain.com)。LangSmith 是一款互补型 SaaS 产品，可全面管理 LLM 驱动型应用的整个生命周期——同时提升开发者生产力、缩短上线周期，并增强应用在规模化部署下的可靠性与健壮性。对众多企业而言，“LangChain / LangSmith + Azure 服务”（例如：[Azure Machine Learning](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/blog/building-for-the-future-the-enterprise-generative-ai-application-lifecycle-with-azure-ai/?ref=blog.langchain.com)、[Azure AI Search](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/blog/building-for-the-future-the-enterprise-generative-ai-application-lifecycle-with-azure-ai/?ref=blog.langchain.com) 和 [Microsoft Fabric](https://ignite.microsoft.com/en-US/sessions/4980b805-a465-4624-a966-b1b67f32c900?source=sessions)）这一组合，正是应对生成式 AI（GenAI）时代应用开发所面临机遇与挑战的理想工具集。

> “Microsoft Azure is the most trusted AI Cloud for the enterprise, and we continue to invest in areas that strengthen our leadership position in language model operations and prompt orchestration. We’re excited to work closely with LangChain to streamline product adoption and to explore continued integration opportunities across the Azure and LangChain ecosystem to better serve our customers.” **– Eric Boyd, CVP of Azure AI Platform.**

> “Microsoft Azure 是最受企业信赖的 AI 云平台；我们持续加码投入，在大语言模型运维（LLMOps）与提示词编排（prompt orchestration）等领域进一步巩固自身领导地位。我们非常期待与 LangChain 紧密协作，加速产品落地进程，并深入探索 Azure 与 LangChain 生态系统之间的持续集成机会，从而更好地服务我们的客户。” **—— Eric Boyd，Azure AI 平台执行副总裁（CVP）**

> “We have tremendous respect for Microsoft and the AI services that they provide to enterprises across the world. We have many customers of LangSmith – Rakuten Group, Moody’s to name a couple — that build with Azure services, and we’re excited to meet them where they are.” – Harrison Chase, CEO LangChain.

> “我们对微软及其面向全球企业的 AI 服务怀有极高的敬意。LangSmith 的众多客户——例如乐天集团（Rakuten Group）、穆迪公司（Moody’s）等——均基于 Azure 服务进行开发；我们非常高兴能够‘就地’与他们携手合作。” —— Harrison Chase，LangChain 首席执行官

LangChain’s collaboration with Microsoft will give joint users and customers:

LangChain 与微软的合作将为双方用户和客户带来以下优势：

- **Deeper product integrations.** LangChain and Microsoft will continue to invest in breadth and depth of product integrations. Users of both benefit from LangChain’s orchestration and LangSmith’s monitoring of [Azure OpenAI Service](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/products/ai-services/openai-service/?ref=blog.langchain.com#overview), [Azure AI Search](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/products/ai-services/cognitive-search?ref=blog.langchain.com), [Azure AI Document Intelligence](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/products/ai-services/ai-document-intelligence?ref=blog.langchain.com), [Microsoft Presidio](https://microsoft.github.io/presidio/?ref=blog.langchain.com), and more. Microsoft and LangChain will also collaborate to design and identify improvement areas that will make LangServe easier to deploy on Azure.

- **更深层次的产品集成。** LangChain 与微软将持续投入资源，拓展并深化双方产品集成的广度与深度。双方用户均可受益于 LangChain 的编排能力以及 LangSmith 对 [Azure OpenAI Service](https://azure.microsoft.com/zh-cn/products/ai-services/openai-service/?ref=blog.langchain.com#overview)、[Azure AI Search](https://azure.microsoft.com/zh-cn/products/ai-services/cognitive-search?ref=blog.langchain.com)、[Azure AI Document Intelligence](https://azure.microsoft.com/zh-cn/products/ai-services/ai-document-intelligence?ref=blog.langchain.com)、[Microsoft Presidio](https://microsoft.github.io/presidio/?ref=blog.langchain.com) 等服务的监控能力。此外，微软与 LangChain 还将协同开展设计工作，识别优化方向，使 LangServe 在 Azure 上的部署更加便捷。

- **Ease of procurement of LangSmith in Azure Marketplace.** After completion of the Microsoft for Startups Pegasus Program, LangChain will offer customers the ability to procure LangSmith in the Azure Marketplace as a transactable offering, and customers will be able to retire their Microsoft Azure Consumption Commitment (MACC) on their purchase.

- **在 Azure Marketplace 中便捷采购 LangSmith。** 在完成微软“面向初创企业的飞马计划（Microsoft for Startups Pegasus Program）”后，LangChain 将在 Azure Marketplace 中以可交易（transactable）产品形式提供 LangSmith，客户可在采购时直接抵扣其微软 Azure 消费承诺（Microsoft Azure Consumption Commitment, MACC）。

- **Data security peace-of-mind with LangSmith deployments within the customer’s Azure VPC.** For enterprises especially, data security and privacy are non-negotiables. Many customers want full control over their data, and LangSmith will be offered as a transactable Azure Application, deployed wholly in the customer’s VPC.

- **LangSmith 部署于客户 Azure VPC 内，保障数据安全无忧。** 对企业客户而言，数据安全与隐私保护是不可妥协的核心要求。许多客户希望对其数据拥有完全控制权；为此，LangSmith 将作为一款可交易的 Azure 应用程序提供，并完整部署于客户的 Azure 虚拟私有云（VPC）之中。

We’re at the starting line of a fantastic collaboration that will benefit our joint customers greatly. If your company is using LangChain / LangSmith and Microsoft Azure, we want to support you better; get in touch and tell us how at hello@langchain.dev.

我们正站在一场卓越合作的起跑线上，此次合作将极大惠及双方共同客户。若您所在公司正在使用 LangChain / LangSmith 及 Microsoft Azure，我们期待为您提供更优质的支持——欢迎通过 hello@langchain.dev 与我们联系，并告诉我们您的具体需求。
