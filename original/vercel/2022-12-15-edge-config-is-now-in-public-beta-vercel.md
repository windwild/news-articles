![](images/edge-config-is-now-in-public-beta-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/edge-config-is-now-in-public-beta-vercel/img_002.jpg)

Edge Config allows you to distribute data to our Edge Network without needing to perform a deployment. Instead, your data is actively replicated to all our regions before it’s requested. This means your data will always be available instantly, with most lookups returning in 5 ms or less, and 99% of reads returning under 15 ms **.**

Edge Config is now available in public beta—you can get started with our new [Statsig](https://vercel.com/integrations/statsig) and [HappyKit](https://vercel.com/integrations/happykit) integrations, [Edge Config SDK](https://github.com/vercel/edge-config), or by using [one of our examples](https://vercel.com/templates?type=edge-config).

[Check out the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/edge-network/edge-config) to learn more.
