Feb 25, 2026

The [activity log](https://vercel.com/docs/activity-log) now captures every change made to your team and project settings, giving you complete visibility into who changed what and when.

Previously, some settings changes went untracked. With 88 new events added, activity log coverage is now 100%, so no action goes unrecorded.

[Try it out](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%7E%2Factivity) or learn more about the [activity log](https://vercel.com/docs/activity-log).
