Apr 24, 2024

![](images/improved-team-onboarding-experience-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/improved-team-onboarding-experience-vercel/img_002.jpg)

It’s now easier to join a team on Vercel. New team members no longer need to re-enter their email, or create a Hobby team or Pro trial. Team invite emails now lead to a sign up page customized for the team. This includes simplified sign up options that reflect the team's [SSO settings](https://vercel.com/docs/security/saml).

You can invite new team members under "Members" in your team settings. [Learn more about managing team members in the documentation.](https://vercel.com/docs/accounts/team-members-and-roles/managing-team-members)
