Apr 8, 2025

New native integrations from Sentry, Checkly, and Dash0 are now available on the Vercel Marketplace

The Vercel Marketplace adds new integrations from [Sentry](https://vercel.com/marketplace/sentry), [Checkly](https://vercel.com/marketplace/checkly), and [Dash0](https://vercel.com/marketplace/dash0). You can now use the tools you already trust to monitor, measure, and debug your apps. No custom setup. No change to how you build or deploy.

These tools connect directly through the [Vercel Marketplace](https://vercel.com/marketplace) with integrated billing, single sign-on, and access to provider dashboards, giving you deep visibility without the setup overhead.

![](images/expanding-observability-on-vercel-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/expanding-observability-on-vercel-vercel/img_002.jpg)

### A unified, flexible workflow

Great web experiences don’t stop at the code. They involve understanding what happens after deploy. We believe developers should be able to choose the best tool for the job, and that includes observability. Whether you're debugging a [Vercel Function](https://vercel.com/docs/functions), tracking frontend performance, or running synthetic checks on your production site, you can now do it all from the same place where you deploy your code.

This launch also introduces Log Drains for [native integrations](https://vercel.com/docs/integrations#native-integrations)—a capability that was previously only available to [connectable accounts](https://vercel.com/docs/integrations#connectable-accounts). Dash0 is the first to provide support for this feature. Users of the Dash0 integration will also get support for [Log Drains](https://vercel.com/docs/log-drains), allowing you to send logs from your Vercel projects to Dash0 for deeper insights and centralized monitoring.

![](images/expanding-observability-on-vercel-vercel/img_003.jpg)![](images/expanding-observability-on-vercel-vercel/img_004.jpg)

### Built for developers, not just operators

We're bringing observability tools closer to where development happens, eliminating context switching or managing multiple dashboards. Add your favorite integration from the Marketplace and start getting integrated value immediately.

Whether you need alerts for production regressions, want to track performance over time, or are just looking for a faster way to debug issues, Vercel makes it easier to bring observability into your development process on your terms.

**Explore the** [**observability integrations on the Vercel Marketplace**](https://vercel.com/marketplace?category=observability) **.** If you're a service provider interested in joining the Marketplace, you can fill out our [provider application](https://vercel.com/marketplace-providers).
