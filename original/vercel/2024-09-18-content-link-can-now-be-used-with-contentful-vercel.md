![](images/content-link-can-now-be-used-with-contentful-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/content-link-can-now-be-used-with-contentful-vercel/img_002.jpg)

With [Content Link](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/edit-mode#content-link), previously known as Visual Editing, you can click-to-edit content on your Vercel site, with a direct link to exactly where your content lives in your CMS.

This functionality is now available for Pro and Enterprise customers using Contentful as their CMS and are on their Premium plan. To see all supported CMSs, [visit our docs](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/edit-mode#content-link).

When enabled, Contentful’s APIs return [source maps](https://www.contentful.com/developers/docs/tools/vercel/content-source-maps-with-vercel/) for certain visual fields that have links to the correct field in Contentful, such as rich text, description fields, and lists. Markdown is currently not supported.

[Check out the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/integrations/cms/contentful#content-link) to get started.
