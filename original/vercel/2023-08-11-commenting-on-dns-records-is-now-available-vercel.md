![](images/commenting-on-dns-records-is-now-available-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/commenting-on-dns-records-is-now-available-vercel/img_002.jpg)

You can now leave comments when creating a new DNS record in Vercel. In addition, you can also edit comments on existing DNS records.

New records created by an Email Preset will include a comment explaining why a record was added.

[Check out our documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/projects/domains/managing-dns-records) to learn more about DNS records.
