![](images/visual-editing-can-now-be-used-with-builder_io-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/visual-editing-can-now-be-used-with-builder_io-vercel/img_002.jpg)

With [Visual Editing](https://vercel.com/blog/visual-editing) you can click-to-edit content on your Vercel site, with a direct link to exactly where your content lives in your CMS.

This functionality is now possible for Enterprise customers using [Builder.io](https://www.builder.io/) as their CMS. Builder.io is now the second CMS to adopt content source-mapping technology that enables Visual Editing from a [headless CMS](https://vercel.com/blog/your-guide-to-headless-commerce) with zero code changes to your website.

Check out the [documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/visual-editing#get-started) to learn more or [contact us for access](https://vercel.com/contact/sales).
