### Understanding the benefits of Vercel Marketplace integrations

The Vercel Marketplace allows you to integrate services directly into your Vercel projects, without leaving your Vercel dashboard, giving you:

1. **Ease of use**: Developers can now deploy and integrate Supabase directly within the Vercel environment, reducing setup time and complexity.

2. **Frictionless onboarding**: With the Marketplace’s seamless onboarding, developers can start using Supabase in less than 60 seconds via the dashboard and CLI (`vc i supabase`), maintaining high fidelity and an exceptional developer experience. Users can access the Supabase dashboard with just one click, driving higher adoption rates and improved user satisfaction.

3. **Integrated billing**: Users can manage billing for Supabase directly through their Vercel accounts, streamlining the financial management process.


### **Business impact for Supabase**

Since launching on the Vercel Marketplace, Supabase has seen a marked increase in adoption from Vercel’s developer base. The seamless integration and frictionless onboarding process have led to accelerated sign-ups, making it easier for developers to adopt both platforms in their projects.
