Based on your feedback, Hobby customers can now run functions **up to 60 seconds**.

Starting today, all new deployments will be able to increase the maximum duration of functions on the free tier from 10 seconds up to 60 seconds. If you need longer than 60 seconds, you can upgrade to Pro for [up to 5 minutes](https://vercel.com/changelog/serverless-functions-can-now-run-up-to-5-minutes).

[Check out our documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/functions/configuring-functions/duration) to learn more.
