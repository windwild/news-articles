![](images/domain-renewal-status-filtering-now-available-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/domain-renewal-status-filtering-now-available-vercel/img_002.jpg)

You can now filter your account's Domains based on renewal status. This makes it easier to quickly determine which of your domains is set to expire soon or has an upcoming renewal.

[Check out the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/projects/domains/renew-a-domain) to learn more.
