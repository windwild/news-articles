![](images/included-pro-usage-is-now-credit-based-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/included-pro-usage-is-now-credit-based-vercel/img_002.jpg)

The Pro plan now includes $20 in monthly usage credit instead of fixed allocations across metrics like data transfer, compute, caching, and more. This plan update replaces static usage buckets with a more flexible system that adapts to your workload.

In addition to the above, the new Pro pricing model includes:

- [**Free viewer seats**](https://vercel.com/changelog/free-viewer-seats-now-available-on-pro)

- **Self-serve enterprise features like** [**SAML SSO**](https://vercel.com/changelog/saml-sso-is-now-available-to-pro-teams) **and** [**HIPAA BAA**](https://vercel.com/changelog/hipaa-baas-are-now-available-to-pro-teams)

- [**Better Spend Management, enabled by default**](https://vercel.com/changelog/spend-management-now-enabled-by-default-on-pro)


Read more about the [updates to Pro](https://vercel.com/blog/new-pro-pricing-plan) and [switch to the new Pro pricing](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%7E%2Fsettings%2Fbilling&title=Switch+to+new+Pro+pricing).
