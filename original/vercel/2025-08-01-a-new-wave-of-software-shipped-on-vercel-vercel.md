Aug 1, 2025

Highlighting the teams shipping AI, platforms, apps, and more

[Shipped on Vercel](https://vercel.com/shipped) showcases real apps in production on Vercel, built by teams rethinking how the web works.

![](images/a-new-wave-of-software-shipped-on-vercel-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/a-new-wave-of-software-shipped-on-vercel-vercel/img_002.jpg)

What gets built on Vercel spans categories and disciplines. AI assistants, internal dashboards, developer tools, creative workflows, consumer apps. Some are minimal and some are complex, while some are built by one person and others by teams of hundreds. All of them share a high standard of craft and a clear sense of what makes a great experience.

We’re seeing a new wave of software that’s thoughtful, expressive, and opinionated. Interfaces feel intentional and interactions feel immediate, while the architecture behind them is invisible to the user, but scales effortlessly.

The first showcase wave includes work from Browserbase, Chatbase, Cursor, Decagon, Devin, Exa, [Frame.io](http://frame.io/), Hume, Leonardo, OpenAI, Polymarket, Ramp, Sonos, Suno, and Whop.
