The Active Branches view for Deployments has an updated design which is now filtered on open branches with deployments. This update allows you to access your work in progress, surfaces useful data including deployment status and [comments](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/comments) metadata, and also gives you a quick link to your [Preview Deployment](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/preview-deployments).

Check out [the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/projects/project-dashboard#active-branches) to learn more.
