Oct 29, 2025

We’re proud to share that Vercel has successfully completed its assessment for the [Trusted Information Security Assessment Exchange](https://enx.com/en-US/TISAX/) (TISAX) Level 2 (AL2). This milestone reinforces our commitment to delivering secure, reliable, and compliant infrastructure to our global customers, particularly those in the automotive and manufacturing sectors that require specific security and data protections. This achievement builds on our broader compliance program, which includes ISO/IEC 270001:2022, SOC 2 Type II, PCI DSS, HIPAA and more.

## What is TISAX?

TISAX is an internationally-recognized standard developed by the [German Association of the Automotive Industry (VDA)](https://www.vda.de/en) and governed by the [ENX Association](https://enx.com/en-US/). TISAX streamlines information security assessments across complex supply chains, allowing automotive Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs), suppliers, and service providers to evaluate partners through a unified framework that drives trust and efficiency.

Vercel’s security and privacy practices were independently reviewed by an ENX-accredited auditor against TISAX Level 2 (AL2) criteria. This level affirms Vercel’s commitment to safeguarding sensitive information and maintaining strong data protection standards across automotive and manufacturing supply chains.

If you’re an automotive manufacturer, supplier, or partner working in a regulated environment, Vercel provides a secure cloud platform to help you build and scale applications with confidence.

By working with Vercel, automotive partners benefit from:

- **Trusted use of Vercel within automotive supply chains:** Meet OEM and supplier requirements by working with service providers with TISAX labels

- **Simplified and accelerated procurement:** Customers and partners can rely on Vercel's TISAX results within the ENX portal to meet its third party service provider assessment needs for faster vendor onboarding

- **Alignment with established security expectations:** Demonstrate compliance with industry-accepted practices for data protection, risk management, and secure development

- **Confidence in cloud security:** Leverage Vercel’s infrastructure for building, deploying, and scaling applications that handle confidential or regulated information
