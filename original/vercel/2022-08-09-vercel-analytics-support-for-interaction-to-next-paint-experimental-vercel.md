Vercel Analytics now supports measuring [Interaction to Next Paint (INP)](https://web.dev/inp/).

INP measures your site’s responsiveness to user interactions on the page. The faster your page responds to user input – the better. INP is an experimental metric to develop a better way of measuring responsiveness than [First Input Delay (FID)](https://vercel.com/blog/core-web-vitals#first-input-delay).

[Try Vercel Analytics today](https://vercel.com/analytics) to start measuring your performance.
