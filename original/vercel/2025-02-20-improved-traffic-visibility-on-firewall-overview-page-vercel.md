![](images/improved-traffic-visibility-on-firewall-overview-page-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/improved-traffic-visibility-on-firewall-overview-page-vercel/img_002.jpg)

The Vercel Firewall overview page now shows improved visibility into your traffic and the Firewall status. Navigate to [your Firewall page](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%5Bproject%5D%2Ffirewall&title=Firewall+tab) to see:

- Status of the system firewall

- A warning banner if a [reverse proxy](https://vercel.com/guides/can-i-use-a-proxy-on-top-of-my-vercel-deployment) is inhibiting Vercel's ability to protect your site

- Tabbed view for easier traffic filtering

- Rules displayed below the chart with better readability


The Vercel Firewall automatically mitigates DDoS attacks for all Vercel deployments. You can further secure your site with [custom rules and IP blocking](https://vercel.com/docs/security/vercel-waf), and by turning on [Attack Challenge Mode](https://vercel.com/docs/security/attack-challenge-mode) when under high-volume attacks.

Learn more about the [Vercel Firewall](https://vercel.com/docs/security/vercel-firewall).
