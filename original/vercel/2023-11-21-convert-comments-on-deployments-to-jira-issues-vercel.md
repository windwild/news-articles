![](images/convert-comments-on-deployments-to-jira-issues-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/convert-comments-on-deployments-to-jira-issues-vercel/img_002.jpg)

[Comments](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/comments) on your deployments can now be converted into [Jira issues](https://vercel.com/integrations/jira). This makes it easy to take action on feedback in the workflows your team is already using.

You can name your issue and select the project and issue type without leaving your deployment. Issues retain the full thread history with any attached images and include a link back to where the comment was left.

[Jira](https://vercel.com/integrations/jira) is part of our growing collection of integrations for comments which includes [Slack](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/comments/integrations#use-the-vercel-slack-app) and [Linear](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/comments/integrations#use-the-vercel-comments-linear-integration), available to Pro and Enterprise users as well as Hobby users with public git repositories.

[Check out the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/comments/integrations#using-the-vercel-comments-jira-integration) to learn more.
