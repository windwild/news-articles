![](images/unlimited-custom-domains-for-all-pro-teams-vercel/img_001.jpg)

You can now add unlimited [Custom Domains](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/projects/custom-domains) to your project on a Pro team. This enables creators, entrepreneurs, and platforms on Vercel to create the next big thing. To help enable this, we've created a [Platforms Starter Kit](https://demo.vercel.pub/platforms-starter-kit).

Try out [the demo](https://demo.vercel.pub/platforms-starter-kit) and [create your own](https://vercel.com/guides/nextjs-multi-tenant-application) platform.
