[Visual Editing](https://vercel.com/blog/visual-editing) from Vercel allows you to click-to-edit content on your site, with a direct link to exactly where your content lives in your CMS.

This functionality is now available for Enterprise customers using [DatoCMS](https://www.datocms.com/) as their CMS. DatoCMS is now the fourth CMS to adopt content source-mapping technology that enables Visual Editing from a headless CMS with zero code changes to your website.

[Check out the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/visual-editing#get-started) to learn more or [contact us for access](https://vercel.com/contact/sales).
