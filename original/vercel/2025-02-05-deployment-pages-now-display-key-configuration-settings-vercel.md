![](images/deployment-pages-now-display-key-configuration-settings-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/deployment-pages-now-display-key-configuration-settings-vercel/img_002.jpg)

Project Overview and Deployment Details pages now include a Deployment Configuration section under the deployment card.

Expand to view snapshots of [Fluid Compute](https://vercel.com/docs/functions/fluid-compute), [Function CPU](https://vercel.com/docs/functions/configuring-functions/memory#configuring-memory-and-cpu-for-vercel-functions), [Deployment Protection](https://vercel.com/docs/security/deployment-protection#deployment-protection-on-vercel), [Skew Protection](https://vercel.com/docs/deployments/skew-protection), and [Secure Compute](https://vercel.com/docs/security/secure-compute#vercel-secure-compute) settings.

This section is available for all new deployments moving forward. It will appear on your Project Overview page after your next production deployment.
