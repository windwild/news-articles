Oct 22, 2025

Turbo build machines are now available for all paid plans, offering our fastest build performance yet with 30 vCPUs and 60GB of memory.

Turbo machines are ideal for [Turbopack](https://nextjs.org/docs/app/api-reference/turbopack) builds, and large [monorepos](https://vercel.com/docs/monorepos) that run tasks in parallel, accelerating static generation and dependency resolution.

[Enable Turbo build machines](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%5Bproject%5D%2Fsettings%2Fbuild-and-deployment%23build-machine&title=Enable+Turbo+build+machines) per project, with usage-based pricing.

Learn more in [the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/builds/managing-builds#build-machine-types).
