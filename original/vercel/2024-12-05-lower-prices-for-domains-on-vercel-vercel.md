Dec 5, 2024

We have lowered the prices for purchasing domains by **up to 50%**.

Vercel offers hundreds of top-level domains (TLDs) for purchase, including the most popular TLDs like `.com` and [our most recent addition](https://vercel.com/changelog/vercel-now-supports-ai-domains) of `.ai` domains.

Vercel automatically configures and manages nameservers and SSL certificates for your domain, with fast domain search and automatic DNS setup for easy deployment of your next idea.

[Buy your first domain](https://vercel.com/domains) or [explore all supported domains](https://vercel.com/docs/projects/domains/supported-domains#supported-domains-for-purchase).
