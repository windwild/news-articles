Jun 25, 2024

v0 now supports themes.

You can create custom themes from prompts, modify individual design tokens, and switch between different themes for your generations. For example, try out our theme for Windows 95. v0 supports all default [Shadcn UI themes](https://ui.shadcn.com/themes).

[Try out v0 today](https://v0.dev/) and build your own theme.
