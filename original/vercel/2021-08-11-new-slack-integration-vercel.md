Aug 11, 2021

![](images/new-slack-integration-vercel/img_001.jpg)

We’re happy to share that Vercel's **new and improved Slack Integration** is now ready for use.

It can be installed from the [Integration Marketplace](https://vercel.com/integrations/slack) and comes with a number of advantages over the previous Slack Integration. You can now:

- Choose specific events you’re interested in.

- Send all events to one Slack channel or to multiple on a per-project basis.

- Send events to private Slack channels.


If you're currently using the previous legacy Slack Integration (you can tell by opening the Integration's configuration view from your Personal Account or Team dashboard), please remove it and [add the new one](https://vercel.com/integrations/slack), as the **legacy Integration will stop working on August 20th**.

Furthermore, if you're using the legacy Integration, you will also receive a notification directly on your Slack channel about the next steps you can take to upgrade to its latest version.
