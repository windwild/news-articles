[**Braintrust**](https://vercel.com/marketplace/braintrust) is now available on the Vercel Marketplace, bringing AI evaluation and observability directly into the Vercel workflow.

With this new integration, developers can automatically stream [traces](https://vercel.com/changelog/introducing-trace-drains-on-the-vercel-marketplace) and evaluation data from Vercel to Braintrust with just a few clicks, gaining full visibility into model quality and user experience in real time.

With [Braintrust](https://www.braintrust.dev/) on Vercel Marketplace, you can:

- Ship agents and AI features with built-in evaluation and observability

- Run evals and monitor model quality in production

- Benchmark and compare performance across LLMs
