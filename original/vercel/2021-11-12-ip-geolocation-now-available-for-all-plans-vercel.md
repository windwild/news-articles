Nov 12, 2021

![](images/ip-geolocation-now-available-for-all-plans-vercel/img_001.jpg)

IP Geolocation is now available on all plans, [Hobby, Pro and Enterprise teams](https://vercel.com/pricing), for requests received by Edge and Serverless Functions.

In this Edge Functions [example](https://edge-functions-geolocation.vercel.sh/), you can determine a user's location.

Check out [our documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/edge-network/headers#x-vercel-ip-city) to learn more.
