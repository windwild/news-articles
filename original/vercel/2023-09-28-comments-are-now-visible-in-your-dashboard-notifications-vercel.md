![](images/comments-are-now-visible-in-your-dashboard-notifications-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/comments-are-now-visible-in-your-dashboard-notifications-vercel/img_002.jpg)

You can now receive and view [Comment](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/comments) notifications in the Vercel dashboard.

﻿ [Notifications](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/comments/managing-comments#notifications) for new Comments are shown in the dashboard with a counter on the bell icon. You can quickly resolve Comments there or filter by specific pages, branches, or authors.

﻿ [Check out our documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/comments/managing-comments#notifications) to learn more.
