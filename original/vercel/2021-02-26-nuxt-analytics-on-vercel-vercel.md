Feb 26, 2021

Monitor your page speeds over time with reporting from real user experiences.

Since the last Next.js Conf, we have expanded Vercel analytics offerings to include [Next.js](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics#next.js-(v10+)) and [Gatsby](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics#gatsby-(v2+)). Today, we expand that offering to include **Nuxt analytics**, providing developers with their [Real Experience Score](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics#real-experience-score) through data from actual visitors.

## Get Started

Nuxt analytics for projects deployed to Vercel are available with zero configuration:

1. **Open a Nuxt project in your** [**Vercel dashboard**](https://vercel.com/dashboard?analytics=1&utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics)

2. **Select the "Analytics" tab and follow the flow**


No code changes are required ( [docs](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics)), and [options for self-hosted applications](https://vercel.com/contact/sales?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics) are available on the [Enterprise plan](https://vercel.com/contact/sales).

Once deployed, your application will automatically report [Core Web Vitals](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics#web-vitals?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics) to Vercel.

![An example of the Analytics tab on the project view.](images/nuxt-analytics-on-vercel-vercel/img_001.jpg)An example of the Analytics tab on the project view.

Next.js, Gatsby, and Nuxt are the first three [frameworks supported](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics#supported-frameworks?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics). [Let us know on Twitter](https://twitter.com/vercel) which frameworks you'd like to see next.

Next.js, Gatsby, and Nuxt analytics are new essentials for ensuring your sites continue to perform beyond initial launch testing.

Not yet on Vercel? [Import your Nuxt project today](https://vercel.com/import/git?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics).
