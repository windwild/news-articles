![](images/improved-monorepo-support-in-recent-previews-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/improved-monorepo-support-in-recent-previews-vercel/img_002.jpg)

When you make a commit to a monorepo, the Recent Previews section on your team overview page will now show an expandable row containing preview, source, and deployment links for all deployments triggered by your commit, across all projects.

Recent Previews gives you easy access to the previews you have viewed or deployed recently. Learn more in the [dashboard overview](https://vercel.com/docs/dashboard-features#recent-previews) documentation.
