Deploying your Serverless Functions in a region close to your data can greatly improve performance by reducing latency.

Previously, Hobby customers could only choose [US East](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/functions/serverless-functions/regions) (`iad1`) regardless of where their data was hosted. Starting today, all plans can co-locate their Functions with their data for lower latency when server-rendering with hybrid frameworks like Next.js, SvelteKit, and more, or when using API Routes.

[Check out the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/functions/serverless-functions/regions) as well.
