Jan 13, 2026

It's a thrilling time to work in Sales at Vercel. The web is transitioning from pages to agents, and Vercel is building the self-driving infrastructure to power it. We've assembled a Sales organization that equally understands the continually shifting technical landscape and pressing business needs to stay flexible, move fast, and be secure in the AI era.

We're rethinking how Sales operates, and we're building the most AI-forward go-to-market organization in the industry. To lead this charge, we're welcoming **Nick Bogaty** as our Chief Revenue Officer.

![Chief Revenue Officer Nick Bogaty](images/nick-bogaty-joins-vercel-as-chief-revenue-officer-vercel/img_001.jpg)

Chief Revenue Officer Nick Bogaty

## Built for every stage of growth

Nick brings more than two decades of experience building and scaling enterprise GTM organizations, and he'll lead our global Sales team as we deepen relationships with the world's largest enterprises and fastest growing startups alike.

Nick has a diverse background. He has built teams from scratch, scaled them through hypergrowth, and run large global organizations. He joins Vercel from Adobe, where he started and scaled the enterprise GTM for Adobe Express, competing with Canva in the enterprise. Before that, he served as CRO at AppDynamics, growing the business from $100M to $700M through its acquisition by Cisco and leading a 1,250-person team spanning sales and post-sales globally. He also served as CRO at Instabug, a Series B startup.

Like many at Vercel, Nick is a founder. Nick co-founded Adobe Digital Publishing Suite, the largest homegrown Adobe business since Acrobat at the time. He also developed the EPUB standard for electronic books while serving as Executive Director of the International Digital Publishing Forum (IDPF), a format still used today by Apple and Amazon. He even holds a patent for an electronic book distribution system.
