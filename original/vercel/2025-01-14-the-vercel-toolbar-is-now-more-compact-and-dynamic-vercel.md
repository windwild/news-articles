The Vercel Toolbar has a new compact design, making it easier to access the tools you use most.

- **Compact design:** Thetoolbar is now smaller and only runs when you click or tap to activate it or when visiting from a link that contains a comment thread, draft link, or flag override

- ​ **Shortcuts:** Your most recently used tools will pin to the top of your menu for easy access

- **Visit with Toolbar:** When visitingprojects and deployments from the dashboard, you'll see a "Visit" button that gives you the option to load the toolbar upon opening

- ​ **Browser extension controls:** Users with the browser extension enabled can control when the toolbar is active or hidden under "Preferences" in the toolbar menu


Learn more about the [Vercel Toolbar](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/vercel-toolbar) and its features.
