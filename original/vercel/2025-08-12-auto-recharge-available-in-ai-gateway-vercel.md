[Vercel AI Gateway](https://vercel.com/ai-gateway) now supports automatic credit recharging (top-ups), optionally refilling your balance before it runs out to keep your apps running without interruption.

Auto-recharge is off by default and can be enabled or updated anytime in the [AI Gateway dashboard](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%7E%2Fai&title=Enable+auto-recharge) or [team billing settings](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%7E%2Fsettings%2Fbilling&title=Enable+auto-recharge). Set your top-up amount and trigger balance, optionally add a monthly spend limit, and your credits will automatically refill.

Learn more about [AI Gateway](https://vercel.com/docs/ai-gateway).
