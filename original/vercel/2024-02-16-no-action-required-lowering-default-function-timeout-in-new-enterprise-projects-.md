Feb 16, 2024

The default Vercel Function timeout of all new projects for Enterprise customers will be reduced to 15 seconds on Feb 20th. This change helps prevent unintentional function usage, unless explicitly opted into the [longer function duration](https://vercel.com/docs/functions/configuring-functions/duration) (up to 15 minutes).

**Existing Enterprise projects will not have their defaults changed.**

[Check out our documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/functions/configuring-functions/duration) to learn more.
