We are excited to announce our integration marketplace has nine new additions:

- [**Highlight**](https://vercel.com/integrations/highlight): send sourcemaps to Highlight for better debugging

- [**Inngest**](https://vercel.com/integrations/inngest): run Vercel functions as background jobs or Cron jobs

- [**Knock**](https://vercel.com/integrations/knock): add Knock’s notification system to your application

- [**Novu**](https://vercel.com/integrations/novu): add real-time notifications to your app

- [**Sitecore XM Cloud**](https://vercel.com/integrations/sitecore-xm-cloud): deploy to Vercel from Sitecore’s headless CMS

- [**Svix**](https://vercel.com/integrations/svix): add Svix’s webhook service to your application

- [**TiDB Cloud**](https://vercel.com/integrations/tidb-cloud): connect your app to a TiDB Cloud cluster

- [**Tigris Data**](https://vercel.com/integrations/tigris): connect a Tigris database to your Vercel project

- [**Zeitgeist**](https://vercel.com/integrations/zeitgeist): manage deployments from mobile


The integration marketplace allows you to extend and automate your workflow by integrating with your favorite tools.

Explore these integrations and more at our [Integrations Marketplace](https://vercel.com/integrations).
