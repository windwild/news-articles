Aug 22, 2022

![](https://vercel.com/vc-ap-vercel-marketing/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fassets.vercel.com%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fcontentful%2Fimage%2Fe5382hct74si%2F4YHG2hHdsLAiKI3YBIPySB%2F4336bad487699e5dd4e034af5c8d54f3%2Fpapercuts-light.png&w=1920&q=75)![](images/improvements-and-fixes-vercel/img_002.jpg)

With your feedback, we've shipped dozens of bug fixes and small feature requests to improve your product experience.

- **Vercel CLI:** [v28 was released](https://vercel.com/changelog/vercel-cli-v28) with new commands and bug fixes.

- **Integrations:** Team Owners can now transfer ownership of integrations installed on a Team to another member. This helps prevent disruption of work when a member leaves a Team.

- **Domain emails:** Domain email notifications are now only sent to account owners. This includes domain transfer, expiration, and renewal emails.

- **Incremental Static Regeneration logs:** Function logs from [Incremental Static Regeneration](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/next.js/incremental-static-regeneration) now appear in the Vercel.com console, making it easier to understand when your pages are revalidated and monitor the usage of your revalidation functions.

- **Usage summaries:** [Usage summaries](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/limits/usage) for Hobby accounts are now available in Account Settings → Billing.

- **Branch URLs on mobile:** The deployment overview now includes a popover that lists [branch URLs](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/generated-urls#automatic-branch-urls) so that you can easily access them on your mobile device.
