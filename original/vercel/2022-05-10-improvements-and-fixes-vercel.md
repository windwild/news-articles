May 10, 2022

![](images/improvements-and-fixes-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/improvements-and-fixes-vercel/img_002.jpg)

With your feedback, we've shipped dozens of bug fixes and small feature requests to improve your product experience. Here are some of our most recent.

- **Vercel CLI:** Vercel CLI [v24.2.1](https://github.com/vercel/vercel/releases/tag/vercel%4024.2.1) is now available, which includes support for [Node.js 16](https://vercel.com/changelog/node-js-16-lts-is-now-available).

- **Terms of Service (ToS):** The Vercel ToS has been updated to provide additional information about what using a beta product or feature means.

- **Documentation improvements:** The [documentation for integrations](https://vercel.com/docs/integrations) now more clearly show how to use webhooks, Log Drains, and Checks.

- **Improvements for URL filters:** When you select a time period on the [usage tab](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/limits/usage), the filter is persisted in the URL which makes sharing that specific view with your team easier.

- **Design improvements for the usage tab:** If limits have been exceeded on the [usage tab](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/limits/usage), the color of the usage meter changes to better indicate the current state.

- **Project settings improvements:** [Transferring](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/projects/overview#transferring-a-project) or [deleting](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/projects/overview#deleting-a-project) a project is now available on the general settings for that project.


[Let us know](https://vercel.com/contact) other opportunities where we can improve your experience with Vercel.
