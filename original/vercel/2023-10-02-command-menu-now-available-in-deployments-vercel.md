Oct 2, 2023

![](images/command-menu-now-available-in-deployments-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/command-menu-now-available-in-deployments-vercel/img_002.jpg)

You can now use `⌘K` (or `Ctrl+K` on Windows) to open the Command Menu on any deployment where the Vercel Toolbar is enabled, including [production and localhost](https://vercel.com/changelog/vercel-toolbar-now-available-to-use-collaboration-features-in-production). You can use `Cmd + Shift + K` if you're viewing a deployment of a website that has its own `⌘K` menu.

Users can now navigate between a deployment and other Vercel pages relevant to the project directly through the menu.

[Check out our documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/deployments/preview-deployments#cmd+k-navigation) to learn more.
