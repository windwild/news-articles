![](images/pinecone-integration-now-available-for-vector-databases-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/pinecone-integration-now-available-for-vector-databases-vercel/img_002.jpg)

You can now use the [Pinecone](https://vercel.com/integrations/pinecone) integration to create vector databases for your [AI applications](https://vercel.com/ai). [Vector databases](https://vercel.com/guides/vector-databases) enable augmenting LLMs with the ability to retrieve additional knowledge ( [RAG](https://vercel.com/guides/retrieval-augmented-generation)) from your provided sources.

This integration is available for users on all plans.

[Check out the integration](https://vercel.com/integrations/pinecone) to get started.
