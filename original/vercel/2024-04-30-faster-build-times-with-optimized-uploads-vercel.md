Apr 30, 2024

We've optimized our [build process](https://vercel.com/docs/deployments/builds#build-process) to reduce upload times by 15% on average for all customers.

For customers with large builds (10,000 outputs or more), upload times have decreased by 50%. This results in a time saving of up to 5 minutes per build for several customers.

Learn more about builds in [our documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/deployments/builds).
