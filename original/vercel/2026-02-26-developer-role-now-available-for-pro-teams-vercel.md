Feb 26, 2026

[Pro teams](https://vercel.com/pricing) can now assign the Developer role to their members. Previously only available for Enterprise teams, the Developer role gives Pro teams more granular access control.

Developers can safely deploy to projects on a team, with more limited team-wide configuration control and environment variables visibility.

Owners can assign the Developer role to any existing seat or invite new members from the team members settings.

Learn more about [team level roles](https://vercel.com/docs/rbac/access-roles/team-level-roles).
