Feb 14, 2025

![](images/additional-options-for-sharing-deployments-externally-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/additional-options-for-sharing-deployments-externally-vercel/img_002.jpg)

You can now [share deployments](https://vercel.com/docs/deployments/sharing-deployments) with external collaborators. Previously, invitations, access requests, and shareable links were limited to the [preview URL](https://vercel.com/docs/deployments/preview-deployments#preview-urls) for a branch or custom aliases.

The share modal—accessible by selecting **Share** on a deployment page or from the [Vercel Toolbar](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/vercel-toolbar#sharing-deployments) menu—now allows sharing the specific deployment you are on or the always up-to-date [preview URL](https://vercel.com/docs/deployments/preview-deployments#preview-urls) for the branch.

Read more about [sharing deployments](https://vercel.com/docs/deployments/sharing-deployments).
