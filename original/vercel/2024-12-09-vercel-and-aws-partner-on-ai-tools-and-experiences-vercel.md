Dec 9, 2024

Last week at AWS re:Invent 2024, the Vercel team met with thousands of builders in the Developer Solutions Zone, celebrated v0's launch on AWS Marketplace, and hosted hundreds of customers and partners with various event activations. Now, we're taking our AWS Partnership further:

[**Vercel has been selected for a Strategic Collaboration Agreement (SCA) with AWS**](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2024/12/06/2993168/0/en/Vercel-Enters-into-Strategic-Collaboration-Agreement-with-AWS-to-Deliver-Next-Generation-of-AI-Enabled-Developer-Tooling-and-Experience.html) **—to deliver the next generation of AI-enabled developer tooling and experiences.**

This collaboration underscores the value of Vercel and AWS together as a one-stop shop for teams building AI experiences.

![Vercel VP of Partnerships Jen Chang was a panelist for "Power of Partnerships to Scale with AWS" at re:Invent. ](images/vercel-and-aws-partner-on-ai-tools-and-experiences-vercel/img_001.jpg)Vercel VP of Partnerships Jen Chang was a panelist for "Power of Partnerships to Scale with AWS" at re:Invent.

## Building the future of Generative AI

The SCA is a three-year investment by AWS into Vercel’s AI products, including:

- [v0.dev](https://v0.dev/): Generate frontend layouts, components, and code with an AI-powered developer assistant

- [AI SDK](https://sdk.vercel.ai/): Develop AI-powered apps with support for Generative UI in an open source SDK, [including AWS Bedrock](https://sdk.vercel.ai/providers/ai-sdk-providers/amazon-bedrock#amazon-bedrock-provider)

- [AI Integrations](https://vercel.com/docs/integrations/ai): Connect powerful AI models like GPT-3, Pinecone, and more to your Vercel projects with one-click integrations

- [AI Templates](https://vercel.com/templates/ai): Quickly create a new project, fast-track setup, and leverage popular frontend frameworks


Together with AWS, we’re making it easier to integrate AI into your workflow. Whether you're an individual creating an app or an enterprise engineering team managing billions in transactions, Vercel’s Managed Infrastructure and DX Platform with AI features enable anyone to build and ship.

## Procure v0 on AWS Marketplace

[v0 is now available on the AWS Marketplace](https://aws.amazon.com/marketplace/pp/prodview-efzbdu2thzzly), meaning anyone with an AWS account can now onboard and build with v0.

Customers who purchase v0 Enterprise through the AWS Marketplace have seamless access to Vercel features like:

- **Technical support and code generation**: Debug, [write tests](https://www.v0.dev/chat/cvgVNWjxtnUtw2euMmX2S), and [generate high-quality React UIs](https://v0.dev/chat/4Tg7Nr01JGY?b=b_HBE88gXAa0u) with third-party libraries for dynamic applications

- **Product and feature planning**: Iterate on new features and ideas quickly within your web application context, streamlining your development process

- **Prototyping, wireframing, and design**: Create advanced [animations](https://v0.dev/chat/Zv2IAWG3MqW?b=EWb9GiC), [3D assets](https://v0.dev/chat/H3enEFWMjXy?b=wwqD2IafjAC), and interactive mockups built on your team's tech stack

- **Sales demos and landing pages**: Rapidly build [landing pages](https://v0.dev/chat/b/H9Tcy1j) or create tailored demos for specific customers

## Retail and Consumer Goods Competencies

It's more important than ever for ecommerce brands to deliver a fast and personalized user experience. If a page is slow, or a product shows “sold out” when it isn’t, your company can lose sales or damage relationships with shoppers. To further our commitment to helping companies build great ecommerce experiences:

**Vercel has officially obtained AWS Retail and Consumer Goods Competencies.**

The [AWS Consumer Goods Competency](https://aws.amazon.com/cpg/partner-solutions/?partner-case-studies-cards.sort-by=item.additionalFields.sortDate&partner-case-studies-cards.sort-order=desc) is a newly launched AWS Specialization that connects industry customers with AWS Partners offering cutting-edge technology solutions. [AWS Retail Competency Partners](https://aws.amazon.com/retail/partner-solutions/?blog-posts-cards.sort-by=item.additionalFields.createdDate&blog-posts-cards.sort-order=desc&partner-case-studies-cards.sort-by=item.additionalFields.sortDate&partner-case-studies-cards.sort-order=desc) offer innovative retail solutions, like Vercel and AWS have done with joint customers like Ruggable and Hydrow.

As an AWS Retail and Consumer Goods Partner, Vercel is providing customers with seamless, cost-efficient, AI-ready solutions for modern web apps.
