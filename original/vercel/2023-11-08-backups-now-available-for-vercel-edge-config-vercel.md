[Vercel Edge Config](https://vercel.com/docs/storage/edge-config) is our global low-latency data store for feature flags, experiments, and configuration metadata. Now, backups of your Edge Config are automatically created with every update to an Edge Config's items. You can restore backups from the **Storage** tab in your Vercel dashboard.

[Customers on all plans](https://vercel.com/docs/storage/edge-config/edge-config-limits) can take advantage of backups. Hobby customers have 7 days of backup retention, Pro customers have 90 days of backup retention, and Enterprise customers have 365 days of backup retention.

[Check out the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/storage/edge-config/edge-config-dashboard#restoring-edge-config-backups) to learn more.
