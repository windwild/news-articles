[Stripe](https://vercel.com/marketplace/stripe) is now available in beta on the [Vercel Marketplace](https://vercel.com/marketplace) as a new payment provider.

You can now provision a fully functional [Stripe claimable sandbox](https://docs.stripe.com/sandboxes/claimable-sandboxes) directly from Vercel with no setup required. When ready, link it to a Stripe account and soon promote it to production.

This makes it easy for teams to move from prototype to production for use cases like:

- **Ecommerce storefronts**: Test complete checkout flows before launch.

- **SaaS billing**: Validate subscriptions, usage-based pricing, and invoicing.

- **Demos and templates**: Share preconfigured environments for testing or client demos.

- **Developer onboarding**: Give teams instant access to ready-to-use Stripe sandboxes.


Get started today with this [example](https://vercel.com/templates/next.js/simple-online-store-with-stripe) to build your first online simple store using Vercel and Stripe.
