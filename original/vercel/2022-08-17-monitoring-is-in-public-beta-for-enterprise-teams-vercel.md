The Monitoring tab is now in [public beta](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/release-phases#public-beta) for all Enterprise accounts. This new feature allows you to visualize, explore, and monitor your usage & traffic data. Using the query editor, you can create custom queries to gain greater insights into your data - allowing you to more efficiently debug issues and optimize all of the projects on your Vercel Team.

[Check out the documentation](https://www.vercel.com/docs/concepts/dashboard-features/monitoring) to learn more.
