Vercel Web Analytics, previously known as Audiences, is now available to all users for insights on your top pages, top referrers, and user demographics such as countries, operating systems, and browser information.

Web Analytics now also includes [custom event](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/analytics/custom-events) tracking to dive deeper into your user's interactions, such as button clicks, form submissions, and conversions.

[Speed Insights](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/speed-insights) is still a separate product that allows you to track application performance through your Real Experience Score.

Web Analytics is available on all plans with the Hobby plan getting 2,500 events per month, the Pro plan getting 25,000 events per month, and custom amounts for Enterprise. Pro users can also add on [Web Analytics Plus](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/analytics/limits-and-pricing) for $50/month to get500k included events.

[Check out the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/analytics) to learn more.
