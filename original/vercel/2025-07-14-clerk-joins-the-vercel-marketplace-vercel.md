[Clerk](https://vercel.com/marketplace/clerk) is now available as an [authentication provider](https://vercel.com/marketplace/category/authentication) on the Vercel Marketplace.

Built for modern frameworks like Next.js, [Clerk](https://clerk.com/changelog/2025-07-14-vercel-marketplace-integration) simplifies authentication while giving teams full control over UI, sessions, and user roles, all tightly integrated with Vercel’s deployment model.

With the integration, you get access to:

- Instant provisioning of Clerk apps from your Vercel dashboard

- Complete user management with hosted dashboards, sessions, and roles

- Built-in and scalable [billing](https://clerk.com/billing) and subscription management
