Dec 19, 2022

![Preview Protection Beta - Dark Mode](images/protected-preview-deployments-available-on-all-plans-vercel/img_001.jpg)![Preview Protection Beta - Dark Mode](images/protected-preview-deployments-available-on-all-plans-vercel/img_002.jpg)

You can now make Preview Deployments private for free, across all plans.

- [**Shareable Links**](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/deployment-protection#shareable-links-(beta)) **:** Share private Preview Deployments with external collaborators without the need to log in. See [docs](https://www.vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/deployment-protection) for limits.

- [**Vercel Authentication**](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/deployment-protection#vercel-authentication) **:** Team members can log in with their Vercel account to access secure previews.


Password Protection is now [**Advanced Deployment Protection**](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/advanced-deployment-protection) and keeps the same price at $150/mo. Pro and Enterprise customers can add on Advanced Deployment Protection, which includes:

- [**Private Production Deployments**](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/advanced-deployment-protection#private-production-deployments) **:** Make your **production** deployment private with the click of a button.

- [**Protection Bypass for Automation**](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/advanced-deployment-protection#protection-bypass-for-automation-(beta)) **:** Allows you to bypass user authentication by using header secret for automated tooling.

- [**Password Protection**](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/advanced-deployment-protection#password-protection) **:** Secure preview and production deployments with a password.


Enterprise customers will also have access to audit logs allowing them to track who generated a given Sharable Link at what time and from which device.

[Check out the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/deployment-protection) to learn more.
