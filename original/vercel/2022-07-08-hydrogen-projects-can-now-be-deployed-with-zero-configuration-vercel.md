![](images/hydrogen-projects-can-now-be-deployed-with-zero-configuration-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/hydrogen-projects-can-now-be-deployed-with-zero-configuration-vercel/img_002.jpg)

Vercel now automatically optimizes your [Hydrogen](https://hydrogen.shopify.dev/) projects. When importing a new project, it will detect Hydrogen and configure the right settings for optimal performance — including using [Vercel Edge Functions](https://vercel.com/edge) for server-rendering pages.

Deploy the [Hydrogen template](https://vercel.com/new/clone?s=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fvercel%2Fvercel%2Ftree%2Fmain%2Fexamples%2Fhydrogen&template=hydrogen) to get started.
