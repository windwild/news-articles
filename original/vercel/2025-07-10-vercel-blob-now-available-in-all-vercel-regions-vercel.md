![](images/vercel-blob-now-available-in-all-vercel-regions-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/vercel-blob-now-available-in-all-vercel-regions-vercel/img_002.jpg)

You can now create Vercel Blob stores in any of the [19 Vercel Regions](https://vercel.com/docs/edge-network/regions).

Selecting a region closer to your [Functions](https://vercel.com/docs/functions) and users allows you to optimize upload speed and comply with data-residency requirements when required.

Selecting a region is available at creation time in the Vercel dashboard or when using the [Vercel CLI](https://vercel.com/docs/cli/blob#store-add) (version 44.3.0).

Learn more about [Vercel Blob](https://vercel.com/docs/storage/vercel-blob) in the documentation.
