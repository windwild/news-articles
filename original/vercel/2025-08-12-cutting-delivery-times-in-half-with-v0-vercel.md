[Ready.net](http://ready.net/) is a core platform that helps utility companies manage their financing and compliance, the company works with a wide network of state-level stakeholders. New feature requirements come in fast, often vague, and always critical.

With limited design resources supporting three teams, the company needed a way to speed up the loop between ideation, validation, and delivery. That’s where v0 came in.

### From ambiguity to clarity

Customers often arrive with urgent requests, but the path to tangible feature is not always immediately clear.

Before v0, these moments led to weeks of back-and-forth, speculative flows, and alignment meetings. Now, the process starts with dropping an unedited PRD into v0. If the output is unclear, that’s a signal: the requirements need work. That instant feedback turns ambiguity into action.

Instead of guessing, the team can actually get to a working prototype in minutes. It accelerates alignment, surfaces gaps early, and avoids wasted effort before engineering ever gets involved.

Interactive dashboard prototype the team was able to spin up from a customer request.

### Faster feedback, faster handoffs

With v0, early ideas become real, reviewable features in minutes. Once validated, these flows go straight to customers for further review or sign-off. What once took multiple sprints now takes a week from request to production.
