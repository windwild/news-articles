Aug 26, 2025

Teams with Observability Plus now have 30 days of runtime log retention. These logs include detail about requests, [Vercel Functions](https://vercel.com/docs/functions) and Routing Middleware invocations, cache activity, and more.

You can view, query, inspect, and share up to 14 consecutive days of log data at once.

This extended retention is available at no additional cost for Pro and Enterprise plans with Observability Plus enabled.

[Try it out](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%5Bproject%5D%2Flogs) or learn more about [Runtime Logs](https://vercel.com/docs/logs/runtime).
