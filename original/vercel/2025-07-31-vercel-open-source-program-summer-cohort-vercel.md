Jul 31, 2025

In April, we launched the [Vercel Open Source Program](https://vercel.com/open-source-program), a developer initiative that gives maintainers the resources, credits, and support they need to ship faster and scale confidently, starting with the [spring 2025 cohort](https://vercel.com/blog/spring25-oss-program).

We're now honored to announce the summer 2025 cohort.

From AI-powered calendars to beautifully styled React Native components, open source builders continue to amaze us. Here are the 28 projects from the summer cohort.
