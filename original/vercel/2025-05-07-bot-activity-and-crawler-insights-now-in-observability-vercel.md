Vercel Observability now provides detailed breakdowns for [individual bots](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%5Bproject%5D%2Fobservability%2Fedge-requests?tab=botId&title=Edge%20Requests%20by%20Bot%20Name) and [bot categories](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%5Bproject%5D%2Fobservability%2Fedge-requests?tab=botCategory&title=Edge%20Requests%20by%20Bot%20Category), including AI crawlers and search engines. Users across all plans can view this data in the **Observability > Edge Requests** dashboard.

Additionally, Observability Plus users can:

- Filter traffic by bot category, such as AI

- View metrics for individual bots

- Break down traffic by bot or category in the query builder


Inspect bot and crawler activity in [your Observability dashboard](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%5Bproject%5D%2Fobservability%2Fedge-requests?tab=botId&title=Edge%20Requests%20by%20Bot%20Name) now.
