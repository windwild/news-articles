![The accessibility audit tool is available on any page of your website and groups issues by impact.](images/uncover-accessibility-issues-on-your-deployments-from-the-vercel-toolbar-vercel/img_001.jpg)![The accessibility audit tool is available on any page of your website and groups issues by impact.](images/uncover-accessibility-issues-on-your-deployments-from-the-vercel-toolbar-vercel/img_002.jpg)

Accessibility Audit now runs in the background for you everywhere you have the Vercel Toolbar. You can view the compliance of [Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.0](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG20/) level A and AA rules for the page you are on from the toolbar menu. The rules will show grouped by impact as defined by [deque axe](https://github.com/dequelabs/axe-core/blob/develop/doc/rule-descriptions.md#wcag-21-level-a--aa-rules).

You can also turn on recording to keep track of issues that turn up as you move around a page. This feature is available to all Vercel users.

See the [Accessibility Audit documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/accessibility-audit-tool) to learn more.
