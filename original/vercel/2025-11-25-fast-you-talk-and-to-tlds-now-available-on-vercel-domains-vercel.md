Nov 25, 2025

Vercel Domains now supports the TLDs `.fast`, `.you`, `.talk`, and `.to`..

Domains with these TLDs can now be purchased at [vercel.com/domains](https://vercel.com/domains), and you can also now transfer domains with these TLDs onto the Vercel platform for easy integration and use with projects and deployments.

Try it [here](https://vercel.com/domains).
