Nov 8, 2024

Vercel has been recognized as a Visionary in the 2024 Gartner Magic Quadrant for Cloud Application Platforms, the first-ever Magic Quadrant for the sector.

The Frontend Cloud is designed for developers and teams that care deeply about user experiences. Whether you're serving billions of users or building your first project, the Frontend Cloud helps you remove friction from the development and delivery process. This allows you to focus on building your product instead of managing and configuring the infrastructure required to make it work.

**Today, Vercel is proud to be recognized as a Visionary; we view this** [**Gartner® Magic Quadrant™ for Cloud Application Platforms report**](https://vercel.com/gartner-mq) **as a validation of the Frontend Cloud ecosystem.**

Gartner evaluated 12 cloud application platform vendors based on their ability to execute and completeness of vision. "By abstracting the complexities associated with infrastructure management, cloud application platforms enable product teams to deliver faster customer value," said Gartner. "Visionaries have a clear vision of the future and are making significant investments in the development of unique technologies."

## Empowering developers, building the future

From supporting the latest frameworks to integrating AI capabilities, Vercel is committed to evolving the web as place where everyone can bring ideas to life. Vercel helps companies and developers achieve their goals through innovations including:

- [**Framework-defined infrastructure**](https://vercel.com/blog/framework-defined-infrastructure) reimagining how infrastructure automatically adapts to a developer's code, not the other way around

- [**AI SDK**](https://sdk.vercel.ai/) providing the tools you need to build AI-powered products fast and with minimal code

- [**Vercel Marketplace**](https://vercel.com/marketplace) seamlessly connecting first-party services to your Vercel projects

- [**v0**](https://v0.dev/) empowering engineers with AI coding assistant, while non-technical team members bring ideas to life faster without knowing how to code through a generative chat interface

- [**Vercel Web Application Firewall (WAF)**](https://vercel.com/blog/introducing-the-vercel-waf) blocking [billions of malicious requests](https://vercel.com/blog/preventing-infrastructure-abuse-with-vercel-firewall), ensuring all apps are secure by default

- [**Next.js**](https://nextjs.org/) enabling developers to create ambitious web applications—becoming the React framework trusted by some of the world's largest companies
