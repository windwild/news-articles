# Introducing OpenAI Frontier

# 推出 OpenAI Frontier

AI has let teams take on things they used to talk about but never execute. In fact, 75% of enterprise workers say AI helped them do tasks they couldn’t do before. We’re hearing this from every department, not just technical teams. The way work gets done has changed, and enterprises are starting to feel it in big ways.

人工智能已使团队得以真正落地执行那些过去仅停留在讨论层面的任务。事实上，75% 的企业员工表示，AI 帮助他们完成了此前根本无法完成的工作。我们从各个部门——而不仅限于技术团队——都听到了这样的反馈。工作方式已然改变，企业正切身感受到这种变革带来的深远影响。

We’ve seen this in action with over 1 million businesses over the past few years. At a major manufacturer, agents reduced production optimization work from six weeks to one day. A global investment company deployed agents end-to-end across the sales process to open up over 90% more time for salespeople to spend with customers. And, at a large energy producer, agents helped increase output by up to 5%, which adds over a billion in additional revenue.

过去几年中，我们已在超过 100 万家企业中见证了这一变化的实际成效：一家大型制造企业借助 AI 智能体，将生产优化工作周期从六周缩短至一天；一家全球性投资公司，在整个销售流程中端到端部署智能体，使销售人员可直接用于客户互动的时间增加了 90% 以上；一家大型能源生产商则通过智能体将产量提升最高达 5%，由此带来逾 10 亿美元的额外收入。

This is happening for AI leaders across every industry, and the pressure to catch up is increasing. What’s slowing them down isn’t model intelligence, it’s how agents are built and run in their organizations.

这一趋势正席卷各行各业的 AI 先行者，而追赶压力也与日俱增。真正制约他们步伐的，并非模型本身的智能水平，而是其组织内部构建与运行智能体的方式。

Today, we’re introducing [**Frontier**](https://openai.com/business/frontier/), a new platform that helps enterprises build, deploy, and manage AI agents that can do real work. Frontier gives agents the same skills people need to succeed at work: shared context, onboarding, hands-on learning with feedback, and clear permissions and boundaries. That’s how teams move beyond isolated use cases to AI coworkers that work across the business.

今天，我们正式推出 [**Frontier**](https://openai.com/business/frontier/)——一个全新平台，助力企业构建、部署并管理能够执行真实业务任务的 AI 智能体。Frontier 赋予智能体人类职场成功所必需的核心能力：共享上下文、入职培训、带反馈的实操式学习，以及清晰的权限与边界设定。正是凭借这些能力，团队才能突破零散的单点用例，迈向真正融入全业务流程的 AI 同事。

## The AI opportunity gap  
## AI 机遇鸿沟  

Companies are already overwhelmed with the disconnected systems and governance spread across clouds, data platforms, and applications. AI made that fragmentation more visible, and in many cases, more acute. Agents are now getting deployed everywhere, and each one is isolated in what it can see and do. Every new agent can end up adding complexity instead of helping, because it doesn’t have enough context to do the job well.  
企业早已被分散在云环境、数据平台和各类应用中的孤立系统与割裂的治理机制所压垮。AI 使这种碎片化问题更加凸显，且在许多情况下进一步加剧。如今，智能体（agents）正被部署到各个角落，但每个智能体所能“看见”和“做到”的范围都是彼此隔离的。每新增一个智能体，非但未能提供帮助，反而可能增加系统复杂性——因为它缺乏足够的上下文来高质量地完成任务。

As agents have gotten more capable, the opportunity gap between what models can do and what teams can actually deploy has grown. The gap isn’t just driven by technology. Teams are still building the knowledge to move agents past early pilots and into real work as fast as AI is improving. At OpenAI alone, something new ships roughly every three days, and that pace is getting faster.¹ Keeping up means balancing control and experimentation, and that’s hard to get right.  
随着智能体能力不断提升，模型理论上能实现的功能与团队实际可部署落地的能力之间的“机遇鸿沟”也在持续扩大。这一鸿沟的成因并不仅限于技术本身：团队仍在艰难积累经验，努力将智能体从早期试点阶段快速推进至真实业务场景中——而这一进程，必须跟上 AI 技术日新月异的发展速度。仅在 OpenAI，平均约每三天就有一项新成果发布，且这一节奏还在不断加快。¹ 要跟上步伐，就必须在管控与实验之间取得精妙平衡——而这恰恰最难把握。

Enterprises are feeling the pressure to figure this out now, because the gap between early leaders and everyone else is growing fast.  
企业正感受到迫切压力，亟需立刻厘清这一挑战，因为先行者与后来者之间的差距正在迅速拉大。

## OpenAI Frontier  
## OpenAI 前沿计划  

We've learned that teams don't just need better tools that solve pieces of the puzzle. They needed help getting agents into production with an end-to-end approach to build, deploy, and manage agents.  
我们已认识到：团队所需要的，远不止是解决局部问题的更优工具；他们真正需要的是端到端的支持——一套覆盖构建、部署与管理全流程的方法论，助力智能体真正走向生产环境。

We started by looking at how enterprises already scale people. They create onboarding processes. They teach institutional knowledge and internal language. They allow learning through experience and improve performance through feedback. They grant access to the right systems and set boundaries. AI coworkers need the same things.  
我们首先回溯了企业规模化培养人才的既有实践：建立标准化入职流程；传授组织知识与内部术语体系；支持员工在实践中学习，并通过反馈持续提升绩效；授予其访问恰当系统所需的权限，同时设定清晰的行为边界。AI 同事（AI coworkers）同样需要这一切。

For AI coworkers to actually work, a few things matter:  
要让 AI 同事真正发挥作用，以下几点至关重要：

- They need to understand how work actually gets done across systems.  
  - 它们需要理解工作在跨系统环境中究竟是如何实际开展的。  

- They need access to a computer and tools to plan, act, and solve real-world problems.  
  - 它们需要接入计算机及相应工具，以进行规划、执行操作，并解决现实世界中的具体问题。  

- They need to understand what good looks like, so quality improves as the work changes.  
  - 它们需要理解何为“优质成果”，从而在工作任务动态演进的过程中持续提升输出质量。  

- And they need an identity, permissions, and boundaries teams can trust.  
  - 并且，它们需要具备明确的身份标识、合理的权限配置，以及团队可信赖的行为边界。

And all of this has to work across many systems, often spread across multiple clouds. Frontier works with the systems teams already have, without forcing them to replatform. You can bring your existing data and AI together where it lives — as well as integrate the applications you already use — using open standards. That means no new formats and no abandoning agents or applications you’ve already deployed.

所有这一切都必须在众多系统上协同运行，而这些系统往往分布于多个云环境中。Frontier 与团队已有的系统无缝协作，无需强制其重构平台。您可借助开放标准，将现有数据与 AI 在其原生位置整合，并接入您已在使用的各类应用。这意味着无需采用新格式，也无需弃用您已部署的任何智能体（agents）或应用程序。

The superpower of this approach is that AI coworkers are accessible and useful through any interface, not trapped behind a single UI or application. They can partner with people wherever work happens, whether that is interacting with ChatGPT, through workflows with Atlas, or inside existing business applications. This is true whether agents are developed in-house, acquired from OpenAI, or are integrated from other vendors you already use.

这一方法的核心优势在于：AI 同事可通过任意界面被访问和使用，而非被束缚于单一用户界面（UI）或某个特定应用之中。无论工作发生于何处——无论是与 ChatGPT 交互、通过 Atlas 编排工作流，还是嵌入您现有的业务应用内部——AI 同事都能与人类员工紧密协作。无论这些智能体是企业自研、从 OpenAI 获取，还是由您当前已合作的其他厂商提供并集成而来，该能力均完全适用。

#### Understand the work

#### 理解业务运作

Every effective employee knows how the business works, where information lives, and what good decisions look like.

每一位高效员工都深知企业如何运转、关键信息存于何处，以及何谓优质决策。

Frontier connects siloed data warehouses, CRM systems, ticketing tools, and internal applications to give AI coworkers that same shared **business context**. They understand how information flows, where decisions happen, and what outcomes matter. It becomes a semantic layer for the enterprise that all AI coworkers can reference to operate and communicate effectively.

Frontier 将彼此孤立的数据仓库、CRM 系统、工单工具及内部应用连接起来，赋予 AI 同事同样共享的 **业务上下文（business context）**。它们由此理解信息如何流转、决策在何处产生、哪些结果真正重要。这一能力使 Frontier 成为企业级语义层（semantic layer），所有 AI 同事均可调用该层以实现高效运行与有效沟通。

#### Plan, act, and solve problems

#### 规划、执行与解决问题

With shared context in place, agents need to be able to actually do the work.

在共享上下文就绪之后，智能体还需具备切实开展工作的能力。

Teams across the organization, technical and non-technical, can use Frontier to hire AI coworkers who take on many of the tasks people already do on a computer. Frontier gives AI coworkers the ability to reason over data and complete complex tasks, like working with files, running code, and using tools, all in a dependable, open **agent execution** environment. As AI coworkers operate, they build memories, turning past interactions into useful context that improves performance over time.

组织内各团队——无论技术背景与否——均可借助 Frontier “聘用”AI 同事，承担大量原本由人类在计算机上完成的任务。Frontier 赋予 AI 同事基于数据进行推理的能力，并在可靠、开放的 **智能体执行（agent execution）** 环境中完成复杂任务，例如处理文件、运行代码及调用各类工具。随着 AI 同事持续运行，它们不断积累“记忆”，将过往交互转化为有价值的上下文，从而随时间推移不断提升性能。

Once deployed, AI coworkers can run across local environments, enterprise cloud infrastructure, and OpenAI-hosted runtimes without forcing teams to reinvent how work gets done. And for time-sensitive work, Frontier prioritizes low-latency access to OpenAI’s models so responses stay quick and consistent.

部署后，AI 同事可在本地环境、企业云基础设施及 OpenAI 托管的运行时环境中自由运行，无需团队重新设计工作方式。对于时效性要求高的任务，Frontier 优先保障对 OpenAI 模型的低延迟访问，确保响应始终快速且稳定。

#### Improve quality on real work

#### 提升实际工作中的质量

For agents to be useful over time, they need to learn from experience, just like people do.

要让智能体长期发挥实用价值，它们必须像人类一样，从经验中持续学习。

Built-in ways to **evaluate and optimize** performance make it clear to human managers and AI coworkers what’s working and what isn’t, so good behaviors improve over time. Over time, AI coworkers learn what good looks like and get better at the work that matters most.

系统内置的**评估与优化**机制，让人类管理者和AI同事都能清晰识别哪些做法行之有效、哪些尚需改进，从而推动优秀行为不断演进。久而久之，AI同事将逐步理解“什么是高质量表现”，并在最关键的工作任务上持续精进。

This is how agents move from impressive demos to dependable teammates.

这正是智能体从令人惊艳的演示，蜕变为值得信赖的团队成员的关键路径。

#### Identity, permissions, and boundaries

#### 身份、权限与边界

Frontier makes sure AI coworkers operate within clear boundaries. Each AI coworker has its own identity, with explicit permissions and guardrails. That makes it possible to use them confidently in sensitive and regulated environments. [Enterprise security and governance⁠](https://openai.com/business-data/) are built in, so teams can scale without losing control.

Frontier 确保 AI 同事始终在清晰界定的边界内运行。每位 AI 同事都拥有独立身份，并配备明确的权限设定与安全护栏（guardrails）。这使其能够被放心地部署于敏感场景与强监管环境中。[企业级安全与治理能力⁠](https://openai.com/business-data/) 已深度集成于平台之中，使团队在规模化扩展的同时，始终牢牢掌握控制权。

## Combining technology with know-how

## 技术能力与行业经验相结合

Closing the opportunity gap isn’t just a technology problem.

弥合机会差距，绝非单纯的技术问题。

We’ve worked closely with large enterprises on complex AI deployments for years, so we’ve seen what works and what doesn’t. Now we’re helping teams apply those lessons to their toughest problems.

多年来，我们与众多大型企业紧密合作，共同推进复杂的人工智能落地项目，因而深刻理解哪些实践行之有效、哪些存在局限。如今，我们正协助各团队将这些宝贵经验，应用于自身最具挑战性的业务难题。

We pair OpenAI Forward Deployed Engineers (FDEs) with your teams, working side by side to help you develop the best practices to build and run agents in production.

我们为您的团队配备 OpenAI 前线部署工程师（Forward Deployed Engineers, FDEs），与您并肩协作，共同探索并建立面向生产环境的智能体开发与运维最佳实践。

The FDEs also give teams **a direct connection to OpenAI Research**.

FDE 还为团队提供了**与 OpenAI 研究团队的直接联系**。

As you deploy agents, we learn not just how to improve your systems around the model. We also learn how the models themselves need to evolve to be more useful for your work. That feedback loop, from your business problem to deployment to research and back, helps both sides move faster.

当您部署智能体（agents）时，我们不仅学习如何围绕模型优化您的系统，还学习模型自身需如何演进，才能更切实地服务于您的工作。这一闭环反馈——从您的业务问题出发，经由部署实践，再反哺至基础研究，并最终回归业务——助力双方加速前进。

#### Business problem

#### 业务问题

Millions of hardware tests failed, and engineers spent thousands of hours each year (nearly half their time) manually hunting down the cause by digging through logs, docs, and code.

数以百万计的硬件测试失败；工程师每年耗费数千小时（近一半工作时间）手动排查原因——反复翻阅日志、文档和代码。

#### What we solved

#### 我们解决了什么

We reduced root-cause identification from ~4 hours per failure to a few minutes, accelerating troubleshooting.

我们将单次故障的根本原因定位时间从约 4 小时缩短至几分钟，显著加快了故障排查速度。

#### How it works

#### 工作原理

AI coworkers pull together simulation logs, internal docs, workflows, and code, then run an end-to-end investigation to identify the most likely root cause and what to do next.

AI 同事自动整合仿真日志、内部文档、工作流程与源代码，并执行端到端的深度调查，精准识别最可能的根本原因及后续应对措施。

#### Outcome

#### 成果

Debugging went from hours to minutes, saving thousands of engineering hours annually and speeding up development.

调试耗时从数小时缩短至数分钟，每年节省数千工程师工时，大幅加快产品开发节奏。

## Opening the AI ecosystem

## 打开 AI 生态系统

AI works best in the enterprise when the platform and the applications work together. Because Frontier is built on open standards, software teams can plug in and build agents that benefit from the same shared context.

在企业环境中，当平台与应用程序协同工作时，AI 才能发挥最佳效果。由于 Frontier 基于开放标准构建，软件团队可轻松接入并开发智能体（agents），从而共享同一上下文环境。

This matters because many agent apps fail for a simple reason: they don’t have the context they need. Data is scattered across systems, permissions are complex, and each integration becomes a one-off project. Frontier makes it easier for applications to access the business context they need (with the right controls), so they can work inside real workflows from day one. For enterprises, that means faster rollouts without a long integration cycle every time.

这一点至关重要，因为许多智能体应用失败的原因很简单：缺乏所需的上下文。数据分散在各个系统中，权限管理复杂，而每次集成都变成一个定制化项目。Frontier 让应用程序更便捷地获取所需业务上下文（同时配备恰当的管控机制），从而从第一天起就能嵌入真实工作流中运行。对企业而言，这意味着无需每次部署都经历漫长的集成周期，即可实现更快的规模化落地。

## Let’s build
