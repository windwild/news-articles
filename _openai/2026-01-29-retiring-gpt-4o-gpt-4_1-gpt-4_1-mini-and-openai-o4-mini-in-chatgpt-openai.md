# Retiring GPT‑4o, GPT‑4.1, GPT‑4.1 mini, and OpenAI o4-mini in ChatGPT

# ChatGPT 将停用 GPT‑4o、GPT‑4.1、GPT‑4.1 mini 和 OpenAI o4-mini

On February 13, 2026, alongside the [previously announced retirement⁠](https://openai.com/index/gpt-5-1/) of GPT‑5 (Instant and Thinking), we will retire GPT‑4o, GPT‑4.1, GPT‑4.1 mini, and OpenAI o4-mini from ChatGPT. In the API, there are no changes at this time.

2026年2月13日，我们将与此前已宣布停用的 [GPT‑5（Instant 和 Thinking 版本）⁠](https://openai.com/index/gpt-5-1/) 同步，在 ChatGPT 中停用 GPT‑4o、GPT‑4.1、GPT‑4.1 mini 和 OpenAI o4-mini。目前，API 端暂无任何变更。

While this announcement applies to several older models, GPT‑4o deserves special context.

尽管本次公告涉及多款较早发布的模型，但 GPT‑4o 尤其值得单独说明。

After we first deprecated it and later restored access during the GPT‑5 release, we learned more about how people actually use it day to day. We brought GPT‑4o back after hearing clear feedback from a subset of Plus and Pro users, who told us they needed more time to transition key use cases, like creative ideation, and that they preferred GPT‑4o’s conversational style and warmth.

在我们最初将其弃用、又于 GPT‑5 发布期间恢复访问后，我们更深入地了解到用户日常实际使用 GPT‑4o 的方式。在听取部分 Plus 和 Pro 用户明确反馈后——他们表示需要更多时间迁移关键使用场景（例如创意构思），且更偏爱 GPT‑4o 的对话风格与亲和力——我们重新启用了 GPT‑4o。

That feedback directly shaped GPT‑5.1 and GPT‑5.2, with improvements to personality, stronger support for creative ideation, and more ways to [customize how ChatGPT responds⁠(opens in a new window)](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/11899719-customizing-your-chatgpt-personality). You can choose from base styles and tones like Friendly, and controls for things like warmth and enthusiasm. Our goal is to give people more control and customization over how ChatGPT feels to use—not just what it can do.

这些反馈直接推动了 GPT‑5.1 和 GPT‑5.2 的迭代：优化了模型个性表现，强化了对创意构思的支持，并新增了更多 [自定义 ChatGPT 回应方式⁠(在新窗口中打开)](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/11899719-customizing-your-chatgpt-personality) 的选项。您可从“友好”（Friendly）等基础风格与语调中进行选择，并调节温暖感、热情度等维度。我们的目标是赋予用户更多掌控权与个性化能力，不仅关注 ChatGPT “能做什么”，更重视它“用起来是什么感受”。

We’re announcing the upcoming retirement of GPT‑4o today because these improvements are now in place, and because the vast majority of usage has shifted to GPT‑5.2, with only 0.1% of users still choosing GPT‑4o each day.

我们今日即宣布 GPT‑4o 即将停用，原因有二：一是上述改进现已全面落地；二是当前绝大多数用户已转向 GPT‑5.2，每日仅剩 0.1% 的用户仍在使用 GPT‑4o。

More broadly, we’re continuing to improve ChatGPT across areas users have told us need work. This includes further improvements to personality and creativity, as well as addressing unnecessary refusals and overly cautious or preachy responses, with updates coming soon. We’re continuing to make progress toward a version of ChatGPT designed for adults over 18, grounded in the principle of treating adults like adults, and expanding user choice and freedom within appropriate safeguards. To support this, we’ve rolled out [age prediction⁠(opens in a new window)](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/12652064-age-prediction-in-chatgpt) for users under 18 in most markets.

更广泛而言，我们正持续优化 ChatGPT —— 聚焦于用户明确指出有待提升的多个方面。这包括进一步增强模型个性与创造力，同时着力解决不必要的拒绝回应、过度谨慎或说教式回答等问题，相关更新即将上线。我们正稳步推进面向 18 岁以上成年用户的 ChatGPT 版本建设，其核心原则是“以对待成年人的方式对待成年人”，在合理安全边界内不断拓展用户的选择权与自由度。为此，我们在多数市场已上线面向 18 岁以下用户的 [年龄预测功能⁠(在新窗口中打开)](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/12652064-age-prediction-in-chatgpt)。

Changes like this take time to adjust to, and we’ll always be clear about what’s changing and when. We know that losing access to GPT‑4o will feel frustrating for some users, and we didn’t make this decision lightly. Retiring models is never easy, but it allows us to focus on improving the models most people use today.

此类调整需要时间适应，而我们将始终清晰告知用户“哪些内容正在变化”以及“何时变化”。我们深知，部分用户失去对 GPT‑4o 的访问权限会感到沮丧；这一决定绝非轻率作出。停用模型从来不是一件容易的事，但它使我们得以集中资源，持续优化当下绝大多数用户正在使用的模型。
