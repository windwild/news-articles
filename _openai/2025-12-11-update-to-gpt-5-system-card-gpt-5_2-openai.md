# Update to GPT‑5 System Card: GPT‑5.2

# GPT‑5 系统卡更新：GPT‑5.2

[Read the System Card(opens in a new window)](https://deploymentsafety.openai.com/gpt-5-2)

[查看系统卡（在新窗口中打开）](https://deploymentsafety.openai.com/gpt-5-2)

## Introduction

## 引言

GPT‑5.2 is the latest model family in the GPT‑5 series, and explained in our [blog⁠](https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-5-2/). The comprehensive safety mitigation approach for these models is largely the same as that described in the [GPT‑5 System Card⁠](https://openai.com/index/gpt-5-system-card/) and [GPT‑5.1 System Card⁠](https://openai.com/index/gpt-5-system-card-addendum-gpt-5-1/).

GPT‑5.2 是 GPT‑5 系列中的最新模型家族，相关介绍详见我们的[博客](https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-5-2/)。这些模型所采用的全面安全缓解策略，与[《GPT‑5 系统卡》](https://openai.com/index/gpt-5-system-card/)及[《GPT‑5.1 系统卡附录》](https://openai.com/index/gpt-5-system-card-addendum-gpt-5-1/)中所述基本一致。

In this card we also refer to GPT‑5.2 Instant as gpt-5.2-instant and GPT‑5.2 Thinking as gpt-5.2-thinking.

本系统卡中，我们亦将 GPT‑5.2 Instant 称为 `gpt-5.2-instant`，将 GPT‑5.2 Thinking 称为 `gpt-5.2-thinking`。
