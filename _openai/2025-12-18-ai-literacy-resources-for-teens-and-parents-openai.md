# AI literacy resources for teens and parents

# 面向青少年与家长的AI素养资源

A teen’s guide to using ChatGPT thoughtfully—plus tips for parents to support healthy, responsible use.

面向青少年的审慎使用ChatGPT指南——以及帮助家长支持孩子健康、负责任地使用AI的实用建议。

[Download tips for parents(opens in a new window)](https://cdn.openai.com/pdf/tips-for-talking-to-your-teen-about-ai.pdf) [下载致家长的建议（在新窗口中打开）](https://cdn.openai.com/pdf/tips-for-talking-to-your-teen-about-ai.pdf)

We’re sharing two new AI literacy resources to help families use ChatGPT thoughtfully, safely, and confidently. First is a family-friendly guide that explains—in plain language—how AI models are trained, why AI can sometimes get things wrong, and the importance of double checking the information received. It also includes practical tips for responsible use, like how to write better prompts, why you might get different answers to the same question, and how to manage your data and settings.

我们现推出两项全新的AI素养资源，助力家庭更审慎、安全、自信地使用ChatGPT。其一是面向家庭的通俗指南，以简明易懂的语言阐释：AI模型如何训练、AI为何有时会出错，以及为何必须对所获信息进行二次核查。该指南还提供了诸多负责任使用的实用技巧，例如如何撰写更有效的提示词（prompts）、为何同一问题可能得到不同答案，以及如何管理个人数据与账户设置。

We’re also publishing a set of tips for parents, with conversation starters and practical guidance to help parents talk with their teens about what AI can (and can’t) do, build critical thinking, set healthy boundaries, and navigate emotional or sensitive topics.

我们同时发布一份专为家长设计的建议清单，内含开启对话的切入点与实操性指导，协助家长与青少年探讨AI的能力边界（即AI能做什么、不能做什么），培养批判性思维，设立健康的使用边界，并共同应对情绪化或敏感话题。

Both resources were developed with expert input, including members of our [Expert Council on Well-Being and AI⁠](https://openai.com/index/expert-council-on-well-being-and-ai/) and [ConnectSafely⁠(opens in a new window)](https://connectsafely.org/), groups with deep expertise in online safety, human-computer interaction, teen development, and mental health. We’ll continue expanding our AI literacy work over time with additional guidance for different ages and stages, and we’ll keep updating these resources as we learn more.

这两项资源均在专家指导下开发完成，合作方包括我们的[“福祉与AI专家委员会”（Expert Council on Well-Being and AI）](https://openai.com/index/expert-council-on-well-being-and-ai/)以及[ConnectSafely（在新窗口中打开）](https://connectsafely.org/)。这些机构在网络安全、人机交互、青少年发展及心理健康等领域拥有深厚的专业积淀。未来，我们将持续拓展AI素养相关工作，陆续推出针对不同年龄段与成长阶段的更多指导材料，并根据最新实践与研究持续更新上述资源。
