# An update on our mental health-related work

# 关于我们在心理健康相关工作方面的最新进展

Each week, more than 900 million people use ChatGPT to improve their daily lives through uses such as learning new skills or navigating complex healthcare systems. Our ongoing safety work continues to play an important role in delivering these benefits to everyday people, as well as supporting scientific research and discovery.

每周，超过9亿人使用 ChatGPT 来改善日常生活，例如学习新技能或应对复杂的医疗保健系统。我们持续开展的安全工作，对于将这些益处惠及普通用户，以及支持科学研究与发现，始终发挥着重要作用。

Since introducing [parental controls](https://openai.com/index/introducing-parental-controls/) in September 2025, we’ve seen encouraging engagement from families and will continue building on these protections. Working closely with experts from our Council on Well-Being and AI and our Global Physicians Network, we will also soon be introducing a trusted contact feature, which will allow adult users to designate someone to receive notifications when they may need additional support. As a reminder, parents also receive safety notifications about their teens’ use of ChatGPT through [parental controls](https://openai.com/index/introducing-parental-controls/). We’ll share more as these updates roll out in ChatGPT.

自2025年9月推出[家长控制功能](https://openai.com/index/introducing-parental-controls/)以来，我们已观察到家庭用户的积极反馈，并将持续完善此类保护机制。与此同时，我们将与“福祉与人工智能理事会”（Council on Well-Being and AI）及“全球医师网络”（Global Physicians Network）的专家紧密合作，即将推出“可信联系人”（trusted contact）功能——该功能允许成年用户指定一位联系人，在其可能需要额外支持时接收通知。需要提醒的是，家长亦可通过[家长控制功能](https://openai.com/index/introducing-parental-controls/)接收有关其青少年使用 ChatGPT 的安全通知。随着这些更新在 ChatGPT 中陆续上线，我们将进一步分享更多细节。

We are also [continuing](https://openai.com/index/strengthening-chatgpt-responses-in-sensitive-conversations/) to advance how our models detect and respond to signs of emotional distress. This includes new evaluation methods that simulate extended mental health-related conversations, helping us better identify potential risks and improve how ChatGPT responds in sensitive moments. We’ll share more about this work in the coming weeks as we continue strengthening ChatGPT’s safeguards.

我们也在[持续推进](https://openai.com/index/strengthening-chatgpt-responses-in-sensitive-conversations/)模型对情绪困扰迹象的识别与响应能力。这包括引入新型评估方法，通过模拟长时间的心理健康相关对话，帮助我们更准确地识别潜在风险，并优化 ChatGPT 在敏感情境下的回应方式。在接下来的几周中，我们将继续强化 ChatGPT 的安全防护机制，并就这项工作发布更多信息。

## Litigation updates

## 诉讼进展更新

Separately, the Court recently coordinated a number of mental health-related cases involving ChatGPT into a single proceeding in California. In the coming days, the Court will assign the coordination judge for this proceeding. As part of this consolidation process, plaintiffs’ attorneys involved in these proceedings have informed the Court that they intend to file a number of new cases\*.

此外，法院近期已将多起涉及 ChatGPT 的心理健康相关案件整合至加利福尼亚州的一起统一诉讼程序中。未来数日内，法院将指定该案的协调法官。作为此次案件整合工作的一部分，参与上述诉讼程序的原告律师已向法院表示，他们计划提交若干起新案件\*。

As with the earlier filed mental health-related litigation, OpenAI will continue to handle any additional cases with care, transparency, respect for the people involved, and in line with the following principles:

与此前已提起的心理健康相关诉讼一样，OpenAI 将以审慎、透明、尊重当事人的方式处理所有新增案件，并严格遵循以下原则：

- We start with the facts and put genuine effort into understanding them.  
- 我们从客观事实出发，并真诚努力地理解事实全貌。

- We will respectfully make our case in a way that is cognizant of the complexity and nuances of situations involving real people and real lives.  
- 我们将以尊重的态度陈述己方立场，充分体察涉及真实人物与真实生活的各类情形所固有的复杂性与细微差别。

- We recognize that these cases inherently involve certain types of private information that require sensitivity when in a public setting like a court.  
- 我们深知，此类案件天然涉及某些类型的个人信息，在法庭等公开场合中处理时需格外审慎。

- And independent of any litigation, we’ll remain focused on improving our technology in line with our mission.  
- 此外，无论诉讼进程如何，我们都将始终秉持使命，持续改进自身技术。

We recognize that court processes can be lengthy and, at times, opaque due to strict legal rules. It can also take time to collect and understand the relevant facts, and present them to the court in line with its evidence procedures. We work to understand the details in good faith, and we only seek information as part of the court process that’s relevant to the case and the specific allegations that have been made.

我们深知，法院程序往往耗时较长，且因严格的法律规则而有时显得不透明。同时，收集和理解相关事实也需要时间，并需依照法院的证据规则将其呈交法庭。我们本着善意努力厘清各项细节，仅在法院程序框架内，就案件本身及所涉具体指控，索取必要且相关的信息。

It’s important to reserve judgment and allow the facts to appropriately emerge through the court process, as these are complex and nuanced cases with many factors and circumstances that are often not reflected in the initial filings.

在此类案件中，保持审慎、暂缓判断至关重要；唯有通过法院程序，事实才能得以全面、恰当地呈现。这些案件往往错综复杂、细节微妙，涉及诸多因素与情境，而这些通常无法在最初的诉状中充分体现。

Our thoughts are with all those impacted by these incredibly heartbreaking situations. We continue to improve ChatGPT’s training to recognize and respond to signs of distress, de-escalate conversations in sensitive moments, and guide people toward real-world support, working closely with mental health clinicians and experts.

我们深切关怀所有受这些令人心碎事件影响的人们。我们持续优化 ChatGPT 的训练，以提升其识别与回应心理困扰迹象的能力，在敏感时刻缓和对话情绪，并引导用户获取现实世界中的专业支持——这一工作始终与心理健康临床医生及领域专家紧密协作开展。
