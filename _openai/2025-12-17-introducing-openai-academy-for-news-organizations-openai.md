# Introducing OpenAI Academy for News Organizations

# 推出面向新闻机构的 OpenAI 学院

Working with the American Journalism Project and The Lenfest Institute to launch a new learning hub for journalists and publishers using AI.

携手美国新闻项目（American Journalism Project）与伦费斯特新闻研究所（The Lenfest Institute for Journalism），共同推出面向记者与出版机构的全新学习平台——OpenAI 学院，助力新闻从业者更高效、更负责任地应用人工智能技术。

At OpenAI, we believe journalism is essential to a healthy democracy. People depend on reliable local and national reporting to understand their communities and the world around them, and we’re committed to being a strong partner to news organizations—supporting their work and convening the right people to move the industry forward.

在 OpenAI，我们坚信新闻业是健康民主社会的基石。公众依赖可靠的地方与全国性报道来理解自身所处的社区及周遭世界；我们亦坚定承诺，成为新闻机构值得信赖的合作伙伴——支持其核心工作，并汇聚关键力量，共同推动整个行业向前发展。

We’re building on our partnership with the [American Journalism Project⁠(opens in a new window)](https://www.theajp.org/) and [The Lenfest Institute for Journalism⁠(opens in a new window)](https://www.lenfestinstitute.org/) to launch [**OpenAI Academy for News Organizations** ⁠(opens in a new window)](https://academy.openai.com/public/clubs/news-organizations-b9osl/overview), a new learning hub for journalists, editors, and publishers using AI.

我们基于与[美国新闻项目（新窗口打开）](https://www.theajp.org/)及[伦费斯特新闻研究所（新窗口打开）](https://www.lenfestinstitute.org/)的既有合作，正式推出[**面向新闻机构的 OpenAI 学院（新窗口打开）**](https://academy.openai.com/public/clubs/news-organizations-b9osl/overview)——一个专为记者、编辑与出版人打造的全新 AI 学习平台。

We shared this initiative yesterday at the [AI and Journalism Summit⁠(opens in a new window)](https://brown.columbia.edu/ai-summit/), which we co-hosted with the Brown Institute for Media Innovation and Hearst, which brought together leaders from the newsroom, academic, and technology community. The OpenAI Academy for News Organizations goes live today with hands-on training, playbooks, and real-world examples that help teams save time and focus on high-impact journalism – from reporting and fact-gathering, to the business and operational work that keeps news organizations strong.

昨日，我们在由布朗媒体创新研究所（Brown Institute for Media Innovation）与赫斯特集团（Hearst）联合主办的[“人工智能与新闻峰会（新窗口打开）”](https://brown.columbia.edu/ai-summit/)上发布了这一倡议。峰会汇聚了来自新闻编辑部、学术界与科技界的行业领袖。今日起，“面向新闻机构的 OpenAI 学院”正式上线，提供实操培训、实用指南（playbooks）及真实案例，帮助新闻团队节省时间、聚焦高影响力报道——涵盖一线采访与事实核查，也延伸至维系新闻机构稳健运营的商业与日常管理工作。

At launch, the OpenAI Academy for News Organizations includes:

上线初期，“面向新闻机构的 OpenAI 学院”包含以下内容：

- **On-demand training**, including _AI Essentials for Journalists_, which introduces core AI concepts and newsroom-relevant use cases, along with sessions for more technical and product-focused teams exploring advanced tools and custom solutions to their business needs.

- **按需式培训课程**，包括《记者必备 AI 基础》（_AI Essentials for Journalists_），系统介绍人工智能核心概念及适用于新闻编辑部的实际应用场景；另设面向技术团队与产品团队的进阶课程，探讨高级工具应用及契合自身业务需求的定制化解决方案。

- **Practical use cases** focusing on investigative and background research, translation and multilingual reporting, data analysis, and production efficiency.

- **实用性应用场景**，涵盖深度调查与背景调研、翻译与多语种报道、数据分析，以及内容生产效率提升等方向。

- **Open-source projects and shared resources** to make it easier for other news organizations to adapt for their own needs.

- **开源项目与共享资源**，便于其他新闻机构根据自身需求灵活调整与复用。

- **Guidance on responsible uses,** including tips and examples for developing internal policies and governance frameworks.

- **关于负责任使用的指导建议**，包括制定内部政策与治理框架的具体提示与实践范例。

The OpenAI Academy for News Organizations builds on years of collaboration with the journalism community, including our ongoing work with American Journalism Project and The Lenfest Institute to support local news organizations as they responsibly adopt AI to assist in critical newsroom research and investigative work, accelerate product development, and explore new ways of using AI to improve business sustainability. Those efforts, alongside partnerships with publishers and industry groups around the world, helped inform the new Academy’s emphasis on practical guidance, transparency, and shared learning grounded in real newsroom needs.

OpenAI 新闻机构学院建立在多年来与新闻界合作的基础之上，其中包括我们与美国新闻项目（American Journalism Project）和伦费斯特研究所（The Lenfest Institute）持续开展的合作——支持地方新闻机构负责任地采用人工智能，以辅助关键的编辑部研究与调查工作、加速产品开发，并探索利用人工智能提升业务可持续性的新路径。这些努力，连同我们与全球出版商及行业组织的合作，共同塑造了新学院的核心理念：立足真实编辑部需求，提供切实可行的指导、坚持透明原则，并推动全行业的经验共享与协同学习。

AI is already reshaping how newsrooms work, and the Academy is intended to provide immediate value. We recognize that adopting new technology raises important questions for journalists and publishers, including concerns about trust, accuracy, and jobs. The Academy is built with those realities in mind.

人工智能已在重塑新闻编辑部的工作方式，而本学院旨在即刻为从业者创造实际价值。我们深知，新技术的引入为记者与出版商带来了诸多重要问题，包括公众对新闻可信度、内容准确性以及就业岗位的关切。本学院正是基于这些现实挑战而设计。

We’ve invested for years in supporting a healthy news ecosystem while helping more than 800 million weekly ChatGPT users access timely, high-quality information from trusted news outlets. Today, that work includes partnerships with organizations such as News Corp, Axios, the Financial Times, Condé Nast, and Hearst as well as collaborations with groups like AJP, The Lenfest Institute, the World Association of News Publishers (WAN-IFRA), and the International News Media Association (INMA) that focus on shared learning and capacity building across the industry. Together, these partners provide content in more than 20 languages globally.

多年来，我们持续投入资源，致力于构建健康、可持续的新闻生态系统，同时助力每周超 8 亿 ChatGPT 用户便捷获取来自可信赖新闻媒体的及时、高质量信息。目前，这一工作已涵盖与新闻集团（News Corp）、Axios、《金融时报》（Financial Times）、康泰纳仕（Condé Nast）、赫斯特集团（Hearst）等机构的战略合作；同时也包括与美国新闻项目（AJP）、伦费斯特研究所（The Lenfest Institute）、世界报业协会（WAN-IFRA）及国际新闻媒体协会（INMA）等组织的协作——这些合作聚焦于全行业的知识共享与能力建设。通过上述合作伙伴的共同努力，相关资源与内容现已覆盖全球逾 20 种语言。

In the year ahead, we plan to work with additional news organizations and industry partners to expand the Academy with new courses, case studies, and live programming. Through this effort, we’ll provide tools to help journalists everywhere do their work even better and support news organizations as they build for the future.

在未来一年中，我们计划携手更多新闻机构与行业伙伴，持续拓展学院内容体系，新增课程、案例研究及实时互动项目。借此，我们将为全球各地的记者提供实用工具，助其更高效、更出色地完成新闻工作；同时也为新闻机构面向未来的发展提供坚实支持。
