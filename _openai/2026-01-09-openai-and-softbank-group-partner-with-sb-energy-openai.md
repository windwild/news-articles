# OpenAI and SoftBank Group partner with SB Energy

# OpenAI 与软银集团携手 SB Energy

- _SoftBank Group and OpenAI invest $1 billion in SB Energy to support its growth as a leading development and execution partner for data center campuses_  
- _软银集团与 OpenAI 向 SB Energy 投资 10 亿美元，以支持其成长为数据中心园区开发与建设领域的领先合作伙伴_

- _OpenAI signs 1.2 GW data center lease for initial data center buildout_  
- _OpenAI 签署一项 1.2 吉瓦（GW）数据中心租赁协议，用于首批数据中心的建设_

- _SB Energy will become a major customer of OpenAI, leveraging its APIs and deploying ChatGPT for its employees_  
- _SB Energy 将成为 OpenAI 的重要客户，通过调用其 API 并为员工部署 ChatGPT_

- _SB Energy additionally secured $800 million of Redeemable Preferred Equity from Ares to further support the company’s growth_  
- _SB Energy 还额外获得 Ares 提供的 8 亿美元可赎回优先股，以进一步支持公司发展_

**Redwood City, CA, Jan. 9, 2026**— [SB Energy⁠(opens in a new window)](https://sbenergy.com/), a SoftBank Group company, announced today a strategic partnership with OpenAI as part of [Stargate⁠(opens in a new window)](https://group.softbank/en/news/press/20250924), marking a significant step forward in the build out of next-generation artificial intelligence (AI) and energy infrastructure in the United States. The investment builds on the $500 billion Stargate commitment announced in January at the White House.

**美国加利福尼亚州红木城，2026年1月9日**——软银集团旗下公司 [SB Energy⁠（在新窗口中打开）](https://sbenergy.com/) 今日宣布与 OpenAI 达成战略合作，该合作是 [“星门”（Stargate）计划⁠（在新窗口中打开）](https://group.softbank/en/news/press/20250924) 的重要组成部分，标志着美国新一代人工智能（AI）与能源基础设施建设迈出关键一步。本次投资是对今年1月于白宫宣布的 5000 亿美元“星门”承诺的进一步落实。

To support the partnership and as demand for AI compute accelerates, OpenAI and SoftBank Group are each investing $500 million into SB Energy. OpenAI has also selected SBE to build and operate its [previously-announced](https://openai.com/index/five-new-stargate-sites/) 1.2 GW data center site in Milam County. The equity funding supports SB Energy’s growth as a leading development and execution partner for data center campuses and associated energy infrastructure. SB Energy is currently developing several multi-gigawatt data center campuses, with initial facilities under construction and expected to enter service starting in 2026.

为支持此次合作，并应对日益增长的 AI 算力需求，OpenAI 与软银集团将各自向 SB Energy 投资 5 亿美元。此外，OpenAI 已选定 SB Energy（SBE）承建并运营其此前公布的位于德克萨斯州米勒姆县（Milam County）的 1.2 吉瓦（GW）数据中心项目。此项股权融资将助力 SB Energy 巩固其作为数据中心园区及配套能源基础设施领域领先开发与建设合作伙伴的地位。目前，SB Energy 正在开发多个总规模达数吉瓦的数据中心园区，首批设施已开工建设，预计将于 2026 年起陆续投入运营。

**OpenAI co-founder and President Greg Brockman** said, “Partnering with SB Energy brings together their strength in data center infrastructure and energy development and OpenAI’s deep domain expertise in data center engineering. The result is a fast, reliable way to scale compute through large, highly optimized AI data centers.”

**OpenAI 联合创始人兼总裁格雷格·布罗克曼（Greg Brockman）** 表示：“与 SB Energy 的合作，将双方在数据中心基础设施与能源开发方面的优势，同 OpenAI 在数据中心工程领域的深厚专业能力相结合。其成果是一种快速、可靠的规模化算力扩展路径——即依托大型、高度优化的人工智能专用数据中心。”

**SB Energy co-CEO Rich Hossfeld** said, “SB Energy’s strategic partnership with OpenAI accelerates our delivery of advanced AI data center campuses and associated energy infrastructure at the scale required to advance Stargate and secure America’s AI future. We are grateful for our longstanding sponsor SoftBank Group and new partner OpenAI for their investment in our platform, our team, and our long-term vision.”

**SB Energy 联合首席执行官里奇·霍斯菲尔德（Rich Hossfeld）** 表示：“SB Energy 与 OpenAI 的战略合作，将加速我们交付先进 AI 数据中心园区及配套能源基础设施的能力，从而满足‘星门’计划推进及保障美国 AI 未来所需的规模。我们衷心感谢长期支持者软银集团以及新合作伙伴 OpenAI 对我们平台、团队和长远愿景所给予的投资。”

As part of this transaction, OpenAI, SoftBank Group, and SB Energy have also formed a non-exclusive preferred partnership to develop a new model for data center builds that brings together OpenAI’s first-party data center design with SB Energy’s proven expertise in speed, cost discipline, and integrated energy delivery to deliver purpose-built AI infrastructure at scale. With each project, SB Energy and OpenAI will invest in communities through well-paying jobs, workforce development, and grid modernization to deliver durable economic growth for partner communities.

作为本次交易的一部分，OpenAI、软银集团与 SB Energy 还共同建立了一种非排他性的优先合作伙伴关系，旨在打造一种新型数据中心建设模式：融合 OpenAI 自主研发的数据中心设计能力，与 SB Energy 在建设速度、成本管控及一体化能源交付方面久经验证的专业优势，从而实现大规模定制化 AI 基础设施的高效交付。在每一个项目中，SB Energy 与 OpenAI 都将通过提供高薪就业岗位、开展劳动力技能培养以及推动电网现代化升级等方式，积极投资所在社区，为合作伙伴社区带来可持续的经济增长。

The Milam County Data Center will create thousands of construction jobs.  
米勒姆县数据中心将创造数千个建筑工作岗位。

OpenAI and SB Energy have designed the data center to minimize water usage, and plan to build new generation to support the Milam County Data Center’s energy needs and protect Texas ratepayers.  
OpenAI 与 SB Energy 已针对该数据中心进行优化设计，以最大限度减少用水量；同时计划建设新型发电设施，以满足米勒姆县数据中心的能源需求，并保障德克萨斯州电力用户的权益。
