# Stargate Community

# 星门社区

OpenAI’s mission is to ensure that AGI benefits all of humanity, and in order to do that, we are working to ensure our Stargate campuses benefit the local communities that make them possible. We believe that AI [infrastructure⁠(opens in a new window)](https://cdn.openai.com/global-affairs/openai-infra-economics-10.09.24.pdf) is vital for American competitiveness and economic opportunity, while boosting local economies by creating jobs and bringing in local revenue.

OpenAI 的使命是确保通用人工智能（AGI）惠及全人类；为实现这一目标，我们正努力确保“星门”（Stargate）园区惠及使其成为可能的当地社区。我们认为，人工智能[基础设施⁠（在新窗口中打开）](https://cdn.openai.com/global-affairs/openai-infra-economics-10.09.24.pdf)对美国的竞争力与经济机遇至关重要，同时通过创造就业机会、引入本地税收等方式提振地方经济。

When we announced Stargate one year ago in January 2025, we set out to expand our U.S. AI infrastructure to 10GW by 2029—and just one year in, we are already well beyond halfway to that goal in planned capacity, with the first site in Abilene, Texas already training and serving frontier AI systems and multiple Stargate sites under development across Texas, New Mexico, Wisconsin, and Michigan. We are committed to working with communities to ensure that our Stargate campuses are built and run in a way that strengthens communities and demonstrates that we’re being good neighbors.

去年1月（2025年1月），我们首次宣布“星门”计划时，设定了到2029年将美国人工智能基础设施扩展至10吉瓦（GW）的目标；而仅一年之后，我们在规划容量方面已远超该目标的一半：位于德克萨斯州阿比林（Abilene）的首个站点已投入运行，正在训练并服务前沿人工智能系统；此外，多个“星门”站点也正在德克萨斯州、新墨西哥州、威斯康星州和密歇根州同步建设中。我们承诺与各地社区紧密协作，确保“星门”园区的规划、建设与运营切实增强社区韧性，并彰显我们作为良好邻里的责任担当。

AI is delivering tangible benefits already, from helping hundreds of millions of people with their [health and wellness](https://openai.com/index/introducing-chatgpt-health/) every week to unlocking new scientific advances. At the same time, we are making the AI models themselves much more capable and power efficient each year. As we’ve done so, demand has increased even more, since intelligence can more affordably be applied to more important problems, and there’s no limit to the problems that people want to solve. That’s why we and the industry see a critical need for such large-scale AI infrastructure.

人工智能已带来切实可感的益处——从每周助力数亿人改善[健康与福祉](https://openai.com/index/introducing-chatgpt-health/)，到推动全新科学突破。与此同时，我们每年都在显著提升人工智能模型本身的能力与能效。随着这些进步，市场需求进一步激增：因为更经济、更普惠的智能技术，正被应用于越来越多关键领域；而人类亟待解决的问题，其数量本身并无上限。正因如此，我们与整个行业均认为，大规模人工智能基础设施建设势在必行、刻不容缓。

Partnering with local communities starts with understanding local needs. Going forward, every Stargate site will have its own locally tailored **Stargate Community** plan, driven by community input and local concerns. They will all rest on this same core premise: Stargate is a partnership with communities, and we can only achieve our mission by being good neighbors.

与本地社区合作，始于深入理解其实际需求。未来，每一处“星门”站点都将制定专属的、因地制宜的**星门社区**（Stargate Community）计划，该计划将以社区意见与本地关切为核心驱动力。所有此类计划均基于同一根本原则：“星门”是与社区共建共享的伙伴关系；唯有成为值得信赖的好邻居，我们才能真正践行自身使命。

Across all of our **Stargate Community** plans, **we commit to paying our own way on energy, so that our operations don’t increase your electricity prices**. Every community and region has unique energy needs and grid conditions, and our commitment will be tailored to the region. Depending on the site, this can range from bringing new dedicated power and storage that the project fully funds, to adding and paying for new energy generation and transmission resources. This commitment can be grounded in specific approaches like:

在所有**星门社区**计划中，**我们郑重承诺：自行承担全部能源成本，确保我们的运营不会推高您的电价**。每个社区与地区都拥有独特的能源需求与电网条件，因此我们的承诺也将因地制宜、精准适配。具体形式将视站点情况而定，包括但不限于：由项目全额出资新建专用电源与储能设施；或投资新建发电与输电资源并承担全部费用。该承诺可通过以下具体举措予以落实：

1. Funding the incremental generation and grid upgrades our load requires  
1. 为满足本项目负荷增长所需，出资建设新增发电能力及配套电网升级工程  

2. Planning transparently and proactively with local utilities, transmission providers, state utility regulators (public utility/service commissions) and regional grid operators  
2. 与地方公用事业公司、输电服务商、州级公用事业监管机构（即公共事业/公共服务委员会）以及区域电网运营商开展透明、前瞻性的协同规划  

3. Working with utilities, grid operators, and the industry to develop strategies for operating AI campuses as flexible loads, so when peak conditions or grid stress are forecast, we can reduce or curtail consumption and participate in demand-response and grid-stability programs  
3. 联合公用事业公司、电网运营商及整个行业，共同制定策略，将人工智能园区作为“柔性负荷”进行运营——即当预测到用电高峰或电网承压时，主动降低或暂停用电，并积极参与需求响应与电网稳定支持项目

We are already working with partners to deliver this on existing Stargate campuses. For example:

我们已与合作伙伴携手，在现有“Stargate”园区推进此项工作。例如：

- In [Wisconsin⁠(opens in a new window)](https://vantage-dc.com/news/openai-oracle-and-vantage-data-centers-announce-stargate-data-center-site-in-wisconsin/), our partners Oracle and Vantage are working with WEC Energy Group to develop new energy generation and capacity, including solar and battery storage. Our developer partners are also underwriting 100% of the power infrastructure investment through a dedicated electricity rate from WEC. The proposed rate is designed to protect other customers from price increases associated with the new investments needed to serve the facility.

- 在[威斯康星州⁠（新窗口打开）](https://vantage-dc.com/news/openai-oracle-and-vantage-data-centers-announce-stargate-data-center-site-in-wisconsin/)，我们的合作伙伴甲骨文（Oracle）和Vantage数据中心正与WEC能源集团合作，开发新型发电能力与容量，包括太阳能发电与电池储能系统。我们的开发合作伙伴还通过WEC提供的专属电价，全额承担电力基础设施投资。该电价方案旨在保护其他用户免受为服务本设施而新增投资所引发的电价上涨影响。

- In [Michigan⁠(opens in a new window)](https://www.related-digital.com/michigan), our partners Oracle and Related Digital are working with DTE Energy to supply the project’s power using existing resources, augmented by a new battery storage investment financed entirely by the project. The structure is designed to help ensure there is no impact on DTE’s existing customers’ energy supply or rates. DTE customers can also benefit from the project contributing its share to the fixed costs of maintaining and improving the grid.

- 在[密歇根州⁠（新窗口打开）](https://www.related-digital.com/michigan)，我们的合作伙伴甲骨文（Oracle）与Related Digital正与DTE能源公司合作，利用现有电力资源为项目供电，并辅以一项由项目方全额出资新建的电池储能投资。该合作架构旨在确保DTE现有用户的电力供应与电价不受影响；同时，DTE用户还可受益于本项目分摊电网运维与升级所需的固定成本。

- In [Texas](https://openai.com/index/stargate-sb-energy-partnership/), our partners SB Energy plans to fund and build new energy generation and storage to supply the majority of the power needed to run the Stargate campus we’re developing together in Milam County.

- 在[得克萨斯州](https://openai.com/index/stargate-sb-energy-partnership/)，我们的合作伙伴SB Energy计划出资并建设新型发电与储能设施，以满足我们在米勒姆县（Milam County）联合开发的Stargate园区绝大部分运行用电需求。

We’re also encouraged to see one of our key providers, Microsoft, announce [community-first AI commitments⁠(opens in a new window)](https://blogs.microsoft.com/on-the-issues/2026/01/13/community-first-ai-infrastructure/), which apply to the AI campuses they build for us.

我们也欣喜地看到核心合作伙伴之一微软（Microsoft）宣布了[“以社区为先”的AI承诺⁠（新窗口打开）](https://blogs.microsoft.com/on-the-issues/2026/01/13/community-first-ai-infrastructure/)，该承诺将适用于其为我们建设的AI园区。

These are early examples, and we’ll continue to find meaningful ways to benefit local communities including:

这些尚属初步实践，我们将持续探索切实可行的方式，为当地社区创造价值，包括：

- **Minimizing water use and protecting local ecosystems** by prioritizing closed-loop or low-water cooling systems. **AI campuses and deep learning workloads use innovations in cooling water systems design that drastically reduce the water use compared to traditional datacenters**. Water required by our facilities should be a fraction of a community’s overall water use. As Mayor Weldon Hurt of Abilene, Texas [noted,⁠(opens in a new window)](https://www.reporternews.com/story/news/2025/10/14/water-electricity-concerns-addressed-by-stargate-data-center-leaders-in-abilene-texas/86585222007/?gnt-cfr=1&gca-cat=p&gca-uir=true&gca-epti=undefined&gca-ft=0&gca-ds=sophi) the water use at the Abilene site in a year will be half as much as Abilene uses in a single day. These designs are used across all of our Stargate AI campuses - including with Oracle in [Abilene⁠(opens in a new window)](https://www.crusoe.ai/resources/blog/an-inside-look-at-the-abilene-ai-data-center) and [Shackelford County⁠(opens in a new window)](https://vantage-dc.com/news/vantage-data-centers-unveils-plans-for-frontier-a-25b-mega-campus-in-texas-to-meet-unprecedented-ai-demand/), Texas; [Doña Ana County, New Mexico⁠(opens in a new window)](https://www.stackinfra.com/about/news-press/press-releases/stack-infrastructure-delivers-digital-infrastructure-for-data-center-campus-in-new-mexico/); [Saline Township, Michigan⁠(opens in a new window)](https://www.related-digital.com/news/openai-oracle-and-related-digital-announce-stargate-data-center-site-in-michigan), [Port Washington, Wisconsin⁠(opens in a new window)](https://vantage-dc.com/news/openai-oracle-and-vantage-data-centers-announce-stargate-data-center-site-in-wisconsin/) and our latest AI campus with Microsoft in [Mount Pleasant, Wisconsin.⁠(opens in a new window)](https://blogs.microsoft.com/on-the-issues/2025/09/18/made-in-wisconsin-the-worlds-most-powerful-ai-datacenter/) This approach is paired with site-specific environmental and infrastructure investments developed with local partners. For example, in Wisconsin our partners will [invest a minimum of $175M in local infrastructure upgrades and water restoration projects.⁠(opens in a new window)](https://vantage-dc.com/news/openai-oracle-and-vantage-data-centers-announce-stargate-data-center-site-in-wisconsin/)

- **最大限度减少用水并保护当地生态系统**：优先采用闭环式或低耗水冷却系统。**AI园区及深度学习工作负载采用了创新的冷却水系统设计，相较传统数据中心可大幅降低用水量**。我方设施所需用水量仅占所在社区总用水量的一小部分。正如得州阿比林市（Abilene）市长韦尔登·赫特（Weldon Hurt）[指出⁠（新窗口打开）](https://www.reporternews.com/story/news/2025/10/14/water-electricity-concerns-addressed-by-stargate-data-center-leaders-in-abilene-texas/86585222007/?gnt-cfr=1&gca-cat=p&gca-uir=true&gca-epti=undefined&gca-ft=0&gca-ds=sophi)，阿比林园区一年的用水量仅为阿比林市单日用水量的一半。此类设计已全面应用于所有Stargate AI园区——包括与甲骨文（Oracle）合作的得州[阿比林⁠（新窗口打开）](https://www.crusoe.ai/resources/blog/an-inside-look-at-the-abilene-ai-data-center)和[沙克尔福德县⁠（新窗口打开）](https://vantage-dc.com/news/vantage-data-centers-unveils-plans-for-frontier-a-25b-mega-campus-in-texas-to-meet-unprecedented-ai-demand/)、新墨西哥州[多娜安娜县⁠（新窗口打开）](https://www.stackinfra.com/about/news-press/press-releases/stack-infrastructure-delivers-digital-infrastructure-for-data-center-campus-in-new-mexico/)、密歇根州[萨林镇⁠（新窗口打开）](https://www.related-digital.com/news/openai-oracle-and-related-digital-announce-stargate-data-center-site-in-michigan)、威斯康星州[波特华盛顿⁠（新窗口打开）](https://vantage-dc.com/news/openai-oracle-and-vantage-data-centers-announce-stargate-data-center-site-in-wisconsin/)，以及我们与微软最新共建的威斯康星州[芒特普莱森特⁠（新窗口打开）](https://blogs.microsoft.com/on-the-issues/2025/09/18/made-in-wisconsin-the-worlds-most-powerful-ai-datacenter/)AI园区。该策略还结合了与本地合作伙伴共同制定的、因地制宜的环境与基础设施投资方案。例如，在威斯康星州，我们的合作伙伴将[至少投入1.75亿美元用于本地基础设施升级改造与水资源修复项目⁠（新窗口打开）](https://vantage-dc.com/news/openai-oracle-and-vantage-data-centers-announce-stargate-data-center-site-in-wisconsin/)。

- **Investing early in local jobs and workforce pathways** by establishing OpenAI Academies as the backbone of regional workforce development in Stargate communities. These Academies will be customized for each site, [delivering credentials](https://openai.com/index/openai-certificate-courses/) and clear pathways into high-quality jobs aligned with local employers and the region’s evolving AI economy. We will launch our first Stargate community OpenAI Academy in Abilene, Texas this spring. We are also engaging alongside our partners with labor unions and workforce partners to support the skilled trades and technical workforce needed to build and operate AI infrastructure at this scale.

- **早期投资本地就业与人才发展路径**：以“OpenAI学院”作为Stargate社区区域人才发展的核心支柱。各学院将根据所在地特点定制化建设，[颁发行业认可的资质认证](https://openai.com/index/openai-certificate-courses/)，并提供清晰的职业发展路径，对接本地雇主需求及区域AI经济的演进趋势，助力学员获得高质量就业岗位。我们将于今春在得州阿比林市启动首家Stargate社区OpenAI学院。同时，我们正与合作伙伴协同工会组织及劳动力发展机构紧密协作，支持培养建设与运营大规模AI基础设施所需的高技能技工与专业技术人才。

Stargate is a physical infrastructure program that requires deep partnership. We’re reliant on and grateful to the communities that make it possible, and we are committed to showing up as long-term partners.

Stargate是一项需深度协作的实体基础设施计划。我们高度依赖并衷心感谢使这一计划成为可能的各地社区，也郑重承诺将以长期合作伙伴的姿态持续投入、共担责任。
