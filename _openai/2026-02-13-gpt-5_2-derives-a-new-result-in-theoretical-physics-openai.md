# GPT‑5.2 derives a new result in theoretical physics

# GPT‑5.2 在理论物理学中推导出一项新成果

In a new preprint, GPT‑5.2 proposed a formula for a gluon amplitude later proved by an internal OpenAI model and verified by the authors.

在一篇新发布的预印本中，GPT‑5.2 提出了一种胶子散射振幅的公式，该公式随后由 OpenAI 内部模型严格证明，并经论文作者验证。

[Read the preprint(opens in a new window)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.12176)

[阅读该预印本（在新窗口中打开）](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.12176)

We’ve published a new preprint showing that a type of particle interaction many physicists expected would not occur can in fact arise under specific conditions. The work focuses on gluons, the particles that carry the strong nuclear force. The [preprint⁠(opens in a new window)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.12176) is available on arXiv and is being submitted for publication. In the meantime, we welcome feedback from the community.

我们已发布一篇新预印本，表明一种曾被众多物理学家认为不可能发生的粒子相互作用，实际上可在特定条件下出现。本研究聚焦于胶子——传递强核力的基本粒子。该[预印本（在新窗口中打开）](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.12176)现已发布于 arXiv 平台，并正提交至学术期刊审稿。在此期间，我们诚挚欢迎学界同仁提出宝贵意见。

The preprint, titled “ _Single-minus gluon tree amplitudes are nonzero_,” is authored by Alfredo Guevara (Institute for Advanced Study), Alex Lupsasca (Vanderbilt University and OpenAI), David Skinner (University of Cambridge), and Andrew Strominger (Harvard University), and Kevin Weil (OpenAI) on behalf of OpenAI.

该预印本题为《单负螺旋度胶子树图振幅非零》（*Single-minus gluon tree amplitudes are nonzero*），作者包括阿尔弗雷多·格瓦拉（普林斯顿高等研究院）、亚历克斯·卢帕斯卡（范德堡大学与 OpenAI）、大卫·斯金纳（剑桥大学）、安德鲁·斯特罗明格（哈佛大学），以及凯文·韦尔（OpenAI），并以 OpenAI 名义联合署名。

The preprint studies a central concept in particle physics called a scattering amplitude. A scattering amplitude is the quantity physicists use to compute the probability that particles interact in a particular way. For gluons, the particles that carry the strong nuclear force, many amplitudes take unexpectedly simple forms “at tree level” (meaning calculations that keep only the simplest diagrams with no quantum loops). These simplifications have repeatedly revealed deeper structure in quantum field theory, the framework that provides a description of physics that unifies special relativity with quantum mechanics.

该预印本探讨了粒子物理学中的一个核心概念——散射振幅（scattering amplitude）。散射振幅是物理学家用于计算粒子以某种特定方式发生相互作用之概率的物理量。对于传递强核力的胶子而言，许多散射振幅在“树图层次”（tree level）——即仅保留最简单费曼图、不包含量子圈图的近似计算中——呈现出出人意料的简洁形式。此类简化现象屡次揭示出量子场论（quantum field theory）更深层的数学结构；而量子场论正是将狭义相对论与量子力学统一起来、用以描述基本物理规律的理论框架。

One case, however, has generally been treated as absent (having zero amplitude). When one gluon has negative helicity (meaning one of the two possible spin orientations a massless particle can have) and the remaining n−1 n-1 n−1 gluons have positive helicity, standard textbook arguments suggest that the corresponding tree-level amplitude must be zero. As a result, this configuration has largely been set aside.

然而，其中一种情形长期被视作“不存在”（即对应散射振幅恒为零）。当一个胶子具有负螺旋度（即无质量粒子两种可能自旋取向之一），其余 $n-1$ 个胶子均具有正螺旋度时，标准教科书论证指出：相应树图层次的散射振幅必然为零。因此，这一构型在过往研究中基本被忽略。

The preprint shows that this conclusion is too strong. The standard argument assumes generic particle momenta, meaning the directions and energies are not in any special alignment.  
预印本表明，这一结论过于强烈。标准论证假设粒子动量是“通用的”（generic），即其方向与能量之间不存在任何特殊对齐关系。

We identify a specific and precisely defined slice of momentum space where that reasoning no longer applies, known as the half-collinear regime.  
我们识别出动量空间中一个特定且精确定义的子区域——即所谓“半共线（half-collinear）区域”，在该区域内上述推理不再成立。

Half-collinear here means the gluon momenta obey a special alignment condition that is not typical, but is mathematically well-defined and consistent.  
此处“半共线”指胶子动量满足一种非典型的特殊对齐条件；该条件虽不常见，但在数学上定义清晰、自洽。

On this slice, the amplitude does not vanish, and we compute it in a special kinematic regime.  
在此动量子区域上，散射振幅并不消失，而我们在一种特殊的运动学区域中对其进行了计算。

This result opens the door to many new questions that will be the subject of subsequent investigations.  
该结果为大量新问题打开了大门，这些问题将成为后续研究的主题。

Important extensions include the computation of the analogous amplitudes for gravitons (the particles that mediate the gravitational force).  
重要拓展方向包括：计算引力子（传递引力相互作用的粒子）所对应的类似散射振幅。

A central aspect of the work concerns methodology. The final formula, Eq. (39) in the preprint, was first conjectured by GPT‑5.2 Pro.  
本工作的核心之一在于方法论。预印本中最终公式（式(39)）最初由 GPT‑5.2 Pro 提出猜想。

The human authors worked out the amplitudes for integer $n$ up to $n=6$ by hand, obtaining very complicated expressions shown in Eqs. (29)--(32), which correspond to a “Feynman diagram expansion” whose complexity grows superexponentially in $n$.  
人类作者手工推导了整数 $n$ 从 1 到 6 的散射振幅，得到极为复杂的表达式（见式(29)–(32)），这些表达式对应于一种“费曼图展开”，其复杂度随 $n$ 超指数增长。

GPT‑5.2 Pro was able to greatly reduce the complexity of these expressions, providing the much simpler forms in Eqs. (35)--(38).  
GPT‑5.2 Pro 成功大幅简化了这些表达式，给出了简洁得多的形式（见式(35)–(38)）。

From these base cases, it was then able to spot a pattern and posit a formula valid for all $n$.  
基于这些基础情形，它进一步识别出其中的规律，并提出一个适用于所有 $n$ 的普适公式。

An internal scaffolded version of GPT‑5.2 then spent roughly 12 hours reasoning through the problem, coming up with the same formula and producing a formal proof of its validity.  
随后，一个内部增强版（scaffolded version）的 GPT‑5.2 花费约 12 小时深入推理该问题，不仅得出了相同公式，还构造出其有效性的一个形式化证明。

The equation was subsequently verified analytically to solve the Berends-Giele recursion relation, a standard step-by-step method for building multi-particle tree amplitudes from smaller building blocks.  
该公式随后经解析验证，确认其满足 Berends–Giele 递推关系——这是从更小的基本单元出发、逐步构建多粒子树图振幅的一种标准方法。

It was also checked against the soft theorem, which constrains how amplitudes behave when a particle becomes soft.  
此外，该公式还通过了“软定理”（soft theorem）检验；软定理约束了当某一粒子动量趋于零（即“变软”）时，散射振幅应满足的行为特征。

With the help of GPT‑5.2, these amplitudes have already been extended from gluons to gravitons, and other generalizations are also on their way.  
在 GPT‑5.2 的协助下，这些振幅已成功从胶子推广至引力子，其他各类推广工作也正在推进之中。

These AI-assisted results, and many others, will be reported on elsewhere.  
此类人工智能辅助所得成果，以及诸多其他成果，将在别处另行报道。
