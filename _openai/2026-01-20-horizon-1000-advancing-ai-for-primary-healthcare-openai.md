# Horizon 1000: Advancing AI for primary healthcare

# Horizon 1000：推动人工智能赋能基层医疗

Together, with the Gates Foundation, we’re committing $50 million in funding and technology to help strengthen primary healthcare for 1,000 African clinics and their communities.

我们将与比尔及梅琳达·盖茨基金会（Gates Foundation）携手，共同投入5000万美元的资金与技术资源，助力非洲1000家基层诊所及其所在社区加强基层医疗服务能力。

_Editor’s Note: On behalf of The Gates Foundation, Bill Gates also shared this news on_ [_Gates Notes_ ⁠(opens in a new window)](https://www.gatesnotes.com/) _._

_编辑注：比尔·盖茨代表盖茨基金会，亦在_[_Gates Notes_（新窗口打开）](https://www.gatesnotes.com/) _上分享了这一消息。_

AI capabilities have advanced much faster than their broad, real-world deployment, leaving a growing gap between what’s possible and what people experience. These systems have become so capable that they’ve made new kinds of things possible—some we couldn’t have imagined not long ago, and some we’re still discovering. This is especially clear in healthcare, where the challenge is now [turning powerful models into tools⁠](https://openai.com/index/ai-clinical-copilot-penda-health/) that work in everyday care.

人工智能技术能力的发展速度，远超其在现实世界中的广泛落地应用，导致“技术潜力”与“民众实际体验”之间的鸿沟日益扩大。这些系统已变得如此强大，以至于催生了诸多全新可能性——其中一些是我们不久前尚无法想象的，另一些则仍在不断被我们发掘。这一点在医疗健康领域尤为明显：当前的核心挑战，正从模型研发转向如何将强大的人工智能模型转化为可在日常诊疗中切实可用的工具——[参见：AI临床助手与Penda Health合作项目](https://openai.com/index/ai-clinical-copilot-penda-health/)。

Today, we’re announcing **Horizon 1000**, a pilot initiative with the Gates Foundation to support leaders in African countries, beginning in Rwanda, as they advance these AI capabilities for health. Together, the Gates Foundation and OpenAI are committing $50 million in funding, technology, and technical support to support their work, with the ambitious goal of reaching 1,000 primary healthcare clinics and their surrounding communities by 2028.

今天，我们正式宣布启动 **Horizon 1000** 计划——一项由盖茨基金会与OpenAI联合开展的试点倡议，旨在支持非洲各国（首站为卢旺达）的医疗领导者，将前沿人工智能能力切实应用于改善本地健康服务。盖茨基金会与OpenAI将共同投入5000万美元，涵盖资金、技术及专业技术支持，全力协助该计划实施，并设定了雄心勃勃的目标：到2028年，惠及1000家基层医疗机构及其周边社区。

> “AI is going to be a scientific marvel no matter what, but for it to be a societal marvel, we’ve got to figure out ways that we use this incredible technology to improve people’s lives.”

> “无论结果如何，人工智能都必将是一项科学奇迹；但若要使之成为一项社会奇迹，我们就必须探索出切实可行的路径，运用这项非凡技术来改善人们的生活。”  

Sam Altman, CEO of OpenAI  

萨姆·阿尔特曼（Sam Altman），OpenAI首席执行官

Primary health care is the foundation of strong, resilient health systems, yet remains inaccessible for half the world’s population.  
初级卫生保健是强大、有韧性的卫生体系的基石，但全球仍有半数人口无法获得这一基本服务。

Sub-Saharan Africa alone faces a health workforce shortfall of approximately 5.6 million workers—placing extraordinary strain on existing clinicians and underscoring the scale of unmet demand for care.  
仅撒哈拉以南非洲地区就面临约560万名卫生工作者的缺口——这给现有临床人员带来巨大压力，也凸显了未被满足的医疗需求之庞大。

Quality of care is also highly variable, and is a major driver of preventable deaths.  
医疗服务的质量也存在显著差异，是导致本可避免死亡的重要因素。

At the same time, many countries across Sub-Saharan Africa have been at the forefront of re-imagining how care can be delivered at scale.  
与此同时，撒哈拉以南非洲众多国家正走在重新构想规模化医疗服务交付方式的前沿。

Governments and health leaders across the region are actively exploring how digital tools and AI can help extend the reach of their existing health workforce, improve quality, and bring more consistent care to communities.  
该地区各国政府与卫生领域领导者正积极探求：如何借助数字工具与人工智能，拓展现有卫生工作者的服务覆盖范围、提升服务质量，并为社区提供更均质化的照护。

Horizon 1000 aims to meet this moment by supporting African leadership and medical experts with resources and technical expertise so they can move from innovation to deployment.  
“地平线1000”（Horizon 1000）计划旨在把握这一历史契机，通过向非洲本土领导力量与医学专家提供资源与专业技术支持，助力其将创新成果切实转化为实际部署。

In practice, AI tools can help frontline health workers navigate complex guidelines and reduce administrative burden, so clinicians can spend more time on care.  
在实践中，人工智能工具可协助一线卫生工作者应对复杂的诊疗指南、减轻行政事务负担，从而让临床医生能将更多时间投入患者照护。

We also know people want more agency over their health, and many are already turning to AI to help navigate their own care.  
我们也深知，人们渴望在自身健康事务中拥有更大自主权；事实上，许多人已开始借助人工智能来辅助管理个人健康。

We look forward to learning openly along the way and measuring success by what meaningfully improves care for patients and the health workforce who serve them.  
我们期待在前行过程中保持开放学习的态度，并以能否切实改善患者照护质量、以及提升服务患者的卫生工作者工作体验，作为衡量成功的根本标准。
