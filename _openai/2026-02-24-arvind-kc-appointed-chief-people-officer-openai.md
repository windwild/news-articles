# Arvind KC appointed Chief People Officer

# 阿尔文德·KC 被任命为首席人事官

Helping OpenAI grow and adapt as AI changes how work gets done.

在人工智能重塑工作方式的时代，助力 OpenAI 持续成长与适应。

We're excited to welcome Arvind KC to OpenAI as our Chief People Officer.

我们非常高兴地欢迎阿尔文德·KC（Arvind KC）加入 OpenAI，担任首席人事官。

KC brings a rare combination of engineering depth and people leadership. Over the course of his career, he has held senior roles at Roblox, Google, Palantir Technologies, and Meta, helping build both products and the organizations behind them at meaningful scale. He understands how high-performing technical teams operate and how strong, practical systems can help people do their best work without slowing them down.

KC 兼具深厚的工程背景与卓越的人才领导力，这种组合实属罕见。在其职业生涯中，他曾在 Roblox、Google、Palantir Technologies 和 Meta 等公司担任高级管理职务，既推动了重要产品的构建，也深度参与了支撑这些产品发展的规模化组织建设。他深刻理解高绩效技术团队的运作机制，也深知健全而务实的体系如何赋能员工发挥最佳水平，同时不拖慢创新节奏。

![Arvind KC](images/arvind-kc-appointed-chief-people-officer-openai/img_001.png)

![阿尔文德·KC](images/arvind-kc-appointed-chief-people-officer-openai/img_001.png)

KC's role will be to help OpenAI grow in a way that supports the people doing the work, from the foundations of hiring, onboarding, and development, to the systems and policies that make it easier to collaborate, move quickly, and sustain high performance.

KC 的职责，是助力 OpenAI 以一种真正支持一线工作者的方式实现增长——从招聘、入职与人才培养等基础环节，到促进协作、加速决策、持续保持高绩效的各类制度与政策体系，均将纳入其统筹范畴。

> “We believe the way we scale OpenAI should reflect the future we’re helping to create. KC will play a key role in ensuring our people processes, policies, and systems match our ambition, while preserving the culture and operating principles that have helped us get here.”

> “我们坚信，OpenAI 的规模化发展路径，应映照出我们正共同塑造的未来。KC 将在确保人才管理流程、政策与体系与公司雄心相匹配方面发挥关键作用，同时坚守一路走来支撑我们成功的文化内核与运营准则。”

— Fidji Simo, CEO of Applications, OpenAI

— Fidji Simo，OpenAI 应用事业部首席执行官

As AI reshapes how work gets done, our Chief People Officer will be central to how we navigate that transition responsibly.  

随着人工智能重塑工作方式，我们的首席人事官将在我们负责任地应对这一转型的过程中发挥核心作用。

We believe we have both an opportunity and an obligation, to our own incredible talent and to society, to provide a model for how companies can navigate the transition to more AI-enabled work that expands what people can do with AI and invest in their growth.  

我们认为，我们既面临机遇，也肩负责任——既要面向自身卓越的人才，也要面向整个社会——为各企业树立典范，探索如何顺利迈向更深度集成AI的工作模式：这种模式不仅能拓展人类借助AI所能实现的能力边界，更能持续投资于员工的成长与发展。

This work spans how roles evolve, how new skills are developed, and how education and re-skilling can keep pace with technological change.  

这项工作涵盖岗位职能的演进、新技能的培养，以及教育体系与再培训机制如何与时俱进，以跟上技术变革的步伐。

Over time, we plan to share what we learn with our customers and partners, and hopefully help the broader economy move from theory to practice as AI increasingly becomes part of everyday work.  

未来，我们计划将实践过程中积累的经验与客户及合作伙伴共享，并期望助力更广泛的经济体系，推动AI从理论走向实践——因为AI正日益融入日常工作的方方面面。

> “This is a moment where every organization is being asked to rethink how work happens, what teams need, how people grow, and how to adapt as the tools change. I'm excited to join OpenAI as we work through those questions ourselves, and alongside our ecosystem of users, customers, and partners building the future with us.”

> “当下，每一家组织都被要求重新思考：工作究竟如何开展？团队真正需要什么？员工如何成长？又该如何随工具的迭代而持续适应？我很荣幸加入OpenAI，与我们自身、与广大的用户、客户及合作伙伴一道，共同探索这些问题，并携手共建未来。”

— Arvind KC, Chief People Officer, OpenAI  

— Arvind KC，OpenAI 首席人事官
