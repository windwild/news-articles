# Introducing OpenAI for Healthcare

# 推出 OpenAI 医疗健康解决方案

Secure AI products to help healthcare organizations scale high-quality care, reduce admin work for teams, and power custom clinical solutions—while protecting health data.

面向医疗健康行业的安全 AI 产品，助力医疗机构规模化提供高质量医疗服务、减轻团队行政负担、赋能定制化临床解决方案——同时严格保护患者健康数据。

We’re introducing OpenAI for Healthcare, a set of products designed to help healthcare organizations deliver more consistent, high-quality care for patients—while supporting their HIPAA compliance requirements.

我们正式推出 OpenAI 医疗健康解决方案，这是一套专为医疗健康机构打造的产品组合，旨在帮助其为患者提供更一致、更高质量的医疗服务，同时全面满足 HIPAA 合规性要求。

This includes **ChatGPT for Healthcare**, available starting today and already rolling out to leading institutions like AdventHealth, Baylor Scott & White Health, Boston Children’s Hospital, Cedars-Sinai Medical Center, HCA Healthcare, Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center, Stanford Medicine Children’s Health, and University of California, San Francisco (UCSF).

该方案包括即日起正式发布的 **ChatGPT for Healthcare**，目前已在多家领先医疗机构部署落地，例如 AdventHealth、Baylor Scott & White Health、波士顿儿童医院（Boston Children’s Hospital）、西达赛奈医学中心（Cedars-Sinai Medical Center）、HCA Healthcare、纪念斯隆·凯特琳癌症中心（Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center）、斯坦福儿童健康（Stanford Medicine Children’s Health）以及加州大学旧金山分校（University of California, San Francisco, UCSF）。

It also includes the **OpenAI API**, which powers much of today’s healthcare ecosystem. Thousands of organizations have configured it to support HIPAA-compliant use—such as Abridge, Ambience, and EliseAI.

此外还包括 **OpenAI API**，该接口已深度融入当今医疗健康生态系统的诸多环节。已有数千家机构将其配置为符合 HIPAA 合规要求的使用方式——例如 Abridge、Ambience 和 EliseAI 等公司。

Healthcare is under unprecedented strain. Demand is rising, clinicians are overwhelmed by administrative work, and critical medical knowledge is fragmented across countless sources. At the same time, AI adoption in healthcare is gaining momentum, driven by its potential to help address these challenges. Advances in models have significantly [improved⁠](https://openai.com/index/healthbench/) AI’s ability to support real-world clinical and administrative work, like helping clinicians personalize care using the latest evidence. According to the [American Medical Association⁠(opens in a new window)](https://www.ama-assn.org/system/files/physician-ai-sentiment-report.pdf), physicians’ use of AI nearly doubled in a year. Yet many clinicians still have to rely on their own tools because their organizations aren’t adopting AI fast enough, often due to the constraints of regulated environments.

当前，全球医疗健康体系正面临前所未有的压力：患者需求持续攀升，临床医生深陷繁重的行政事务，而关键医学知识又零散分布于海量信息源中。与此同时，人工智能在医疗健康领域的应用正加速推进，其潜力在于切实缓解上述挑战。大模型能力的持续进步已显著[提升⁠](https://openai.com/index/healthbench/) AI 在真实临床与行政场景中的支持能力——例如，帮助医生基于最新循证依据为患者提供个性化诊疗。据[美国医学会（AMA）⁠（在新窗口中打开）](https://www.ama-assn.org/system/files/physician-ai-sentiment-report.pdf)发布的报告，医生对 AI 工具的使用率在过去一年中几乎翻倍。然而，由于受监管环境等现实约束，许多医疗机构尚未能快速部署 AI 技术，导致大量临床医生仍不得不依赖自行选用的工具。

OpenAI for Healthcare helps close that gap by giving organizations a secure, enterprise-grade foundation for AI—so teams can use the same tools to deliver better, more reliable care, while supporting HIPAA compliance.

OpenAI for Healthcare 通过为组织提供安全、企业级的 AI 基础设施，助力弥合这一差距——使团队能够借助统一工具提供更优质、更可靠的医疗服务，同时支持 HIPAA 合规要求。

## ChatGPT for Healthcare

## ChatGPT for Healthcare（面向医疗行业的 ChatGPT）

ChatGPT for Healthcare is built to support the careful, evidence-based reasoning required in real patient care, while reducing administrative burden so teams can spend more time with patients. Organizations can bring clinicians, administrators, and researchers into a secure workspace with the controls they need to deploy AI securely and at scale.

ChatGPT for Healthcare 专为支撑真实临床诊疗中所需的审慎、循证推理而设计，同时显著减轻行政事务负担，让医护团队得以将更多时间投入到患者照护中。医疗机构可将临床医生、管理人员与研究人员汇聚于一个安全的工作空间内，并通过所需管控机制，安全、规模化地部署 AI。

Here’s what it includes:

其核心功能包括：

- **Models built for healthcare workflows:** High-quality responses for clinical, research, and operational work—powered by GPT‑5 models built for healthcare and evaluated through physician-led testing across benchmarks and real workflows, including [HealthBench⁠](https://openai.com/index/healthbench/) and [GDPval⁠](https://openai.com/index/gdpval/).

- **面向医疗工作流优化的模型：** 针对临床诊疗、科研探索及运营管理等场景提供高质量响应——依托专为医疗领域定制的 GPT‑5 模型，并经由医师主导的多维度评估，覆盖标准基准测试及真实工作流（如 [HealthBench⁠](https://openai.com/index/healthbench/) 和 [GDPval⁠](https://openai.com/index/gdpval/)）。

- **Evidence retrieval with transparent citations:** Answers grounded in relevant medical sources—drawing from millions of peer-reviewed research studies, public health guidance, and clinical guidelines—with clear citations including titles, journals, and publication dates to support quick source-checking. This helps clinicians reason through cases with greater confidence, so patients get to the right diagnosis and treatment sooner.

- **具备透明引文的循证检索：** 回答均基于权威医学资料源——涵盖数百万篇同行评议研究论文、公共卫生指导文件及临床实践指南；所有引用均清晰标注文献标题、期刊名称与发表日期，便于快速溯源核查。这有助于临床医生更自信地开展病例分析与决策，从而加快患者获得准确诊断与恰当治疗的进程。

- **Institutional policy and care pathway alignment:** Integrations with enterprise tools such as Microsoft SharePoint and other systems, so responses can incorporate an institution’s approved policies, pathway documents, and operational guidance to support consistent execution across teams and help ensure patients receive high-quality care.

- **机构政策与临床路径对齐：** 支持与 Microsoft SharePoint 等企业级系统集成，使生成内容可自动融合本机构已批准的政策文件、临床路径文档及运营指引，保障跨团队执行的一致性，助力患者获得高质量照护。

- **Reusable templates to automate workflows:** Shared templates for common tasks like drafting discharge summaries, patient instructions, clinical letters, and prior authorization support. Clinical teams spend less time rewriting and searching, and patients have clearer next steps and smoother transitions of care.

- **可复用模板，实现工作流自动化：** 提供常用任务的共享模板，例如出院小结撰写、患者指导说明、临床信函起草及预先授权支持等。临床团队大幅减少重复撰写与信息检索耗时，患者则能更清晰地了解后续步骤，实现更顺畅的照护衔接。

- **Access management and governance:** A centralized workspace with role-based access controls and organization-wide user management through SAML SSO and SCIM. This gives healthcare organizations the governance and visibility they need to deploy AI across clinical, administrative, and research teams.

- **访问管理与治理能力：** 提供集中式工作空间，支持基于角色的访问控制（RBAC），并通过 SAML 单点登录（SSO）与 SCIM 协议实现全组织范围的用户统一管理。这赋予医疗机构在临床、行政及科研团队中全面部署 AI 所需的治理能力与全局可见性。

- **Data control and support for HIPAA compliance:** Patient data and PHI remain under an organization’s control, with options for data residency, audit logs, customer-managed encryption keys, and a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with OpenAI to support HIPAA-compliant use. Content shared with ChatGPT for Healthcare is not used to train models.

- **数据自主管控与 HIPAA 合规支持：** 患者数据及受保护健康信息（PHI）始终由医疗机构自主掌控；支持数据本地化存储、操作审计日志、客户自管加密密钥，并提供与 OpenAI 签署的《业务伙伴协议》（BAA），全面保障 HIPAA 合规使用。用户向 ChatGPT for Healthcare 提交的内容不会用于模型训练。

[Learn more⁠](https://openai.com/business-data/) about our enterprise-grade security, privacy, and compliance programs.

[了解更多⁠](https://openai.com/business-data/) 关于我们企业级的安全性、隐私保护与合规性计划。

**Supporting clinical and operational workflows:** In practice, teams use ChatGPT for Healthcare to synthesize medical evidence alongside institutional guidance and apply it to a patient’s specific context, draft clinical and administrative documentation, and adapt patient-facing education materials for readability and translation. This reduces time spent on admin, helps teams follow shared standards of care, and supports a better patient experience—while clinicians stay in charge.

**赋能临床与运营工作流：** 实际应用中，团队借助 ChatGPT for Healthcare 将最新医学证据与本机构诊疗规范相结合，精准适配患者个体情况；高效完成临床与行政文书起草；并针对可读性与多语言需求优化面向患者的健康教育材料。此举显著降低行政事务耗时，推动团队遵循统一的照护标准，提升患者体验——同时确保临床医生始终掌握决策主导权。

借助 OpenAI API 平台，开发者可利用我们最新发布的模型（包括 GPT‑5.2）为各类工具与产品赋能，并将 AI 直接嵌入医疗信息系统与工作流中。符合条件的客户可向 OpenAI 申请《商业伙伴协议》（Business Associate Agreement, BAA），以满足《健康保险流通与责任法案》（HIPAA）的合规要求。

## AI models optimized for healthcare

## 针对医疗健康场景优化的 AI 模型

All OpenAI for Healthcare products are powered by GPT‑5.2 models, which outperform earlier OpenAI models and were developed through ongoing research and real-world evaluation that reflect how clinicians actually use AI.

所有面向医疗健康的 OpenAI 产品均由 GPT‑5.2 模型驱动。该模型性能超越此前各代 OpenAI 模型，其研发过程融合了持续性研究与真实世界评估，充分反映临床医生实际使用 AI 的方式与需求。

Over the past two years, we’ve [partnered with⁠](https://openai.com/index/healthbench/) a global network of more than 260 licensed physicians across 60 countries of practice to evaluate model performance using real clinical scenarios. To date, this group has reviewed more than 600,000 model outputs spanning 30 areas of focus. Their continuous feedback has directly informed model training, safety mitigations, and product iteration. ChatGPT for Healthcare went through multiple rounds of physician-led red teaming to tune model behavior, trustworthy information retrieval, and other evaluations.

过去两年间，我们已[携手⁠](https://openai.com/index/healthbench/)覆盖全球 60 个国家/地区的逾 260 名持证医师，组成国际协作网络，依托真实临床场景对模型性能开展系统性评估。截至目前，该团队已审阅超 60 万条模型输出结果，涵盖 30 个重点临床领域。他们持续提供的反馈，直接指导了模型训练、安全性改进及产品迭代优化。ChatGPT for Healthcare 更历经多轮由临床医生主导的“红队测试”（red teaming），以精细调优模型行为、提升信息检索可信度，并完成其他关键评估。

We also look to evidence from live deployments. A study with Penda Health found that an [OpenAI-powered clinical copilot⁠](https://openai.com/index/ai-clinical-copilot-penda-health/) used in routine primary care reduced both diagnostic and treatment errors—early evidence that AI, when deployed with appropriate safeguards and clinician oversight, can improve care quality.

我们同样关注真实部署场景中的实证数据。一项与Penda Health合作开展的研究发现，在常规初级医疗中使用的[由OpenAI提供支持的临床助手⁠](https://openai.com/index/ai-clinical-copilot-penda-health/)，显著降低了诊断错误与治疗错误——这为早期证据表明：只要配备适当的安全保障机制并辅以临床医生监督，AI便有望提升医疗服务的质量。

Benchmarks like [HealthBench⁠](https://openai.com/index/healthbench/), an open, clinician-designed evaluation, also reinforce this progress. HealthBench measures model behavior across realistic medical scenarios using rubrics written by physicians. It goes beyond factual recall to assess clinical reasoning, safety, uncertainty handling, and communication quality—dimensions that better reflect how clinicians use AI in practice. Across these evaluations, GPT‑5.2 models consistently outperform prior generations and comparator models on real clinical workflows.

诸如[HealthBench⁠](https://openai.com/index/healthbench/)等评测基准（一种开放、由临床医生主导设计的评估框架）也进一步印证了这一进展。HealthBench借助医师编写的评分标准，在贴近真实临床场景的任务中评估模型行为；其评估维度不仅限于事实性知识的准确回忆，更涵盖临床推理能力、安全性、不确定性应对能力以及沟通质量——这些维度更能反映临床医生在实际工作中如何使用AI。在所有此类评估中，GPT‑5.2模型在真实临床工作流任务上持续超越此前各代模型及其他对比模型。
