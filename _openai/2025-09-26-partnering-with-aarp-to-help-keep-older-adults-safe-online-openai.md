# Partnering with AARP to help keep older adults safe online

# 与美国退休人员协会（AARP）合作，助力老年人安全上网

![Soft abstract background with blended pastel pink and teal hues, creating a calm and welcoming gradient.](images/partnering-with-aarp-to-help-keep-older-adults-safe-online-openai/img_001.png)

![柔和抽象背景，融合淡粉色与青绿色调，营造宁静而亲切的渐变效果。](images/partnering-with-aarp-to-help-keep-older-adults-safe-online-openai/img_001.png)

Today **,** we’re launching a multi-year effort with AARP and its Older Adults Technology Services (OATS) to help older adults use AI confidently, safely, and securely—starting with a new OpenAI Academy video that teaches them how to use ChatGPT to help spot and avoid scams.

今天，我们正式启动一项为期多年的合作计划，携手美国退休人员协会（AARP）及其下属机构“老年人科技服务部”（OATS），帮助老年人自信、安全、可靠地使用人工智能——首项举措即发布一部全新的 OpenAI 学院视频，指导老年人如何运用 ChatGPT 识别并规避网络诈骗。

In 2024, OpenAI worked with OATS, which helps older adults thrive in a digital world, to launch the Societal Resilience Fund, a $2 million commitment supported by Microsoft, to strengthen OATS’s flagship [_Senior Planet_ ⁠(opens in a new window)](https://seniorplanet.org/) program as well as the work of other key partners.

2024 年，OpenAI 与致力于帮助老年人在数字世界中蓬勃发展的 OATS 合作，共同发起“社会韧性基金”（Societal Resilience Fund）——一项由微软支持、总额达 200 万美元的承诺，旨在强化 OATS 的旗舰项目 [_Senior Planet_（在新窗口中打开）](https://seniorplanet.org/)，以及支持其他关键合作伙伴的相关工作。

Our mission is for artificial intelligence to benefit everyone and this effort helps put transformative tools into the hands of as many people as possible. That mission is especially urgent for communities that have often been left behind in prior technological revolutions, including older Americans. According to an AARP [survey⁠(opens in a new window)](https://www.aarp.org/pri/topics/technology/internet-media-devices/2025-technology-trends-older-adults/), AI use among older adults has doubled and another 30 percent are excited about its potential.

我们的使命是让人工智能惠及每一个人；而此次合作，正是将变革性工具交到尽可能多的人手中的重要一步。这一使命对那些在以往历次技术革命中常被落下的群体而言尤为紧迫——其中包括美国老年人群体。据美国退休人员协会（AARP）一项[调查（在新窗口中打开）](https://www.aarp.org/pri/topics/technology/internet-media-devices/2025-technology-trends-older-adults/)显示，老年人使用人工智能的比例已翻倍，另有 30% 的老年人对其潜力充满期待。

The next phase of our work with OATS introduces new nationwide training and community-driven safety programs such as:

接下来，我们与 OATS 的合作将进入新阶段，推出覆盖全美范围的全新培训及社区主导的安全项目，包括：

- **Expanded AI training nationwide**—updated in-person and online Senior Planet curriculum, including a short educational video showing how to spot scams, and subgrants to local partners to scale AI literacy in communities across the country.

- **面向全国的拓展型人工智能培训**——更新后的线下与线上“Senior Planet”课程体系，包含一部简短教学视频，讲解如何识别网络诈骗；同时向地方合作伙伴提供分拨资助，以在全国各地社区提升人工智能素养。

- **Digital safety and privacy programs**—a refreshed _AI for_ _Older Adults Guide_, a new course on protecting privacy and data, and specialized trainings for AARP state offices.

- **数字安全与隐私保护项目**——全面更新《面向老年人的人工智能指南》（*AI for Older Adults Guide*）；开设一门全新课程，聚焦隐私与数据保护；并为 AARP 各州办事处定制专项培训。

- **Research and community insights**—an annual national survey of AI use among older adults.

- **研究与社区洞察**——开展针对老年人人工智能使用情况的年度全国性调查。

As part of these new efforts, OpenAI and OATS collaborated on educational content designed to help older adults spot scams and use ChatGPT as a “second pair of eyes.”  

作为这些新举措的一部分，OpenAI 与 OATS 合作开发了教育内容，旨在帮助老年人识别诈骗，并将 ChatGPT 用作一双“额外的眼睛”。

Available on the [OpenAI Academy⁠(opens in a new window)](https://academy.openai.com/), the [video⁠(opens in a new window)](https://academy.openai.com/home/videos/using-chatgpt-to-spot-scams-2025-09-25) walks viewers through common warning signs—such as urgent language, secrecy, or suspicious links—and shows how to get quick feedback on whether something might be fraudulent.  

该视频已在 [OpenAI Academy⁠（在新窗口中打开）](https://academy.openai.com/) 上线，[视频链接⁠（在新窗口中打开）](https://academy.openai.com/home/videos/using-chatgpt-to-spot-scams-2025-09-25)。视频向观众详细讲解了常见预警信号——例如紧迫性话术、要求保密、可疑链接等，并演示了如何快速获取反馈，判断某条信息是否可能存在欺诈风险。

The message is simple: pause, think, and ask. ChatGPT can help flag suspicious messages, but it should be combined with and does not replace personal judgment and basic safety steps like avoiding links or sharing personal information.  

核心信息简洁明了：暂停、思考、提问。ChatGPT 可协助标记可疑消息，但它必须与个人判断相结合，而不能取代后者；同时，也绝不能替代基本的安全措施，例如避免点击可疑链接或不随意分享个人信息。

The expanded partnership comes as we mark the first anniversary of the OpenAI Academy— a free online resource to support AI literacy and help people from all backgrounds access tools, best practices and peer insights to use AI more effectively and responsibly. The OpenAI Academy has already helped more than 2 million people— including workers, small businesses, nonprofit leaders, students and others.  

此次合作升级恰逢 OpenAI Academy 成立一周年之际。OpenAI Academy 是一项免费的在线资源平台，致力于提升公众的 AI 素养，帮助来自不同背景的人群获取实用工具、最佳实践及同行经验，从而更高效、更负责任地使用人工智能。截至目前，OpenAI Academy 已惠及逾 200 万人，涵盖职场人士、小微企业主、非营利组织负责人、学生及其他各类人群。

Our collaboration with OATS reinforces this fundamental idea: AI education should be a basic right in the Intelligence Age. Whether you’re a college student, a retiree exploring new tools, or someone contemplating a career change, you should have the ability to gain AI skills and knowledge. Together with OATS and communities across the globe, we’re making AI education accessible to everyone, at every age.  

我们与 OATS 的合作进一步印证了这一根本理念：在智能时代，AI 教育应成为一项基本权利。无论你是大学生、正在探索新技术的退休人士，还是考虑职业转型的从业者，都应当拥有获取 AI 技能与知识的机会。我们正携手 OATS 及全球各地的社区，让 AI 教育真正实现全民可及、全龄覆盖。
