# Making AI work for everyone, everywhere

# 让人工智能惠及全球每一个人

Our approach to localization.

我们对本地化的实践路径。

OpenAI’s mission is to ensure AGI benefits all of humanity, and to fulfill this mission we need to meet people where they are all over the world.

OpenAI 的使命是确保通用人工智能（AGI）造福全人类；而要实现这一使命，我们必须深入世界各地，真正走到人们所处的现实环境中去。

AI is increasingly recognized as critical national infrastructure, on a par with electricity. Governments and institutions around the world want to ensure their citizens and economies can benefit from the AI era by having access to the most capable systems available.

人工智能正日益被视作与电力同等重要的国家关键基础设施。全球各国政府及机构都希望，通过接入当前最先进的人工智能系统，让本国公民和经济切实受益于人工智能时代。

For AI to deliver on that promise, it also needs to be locally relevant. That means speaking in local languages and with local accents, respecting local laws, and reflecting cultural norms and values.

要兑现这一承诺，人工智能还必须具备本地相关性——即使用当地语言、带有本地口音进行交互，尊重当地法律法规，并体现本土的文化规范与价值观。

Only a small number of countries, however, are in a position to develop frontier AI models themselves. For most, the challenge is not how to build a model from scratch, but how to adapt the best available AI so it works for their specific context. This is something we consistently hear from governments around the world: they want sovereign AI they can build with us, not just systems translated into their language.

然而，目前仅有少数国家具备自主研发前沿人工智能模型的能力。对大多数国家而言，挑战不在于从零开始构建模型，而在于如何将全球最先进的 AI 技术适配到其特定的国情与场景中。这正是我们持续从世界各国政府听到的共同诉求：他们渴望的是能与 OpenAI 共同共建的“主权人工智能”（sovereign AI），而不仅仅是将现有系统简单翻译成其本国语言。

Through our [OpenAI for Countries initiative⁠](https://openai.com/global-affairs/openai-for-countries/), we have been exploring how localization could work in practice. The goal is to allow for localized AI systems, while still benefiting from a global, frontier-level model.

通过我们的 [“OpenAI for Countries”（面向各国的 OpenAI）倡议](https://openai.com/global-affairs/openai-for-countries/)，我们正在积极探索本地化在实际应用中的可行路径。其目标是在构建真正适配本地需求的人工智能系统的同时，持续依托并受益于一个全球统一、处于前沿水平的基础大模型。

We are currently piloting a localized version of ChatGPT for students in Estonia as part of our ChatGPT Edu work, incorporating local curricula and pedagogical approaches. We are also exploring pilot localisation efforts with other countries. As part of our commitment to transparency in how AI is researched and deployed, we are sharing more detail on how localization works.

我们目前正在爱沙尼亚面向学生试点本地化的 ChatGPT 版本，这是 ChatGPT Edu 工作的一部分，融入了当地课程体系与教学法。我们也在与其他国家合作探索类似的本地化试点项目。作为我们对人工智能研发与部署过程保持透明度承诺的一部分，我们在此进一步说明本地化工作的具体机制。

Our [Model Spec⁠](https://openai.com/index/sharing-the-latest-model-spec/) is a public document that sets out how we intend our models to behave. We train our models to follow the Spec, and continuously refine it via a collaborative, whole-of-OpenAI process that incorporates what our teams are hearing from people around the world. The Spec speaks to the gamut of ways our models are used, ranging from ChatGPT, to experiences developers build on our platform, to other contexts.  These rules, which apply everywhere our models are deployed, define clear boundaries on what can and cannot be changed and our commitment to be transparent about changes.

我们的 [《模型规范》（Model Spec）](https://openai.com/index/sharing-the-latest-model-spec/) 是一份公开文档，阐明了我们期望模型所应具备的行为准则。我们训练模型严格遵循该规范，并通过全公司协作的方式持续优化完善——这一过程广泛吸纳全球各地团队从用户及合作伙伴处获得的反馈。该规范涵盖模型的所有应用场景，包括 ChatGPT 本身、开发者基于我们平台构建的应用体验，以及其他各类使用情境。这些规则适用于模型在全球所有部署场景，清晰界定了哪些内容可调整、哪些不可更改，并郑重承诺：任何变更都将保持高度透明。

The Model Spec includes “ [red-line principles⁠(opens in a new window)](https://model-spec.openai.com/2025-09-12.html#red_line_principles)” that apply to all deployments, including those under the OpenAI for Countries program. In them, we emphasize that “human safety and human rights are paramount to OpenAI’s mission,” and make clear that:

《模型规范》中包含“[红线原则（red-line principles）](https://model-spec.openai.com/2025-09-12.html#red_line_principles)”（在新窗口中打开），适用于所有部署场景，包括“OpenAI for Countries”计划下的各项部署。其中，我们着重强调：“人类安全与人权是 OpenAI 使命的重中之重”，并明确声明：

- We will not allow our models to enable severe harms such as acts of violence, weapons of mass destruction, terrorism, persecution or mass surveillance.  
- 我们绝不允许模型助长严重危害行为，例如暴力行为、大规模杀伤性武器开发、恐怖主义、迫害或大规模监控。

- We will not allow our models to be used for targeted or scaled exclusion, manipulation, for undermining human autonomy, or eroding participation in civic processes.  
- 我们绝不允许模型被用于定向或规模化排斥、操纵、削弱人类自主权，或侵蚀公众参与社会公共事务的能力。

- We are committed to safeguarding individuals’ privacy in their interactions with AI.  
- 我们致力于保障个人在与人工智能交互过程中的隐私安全。

When OpenAI provides a first-party experience directly to consumers like ChatGPT we also commit that through it:

当 OpenAI 直接面向消费者提供第一方产品体验（例如 ChatGPT）时，我们还承诺通过该产品实现以下目标：

- People should have easy access to trustworthy safety-critical information from our models.  
- 用户应能便捷获取来自我们模型的可信且关乎安全的关键信息。

- Customization, personalization, and localization will not override the binding rules throughout the Model Spec. This includes the [objective point-of-view⁠(opens in a new window)](https://model-spec.openai.com/2025-12-18.html#assume_objective_pov) principle, meaning localization may affect language or tone, but it cannot change the substance or balance of facts presented.  
- 定制化、个性化与本地化均不得凌驾于《模型规范》中各项强制性规则之上。这包括[客观视角原则（objective point-of-view）](https://model-spec.openai.com/2025-12-18.html#assume_objective_pov)（在新窗口中打开）：本地化可影响语言表达或语调风格，但绝不能改变所呈现事实的内容实质或平衡性。

- People should have transparency into the important rules and reasons behind our models’ behavior, e.g., any content omitted due to legal requirements will be transparently indicated to the user in each model response, specifying the type of information removed and the rationale for its removal, without disclosing the redacted content itself. Similarly, any information added will also be transparently identified.  
- 用户应充分了解模型行为背后的重要规则与原因。例如：因法律要求而省略的任何内容，将在每次模型响应中向用户清晰标明——说明被移除信息的类型及其删减依据，但不披露被删减的具体内容本身；同理，任何额外添加的信息也将被明确标识。

As we explore localized, sovereign AI through OpenAI for Countries, we are committed to keep on sharing more about what we learn, and to evolving our approach transparently.

随着我们通过“OpenAI for Countries”计划探索本地化、主权化的人工智能路径，我们将持续分享实践过程中获得的经验与洞见，并以完全透明的方式不断优化和完善我们的方法论。
