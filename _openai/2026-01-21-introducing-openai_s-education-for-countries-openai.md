# Introducing OpenAI’s Education for Countries

# 推出“OpenAI 国家教育计划”

Helping countries build future ready education systems and workforces with AI

借助人工智能，助力各国构建面向未来的教育体系与人才队伍

The history of technology suggests that the biggest economic gains come not from invention alone, but from turning new capabilities into scaled, everyday use. But even as AI capabilities have improved, [we see a widening “capability overhang,”](https://openai.com/index/ai-for-self-empowerment/) defined as the gap between what AI tools can do and how people are using them.

技术发展史表明，最大的经济收益并非仅源于发明本身，而更在于将新能力规模化、日常化地投入实际应用。然而，尽管人工智能能力持续提升，[我们却观察到日益扩大的“能力悬差”（capability overhang）](https://openai.com/index/ai-for-self-empowerment/)——即人工智能工具实际所能实现的功能，与人们当前对其的实际使用程度之间存在的差距。

Education systems are a critical route through which this gap is closed. [Studies⁠(opens in a new window)](https://reports.weforum.org/docs/WEF_Future_of_Jobs_Report_2025.pdf?utm_source=chatgpt.com) project that by 2030 nearly 40% of the core skills workers rely on today will change, driven largely by AI. By embedding AI tools, training, and research into the core infrastructure of schools and universities, education systems can evolve alongside these shifts and better prepare students to thrive in a world with AI.

教育体系是弥合这一差距的关键路径。[相关研究报告⁠（在新窗口中打开）](https://reports.weforum.org/docs/WEF_Future_of_Jobs_Report_2025.pdf?utm_source=chatgpt.com)预测，到2030年，劳动者目前所依赖的核心技能中近40%将发生改变，其中AI是主要驱动因素。通过将AI工具、培训与研究深度融入中小学及高校的核心基础设施，教育体系可同步演进，从而更好地帮助学生适应并蓬勃发展于一个人工智能深度融入的世界。

## OpenAI’s Education for Countries

## “OpenAI 国家教育计划”

It is for this purpose that we are launching **OpenAI’s Education for Countries** as a new pillar of our [OpenAI for Countries](https://openai.com/index/how-countries-can-end-the-capability-overhang/) initiative. We will work with governments and university consortia to bring AI into education systems to personalize learning, reduce administrative burden, and prepare students for the workforce. Working with Ministries of Education, partners, universities and researchers, the initiative will bring together several core elements:

正是出于这一目标，我们正式推出 **“OpenAI 国家教育计划”**，作为我们 [OpenAI for Countries](https://openai.com/index/how-countries-can-end-the-capability-overhang/) 倡议的全新支柱。我们将携手各国政府与高校联盟，将人工智能引入教育体系，以实现学习个性化、减轻行政负担，并助力学生为未来职场做好准备。本计划将联合各国教育部、合作伙伴、高等院校及研究人员，整合以下几项核心要素：

- **AI tools for learning:** Access to [ChatGPT Edu⁠(opens in a new window)](https://chatgpt.com/business/education/?openaicom-did=ff4ef55e-a266-43b3-8611-7f0d4d1b8872&openaicom_referred=true), [GPT‑5.2](https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-5-2/), [study mode](https://openai.com/index/chatgpt-study-mode/), and [canvas](https://openai.com/index/introducing-canvas/) can be customized to shape how the world’s most advanced AI models are used to support local learning priorities.  
- **面向学习的 AI 工具：** 可获取 [ChatGPT Edu⁠（在新窗口中打开）](https://chatgpt.com/business/education/?openaicom-did=ff4ef55e-a266-43b3-8611-7f0d4d1b8872&openaicom_referred=true)、[GPT‑5.2](https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-5-2/)、[学习模式（Study Mode）](https://openai.com/index/chatgpt-study-mode/) 和 [Canvas](https://openai.com/index/introducing-canvas/)，并可根据各地教育发展重点，灵活定制这些全球最先进的 AI 模型，以切实支持本地教学与学习需求。

- **Learning outcomes research:** Collaboration on large-scale, national research initiatives to understand how AI supports learning and affects teacher productivity, which can inform local policy, workforce development, and future technology design.  
- **学习成效研究：** 联合开展大规模国家级研究项目，深入探究 AI 如何促进学生学习、影响教师工作效率，研究成果将为地方教育政策制定、人才队伍建设以及未来教育技术设计提供实证依据。

- **OpenAI Certifications and training:** Tailored training with ministries and education systems, from the [OpenAI Academy⁠(opens in a new window)](https://academy.openai.com/) to ChatGPT‑based certifications, giving educators and students the practical AI skills aligned with national workforce priorities.  
- **OpenAI 认证与培训体系：** 面向各国教育部及教育系统提供定制化培训，涵盖 [OpenAI 学院⁠（在新窗口中打开）](https://academy.openai.com/) 课程及基于 ChatGPT 的系列认证项目，助力教育工作者与学生掌握契合本国劳动力发展战略所需的实用 AI 技能。

- **Global network of partners:** A growing network of governments, researchers, and education leaders who share insights, highlight successful deployments, and help shape responsible approaches to AI in education.  
- **全球合作伙伴网络：** 不断扩大的跨国合作网络，汇聚各国政府机构、研究人员与教育领导者，共同分享实践经验、推广成功案例，并协同构建教育领域负责任使用 AI 的治理框架与实施路径。

## Our growing work with countries globally  

## 我们在全球范围内的持续拓展工作

Our first cohort includes Estonia, Greece, Italy’s Conference of University Rectors (CRUI), Jordan, Kazakhstan, Slovakia, Trinidad & Tobago, and the United Arab Emirates.  

首批合作国家与地区包括：爱沙尼亚、希腊、意大利大学校长会议（CRUI）、约旦、哈萨克斯坦、斯洛伐克、特立尼达和多巴哥、阿拉伯联合酋长国。

AI tools like ChatGPT Edu have already been deployed nationwide in Estonia, across public universities and secondary schools, reaching more than 30,000 students, educators, and researchers in its first year. Longitudinal research partnerships are also underway, such as a large-scale study with the University of Tartu and Stanford, to measure how AI affects learning outcomes among 20,000 students over time.  

以 ChatGPT Edu 为代表的 AI 工具已在爱沙尼亚实现全国范围部署，覆盖所有公立大学与中学，首年即惠及逾 30,000 名学生、教师及研究人员。同时，纵向研究合作亦已启动，例如爱沙尼亚塔尔图大学与美国斯坦福大学联合开展的大规模追踪研究，旨在长期评估 AI 对 20,000 名学生学习成效的影响。

As AI is introduced at scale, rollouts typically follow a phased approach, starting by equipping educators with the tools and training they need to lead AI use in classrooms. In higher education, ChatGPT Edu is already available to students. In high schools, student access begins through small pilots developed in close collaboration with local leaders, to ensure safety and alignment with local curricula. These pilots are paired with ongoing work by OpenAI to strengthen protections for young people who use ChatGPT, including age-appropriate model behavior improvements and developing AI literacy content for educators with trusted partners like Common Sense Media.  

当 AI 大规模进入教育系统时，通常采取分阶段推进策略：首先赋能教师，为其配备必要工具与专业培训，使其成为课堂 AI 应用的引领者。在高等教育领域，ChatGPT Edu 已全面向学生开放；而在中学阶段，则通过与地方教育主管部门密切协作开展小范围试点，确保学生使用安全及与本地课程体系的高度契合。与此同时，OpenAI 正持续加强针对青少年用户的安全保护机制，包括优化模型行为以适配不同年龄段用户，并携手“常识媒体”（Common Sense Media）等值得信赖的合作伙伴，共同开发面向教育工作者的 AI 素养教学资源。

> “We believe AI in education should strengthen how students learn, not just what they know. This partnership with OpenAI supports our nationwide AI Leap program, initiated by President Alar Karis’s call to use AI not the most, but the smartest way, focuses on giving every teacher and high school student equal access to AI tools purpose-built for learning. We’re studying both the benefits and risks of AI in classrooms to ensure it truly supports learning.”  

> “我们坚信，教育领域的 AI 应当强化学生的学习方式，而不仅限于扩充其知识内容。此次与 OpenAI 的合作，有力支撑了我国‘AI 跃升计划’（AI Leap）的全国性实施——该计划由爱沙尼亚总统阿拉尔·卡里斯（Alar Karis）发起，倡导‘不求 AI 使用最多，但求 AI 运用最智’，核心目标是确保每一位教师与高中生都能公平获得专为学习场景深度优化的 AI 工具。我们正同步开展课堂 AI 应用的效益与风险评估研究，以切实保障 AI 真正服务于学习本质。”

Ivo Visak, CEO of AI Leap  

伊沃·维萨克（Ivo Visak），AI 跃升计划（AI Leap）首席执行官

## Ensuring AI benefits everyone  

## 确保 AI 惠及每一个人

OpenAI’s mission is to ensure that advanced AI benefits everyone. We're building AI to help people solve hard problems because by helping with the hard problems, AI can benefit the most people possible—through more scientific discoveries, better healthcare and education, and improved productivity. This work reflects a simple belief: powerful technologies should expand opportunity for all, not exclude people from it.  

OpenAI 的使命是确保先进 AI 技术惠及全人类。我们致力于打造能够帮助人们解决重大难题的 AI——因为唯有攻克这些关键挑战，AI 才能最大限度地造福大众：推动更多科学发现、提升医疗与教育质量、增强社会整体生产力。这项工作根植于一个朴素信念：强大的技术应当为所有人拓展机会，而非将任何人拒之门外。

OpenAI’s Education for Countries builds on this mission, as well as our work to ensure AI expands economic opportunity with [OpenAI Certifications](https://openai.com/index/openai-certificate-courses/), which help individuals build foundational AI skills and give clear signals to employers about their ability to use AI effectively at work.  

“OpenAI 国家教育计划”（Education for Countries）正是这一使命的延伸实践；同时，它也承接了我们通过 [OpenAI 认证项目](https://openai.com/index/openai-certificate-courses/) 推动 AI 经济机遇普惠化的努力——该认证体系帮助个人夯实 AI 基础能力，并向雇主清晰传递其在工作中高效运用 AI 的实际水平。

The program also represents a step forward in OpenAI’s ongoing commitment to supporting learning with AI—complementing programs like [NextGenAI](https://openai.com/index/introducing-nextgenai/) to accelerate research on AI and learning across universities, products to enhance how AI is used in education like [ChatGPT Edu](https://openai.com/index/introducing-chatgpt-edu/) and [study mode](https://openai.com/index/chatgpt-study-mode/), and partnerships to support teacher-led AI adoption like with the [American Federation of Teachers](https://openai.com/global-affairs/aft/) in the United States.

该计划也标志着 OpenAI 持续致力于以人工智能支持教育学习的又一重要进展——它与多项举措相辅相成，例如旨在加速高校人工智能与教育研究的 [NextGenAI](https://openai.com/index/introducing-nextgenai/) 计划；提升人工智能在教育中应用效果的产品，如 [ChatGPT Edu](https://openai.com/index/introducing-chatgpt-edu/) 和 [学习模式（Study Mode）](https://openai.com/index/chatgpt-study-mode/)；以及支持教师主导的人工智能落地实践的合作项目，例如与美国[美国教师联合会（AFT）](https://openai.com/global-affairs/aft/) 开展的合作。

Our next cohort will be announced later in 2026. To learn more about how to join, contact our team.

下一届学员名单将于 2026 年晚些时候公布。如需了解如何加入，请联系我们的团队。
