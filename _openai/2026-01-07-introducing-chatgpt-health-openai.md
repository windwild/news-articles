We’re introducing ChatGPT Health, a dedicated experience that securely brings your health information and ChatGPT’s intelligence together, to help you feel more informed, prepared, and confident navigating your health.

我们正式推出 ChatGPT Health——一款专属健康体验，安全地将您的健康信息与 ChatGPT 的智能能力相结合，助您更全面地掌握健康知识、更充分地做好准备，并更有信心地管理自身健康。

Health is already one of the most common ways people use ChatGPT, with hundreds of millions of people asking health and wellness questions each week. ChatGPT Health builds on the strong privacy, security, and data controls across ChatGPT with additional, layered protections designed specifically for health— including purpose-built encryption and isolation to keep health conversations protected and compartmentalized. You can securely connect medical records and wellness apps to ground conversations in your own health information, so responses are more relevant and useful to you. Designed in close collaboration with physicians, ChatGPT Health helps people take a more active role in understanding and managing their health and wellness—while supporting, not replacing, care from clinicians.

健康已是用户使用 ChatGPT 最常见的场景之一：每周有数亿人向 ChatGPT 提出各类健康与福祉相关问题。ChatGPT Health 在 ChatGPT 已有的强大隐私保护、安全机制和数据管控基础之上，进一步叠加了专为健康场景设计的多重防护措施——包括专为健康数据定制的加密技术与隔离机制，确保健康对话内容受到严格保护并实现逻辑隔离。您可安全地接入个人电子病历及健康类应用程序，使对话基于您自身的健康信息展开，从而获得更贴切、更实用的回复。ChatGPT Health 由 OpenAI 与临床医生紧密协作设计，旨在帮助用户更主动地理解并管理自身健康与福祉，同时作为临床医护服务的有力补充，而非替代。

## A dedicated health experience

## 专属健康体验

Today, health information is often scattered across portals, apps, wearables, PDFs, and medical notes—so it's hard to see the full picture, and people are left to navigate a complex healthcare system on their own. People have shared countless stories of turning to ChatGPT to help make sense of it all. In fact, health is one of the most common ways people use ChatGPT today: based on our de-identified analysis of conversations, over 230 million people globally ask health and wellness related questions on ChatGPT every week.

如今，健康信息常常分散在各类门户网站、应用程序、可穿戴设备、PDF 文件以及医疗记录中——因此很难全面掌握自身健康状况，人们不得不独自应对复杂的医疗保健体系。许多人已分享了大量故事，讲述他们如何借助 ChatGPT 来理清这些纷繁复杂的健康信息。事实上，“健康”已成为当前人们使用 ChatGPT 最普遍的场景之一：根据我们对脱敏对话数据的分析，全球每周有超过 2.3 亿人通过 ChatGPT 提出与健康和福祉相关的问题。

ChatGPT Health builds on this so responses are informed by your health information and context. You can now securely connect medical records and wellness apps—like Apple Health, Function, and MyFitnessPal—so ChatGPT can help you understand recent test results, prepare for appointments with your doctor, get advice on how to approach your diet and workout routine, or understand the tradeoffs of different insurance options based on your healthcare patterns.

ChatGPT Health 在此基础上进一步发展，使回复能够结合您的个人健康信息与具体情境。您现在可以安全地连接电子病历及健康管理类应用（例如 Apple Health、Function 和 MyFitnessPal），从而让 ChatGPT 协助您理解近期检验报告、为就诊提前做好准备、获取关于饮食与运动计划的专业建议，或基于您的就医模式，帮您厘清不同医疗保险方案之间的利弊权衡。

Health is designed to support, not replace, medical care. It is not intended for diagnosis or treatment. Instead, it helps you navigate everyday questions and understand patterns over time—not just moments of illness—so you can feel more informed and prepared for important medical conversations. To keep your health information protected and secure, Health operates as a separate space with enhanced privacy to protect sensitive data. Conversations in Health are not used to train our foundation models. If you start a health-related conversation in ChatGPT, we’ll suggest moving into Health for these additional protections.

Health 的设计初衷是辅助而非替代专业医疗服务；它不用于疾病诊断或治疗。相反，它旨在帮助您解答日常健康疑问，并理解长期健康趋势（而不仅限于生病时的片段信息），从而让您在重要医疗沟通中更有依据、更加从容。为确保您的健康信息得到充分保护与安全保障，Health 作为一个独立空间运行，配备强化隐私保护机制，以守护敏感健康数据。Health 中的对话内容不会用于训练我们的基础大模型。若您在 ChatGPT 中开启一段与健康相关的对话，系统将建议您转入 Health 空间，以获得上述额外保护。

If you’re interested in getting access as it becomes available, you can sign up for the [waitlist⁠(opens in a new window)](https://chatgpt.com/health/waitlist?openaicom-did=fde108b3-cf78-4adf-b324-cfd7972b30a3&openaicom_referred=true). We’re starting by providing access to a small group of early users to learn and continue refining the experience—users with ChatGPT Free, Go, Plus, and Pro plans outside of the European Economic Area, Switzerland, and the United Kingdom are eligible. As we make improvements, we plan to expand access and make Health available to all users on web and iOS in the coming weeks.

如果您希望在功能上线后第一时间体验，欢迎加入 [等候名单⁠(在新窗口中打开)](https://chatgpt.com/health/waitlist?openaicom-did=fde108b3-cf78-4adf-b324-cfd7972b30a3&openaicom_referred=true)。我们初期将面向一小部分早期用户开放访问权限，以便收集反馈、持续优化产品体验——目前，欧洲经济区、瑞士及英国以外地区、且订阅了 ChatGPT Free、Go、Plus 或 Pro 计划的用户均可参与。随着功能不断完善，我们计划在未来几周内逐步扩大覆盖范围，最终向所有 Web 端与 iOS 端用户开放 Health 功能。

Medical record integrations and some apps are available in the U.S. only, and connecting Apple Health requires iOS.

电子病历集成服务及部分第三方应用目前仅限美国用户使用；连接 Apple Health 需要运行 iOS 系统的设备。

## Designed with privacy and security at the core

## 以隐私与安全为核心设计理念

Your health information is deeply personal. That’s why Health is built as a dedicated space with added protections for sensitive health information and easy-to-use controls.

您的健康信息极为私密。正因如此，Health 被设计为一个专属空间，专为敏感健康信息提供额外保护，并配备简洁易用的控制选项。

#### A dedicated space for Health

#### 专属于 Health 的独立空间

Health lives in its own space within ChatGPT, where your conversations, connected apps, and files are stored separately from your other chats. Health has separate memories, ensuring that your health context stays contained within the space. You’ll still see health chats in your chat history so you can easily return to them, but the information itself stays within Health.

Health 在 ChatGPT 内部拥有专属空间，其中您的对话记录、已连接的应用程序及上传文件均与其它聊天内容完全隔离存储。Health 拥有独立的记忆系统，确保您的健康上下文始终限定在该空间内部。您仍可在聊天历史中看到 Health 对话，以便随时返回查看，但所有相关信息本身均保留在 Health 空间内。

When helpful, ChatGPT may use context from your non-Health chats—like a recent move or lifestyle change—to make a health conversation more relevant. However, Health information and memories never flow back into your non-Health chats, and conversations outside of Health can’t access files, conversations, or memories created within Health. You can view or delete Health memories at any time within Health or the “Personalization” section of Settings.

在必要时，ChatGPT 可能会参考您非 Health 类聊天中的上下文信息（例如最近搬家或生活方式变化），以提升健康对话的相关性。然而，Health 中的信息与记忆绝不会反向同步至您的非 Health 类聊天中；同样，非 Health 类聊天也无法访问 Health 空间内生成的任何文件、对话或记忆。您可随时在 Health 界面内，或在“设置”中的“个性化”（Personalization）板块中查看或删除 Health 记忆。

#### Protections and controls for sensitive information

#### 敏感信息的保护与管控

We recognize that people share personal and sensitive information with ChatGPT. That understanding shapes how we design the security, privacy, and [data controls⁠](https://openai.com/consumer-privacy/) for all of our products—from the start.

我们深知，用户会向 ChatGPT 分享个人及敏感信息。这一认知自始至终指导着我们为所有产品设计安全机制、隐私策略以及[数据管控措施](https://openai.com/consumer-privacy/)。

Even before introducing ChatGPT Health, we built foundational protections across ChatGPT to give you meaningful control over your data, including temporary chats, the ability to delete chats from OpenAI’s systems within 30 days, and training our models not to retain personal information from user chats.

早在推出 ChatGPT Health 之前，我们便已在 ChatGPT 全平台构建了基础性保护机制，赋予您对自身数据的实质性控制权——包括支持临时对话、允许您在 30 天内从 OpenAI 系统中删除对话记录，以及训练模型避免留存用户对话中的个人信息。

Conversations and files across ChatGPT are encrypted by default at rest and in transit as part of our core security architecture. Due to the sensitive nature of health data, Health builds on this foundation with additional, layered protections—including purpose-built encryption and isolation—to keep health conversations protected and compartmentalized. Conversations in Health are not used to train our foundation models.

ChatGPT 中的所有对话与文件，在静态存储和传输过程中均默认启用加密，这是我们的核心安全架构的重要组成部分。鉴于健康数据的高度敏感性，ChatGPT Health 在此基础上进一步叠加了多重强化防护机制——包括专为健康场景设计的加密方案与数据隔离机制，以确保健康相关对话得到充分保护并严格分域管理。Health 中的对话内容**不会用于训练我们的基础大模型**。

You can further strengthen access controls by [enabling multi-factor authentication (MFA)⁠(opens in a new window)](https://chatgpt.com/?openaicom-did=fde108b3-cf78-4adf-b324-cfd7972b30a3&openaicom_referred=true#settings/Security), which adds an extra layer of protection to help prevent unauthorized access.

您还可通过[启用多因素身份验证（MFA）](https://chatgpt.com/?openaicom-did=fde108b3-cf78-4adf-b324-cfd7972b30a3&openaicom_referred=true#settings/Security)（在新窗口中打开）进一步强化访问控制，为账户增加一道额外的安全屏障，有效防范未授权访问。

#### Securely connecting your information

#### 安全地连接您的健康信息

When you choose to connect your health data, such as medical records or wellness apps, your responses are grounded in your own health information.

当您选择接入健康数据（例如电子病历或健康类 App）时，ChatGPT Health 的回应将基于您本人的真实健康信息。

To enable access to trusted U.S. healthcare providers, we partner with b.well, the largest and most secure network of live, connected health data for U.S. consumers. b.well adheres to the highest industry standards in data security and privacy. You can remove access to medical records at any time in the "Apps" section of Settings.

为支持您安全、便捷地对接美国境内值得信赖的医疗服务提供方，我们与 b.well 展开合作。b.well 是面向美国消费者规模最大、安全性最高的实时互联健康数据网络，严格遵循业界最高标准的数据安全与隐私保护规范。您可随时在“设置”→“应用”（Apps）页面中撤销对电子病历的访问授权。

You can also connect your Apple Health information and other wellness apps, such as Function and MyFitnessPal. Apps may only be connected to your health data with your explicit permission, even if they’re already connected to ChatGPT for conversations outside of Health. All apps available in Health must meet OpenAI’s privacy and security requirements, including collecting only the minimum data needed, and undergo additional security review specific to inclusion in Health. The first time you connect an app, we’ll help you understand what types of data may be collected by the third party. And you’re always in control: disconnect an app at any time and it immediately loses access.

您还可接入 Apple Health 数据及其他健康类 App（如 Function 和 MyFitnessPal）。所有 App 均须获得您的**明确授权**后，方可访问您的健康数据——即使该 App 已在非 Health 场景下与 ChatGPT 建立过连接，亦不例外。所有上架 Health 的第三方应用，均须满足 OpenAI 制定的隐私与安全要求（例如仅收集实现功能所必需的最少数据），并额外通过针对 Health 场景的专项安全审查。首次连接某款 App 时，我们将清晰告知您：该第三方可能收集哪些类型的数据。您始终掌握完全主动权——可随时断开任一 App 的连接，其访问权限将即时终止。

## Built with physicians

## 由医生共同打造

ChatGPT Health was developed in close collaboration with physicians around the world to provide clear and useful health information.

ChatGPT Health 由全球各地的临床医生深度参与研发，旨在为您提供清晰、实用的健康信息。

Over two years, we’ve worked with more than 260 physicians who have practiced in 60 countries and dozens of specialties to understand what makes an answer to a health question helpful or potentially harmful—this group has now provided feedback on model outputs over 600,000 times across 30 areas of focus. This collaboration has shaped not just what Health can do, but how it responds: how urgently to encourage follow-ups with a clinician, how to communicate clearly without oversimplifying, and how to prioritize safety in [moments that matter⁠](https://openai.com/index/helping-people-when-they-need-it-most/).

两年来，我们携手来自全球 60 个国家、涵盖数十个医学专科的逾 260 名执业医师，深入研究“怎样的健康问题回答才算有益、何时又可能带来潜在风险”。截至目前，该专家团队已在 30 个重点方向上，对模型输出累计提出反馈逾 60 万次。这一紧密协作不仅塑造了 Health 的能力边界，更深刻影响了其响应逻辑：例如，如何恰当地提示用户及时复诊；如何在确保表述清晰的同时避免过度简化；以及如何在[关键时刻](https://openai.com/index/helping-people-when-they-need-it-most/)始终将安全置于首位。

This physician-led approach is built directly into the model that powers Health, which is evaluated against clinical standards using [HealthBench⁠](https://openai.com/index/healthbench/), an assessment framework we created with input from our network of practicing physicians.  

这种以医生为主导的方法已直接内置于驱动 Health 功能的模型之中；该模型依据临床标准进行评估，所采用的评估框架为 [HealthBench⁠](https://openai.com/index/healthbench/)——这是我们联合一线执业医师网络共同开发的评估体系。

Rather than relying on exam-style questions or generic accuracy checks, HealthBench evaluates responses using physician-written rubrics that reflect how clinicians judge quality in practice—prioritizing safety, clarity, appropriate escalation of care, and respect for individual context.

HealthBench 并不依赖考试式问答或泛化的准确性检验，而是采用由医生撰写的评分标准来评估模型输出。这些标准真实反映临床工作者在实践中对质量的判断准则——始终将安全性、表述清晰性、恰当的诊疗升级机制，以及对个体背景的尊重置于首位。

This evaluation-driven approach helps ensure the model performs well on the tasks people actually need help with, including explaining lab results in accessible language, preparing questions for an appointment, interpreting data from wearables and wellness apps, and summarizing care instructions. The result is support that people can trust—always designed to support, not replace, your healthcare providers.

这一以评估为导向的方法，有助于确保模型在人们真正需要帮助的实际任务中表现优异，例如：用通俗易懂的语言解释检验报告、为就诊提前准备问题清单、解读可穿戴设备与健康类 App 所采集的数据，以及归纳整理医嘱与护理说明。最终呈现的是值得信赖的支持服务——其设计初衷永远是辅助、而非替代您的医疗保健提供者。

#### Chat about your health

#### 与 ChatGPT 聊聊您的健康状况

Health conversations feel just like chatting with ChatGPT—but grounded in the information you’ve connected. You can upload photos and files and use search, deep research, voice mode and dictation. When relevant, ChatGPT can automatically reference your connected information to provide more relevant and personalized responses. For example, you might ask: _“How’s my cholesterol trending?”_ or _“Can you summarize my latest bloodwork before my appointment?”_ To use a connected app you can start your question with it, select it from tools (+) or ChatGPT may suggest one when helpful.

健康对话的体验与使用 ChatGPT 聊天几乎一致——但所有回答均基于您已连接的健康信息。您可以上传照片和文件，并使用搜索、深度研究、语音模式及语音听写功能。在相关场景下，ChatGPT 会自动调用您已连接的数据，从而提供更贴切、更个性化的回复。例如，您可以提问：_“我的胆固醇水平近期趋势如何？”_ 或 _“能否在我就诊前帮我总结一下最近的血液检查报告？”_ 若要使用某个已连接的应用，您可在问题开头直接提及该应用名称，或从工具栏（+）中选择；当系统判断有助益时，ChatGPT 也可能主动为您推荐相应应用。
