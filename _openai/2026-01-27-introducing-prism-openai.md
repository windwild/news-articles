Science shapes nearly every part of daily life—from the medicines we rely on, to the energy that powers our homes, to the systems that keep us safe. But the pace of scientific progress is still constrained by how research is done day to day. While AI has advanced rapidly, much of the everyday work of science still relies on tools that haven’t fundamentally changed in decades.

科学塑造着日常生活的几乎每个方面——从我们所依赖的药物，到为家庭供电的能源，再到保障我们安全的各类系统。然而，科学进步的步伐仍受限于日常科研工作的开展方式。尽管人工智能已飞速发展，但科学家日常工作的许多环节，依然依赖于数十年来未发生根本性变革的工具。

We’re introducing **Prism**, a free, AI-native workspace for scientists to write and collaborate on research, powered by GPT‑5.2. Prism offers unlimited projects and collaborators and is available today to anyone with a ChatGPT personal account.

我们正式推出 **Prism**——一款免费、原生集成 AI 的科研工作空间，专为科学家撰写论文与协同研究而设计，由 GPT‑5.2 提供支持。Prism 支持无限项目与协作者，即日起向所有拥有 ChatGPT 个人账户的用户开放。

Prism will be available soon to organizations using ChatGPT Business, Enterprise, and Education plans.

Prism 即将面向使用 ChatGPT 商业版、企业版及教育版的组织用户开放。

## From isolated tools to integrated workflows

## 从彼此割裂的工具走向一体化工作流

Over the past year, we’ve begun to see AI accelerate scientific work across domains. Advanced reasoning systems like GPT‑5 are [helping push the frontiers⁠](https://openai.com/index/gpt-5-mathematical-discovery/) of mathematics, [accelerating the analysis⁠](https://openai.com/index/accelerating-science-gpt-5/) of human immune-cell experiments, and [speeding up experimental iteration⁠](https://openai.com/index/accelerating-biological-research-in-the-wet-lab/) in molecular biology.

过去一年，我们开始看到人工智能正在加速各学科领域的科研工作。诸如 GPT‑5 这样的先进推理系统，正[助力拓展数学研究的前沿边界⁠](https://openai.com/index/gpt-5-mathematical-discovery/)，[加速人类免疫细胞实验的数据分析⁠](https://openai.com/index/accelerating-science-gpt-5/)，并[加快分子生物学领域的实验迭代速度⁠](https://openai.com/index/accelerating-biological-research-in-the-wet-lab/)。

We’re still early, but it’s clear that AI will play a meaningful role in how science advances.

目前仍处于早期阶段，但人工智能将在科学进步进程中发挥切实而重要的作用，这一点已十分明确。

At the same time, much of the everyday work of research—drafting papers, revising arguments, managing equations and citations, and coordinating with collaborators —remains fragmented across disconnected tools. Researchers often move between editors, PDFs, LaTeX compilers, reference managers, and separate chat interfaces, losing context and interrupting focus.

与此同时，科研人员日常工作的大量环节——撰写论文、修改论证、管理公式与参考文献、以及与合作者协调沟通——仍分散在彼此割裂的各类工具之中。研究人员常常需要在文本编辑器、PDF 阅读器、LaTeX 编译器、文献管理软件及独立的聊天界面之间频繁切换，导致上下文丢失、注意力中断。

Prism is our first step toward addressing this fragmentation.

Prism 是我们应对这一碎片化现状迈出的第一步。

## What Prism is

## Prism 是什么

**Prism is a free workspace for scientific writing and collaboration**, with [GPT‑5.2⁠](https://openai.com/index/gpt-5-2-for-science-and-math/)—our most advanced model for mathematical and scientific reasoning—integrated directly into the workflow.

**Prism 是一个面向科研写作与协作的免费工作空间**，原生集成了 [GPT‑5.2⁠](https://openai.com/index/gpt-5-2-for-science-and-math/)——我们迄今最先进、专为数学与科学推理优化的大模型——直接嵌入整个写作流程。

It brings drafting, revision, collaboration, and preparation for publication into a single, cloud-based, LaTeX-native workspace. Rather than operating as a separate tool alongside the writing process, GPT‑5.2 works within the project itself—with access to the structure of the paper, equations, references, and surrounding context.

它将论文起草、修订、协作与出版准备整合至一个统一的、基于云端的、原生支持 LaTeX 的工作空间中。GPT‑5.2 并非作为写作流程之外的独立工具运行，而是深度嵌入项目内部——可直接访问论文结构、公式、参考文献及全部上下文信息。

Prism builds on the foundation of Crixet, a cloud-based LaTeX platform that OpenAI acquired and has since evolved into Prism as a unified product. This allowed us to start with a strong base of a mature writing and collaboration environment, and integrate AI in a way that fits naturally into scientific workflows.

Prism 基于 Crixet 构建——这是一款由 OpenAI 收购的云端 LaTeX 平台，后经持续演进，已发展为如今统一的 Prism 产品。这一基础使我们得以从一个成熟、稳健的写作与协作环境起步，并以自然契合科研工作流的方式集成人工智能能力。

With Prism, researchers can:

借助 Prism，科研人员可以：

- **Chat with GPT‑5.2 Thinking**, to explore ideas, test hypotheses, and reason through complex scientific problems in context  
- **与 GPT‑5.2 Thinking 对话**，在具体语境中探索新思路、验证假设，并深入推演复杂的科学问题  

- **Draft and revise papers with the full document as context**, including surrounding text, equations, citations, figures, and overall structure  
- **基于整篇文档上下文进行论文起草与修订**，涵盖相邻文字、公式、参考文献、图表及整体行文结构  

- **Search for and incorporate relevant literature** (for example, from arXiv) in the context of the current manuscript, and revise text in light of newly identified related work  
- **在当前手稿语境中检索并引入相关文献**（例如来自 arXiv 的论文），并依据新发现的相关研究即时修订正文  

- **Create, refactor, and reason over equations, citations, and figures**, with AI that understands how those elements relate across the paper  
- **创建、重构并推演公式、参考文献与图表**，依托能理解这些元素在全文中相互关联关系的 AI 能力  

- **Turn whiteboard equations or diagrams directly into LaTeX**, saving hours of time manipulating graphics pixel-by-pixel  
- **将白板上的公式或示意图直接转换为 LaTeX 代码**，省去逐像素手动调整图形所耗费的数小时时间  

- **Collaborate with co-authors, students, and advisors in real time**, with edits, comments, and revisions reflected immediately  
- **与合著者、学生及导师实时协作**，所有编辑、批注与修订均即时同步呈现  

- **Make direct, in-place changes to the document** when requested, without copying content between separate editors or chat tools  
- **按需对文档进行直接、原位修改**，无需在不同编辑器或聊天工具间复制粘贴内容  

- **Use optional voice-based editing** to make simple changes without interrupting writing or review  
- **启用可选的语音编辑功能**，以语音方式完成简单修改，全程不打断写作或审阅流程

## Built for collaboration at scale

## 专为大规模协作而构建

Scientific research is inherently collaborative. Papers are shaped over time by co-authors, students, advisors, and reviewers, often across institutions and geographies.

科学研究本质上是协作性的。学术论文往往历经多位合著者、学生、导师及审稿人的反复打磨，而这些参与者常常分属不同机构乃至不同国家与地区。

Prism supports **unlimited collaborators**, allowing research teams to work together without seat limits or access barriers. Because it’s cloud-based, there’s no local LaTeX installation or environment management required, making it easier for teams to collaborate in a shared workspace.

Prism 支持**无限协作者**，使研究团队可在无席位限制、无访问壁垒的情况下协同工作。由于其基于云端架构，用户无需本地安装 LaTeX 或管理复杂运行环境，从而更便捷地在共享工作区中开展协作。

By reducing version conflicts, manual merging, and mechanical overhead, Prism helps teams spend less time managing files and more time engaging with the substance of their work.

通过减少版本冲突、人工合并及机械性事务负担，Prism 帮助团队将更少时间耗费在文件管理上，而将更多精力投入到研究工作的核心内容中。

## Expanding access to scientific tools

## 拓展科研工具的可及性

Just as importantly, Prism is designed to expand access.

同样重要的是，Prism 的设计初衷正是拓展科研工具的可及性。

Prism is **free to use**, and anyone with a ChatGPT account can start writing immediately. There are no subscriptions or seat limits. By making high-quality scientific tools easier to adopt and broadly available, we hope to enable more researchers—across institutions, disciplines, and career stages—to participate fully in the scientific process.

Prism **完全免费使用**，任何拥有 ChatGPT 账户的用户均可立即开始撰写。无需订阅，亦无席位限制。我们致力于让高质量科研工具更易上手、更广泛普及，从而助力来自不同机构、学科及职业发展阶段的更多研究者，全面参与科学探索全过程。

More powerful AI features will be made available through paid ChatGPT plans over time.

更强大的 AI 功能将随时间逐步向付费版 ChatGPT 计划开放。
