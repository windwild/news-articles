# Bringing AI to local businesses: How Podium is arming 10,000+ SMBs with AI agents

# 将 AI 带入本地企业：Podium 如何为 10,000 多家中小型企业（SMB）赋能 AI 智能体

By using GPT‑5.1 to power AI agents that capture leads and close jobs, Podium gives SMBs a faster path to growth.

借助 GPT‑5.1 驱动可获取销售线索、促成交易的 AI 智能体，Podium 为中小型企业（SMB）开辟了一条更快的增长路径。

[Podium⁠(opens in a new window)](https://www.podium.com/) builds AI software for local businesses—such as HVAC providers, auto dealers, and medspas—that help them increase revenue by capturing and converting more demand and providing better service to their customers.

[Podium⁠（在新窗口中打开）](https://www.podium.com/) 为本地企业（如暖通空调服务提供商、汽车经销商和医疗美容机构）开发 AI 软件，帮助它们通过更高效地捕获与转化客户需求、并为客户提供更优质的服务，从而提升营收。

After 11 years working side-by-side with small-to-medium (SMB)-sized operators, Podium saw the opportunity to harness AI and transform how local businesses market, sell, and grow by turning missed calls and slow replies into booked jobs, more revenue, and happier customers.

在与中小型（SMB）企业经营者并肩协作 11 年后，Podium 意识到 AI 所蕴含的巨大潜力——它有望彻底变革本地企业的营销、销售与增长方式：将错失的来电与迟缓的回复，转化为已预约的服务订单、更高的营收以及更满意的客户。

Early experiments with OpenAI in 2020 focused on point solutions like spam filtering and lead enrichment. As models advanced, Podium evolved those tools into an AI agent they affectionately call “Jerry” that converses naturally, follows business policies, and drives business outcomes by scheduling and confirming appointments or following up across channels.

2020 年初，Podium 与 OpenAI 开展早期合作，聚焦于垃圾信息过滤、销售线索增强等单点解决方案。随着大模型能力持续演进，Podium 将这些工具升级为一个功能完备的 AI 智能体，并亲昵地将其命名为“Jerry”。Jerry 能够自然对话、严格遵循企业业务规范，并通过跨渠道预约与确认服务、主动跟进客户等方式，切实推动业务成果落地。

Businesses quickly embraced Jerry; not as a tool, but as a teammate. Many gave their AI agent a name. Some customers even walk into stores asking to speak with “Jerry,” assuming they had talked to a person.

企业迅速接纳了 Jerry——不是将其视作一个工具，而是当作一位真正的团队成员。许多企业甚至为自己的 AI 智能体起了名字；部分顾客走进门店时，还会直接要求与“Jerry”通话，误以为此前沟通的对象是一位真人。

Since launching their initial agent, “Jerry,” in March 2024, Podium has deployed tens of thousands of agents for local businesses and seen **300% year-over-year AI revenue growth**. Podium’s AI agents influence billions in revenue by responding in under a minute and delivering 24/7 service, allowing businesses to grow without adding headcount.

自 2024 年 3 月正式推出首个 AI 智能体“Jerry”以来，Podium 已为本地企业部署了数万个 AI 智能体，并实现 **AI 相关营收同比增长 300%**。Podium 的 AI 智能体可在一分钟内即时响应、提供全天候（24/7）服务，影响数十亿美元的营收规模，助力企业在无需增加人力编制的前提下实现规模化增长。

## Capturing demand at the point of greatest need

## 在需求最迫切的时刻捕捉商机

For local businesses, every lead matters—and many arrive when no one is available. Roughly 40% of inquiries hit after hours. Podium’s analysis showed top converters responded in about two minutes, while the typical human reply landed in over two hours.

对本地企业而言，每一条潜在客户线索都至关重要——而其中许多线索恰恰出现在无人值守时段。约 40% 的咨询发生在非工作时间。Podium 的分析显示，转化率最高的商家平均约在两分钟内响应；而普通人工回复则往往耗时超过两小时。

Missed calls translate directly into missed revenue: an HVAC owner estimated a single lost Saturday lead could cost tens of thousands of dollars; a medspa operator kept her phone in the shower to avoid losing high-ticket appointments.

错失的电话直接意味着错失收入：一位暖通空调（HVAC）企业主估算，单个周六流失的潜在客户可能造成数万美元损失；一家医美中心经营者甚至将手机带进浴室，只为不错过高价值预约。

But developing AI agents for speed alone isn’t enough. Every business has different services, tone, and policies, often shaped over decades, so Podium built AI agents that could be trained in plain language and adjusted on the fly. To keep improving, the team developed a rigorous evaluation framework to measure what mattered: conversion, not just correctness.

但仅以速度为目标开发 AI 助理远远不够。每家企业提供的服务、沟通语调和运营政策各不相同，且往往历经数十年沉淀而成。因此，Podium 构建了可使用自然语言训练、并支持实时动态调整的 AI 助理。为持续优化效果，团队开发了一套严谨的评估框架，聚焦真正关键的指标：转化率，而非仅仅语法或事实正确性。

## Automating for scale with GPT‑5.1

## 借助 GPT‑5.1 实现规模化自动化

Podium’s next generation of their AI agent, “Jerry 2.0,” uses GPT‑5.1 to handle lead capture, scheduling, service requests, sales, and follow-up quickly and without losing nuance, tone, or context across channels.

Podium 下一代 AI 助理“Jerry 2.0”采用 GPT‑5.1，可快速处理客户线索捕获、预约安排、服务请求、销售跟进及后续沟通等任务，同时在跨渠道交互中精准保留细微差别、语气风格与上下文语境。

Owners can update their preferences using natural language, and the system adapts in real time. For example, a medspa customer can write, “say ‘injectable’” instead of ‘fillers,’” and Podium’s next-gen agent makes the change across each channel without engineering expertise required.

商家可通过自然语言随时更新自身偏好，系统随即实时适配。例如，一家医美中心可输入：“请将‘fillers’（填充剂）改为‘injectable’（注射类项目）”，Podium 新一代 AI 助理即可自动在所有渠道完成术语替换，全程无需工程师介入。

The Podium team made three key decisions to build Jerry 2.0 to prioritize response accuracy and scale:

为打造更注重响应准确性与可扩展性的 Jerry 2.0，Podium 团队做出了三项关键决策：

1. **Vertical baselines + business-level tuning:** Podium ships tailored agents for auto, home services, aesthetics, and retail that are best in class, right out of the box. Each business can then customize the AI to its brand’s specific tone, playbooks, and workflows to make it a full extension of their business.  
1. **垂直行业基线 + 业务级调优：** Podium 开箱即用，为汽车服务、家庭服务、美容医美和零售等行业提供高度定制化的智能代理，性能业界领先。各企业还可进一步根据自身品牌语调、标准作业流程（SOP）及业务工作流，对 AI 进行个性化配置，使其真正成为企业业务的完整延伸。

2. **Outcome-first orchestration:** Jerry uses proven service and sales playbooks to qualify leads, handle objections, and close jobs. When needed, it escalates to humans.  
2. **以结果为导向的智能编排：** Jerry 基于经过验证的服务与销售 SOP，自动完成线索筛选、异议处理及订单闭环；必要时无缝转接人工坐席。

3. **Industrial-grade evaluation:** Every release runs through thousands of production-like scenarios. In head-to-head evaluations, GPT‑5.1 won the majority at 65.4%, delivered 41% lower cost per conversation, and responded 39% faster than competitors.  
3. **工业级评估体系：** 每一次版本发布均需通过数千种贴近真实生产环境的测试场景。在与竞品的横向对比评估中，GPT‑5.1 在 65.4% 的测试项中胜出，单次对话成本降低 41%，响应速度提升 39%。
