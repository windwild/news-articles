Interrupt is LangChain’s inaugural conference, where we’re exploring what the future of AI agents looks like — from their untapped potential, to practical use cases, to risks and challenges. This is a meeting of the minds for anyone building AI agents — whether you’re a software engineer, AI/ML practitioner, data scientist, researcher, or engineering leader.

Interrupt 是 LangChain 首届大会，我们将共同探讨 AI 智能体（AI agents）的未来图景——从其尚未被充分发掘的潜力，到切实可行的应用场景，再到所面临的各类风险与挑战。这是一场面向所有 AI 智能体构建者的头脑风暴盛会——无论你是软件工程师、AI/ML 实践者、数据科学家、研究人员，还是工程负责人。

Interrupt will take place on May 13–14 at The Midway in San Francisco. Space is limited to 750 attendees — [grab your early-bird tickets here](http://interrupt.langchain.com/?utm_source=blog&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=earlybird).

Interrupt 大会将于 5 月 13–14 日在旧金山 The Midway 场馆举行。席位仅限 750 人——[立即抢购早鸟票](http://interrupt.langchain.com/?utm_source=blog&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=earlybird)。

## **A new era of AI agents pushing into production**

## **AI 智能体迈入生产落地的新纪元**

LangChain’s goal has always been to help developers build applications that can reason — and today, we’re seeing that accelerate with the movement to reimagine what’s possible using [AI agents](https://blog.langchain.com/top-5-langgraph-agents-in-production-2024/). More enterprises and startups are moving from prototype to production-ready LLM applications — and with that comes new challenges and points of discussion.

LangChain 的使命始终是助力开发者构建具备推理能力的应用程序；而今天，借助 [AI 智能体](https://blog.langchain.com/top-5-langgraph-agents-in-production-2024/) 这一范式，我们正以前所未有的速度重新定义“可能性”的边界。越来越多的企业与初创公司正将大语言模型（LLM）应用从原型阶段推进至可投入生产的成熟阶段——随之而来的，是全新的挑战与亟待深入探讨的关键议题。

As we push the boundaries of what agents can do – including how they can reason, act, and improve – **Interrupt** is a gathering place for the changemakers and builders who want to deep dive into AI agents.

当我们不断拓展智能体的能力边界——包括其推理方式、行动机制与自我优化路径——**Interrupt** 正是为那些渴望深度探索 AI 智能体的变革推动者与一线构建者所打造的思想交汇之地。

## **Learn best practices, explore breakthrough use cases, and meet the LangChain community**

## **学习最佳实践、探索突破性应用场景，并结识 LangChain 社区**

The Interrupt in-person conference is broken into two action-packed days:

Interrupt 线下大会为期两天，日程紧凑、内容丰富：

- **May 13**: This is our pre-conference day with hands-on workshops by the LangChain team + a social hour to connect with fellow builders.  
- **5 月 13 日**：大会前奏日——由 LangChain 团队亲自授课的实操工作坊 + 专为同行构建者打造的社交交流时段。

- **May 14**: Be the first to experience new product features with our keynote, followed by fireside chats, expert talks, lightning demos, and plenty of good conversations. We’ll wrap up with an afterparty!  
- **5 月 14 日**：率先体验全新产品功能的主题演讲拉开序幕，随后是炉边对话、专家分享、闪电演示，以及大量高质量的自由交流；大会将以一场精彩纷呈的闭幕派对圆满收官！

At Interrupt, you’ll be able to:

在 Interrupt 大会上，您将能够：

- **Learn from industry pioneers**. Join thought leaders like Andrew Ng, Cognition Labs, Replit, 11x, and more as they share the latest innovations shaping the AI agent space.  
- **向行业先驱取经**。与吴恩达（Andrew Ng）、Cognition Labs、Replit、11x 等思想领袖并肩而坐，聆听他们分享正在重塑 AI 智能体格局的前沿创新成果。

- **Get hands-on**. May 13th is our pre-conference day, where we’ll have workshops focused on real-world, production-ready agent use cases. Get live instruction from the LangChain team and build up your AI agent toolkit alongside a group of peers.  
- **亲自动手实践**。5 月 13 日为大会前奏日，我们将开设聚焦真实世界、面向生产环境的智能体应用工作坊。现场接受 LangChain 团队指导，在同侪协作中系统构建属于您的 AI 智能体工具集。

- **Explore how companies are using AI agents in production**: Learn how different tools, techniques, and frameworks are helping companies ship impactful LLM apps to their users at scale across a range of verticals.  
- **探究企业如何将 AI 智能体投入实际生产**：了解各类工具、技术与框架如何赋能不同垂直领域的企业，规模化交付真正产生业务价值的大语言模型应用。

- **Collaborate with fellow AI**: Meet with fellow builders, engineers, and product-minded folks to exchange ideas that will propel the AI agent ecosystem forward.  
- **与志同道合的 AI 同行协作共创**：结识来自全球的构建者、工程师与产品思维者，碰撞思想火花，共同推动 AI 智能体生态持续演进。

Stay tuned for our full agenda, which will be announced in the coming weeks.

敬请关注我们的完整议程，将在未来几周内正式公布。

[Interrupt 大会门票现已开放预订](http://interrupt.langchain.com/?utm_source=blog&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=earlybird)！您可抢购早鸟票，选择单日参会票（5月14日）或双日通票（5月13–14日）。
