# Statement on the comments from Secretary of War Pete Hegseth

# 关于美国战争部长彼得·赫格塞斯（Pete Hegseth）相关言论的声明

Feb 27, 2026

2026年2月27日

Earlier today, Secretary of War Pete Hegseth [shared on X](https://x.com/SecWar/status/2027507717469049070?s=20) that he is directing the Department of War to designate Anthropic a supply chain risk. This action follows months of negotiations that reached an impasse over [two exceptions](https://www.anthropic.com/news/statement-department-of-war) we requested to the lawful use of our AI model, Claude: the mass domestic surveillance of Americans and fully autonomous weapons.

今天早些时候，美国战争部长彼得·赫格塞斯在 [X 平台](https://x.com/SecWar/status/2027507717469049070?s=20) 发文表示，他已指示战争部将 Anthropic 列为供应链风险。此举源于我们与战争部长达数月之久的谈判最终陷入僵局——僵局焦点在于我们针对自身人工智能模型 Claude 的合法使用所提出的 [两项例外情形](https://www.anthropic.com/news/statement-department-of-war)：一是对美国民众实施大规模国内监控；二是部署完全自主的武器系统。

We have not yet received direct communication from the Department of War or the White House on the status of our negotiations.

截至目前，我们尚未收到来自战争部或白宫关于此次谈判进展的任何直接沟通。

We have tried in good faith to reach an agreement with the Department of War, making clear that we support all lawful uses of AI for national security aside from the two narrow exceptions above. To the best of our knowledge, these exceptions have not affected a single government mission to date.

我们始终本着诚意与战争部展开磋商，明确表示：除上述两项极为有限的例外情形外，我们支持人工智能在国家安全领域的一切合法应用。据我们所知，迄今为止，这两项例外尚未影响任何一项政府任务的执行。

We held to our exceptions for two reasons. First, we do not believe that today’s frontier AI models are reliable enough to be used in fully autonomous weapons. Allowing current models to be used in this way would endanger America’s warfighters and civilians. Second, we believe that mass domestic surveillance of Americans constitutes a violation of fundamental rights.

我们坚持这两项例外，主要基于两点理由：第一，我们认为当前最前沿的人工智能模型尚不足以可靠地应用于完全自主武器系统；若允许当前模型用于此类场景，将严重危及美军作战人员及普通民众的生命安全。第二，我们认为，对美国民众实施大规模国内监控，构成对基本权利的根本性侵犯。

Designating Anthropic as a supply chain risk would be an unprecedented action—one historically reserved for US adversaries, never before publicly applied to an American company.  

将 Anthropic 列为供应链风险，将是史无前例的举措——此类措施历来仅适用于美国的敌对国家，此前从未公开施加于任何一家美国企业。

We are deeply saddened by these developments. As the first frontier AI company to deploy models in the US government’s classified networks, Anthropic has supported American warfighters since June 2024 and has every intention of continuing to do so.

我们对事态发展深感痛心。作为首家将大模型部署至美国政府涉密网络的前沿人工智能公司，Anthropic 自 2024 年 6 月以来持续为美军作战人员提供支持，并坚定致力于继续履行这一使命。

We believe this designation would both be legally unsound and set a dangerous precedent for any American company that negotiates with the government.

我们认为，此项认定不仅在法律上站不住脚，更将为所有与美国政府开展合作的美国企业树立危险先例。

No amount of intimidation or punishment from the Department of War will change our position on mass domestic surveillance or fully autonomous weapons. We will challenge any supply chain risk designation in court.

无论来自“战争部”（Department of War）的何种恫吓或惩罚，均不会动摇我们在大规模国内监控及完全自主武器问题上的立场。我们将就任何供应链风险认定诉诸法庭。

**What this means for our customers**

**这对我们的客户意味着什么**

Secretary Hegseth has implied this designation would restrict anyone who does business with the military from doing business with Anthropic. The Secretary does not have the statutory authority to back up this statement. Legally, a supply chain risk designation under [10 USC 3252](https://uscode.house.gov/view.xhtml?req=granuleid:USC-prelim-title10-section3252&num=0&edition=prelim) can only extend to the use of Claude as part of Department of War contracts—it cannot affect how contractors use Claude to serve other customers.

赫格塞斯（Hegseth）部长暗示，此项认定将限制所有与军方有业务往来的实体与 Anthropic 开展合作。但该部长并无成文法授权支撑此项表态。依据《美国法典》第 10 编第 3252 条（[10 USC 3252](https://uscode.house.gov/view.xhtml?req=granuleid:USC-prelim-title10-section3252&num=0&edition=prelim)），供应链风险认定仅适用于 Claude 在“战争部”合同项下的使用场景——不得影响承包商将 Claude 用于服务其他客户的相关活动。

In practice, this means:

在实际操作中，这意味着：

- **If you are an individual customer or hold a commercial contract with Anthropic**, your access to Claude—through our API, claude.ai, or any of our products—is completely unaffected.  
- **如果您是个人用户，或与 Anthropic 签有商业合同**，您通过我们的 API、claude.ai 或任一产品访问 Claude 的权限将完全不受影响。

- **If you are a Department of War contractor**, this designation—if formally adopted—would only affect your use of Claude on Department of War contract work. Your use for any other purpose is unaffected.  
- **如果您是“战争部”承包商**，此项认定（若正式生效）仅限于您在“战争部”合同项目中使用 Claude 的情形；您将其用于其他任何用途均不受影响。

Our sales and [support](https://support.claude.com/en/) teams are standing by to answer any questions you may have.

我们的销售团队与 [支持团队](https://support.claude.com/en/) 随时待命，为您解答任何疑问。

We are deeply grateful to our users, and to the industry peers, policymakers, veterans, and members of the public who have voiced their support in recent days. Thank you. Above all else, our priorities are to protect our customers from any disruption caused by these extraordinary events and to work with the Department of War to ensure a smooth transition—for them, for our troops, and for American military operations.

我们由衷感谢广大用户，以及近日以来公开表达支持的行业同仁、政策制定者、退伍军人和公众人士。衷心感谢！归根结底，我们的首要任务是：全力保护客户免受此次异常事件带来的任何干扰，并与“战争部”通力协作，确保平稳过渡——既服务于“战争部”自身，也服务于我军将士，更服务于美国军事行动的整体运行。
