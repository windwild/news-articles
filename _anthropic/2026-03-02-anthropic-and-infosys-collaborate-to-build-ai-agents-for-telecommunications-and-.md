# Anthropic and Infosys collaborate to build AI agents for telecommunications and other regulated industries

# Anthropic 与 Infosys 合作，为电信及其他受监管行业构建 AI 智能体

Feb 17, 2026

2026 年 2 月 17 日

![Anthropic and Infosys collaborate to build AI agents for telecommunications and other regulated industries](images/anthropic-and-infosys-collaborate-to-build-ai-agents-for-telecommunications-and-/img_001.svg)

![Anthropic 与 Infosys 合作，为电信及其他受监管行业构建 AI 智能体](images/anthropic-and-infosys-collaborate-to-build-ai-agents-for-telecommunications-and-/img_001.svg)

Anthropic and [Infosys](https://www.infosys.com/), a global leader in next-generation digital services and consulting founded and headquartered in Bengaluru, today announced a collaboration to develop and deliver enterprise AI solutions across telecommunications, financial services, manufacturing, and software development.

Anthropic 与全球新一代数字服务与咨询领军企业、总部位于班加罗尔的 [Infosys](https://www.infosys.com/) 今日宣布达成合作，共同开发并交付面向电信、金融服务、制造业及软件开发等领域的企业级 AI 解决方案。

The collaboration integrates Anthropic’s Claude models and Claude Code with [Infosys Topaz](https://www.infosys.com/services/data-ai-topaz.html), an AI-first set of services, solutions, and platforms using generative and agentic AI technologies, to help companies speed up software development and adopt AI with the governance and transparency that regulated industries require.

此次合作将 Anthropic 的 Claude 模型与 Claude Code 与 [Infosys Topaz](https://www.infosys.com/services/data-ai-topaz.html)（一套以 AI 为先的服务、解决方案与平台组合，融合生成式 AI 与智能体 AI 技术）深度整合，助力企业加速软件开发进程，并在满足监管行业所要求的治理性与透明度前提下落地 AI 应用。

India is the second-largest market for Claude.ai, home to a developer community doing some of the most technically intense AI work we see anywhere: nearly half of Claude usage in India involves building applications, modernizing systems, and shipping production software. Infosys is one of the first partners in Anthropic’s [expanded presence in India](https://www.anthropic.com/news/bengaluru-office-partnerships-across-india).

印度是 Claude.ai 的全球第二大市场，汇聚着一支技术实力极为雄厚的开发者社群——他们所从事的 AI 工作，堪称全球范围内最具技术挑战性的实践之一：在印度，近半数的 Claude 使用场景聚焦于应用构建、系统现代化改造以及生产级软件交付。Infosys 是 Anthropic [在印度全面拓展布局](https://www.anthropic.com/news/bengaluru-office-partnerships-across-india) 的首批战略合作伙伴之一。

“There’s a big gap between an AI model that works in a demo and one that works in a regulated industry—and if you want to close that gap, you need domain expertise. Infosys has exactly that kind of expertise across important industries: telecom, financial services, and manufacturing. Their developers are already using Claude Code to accelerate their work and to create AI agents for industries that demand precision, compliance, and deep domain knowledge,” said Dario Amodei, CEO and Co-founder of Anthropic.

Anthropic 首席执行官兼联合创始人 Dario Amodei 表示：“一个能在演示中运行的 AI 模型，与一个能在监管严苛的行业中真正落地的 AI 模型之间，存在巨大鸿沟；而要弥合这一鸿沟，关键在于深厚的领域专业知识。Infosys 正是在电信、金融服务和制造业等关键行业拥有此类专业能力的典范。其开发者团队已率先采用 Claude Code 加速日常工作，并面向对精确性、合规性及深度领域知识有严苛要求的行业，构建专属 AI 智能体。”

“AI is not just transforming business, it is redefining the way industries operate and innovate. Our collaboration with Anthropic marks a strategic leap toward advancing enterprise AI, enabling organizations to unlock value and become more intelligent, resilient, and responsible. From modernizing financial services with intelligent risk management and compliance, to enabling engineering businesses to lead with AI-driven design and manufacturing, the goal is to leverage the joint expertise of Infosys and Anthropic to accelerate AI value realization for global enterprises,” said Salil Parekh, Chief Executive Officer, Infosys.

Infosys 首席执行官 Salil Parekh 表示：“AI 不仅正在重塑商业形态，更在重新定义各行业的运营与创新方式。我们与 Anthropic 的合作，标志着企业级 AI 发展的一次战略性跃升——赋能组织充分释放 AI 价值，迈向更智能、更具韧性、更负责任的未来。从借助智能风控与合规能力推动金融服务现代化，到助力工程类企业以 AI 驱动的设计与制造能力引领行业发展，我们的共同目标，是充分发挥 Infosys 与 Anthropic 的协同专长，加速全球企业实现 AI 价值转化。”

## Building agentic AI for regulated industries

## 面向监管行业的智能体 AI 构建

A core focus of the collaboration is agentic AI—systems that go beyond answering questions to independently handling multi-step tasks like processing claims, generating and testing code, or managing compliance reviews. Using tools like the Claude Agent SDK, Infosys and Anthropic will help clients build AI agents that work persistently across long, complex tasks and processes. The collaboration will also help organizations modernize legacy systems, combining Infosys Topaz and Claude to make migration faster and easier and reduce the cost of updating aging infrastructure.

本次合作的核心聚焦于智能体 AI（Agentic AI）——即超越简单问答、可自主执行多步骤任务的系统，例如理赔处理、代码生成与测试、合规审查管理等。依托 Claude Agent SDK 等工具，Infosys 与 Anthropic 将协助客户构建可在长期、复杂任务与流程中持续运行的 AI 智能体。此外，双方还将助力企业推进遗留系统现代化升级：通过 Infosys Topaz 与 Claude 的协同，显著加快迁移进程、降低老旧基础设施更新成本。

Together, Infosys and Anthropic will develop AI agents tailored to specific industries:

Infosys 与 Anthropic 将携手为特定行业定制开发 AI 智能体：

- **Telecommunications:** AI agents will help carriers modernize network operations, simplify customer lifecycle management, and improve service delivery—bringing intelligent automation to one of the most operationally complex and regulated industries in the world.  
- **电信行业：** AI 智能体将助力运营商实现网络运维现代化、简化客户全生命周期管理、提升服务交付质量——为全球运营最复杂、监管最严格的行业之一注入智能化自动化能力。

- **Financial services:** AI agents will help firms detect and assess risk faster, automate compliance reporting, and deliver more personalized customer interactions, such as tailoring financial advice based on a client's full account history and market conditions.  
- **金融服务：** AI 智能体将助力金融机构更快识别与评估风险、自动化合规报告生成，并提供更个性化的客户交互体验——例如，基于客户的完整账户历史与实时市场状况，为其量身定制财务建议。

- **Manufacturing and engineering:** Claude will help accelerate product design and simulation, reducing R&D timelines and enabling engineers to test more iterations before production.  
- **制造业与工程业：** Claude 将加速产品设计与仿真流程，缩短研发周期，并支持工程师在投产前完成更多轮次的方案验证与迭代。

- **Software development:** Teams will use Claude Code to write, test, and debug code, helping developers move faster from design to production.  
- **软件开发：** 开发团队将借助 Claude Code 编写、测试与调试代码，助力开发者更高效地实现从设计到生产的全流程提速。

- **Enterprise operations:** Claude Cowork will help teams automate routine work like document summarization, status reporting, and review cycles.  
- **企业运营：** Claude Cowork 将协助团队自动化处理文档摘要、状态汇报、评审周期等日常事务。
