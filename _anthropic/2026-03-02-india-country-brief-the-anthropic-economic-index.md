# India Country Brief: The Anthropic Economic Index

# 印度国别简报：Anthropic 经济指数

Feb 16, 2026

2026年2月16日

![India Country Brief: The Anthropic Economic Index](images/india-country-brief-the-anthropic-economic-index/img_001.svg)

![印度国别简报：Anthropic 经济指数](images/india-country-brief-the-anthropic-economic-index/img_001.svg)








We tracked 11 observable behaviors across thousands of Claude.ai conversations to build the AI Fluency Index — a baseline for measuring how people collaborate with AI today.

我们追踪了数千场 Claude.ai 对话中的 11 种可观测行为，构建出“AI 熟练度指数”——该指数为衡量当今人类与 AI 协作水平提供了基准。

## Executive summary

## 执行摘要

India, already the world’s largest exporter of IT services, is home to one of the world’s fastest-growing AI user bases. Understanding how AI is being used in India—and how it differs from other countries—is essential for informing AI policy, investment, and deployment in the country. This brief provides insights on Claude.ai use in India, drawing on data from the [fourth Anthropic Economic Index report](https://www.anthropic.com/research/anthropic-economic-index-january-2026-report) covering ~1 million Claude.ai conversations globally during November 2025. India accounts for 5.8% of total Claude.ai use, second only to the United States. Yet current adoption remains concentrated, pointing to significant opportunities to expand access more broadly across the population.

印度已是全球最大的信息技术（IT）服务出口国，同时拥有全球增长最快的 AI 用户群体之一。深入理解 AI 在印度的应用现状及其与其他国家的差异，对制定该国的 AI 政策、引导投资方向及优化技术部署至关重要。本简报基于 [第四期 Anthropic 经济指数报告](https://www.anthropic.com/research/anthropic-economic-index-january-2026-report) 的数据，分析了印度用户使用 Claude.ai 的情况；该报告覆盖了 2025 年 11 月全球约 100 万场 Claude.ai 对话。印度占全球 Claude.ai 总使用量的 5.8%，仅次于美国，位居第二。然而，当前的采用仍高度集中，表明在更广泛的人群中扩大可及性存在巨大潜力。

The findings point to a user base that applies AI more heavily in professional contexts, delegates more autonomy to it, and brings Claude tasks that are substantially more time-consuming to complete without assistance. Higher shares of complex tasks that humans could not complete alone suggest that Indian users are using the technology at the frontier.

研究结果表明，这一用户群体在职业场景中更深度地应用人工智能，赋予其更高的自主决策权，并将那些在无人工智能辅助下耗时显著更长的任务交予 Claude 处理。人类单凭自身难以独立完成的复杂任务占比更高，说明印度用户正处于该技术应用的前沿地带。

## India is among the leading countries in global AI adoption

## 印度位居全球人工智能采用率前列国家之列

India ranks second among all countries by share of total Claude.ai use, trailing only the United States. However, on a per-capita basis, adjusting for the working-age population, India ranks 101st out of 116 countries with sufficient observation volume, below other countries in Asia such as Singapore or Malaysia. This gap suggests that India’s high Claude use overall reflects the sheer size of its population, not that the average person is using Claude heavily. This points to significant opportunities to increase adoption.

就 Claude.ai 全球总使用量占比而言，印度在所有国家中排名第二，仅次于美国。然而，若按工作年龄人口人均使用量计算，在拥有足够观测样本量的 116 个国家中，印度仅排第 101 位，落后于新加坡、马来西亚等其他亚洲国家。这一差距表明：印度整体较高的 Claude 使用量主要源于其庞大的人口基数，而非人均高频使用。这也意味着，印度在提升人工智能普及率方面仍存在巨大潜力。

## Concentrated use within India

## 印度国内使用高度集中

### Geographic concentration

### 地理分布集中

Use is concentrated in a small number of highly economically active states. Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, and Delhi together account for over half of India’s total Claude.ai use. This pattern closely mirrors India’s IT sector geography and urban economic output.

使用高度集中在少数几个经济活跃度极高的邦。马哈拉施特拉邦、泰米尔纳德邦、卡纳塔克邦与德里联邦属地四地合计占印度 Claude.ai 总使用量的一半以上。这一分布格局与印度信息技术产业的地理布局及城市经济产出高度吻合。

![](images/india-country-brief-the-anthropic-economic-index/img_003.jpg)**Figure 2.** Share of India's Claude.ai use by state. Map shows each state’s share of India’s total Claude.ai use. Top states: Maharashtra (15.5%), Tamil Nadu (13.2%), Karnataka (12.7%), Delhi (10.5%). Gray regions indicate insufficient data. November 2025 data. Shapefile for the map from Natural Earth.

![](images/india-country-brief-the-anthropic-economic-index/img_003.jpg)**图 2.** 印度各邦 Claude.ai 使用量占比分布图。地图显示各邦占印度 Claude.ai 总使用量的比例。使用量居前的邦依次为：马哈拉施特拉邦（15.5%）、泰米尔纳德邦（13.2%）、卡纳塔克邦（12.7%）、德里（10.5%）。灰色区域表示数据不足。数据时间为 2025 年 11 月；地图矢量底图源自 Natural Earth。

The concentration in these four states—home to Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Mumbai, and Delhi NCR—suggests that current AI adoption is driven primarily by India’s established technology workforce rather than broad-based consumer uptake.

这四个邦汇聚了班加罗尔、海得拉巴、金奈、孟买以及德里国家首都辖区（NCR），其使用高度集中表明：当前人工智能在印度的普及主要由既有的科技行业从业者推动，而非广泛意义上的大众消费者采纳。

### **Concentration of occupational tasks**

### **职业任务类型的集中化**

The occupational mix of Indian Claude.ai use, inferred by mapping tasks to related occupations, skews towards software development and engineering roles, consistent with the country’s large IT services sector.

印度用户使用 Claude.ai 的职业分布——通过将任务映射至相关职业推断得出——明显偏向软件开发与工程类岗位，这与印度庞大的信息技术服务产业相一致。

印度用户执行频率最高的 O\*NET 任务进一步印证了其高度聚焦软件领域的使用特征：

India ranks 1st globally in the share of AI use devoted to software-related tasks (45.2% of all O\*NET-mapped tasks), ahead of Vietnam (42.1%) and Egypt (39.2%). The presence of educational tasks among the most common individual tasks (see Table 1) and when aggregating tasks to occupation groups (see Figure 3) indicates other common use cases in learning and instruction.

在全球范围内，印度在“AI 使用中用于软件相关任务”的占比高居榜首（占全部 O\*NET 映射任务的 45.2%），领先于越南（42.1%）和埃及（39.2%）。此外，教育类任务既出现在高频个体任务列表中（见表 1），也体现在按职业群聚合后的任务分布中（见图 3），表明学习与教学是印度用户的另一大常见应用场景。

## Economic primitives: how India uses AI differently

## 经济原语：印度如何差异化地使用 AI

[Our latest Economic Index report](https://www.anthropic.com/research/anthropic-economic-index-january-2026-report) introduces “economic primitives”—fundamental measurements of how humans and AI collaborate. Comparing India to the global average reveals several distinctive patterns.

[我们最新发布的《经济指数报告》](https://www.anthropic.com/research/anthropic-economic-index-january-2026-report) 提出了“经济原语”（economic primitives）概念——即衡量人类与 AI 协作方式的基本指标。将印度数据与全球平均水平对比，可识别出若干显著差异模式。

![](images/india-country-brief-the-anthropic-economic-index/img_006.jpg)**Figure 4.** Comparing India's Claude.ai use to the global average. The panels compare India (N = 58,098) against the global average (N = 975,160) across nine economic primitives. November 2025 data.

![](images/india-country-brief-the-anthropic-economic-index/img_006.jpg)**图 4.** 印度 Claude.ai 使用情况与全球平均水平的对比。各子图从九项经济原语维度，将印度（N = 58,098）与全球均值（N = 975,160）进行比较。数据截至 2025 年 11 月。

**Greater productivity speedup.** Indian users take on average 14.8 minutes to complete tasks with AI that would take 3.8 hours without AI—a 15x speedup. Globally, users take on average 15.4 minutes to complete tasks that would take 3.1 hours without AI—a 12x speedup. This suggests that AI is delivering outsized productivity gains on the more complex tasks Indian users bring to it.

**更高的生产力加速比。** 印度用户借助 AI 完成某项任务平均耗时 14.8 分钟，而若无 AI 辅助则需 3.8 小时——提速达 15 倍；相比之下，全球用户平均耗时 15.4 分钟完成同等任务（无 AI 时为 3.1 小时），提速为 12 倍。这表明，AI 正在印度用户所处理的更复杂任务上释放出尤为突出的生产力增益。

**Stronger work orientation.** 51.3% of Indian Claude.ai use is work-related, compared to 46% globally. Coursework accounts for 20.9% (vs. 19.3% globally) and personal use for 27.8% (vs. 34.7% globally). The work-heavy, lower-personal-use profile is consistent with India’s large professional services sector and the finding from the main report that lower-GDP-per-capita countries tend towards work and coursework over personal use.

**更强的工作导向性。** 印度用户 51.3% 的 Claude.ai 使用行为属于工作相关用途，高于全球平均的 46%；课程学习类用途占 20.9%（全球为 19.3%），个人用途占 27.8%（全球为 34.7%）。这种“重工作、轻个人”的使用特征，与印度规模庞大的专业服务产业高度吻合；同时也印证了主报告中的一项发现：人均 GDP 较低的国家，其 AI 使用更倾向于工作与课程学习，而非个人用途。

**Higher AI autonomy.**  
**更高的 AI 自主性。**  

Indian users delegate more decision-making autonomy to AI (3.60 vs. 3.38 globally on a 1–5 scale, where 1 means no delegation and 5 means extreme delegation).  
印度用户将更多决策自主权委托给 AI（得分为 3.60，全球平均为 3.38；量表范围为 1–5，其中 1 表示完全不委托，5 表示极度委托）。  

This suggests greater willingness to let AI operate independently rather than using it purely as an assistant.  
这表明印度用户更愿意让 AI 独立运行，而非仅将其用作辅助工具。

**Lower human-only ability.**  
**较低的人类独立完成能力。**  

One of the data points we measure is whether AI is being used to do something a human couldn’t do on their own, like writing code in a language they don’t know.  
我们衡量的数据点之一是：AI 是否被用于完成人类单凭自身无法完成的任务（例如用一门自己并不掌握的编程语言编写代码）。  

We find that 84.6% of tasks could be completed by a human alone (vs. 87.9% globally), suggesting Indian users more frequently bring tasks to AI that they could not easily accomplish independently.  
我们发现，84.6% 的任务可由人类单独完成（全球平均为 87.9%），表明印度用户更频繁地将那些自身难以独立高效完成的任务交由 AI 处理。

**Prompting skills matter.**  
**提示词技能至关重要。**  

As a proxy for the skills humans and AI bring to the conversation, we estimate the years of education someone would need to understand the user prompt or the AI response in a conversation.  
我们以“理解用户提示或 AI 回复所需受教育年限”作为衡量人类与 AI 在对话中各自所贡献能力的代理指标。  

We find that the human education level of prompts (12.2 years) and AI education level of responses (12.5 years) are relatively similar, mirroring a global pattern where input quality shapes output quality.  
我们发现，用户提示所对应的教育水平（12.2 年）与 AI 回复所对应的教育水平（12.5 年）较为接近，印证了全球普遍存在的规律：输入质量决定输出质量。  

Comparing country averages for AI education, India ranks in the top 10%, indicating Indian users are getting sophisticated outputs from Claude.  
在各国 AI 教育水平均值的横向比较中，印度位居全球前 10%，表明印度用户正从 Claude 获得高度复杂的输出结果。

## Implications  
## 潜在影响  

**Broadening AI’s economic impact will require looking beyond software and IT services.**  
**要拓展 AI 的经济影响，需超越软件与信息技术服务领域。**  

45.2% of tasks map to software-related occupations—the highest share of any country.  
45.2% 的任务对应于软件相关职业——这一比例为全球各国之最。  

Four states (Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, and Delhi) account for over half of all use.  
四个邦/地区（马哈拉施特拉邦、泰米尔纳德邦、卡纳塔克邦和德里）贡献了全部使用量的一半以上。  

This mirrors the geography of India’s IT sector and suggests that current AI adoption is largely an extension of existing professional strengths and workflows focused on IT.  
这一分布格局与印度 IT 产业的地理分布高度一致，表明当前 AI 的采用主要延续并拓展了印度既有的专业优势与聚焦于 IT 领域的工作流程。

**Investing in AI can provide substantial and measurable productivity gains.**  
**投资 AI 可带来显著且可量化的生产力提升。**  

Indian users apply AI to tasks that would otherwise take 3.8 hours, compressing them to ~15 minutes—a 15x speedup, compared to 12x globally.  
印度用户将 AI 应用于原本需耗时 3.8 小时的任务，将其压缩至约 15 分钟——提速达 15 倍，高于全球平均的 12 倍。  

This means that India is already extracting significant value from AI: bringing harder tasks and compressing the time needed to complete these tasks further than the global average.  
这意味着印度已从 AI 中获取了可观价值：不仅承接更具挑战性的任务，而且在任务完成时间压缩方面也超越了全球平均水平。

**Closing the gap between absolute and per-capita use requires addressing structural barriers.**  
**弥合绝对使用量与人均使用量之间的差距，需破除结构性障碍。**  

India ranks 2nd in total use but 101st in per-capita use.  
印度总使用量位居全球第二，但人均使用量仅排第 101 位。  

The gap between these two figures reflects both India’s large population and how narrowly concentrated current adoption is.  
这两项排名间的巨大落差，既反映了印度庞大的人口基数，也凸显出当前 AI 采用在人群和地域上的高度集中性。  

Globally, per-capita AI adoption is strongly correlated with per-capita income.  
在全球范围内，人均 AI 采用率与人均收入呈强正相关关系。  

India’s per-capita use is consistent with what this relationship would predict.  
印度当前的人均使用水平，与其人均收入水平所预测的结果基本吻合。  

Without addressing structural barriers related to income, digital infrastructure, and awareness outside the IT sector, Indian AI adoption is likely to remain concentrated.  
若不着力解决与收入水平、数字基础设施建设以及 IT 行业之外公众认知度相关的结构性障碍，印度的 AI 采用态势很可能持续保持高度集中。

**Embracing AI autonomy appears to be serving Indian users well.**  
**拥抱 AI 自主性似乎正为印度用户带来切实益处。**  

Higher autonomy scores, longer baseline task times, and frequent use for tasks humans could do alone suggest that Indian professionals are trusting AI to make decisions and using it to enhance human capabilities.  
更高的自主性得分、更长的基础任务耗时，以及高频将本可由人类独立完成的任务交由 AI 处理——这些现象共同表明，印度专业人士正信任 AI 做出决策，并借助其增强人类自身能力。

**Investing in AI skills could have high returns.**  
**投资 AI 技能有望获得高回报。**  

The strong correlation between prompt sophistication and response quality in the global data suggests that training programs focused on effective AI use—particularly for workers outside India’s current IT-heavy user base—could meaningfully improve the returns from wider AI adoption.  
全球数据中提示词复杂度与响应质量之间呈现强相关性，表明：面向高效 AI 使用的培训项目——尤其是针对印度当前以 IT 从业者为主的用户群体之外的广大劳动者——有望显著提升更广泛 AI 应用所带来的实际回报。

### Methodology  
### 方法论

This analysis draws on privacy-preserving data from Claude.ai consumer use from November 13–20, 2025, as described in the [fourth Anthropic Economic Index report](https://www.anthropic.com/research/anthropic-economic-index-january-2026-report). Economic primitives are computed using the methodology detailed in that report. Geographic assignment uses IP-based geolocation. Occupation and task classification are based on mappings to the O\*NET task taxonomy and SOC occupation groups. For country-level rankings, we only include countries with at least 200 observations in our sample because of the uncertainty of the measure for low-usage countries in our random sample. The underlying data includes Claude.ai Free, Pro, and Max usage.

本分析基于 Claude.ai 消费者在 2025 年 11 月 13 日至 20 日期间的、经隐私保护处理的使用数据，相关说明详见[第四期《Anthropic 经济指数报告》（2026 年 1 月）](https://www.anthropic.com/research/anthropic-economic-index-january-2026-report)。经济基础指标（economic primitives）的计算方法详见该报告。地理区域归属依据 IP 地址进行地理定位（IP-based geolocation）。职业与任务分类则基于对 O\*NET 任务分类体系（O\*NET task taxonomy）和美国劳工统计局标准职业分类（SOC occupation groups）的映射关系。在国家层面的排名中，我们仅纳入样本中观测值不少于 200 条的国家，以避免随机抽样中低使用量国家因数据稀疏而导致指标不确定性过高。底层数据涵盖 Claude.ai 免费版（Free）、专业版（Pro）及旗舰版（Max）的全部用户使用行为。

_For the full methodology, global findings, and time-series analysis, see the [Anthropic Economic Index January 2026 report](https://www.anthropic.com/research/anthropic-economic-index-january-2026-report)._

_如需了解完整方法论、全球研究发现及时间序列分析，请参阅[《Anthropic 经济指数 2026 年 1 月报告》](https://www.anthropic.com/research/anthropic-economic-index-january-2026-report)。_
