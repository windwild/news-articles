# Societal Impacts

# 社会影响

Working closely with the Anthropic Policy and Safeguards teams, Societal Impacts is a technical research team that explores how AI is used in the real world.

社会影响团队与 Anthropic 政策与保障团队紧密合作，是一支技术研究团队，致力于探索人工智能在现实世界中的实际应用。

### Sociotechnical alignment

### 社技对齐（Sociotechnical Alignment）

Which human values should AI models hold, and how should they operate in the face of conflicting or ambiguous values? How is AI used (and misused) in the wild? How can we anticipate future uses and risks of AI? Societal Impacts researchers develop experiments, training methods, and evaluations to answer these questions.

人工智能模型应秉持哪些人类价值观？当面临相互冲突或含义模糊的价值观时，它们又该如何运作？人工智能在现实场景中如何被使用（以及被滥用）？我们又该如何预判人工智能未来的应用场景与潜在风险？社会影响团队的研究人员通过设计实验、开发训练方法及构建评估体系来回答这些问题。

### Policy relevance

### 政策相关性

Though the Societal Impacts team is technical, they often pick research questions that have policy relevance. They believe that providing trustworthy research concerning topics policymakers care about will lead to better policy (and overall) outcomes for everyone.

尽管社会影响团队属于技术研究团队，但他们常选择具有政策相关性的研究课题。他们相信，围绕政策制定者所关注的议题提供可信可靠的研究成果，将有助于推动更优的公共政策（乃至整体）结果，惠及所有人。

[Societal Impacts｜Apr 21, 2025  
**Values in the wild: Discovering and analyzing values in real-world language model interactions**  
What values does Claude actually express during real conversations? Analyzing 700,000 interactions, this paper creates the first large-scale empirical taxonomy of AI values and finds that Claude adapts its expressed values to context—mirroring users in most cases, but resisting when core principles are at stake.](https://www.anthropic.com/research/values-wild) [社会影响｜2025 年 4 月 21 日  
**真实场景中的价值观：“野生”语言模型交互中价值观的发现与分析**  
Claude 在真实对话中实际表达了哪些价值观？本研究分析了 70 万次真实交互，首次构建了大规模实证的 AI 价值观分类体系，并发现：Claude 会根据语境动态调整其表达的价值观——在多数情况下与用户保持一致，但在核心原则面临挑战时则表现出明确的抵制倾向。](https://www.anthropic.com/research/values-wild)

**集体宪法式人工智能：基于公众意见对齐语言模型**
