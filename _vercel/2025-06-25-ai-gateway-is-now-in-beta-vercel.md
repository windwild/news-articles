2025年6月25日

![](images/ai-gateway-is-now-in-beta-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/ai-gateway-is-now-in-beta-vercel/img_002.jpg)

AI Gateway gives you a single endpoint to access a wide range of AI models across providers, with better uptime, faster responses, no lock-in.

AI 网关为您提供统一接入点，可调用来自多家供应商的丰富 AI 模型，具备更高可用性、更快响应速度，且无厂商锁定风险。

Now in [Beta](https://vercel.com/docs/release-phases#public-beta), developers can use models from providers like OpenAI, xAI, Anthropic, Google, and more with:

现处于 [公开测试阶段（Beta）](https://vercel.com/docs/release-phases#public-beta)，开发者可借助以下功能，调用 OpenAI、xAI、Anthropic、Google 等多家供应商的模型：

- Usage-based billing at provider list prices  
- 按用量计费，费率采用各供应商官方标价  

- Bring-Your-Own-Key support  
- 支持自带 API 密钥（BYOK）  

- Improved observability, including per-model usage, latency, and error metrics  
- 增强可观测性：支持按模型维度查看调用量、延迟与错误率等指标  

- Simplified authentication  
- 简化身份认证流程  

- Fallback and provider routing for more reliable inference  
- 故障自动回退与供应商路由机制，提升推理服务可靠性  

- Higher throughput and rate limits  
- 更高的吞吐量与调用频率限制

[Try AI Gateway](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%7E%2Fai&title=Try+AI+Gateway) 免费试用，或 [查看文档](https://vercel.com/docs/ai-gateway) 了解更多信息。
