Feb 5, 2026

A six-week program offering over $6M in credits from Vercel, v0, AWS, and leading AI platforms

为期六周的加速计划，提供来自 Vercel、v0、AWS 及多家领先 AI 平台总计超 600 万美元的云资源额度。

Building an AI business is no small feat. Delivering a great agentic product requires infrastructure that handles deployment, security, and scale automatically, but that's table stakes. Startups also need community support, mentorship, investor connections, platform credits, and visibility.

打造一家 AI 公司绝非易事。要交付一款出色的智能体（agentic）产品，基础设施必须能自动处理部署、安全与规模化扩展——但这仅是入场门槛。初创公司还需社区支持、导师指导、投资人对接、平台资源额度以及行业曝光度。

That's why we created the [Vercel AI Accelerator](https://vercel.com/ai-accelerator). Last year, we hosted our second cohort of 40 early-stage teams from across the globe. They joined us for six weeks of learning, building, and shipping, hearing from speakers in leadership at AWS, Anthropic, Cursor, Braintrust, MongoDB, HubSpot, Vercel, and more. The program culminated with a demo day in San Francisco that drew hundreds from across the industry.

正因如此，我们推出了 [Vercel AI 加速计划](https://vercel.com/ai-accelerator)。去年，我们举办了第二届加速营，汇聚了来自全球的 40 支早期团队。他们在为期六周的活动中学习、构建并发布产品，并聆听了来自 AWS、Anthropic、Cursor、Braintrust、MongoDB、HubSpot、Vercel 等多家机构高管的分享。该计划最终以在旧金山举办的 Demo Day 圆满收官，吸引了数百位来自整个行业的参与者。

This year, the Accelerator is back with another cohort of 40 teams building the future of AI. [Applications are open](https://vercel.com/ai-accelerator) now until February 16th.

今年，AI 加速计划再度启动，将再次遴选 40 支团队，共同塑造 AI 的未来。[申请现已开放](https://vercel.com/ai-accelerator)，截止日期为 2 月 16 日。

## Infrastructure for AI development

We continue investing across the AI stack, from SDKs to templates, supporting how developers build modern apps and agents. Recent AI releases include [Workflow DevKit](https://useworkflow.dev/), [Sandboxes](https://vercel.com/docs/vercel-sandbox), [Skills.sh](https://skills.sh/), and [git support in v0](https://v0.app/docs/github).  
我们持续在 AI 技术栈各层面加大投入——从 SDK 到模板，全方位支持开发者构建现代化应用与智能体（agents）。近期发布的 AI 工具包括 [Workflow DevKit](https://useworkflow.dev/)、[Sandboxes](https://vercel.com/docs/vercel-sandbox)、[Skills.sh](https://skills.sh/)，以及 [v0 中的 Git 支持](https://v0.app/docs/github)。

These tools are designed to handle infrastructure automatically so teams can focus on building AI products. Companies like [Sensay](https://vercel.com/blog/how-sensay-went-from-zero-to-product-in-six-weeks), [Chatbase](https://vercel.com/blog/how-chatbase-scaled-rapidly-with-vercels-developer-experience-and-ai-sdk), and [Leonardo.ai](https://vercel.com/blog/leonardo-ai-performantly-generates-4-5-million-images-daily-with-next-js-and-vercel) are built with Next.js and deployed on Vercel.  
这些工具可自动处理底层基础设施，使团队得以专注 AI 产品的核心开发。例如 [Sensay](https://vercel.com/blog/how-sensay-went-from-zero-to-product-in-six-weeks)、[Chatbase](https://vercel.com/blog/how-chatbase-scaled-rapidly-with-vercels-developer-experience-and-ai-sdk) 和 [Leonardo.ai](https://vercel.com/blog/leonardo-ai-performantly-generates-4-5-million-images-daily-with-next-js-and-vercel) 等公司均基于 Next.js 构建，并部署于 Vercel 平台。

## Focus on what matters

## 聚焦真正重要的事

AI agents can now operate autonomously on code, making decisions and taking actions without constant human oversight. This shift requires infrastructure that scales automatically and handles operational complexity behind the scenes. Vercel provides that foundation, giving startups the freedom to focus on building AI applications that solve real problems.

如今，AI 智能体已能在代码上自主运行，在无需持续人工监督的情况下做出决策并执行操作。这一转变要求基础设施能够自动伸缩，并在后台处理复杂的运维事务。Vercel 正是为此而生——它为初创企业提供坚实基础，使其得以专注于构建真正解决实际问题的 AI 应用。

In our last cohort we backed early-stage teams like [Stably AI](https://www.stably.ai/), [Cervo](https://usecervo.com/), [Bear AI](https://www.usebear.ai/), and [General Translation](https://generaltranslation.com/en-US).

在上一期加速器中，我们支持了处于早期阶段的团队，例如 [Stably AI](https://www.stably.ai/)、[Cervo](https://usecervo.com/)、[Bear AI](https://www.usebear.ai/) 和 [General Translation](https://generaltranslation.com/en-US)。
