2025年8月11日

v0.dev is now v0.app, the AI builder for everyone

v0.dev 现已更名为 v0.app——面向所有人的 AI 应用构建平台。

With a single prompt, anyone can go from idea to deployed app with UI, content, backend, and logic included.

只需一个提示词，任何人都能从创意出发，快速生成并部署一款完整应用——包含用户界面（UI）、内容、后端服务与业务逻辑。

[v0](https://v0.app/) is now agentic, helping you research, reason, debug, and plan. It can collaborate with you or take on the work end-to-end.

[v0](https://v0.app/) 现已升级为具备“智能体”（agentic）能力的 AI：它可协助你开展调研、推理分析、调试排错与项目规划；既可与你协同工作，也能独立完成端到端开发任务。

From product managers writing specs to recruiters launching job boards, v0 is changing how teams operate.

从产品经理撰写产品需求文档，到招聘人员快速上线职位发布平台，v0 正在重塑团队协作与交付的方式。

## One prompt. Real software.

## 一句提示，真实软件。

AI that generates code is helpful, but agentic AI that thinks, plans, and builds with context is even better.

仅能生成代码的 AI 已很有帮助，但能思考、规划，并结合上下文自主构建的“智能体 AI”更胜一筹。

Agentic AI moves beyond trial-and-error prompts. You describe what you want, and it builds. This means, v0 can figure out the right steps, remember what you’ve built, handle the complexity across every possible aspect of an app, all while [remaining secure](https://vercel.com/blog/v0-vibe-coding-securely).

智能体 AI 摆脱了反复试错式提示。你只需描述所需功能，它便自动构建——这意味着 v0 能自主规划合理步骤、记住你已构建的内容、统筹处理应用各层面（前端、后端、数据、安全、部署等）的复杂性，同时始终[保障安全性](https://vercel.com/blog/v0-vibe-coding-securely)。

Founders are using v0 to create everything from their first investor pitch deck—designed, editable, and on-brand—to a live MVP, including landing pages, onboarding flows, working dashboards, data capture, and custom design. Product managers can go from a user story like “a dashboard that shows usage trends across plans” to a fully functional app with charts, filters, and test data, all without writing a line of code. Sales teams can tailor demo environments on the fly by generating product variations for different customer segments.

创始人正借助 v0 快速打造各类关键资产：从首份投资人路演幻灯片（设计精美、支持编辑、符合品牌调性），到可实时访问的最小可行产品（MVP）——涵盖落地页、用户引导流程、可交互的数据看板、数据采集模块及定制化界面设计。产品经理只需输入一条用户故事，例如“一个展示各付费方案使用趋势的仪表盘”，v0 即可自动生成含图表、筛选器与测试数据的完整可用应用，全程无需编写任何代码。销售团队则可即时生成面向不同客户群体的产品演示环境变体，灵活适配各类销售场景。

## What v0.app can do

## v0.app 的核心能力

With v0.dev, getting to the right outcome often meant prompting again for fixes, better design, or added functionality. v0.app handles that automatically, using agentic intelligence to plan, adjust, and improve with fewer prompts.

使用 v0.dev 时，要达到理想结果往往需要反复调整提示词，以修复问题、优化设计或添加功能；而 v0.app 则能自动完成这些任务，依托“智能体式”（agentic）智能进行规划、动态调整与持续优化，显著减少所需提示次数。

Here are some examples of what v0 does automatically:

以下是 v0 自动完成的一些典型任务示例：

- **Check work:** Spots errors, compares implementations, and reasons through results  

- **检查工作成果**：识别错误、比对不同实现方案，并对结果进行逻辑推理分析

- **Give design inspiration:** Generates image concepts with descriptions based on prompts  

- **提供设计灵感**：依据提示词生成图像概念图及配套文字描述

- **Search the web:** Searches the web, handles failures, and returns results with citations  

- **网页搜索**：执行网络检索，自动处理失败情况，并返回带引用来源的搜索结果

- **Plan out work:** Makes a step-by-step plan to work on your request  

- **规划任务流程**：针对您的需求制定分步执行计划

- **Read files:** Reads files and returns their content  

- **读取文件**：读取指定文件并返回其全部内容

- **Inspect the web:** Inspects live sites, takes screenshots, and summarizes findings  

- **网页检测**：实时检测网站、自动截取屏幕快照，并汇总分析结果

- **To-do management:** Tracks tasks, updates plans, and outputs technical breakdowns  

- **待办事项管理**：跟踪任务进展、动态更新执行计划，并输出技术层面的详细拆解

- **Implement integrations:** Leverages your favorite tools  

- **实现集成**：无缝对接您最常用的各种工具
