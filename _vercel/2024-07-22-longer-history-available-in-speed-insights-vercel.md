We've increased the viewable history in Speed Insights for all plan types:

我们已为所有套餐类型提升了 Speed Insights 中可查看的历史数据时长：

- **Hobby**: Now **7 days** (up from 24 hours)

- **Hobby 套餐**：现为 **7 天**（此前为 24 小时）

- **Pro**: Now **30 days** (up from 7 days)

- **Pro 套餐**：现为 **30 天**（此前为 7 天）

- **Enterprise**: Now **90 days** (up from 30 days)

- **Enterprise 套餐**：现为 **90 天**（此前为 30 天）

Measure your site's performance over longer periods, at no additional cost.

您可免费在更长的时间范围内持续监测网站性能。

[Learn more about Speed Insights](https://vercel.com/docs/speed-insights) or [enable Speed Insights](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%5Bproject%5D%2Fspeed-insights&title=Open+Speed+Insights) for your project.

[进一步了解 Speed Insights](https://vercel.com/docs/speed-insights)，或为您的项目[启用 Speed Insights](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%5Bproject%5D%2Fspeed-insights&title=Open+Speed+Insights)。
