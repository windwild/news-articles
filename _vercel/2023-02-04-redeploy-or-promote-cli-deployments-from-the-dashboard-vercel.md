You can now redeploy or promote all deployments to production from the Vercel dashboard, no matter if you create them with the [CLI](https://vercel.com/docs/cli) or through the [Git integration](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/git).

您现在可以从 Vercel 控制台重新部署或提升所有部署至生产环境，无论这些部署是通过 [CLI](https://vercel.com/docs/cli) 还是通过 [Git 集成](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/git) 创建的。

[Read the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/managing-deployments#redeploy-a-project) to learn more.

[阅读文档](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/managing-deployments#redeploy-a-project) 了解更多信息。
