2025年1月24日

The [project overview page](https://vercel.com/docs/projects/project-dashboard) now shows a preview of your production traffic and firewall status.

[项目概览页面](https://vercel.com/docs/projects/project-dashboard) 现在可预览您的生产环境流量及防火墙状态。

The Vercel Firewall automatically mitigates DDoS attacks for all Vercel deployments. You can further secure your site with [custom rules and IP blocking](https://vercel.com/docs/security/vercel-waf), and by turning on [Attack Challenge Mode](https://vercel.com/docs/security/attack-challenge-mode) when under high-volume attacks. On the project overview page you'll see the status of the firewall, requests blocked and challenged in the past 24 hours, and a warning if a [reverse proxy](https://vercel.com/guides/can-i-use-a-proxy-on-top-of-my-vercel-deployment) is inhibiting Vercel's ability to protect your site.

Vercel 防火墙（Vercel Firewall）会自动为所有 Vercel 部署缓解 DDoS 攻击。您还可通过配置[自定义规则与 IP 封禁](https://vercel.com/docs/security/vercel-waf)，以及在遭遇大规模攻击时启用[攻击挑战模式（Attack Challenge Mode）](https://vercel.com/docs/security/attack-challenge-mode)，进一步增强网站安全性。在项目概览页面上，您将看到防火墙当前状态、过去 24 小时内被拦截和发起挑战的请求数量；若检测到[反向代理](https://vercel.com/guides/can-i-use-a-proxy-on-top-of-my-vercel-deployment)阻碍了 Vercel 对您网站的防护能力，页面还将显示相应警告。

Vercel Web Analytics gives you insight into your site's visitors and traffic. When the feature is [enabled](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics/quickstart), you'll see your site's traffic on the project overview page.

Vercel Web Analytics 可帮助您深入了解网站访客行为与流量状况。当该功能[启用后](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics/quickstart)，您即可在项目概览页面查看网站流量数据。

Learn more about [Vercel Web Analytics](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics) and [Vercel Firewall](https://vercel.com/docs/security/vercel-firewall).

了解更多关于 [Vercel Web Analytics](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics) 和 [Vercel Firewall](https://vercel.com/docs/security/vercel-firewall) 的信息。
