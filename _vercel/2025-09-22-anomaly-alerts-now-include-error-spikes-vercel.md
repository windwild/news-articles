Enterprise customers with [Observability Plus](https://vercel.com/docs/observability/observability-plus) can now be alerted when error events deviate from normal behavior, helping teams catch issues earlier.

已开通 [Observability Plus](https://vercel.com/docs/observability/observability-plus) 的企业客户，现可在错误事件偏离正常行为时即时收到告警，助力团队更早发现并定位问题。

The system automatically detects and groups abnormal error patterns, such as sudden spikes in 5XX responses on a specific route. Alert detail pages include relevant log lines, making it easier to investigate and resolve the underlying cause.

系统可自动识别并归类异常错误模式（例如某条路由上 5XX 响应的突发性激增）。告警详情页中会展示相关日志行，便于快速排查与解决根本原因。

Error anomaly detection, joining anomaly alerts for unusual app metric usage, is available in limited beta for Enterprise customers with [Observability Plus](https://vercel.com/docs/observability/observability-plus)

错误异常检测功能现已上线——与应用指标使用量异常告警一同构成完整的异常告警体系。该功能目前面向开通 [Observability Plus](https://vercel.com/docs/observability/observability-plus) 的企业客户开放限量公测。

[Try it out](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%7E%2Fobservability%2Falerts) or learn more about [Alerts](https://vercel.com/docs/alerts).

[Try it out](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%7E%2Fobservability%2Falerts)（立即体验）或了解更多关于 [Alerts](https://vercel.com/docs/alerts)（告警）的信息。
