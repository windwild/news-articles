2023年2月24日

From Monolithic PHP to Next.js and Vercel, CruiseCritic can move faster, and serve their customers with a better site experience

从单体式 PHP 架构迁移到 Next.js 与 Vercel，CruiseCritic 能够加速迭代，并为用户提供更优质的网站体验。

The web is any traveler’s first stop when it comes to planning vacations. [Cruise Critic](https://www.cruisecritic.com/) (a subsidiary of Tripadvisor) knows just how essential review sites are to today’s traveler; the company serves six million visitors every month. Growing traffic meant that Cruise Critic needed to evaluate their application stack as well as their development workflow in order to scale.

对任何旅行者而言，网页都是规划假期时的第一站。[Cruise Critic](https://www.cruisecritic.com/)（Tripadvisor 旗下子公司）深知点评类网站对当今旅行者的重要性；该公司每月服务六百万访客。流量持续增长，意味着 Cruise Critic 必须对其应用技术栈及开发工作流进行全面评估，以支撑业务规模化发展。

The company’s engineering organization turned to Vercel to help deliver high-performing web experiences at a faster pace. In Cruise Critic’s tightly coupled, monolithic PHP application, the team faced complex rewrites for even the smallest of changes. Delivering high-quality features in an iterative, agile way felt out of reach until developers adopted [Next.js and Vercel](https://vercel.com/solutions/nextjs).

该公司工程团队选择借助 Vercel，以更快地交付高性能的 Web 体验。在 Cruise Critic 原有高度耦合的单体式 PHP 应用中，即便是最微小的变更，团队也不得不进行复杂的代码重写。直到开发者采用 [Next.js 和 Vercel](https://vercel.com/solutions/nextjs)，以迭代化、敏捷化方式交付高质量功能才真正成为可能。

Major improvements to the developer experience means Cruise Critic builds software more efficiently. Engineering build times decreased from 30 minutes to less than eight minutes.The team’s workflow has become so fast they’re able to ship to production multiple times a day—a far cry from the days or weeks it used to take.

开发者体验的显著提升，使 Cruise Critic 的软件构建效率大幅提高：工程构建时间从 30 分钟缩短至不足 8 分钟；团队工作流变得极为高效，如今每天可多次发布至生产环境——这与过去动辄耗时数天甚至数周的情况形成鲜明对比。

_**"The most valuable feature of Vercel is that our team can focus on building products rather than worrying about infrastructure." — Robert Norman, Principal Software Engineer, Cruise Critic**_

_**“Vercel 最有价值的功能在于，我们的团队可以专注于产品构建，而无需操心底层基础设施。” — Robert Norman，首席软件工程师，Cruise Critic**_

Not only is development faster, but so is page performance. Cruise Critic saw an 85% decrease in page download time and a nearly 100% decrease in page size. With Vercel’s superior developer workflow, Cruise Critic is able to perform at a new level of scale.

不仅开发速度大幅提升，页面性能亦显著优化：Cruise Critic 的页面下载时间减少了 85%，页面体积近乎缩减了 100%。依托 Vercel 卓越的开发者工作流，Cruise Critic 已迈入全新规模层级的高效运营阶段。
