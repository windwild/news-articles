Role-based access controls are now available to all Enterprise customers, including:

基于角色的访问控制现已面向所有企业版客户正式开放，支持以下角色：

- **Viewer:** Read-only access

- **查看者（Viewer）：** 只读权限

- **Billing:** View invoices and edit billing settings

- **账单管理员（Billing）：** 查看账单并编辑账单设置

- **Developer:** Grant elevated permissions per-project

- **开发者（Developer）：** 按项目授予更高权限

[Check out the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/teams-and-accounts/team-members-and-roles) to learn more.

[查阅文档](https://vercel.com/docs/teams-and-accounts/team-members-and-roles) 了解更多信息。
