Apr 18, 2025

2025 年 4 月 18 日

In today's rapidly evolving tech landscape, AI has moved from research labs to everyday tools with stunning speed. I wanted to share my perspective, not only as a CTO at Vercel, but as an engineer who's seen a few revolutions over the past 30 years.

在当今快速演进的技术格局中，人工智能（AI）以惊人的速度从研究实验室走向了日常工具。我想分享自己的见解——不仅是以 Vercel 首席技术官（CTO）的身份，更是作为一名在过去三十年间亲历数次技术变革的工程师。

## **The acceleration of AI transformation**

## **AI 转型的加速浪潮**

When the iPhone launched in 2007, it took years for the mobile revolution to fully materialize. Android took three years to launch as a competitor, and it was a decade before smart phones were in everyone’s pocket.

2007 年 iPhone 发布时，移动革命的全面落地耗时数年。Android 作为竞争对手三年后才正式发布，而智能手机真正普及至大众口袋，则用了整整十年。

By comparison, today's AI revolution is happening in months rather than years. ChatGPT reached 100 million users in just three months. GPT-3 quickly evolved to GPT-4, and now we have multiple frontier models from companies like OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, and Meta. This acceleration signals a fundamental disruption. The technological landscape is changing, and every organization needs to find its new position in this transformed space.

相比之下，当今的 AI 革命正以“月”而非“年”为单位推进：ChatGPT 仅用三个月便达到一亿用户；GPT-3 迅速迭代为 GPT-4；如今，OpenAI、Google、Anthropic 和 Meta 等公司已纷纷推出多个前沿大模型。这种加速度预示着一场根本性颠覆——技术格局正在重塑，每一家组织都必须在这片被彻底改变的新疆域中，重新定位自身。

The question is no longer if AI will transform your business, but how you can harness it to maintain your competitive edge. This isn't about chasing the latest trends. It's about understanding the fundamental shifts AI brings, adapting our skills, and finding ways we can integrate into this AI-powered future.

问题已不再是“AI 是否会变革你的业务”，而是“你如何善用 AI 来维系并强化自身的竞争优势”。这并非追逐最新潮流，而是深入理解 AI 所带来的根本性范式转变，主动更新我们的能力，并积极探索融入这一 AI 驱动的未来之路。

## **Finding your AI strategy: Lessons from Vercel**

## **制定你的 AI 战略：来自 Vercel 的经验启示**

Like any other company, Vercel faced the challenge of finding its place within the AI landscape. Our answer was to build what we know, but with AI at its core. Vercel is a company focused on web frameworks and application infrastructure. When incorporating AI, we leveraged our strengths to create solutions that felt natural to our core business model:

与任何其他公司一样，Vercel 也面临着在 AI 格局中找准自身定位的挑战。我们的应对之道是：深耕所长，但以 AI 为内核。Vercel 是一家专注于 Web 框架与应用基础设施的公司。在融合 AI 时，我们充分发挥既有优势，打造与核心业务模式天然契合的解决方案：

- **Vercel's AI SDK**: A framework for building AI JavaScript applications  
- **Vercel AI SDK**：一款用于构建 AI 驱动的 JavaScript 应用的框架

- **v0**: an AI application that converts natural language prompts into user interfaces (UIs) for use in full stack applications.  
- **v0**: 一款 AI 应用，可将自然语言提示词（prompts）转化为可用于全栈应用的用户界面（UI）。

This approach offers a valuable lesson: find the integration that feels native to what your company already does. Don't try to become something you're not—instead, enhance your existing strengths with AI capabilities.  
这一方法带来了一条宝贵启示：寻找一种与贵公司现有业务天然契合的 AI 集成方式。切勿强行转型为自身并不擅长的角色；相反，应借助 AI 能力强化您已有的核心优势。

## **The new AI engineering paradigm**  
## **新一代 AI 工程范式**

We're witnessing a fundamental shift in how AI development works. What used to take advanced degrees and highly-specialized infrastructure now takes reading a few minutes of documentation and one-click deployments.  
我们正见证 AI 开发方式的根本性变革：过去需要高级学位与高度专业化的基础设施才能完成的工作，如今只需花几分钟阅读文档、单击一次即可完成部署。

| The old way (Software 1.0) | The new way (AI Revolution) |  
| --- | --- |  
| Build in Python because that's what universities used | Use familiar languages web and app builders use, like TypeScript |  
| Create infrastructure before building anything | Think frontend first, prioritize user experience |  
| Develop small, purpose-built models | Leverage large foundation models as a service |  
| Spend months or years on training | Simply prompt models rather than training from scratch |  
| Limited to PhDs and specialists | Accessible to any developer who can call an API |  
| Results took quarters or years to materialize | Ship products in days or weeks |  

| 传统方式（软件 1.0） | 新范式（AI 革命） |  
| --- | --- |  
| 因大学教学普遍使用 Python，故用 Python 开发 | 使用 Web 与应用开发者熟悉的语言，例如 TypeScript |  
| 先搭建基础设施，再开始构建功能 | 前端优先，以用户体验为核心 |  
| 开发小型、专用模型 | 借助即服务（as-a-service）形式的大规模基础模型 |  
| 训练耗时数月乃至数年 | 无需从零训练，仅需对模型进行提示（prompting） |  
| 仅限博士与领域专家参与 | 任何能调用 API 的开发者均可上手 |  
| 成果需耗费数个季度甚至数年才能落地 | 数天或数周内即可交付产品 |

The barrier to entry has never been lower. The emphasis shifts from acquiring knowledge and setting everything up "just right" to launching MVPs, getting feedback, and iterating quickly.  
入门门槛前所未有地降低。关注重点已从“掌握知识”与“把一切配置得尽善尽美”，转向快速推出最小可行产品（MVP）、获取用户反馈、并高速迭代优化。

## **How to compete with the tech giants**  
## **如何与科技巨头同台竞技**

Many businesses assume they can't compete with AI giants like OpenAI, Google, and Microsoft. That's simply not true. You need three key elements:  
许多企业认为自己无法与 OpenAI、Google 和 Microsoft 等 AI 巨头竞争——这种想法完全错误。您只需具备以下三个关键要素：

1. **Exclusive data:** You likely have proprietary information that large models don't have access to—customer data, internal documentation, domain-specific information. This data advantage lets you build AI applications that understand your specific business context  
1. **独家数据**：您很可能拥有大型模型无法获取的专有信息——例如客户数据、内部文档、特定行业的专业知识。这一数据优势使您能够构建真正理解自身业务语境的 AI 应用。

2. **Feedback cycle:** You can refine your AI applications continuously by implementing a robust testing and improvement process. While tech giants must balance updates across thousands of customers, your ability to implement, test, and adjust quickly is a pace they can't match  
2. **反馈闭环**：通过建立完善的测试与持续优化机制，您可以不断精进 AI 应用。科技巨头需在成千上万客户间权衡更新节奏，而您快速实施、验证与调整的能力，正是其难以企及的速度优势。

3. **Complicated domain:** Your business has unique complexities that general AI models don't understand. This is your competitive advantage. You've invested in developing this expertise—leverage it to create AI applications that solve real problems general models simply can't address

3. **复杂的业务领域：** 您的业务存在独特的复杂性，而通用 AI 模型无法理解这些复杂性。这正是您的竞争优势所在。您已投入大量资源积累这一专业能力——请充分利用它，构建能够解决真实问题的 AI 应用，而这些问题恰恰是通用模型根本无法应对的。

You already have what you need to compete. The combination of your data, your expertise, and your ability to move quickly is something the giants can’t replicate. Start there.

您已具备参与竞争所需的一切要素。您的数据、专业知识以及快速行动的能力三者结合，正是那些科技巨头无法复制的核心优势。就从这里开始。

## **The AI optimization cycle**

## **AI 优化循环**

One of the most powerful aspects of modern AI development is the ability to start simple and optimize over time:

现代 AI 开发最强大的特性之一，便是能够从简单起步，并随时间持续优化：

1. **Start with what works,** even if it's expensive or slow, get something functional first

1. **从行之有效的方法入手，** 即便成本高昂或速度较慢，也应优先实现一个可用的版本。

2. **Ship early** and collect valuable user data and feedback

2. **尽早交付，** 并收集宝贵的用户数据与反馈。

3. **Optimize gradually** by starting with more economical middle-tier options before moving to expensive frontier models

3. **逐步优化：** 先采用更具成本效益的中端方案，再过渡到昂贵的前沿模型。

4. **Test rigorously** and use evals to ensure performance doesn't degrade

4. **严格测试，** 并借助评估（evals）确保性能不发生退化。

5. **Reduce costs further** through techniques like fine-tuning

5. **进一步降低成本，** 例如通过微调（fine-tuning）等技术手段。

This iterative approach makes AI development accessible to businesses of all sizes.

这种迭代式方法，让不同规模的企业都能切实开展 AI 开发。

## **Building powerful AI applications with existing components**

## **利用现有组件构建强大的 AI 应用**

Our [AI SDK](https://sdk.vercel.ai/) simplifies how developers integrate AI into applications, making it easier experiment with multiple models and change between them with one or two lines of code. With functions like `generateText`, now anyone who can call an API can work with AI.

我们的 [AI SDK](https://sdk.vercel.ai/) 简化了开发者将 AI 集成到应用中的方式，让多模型实验与切换变得轻而易举——仅需一两行代码即可完成。借助 `generateText` 等函数，如今任何能调用 API 的人，都能轻松使用 AI。

The true potential for this tool emerges when AI becomes a part of your application's core rather than a conversational layer added on top. This architectural approach means AI can directly interact with your component system, data layer, and business logic rather than just communicating about them through text.

这一工具的真正潜力，体现在 AI 不再是叠加于应用之上的对话层，而是深度融入应用核心架构之时。这种架构设计使 AI 能够直接与你的组件系统、数据层及业务逻辑交互，而非仅通过文本“谈论”它们。

By embedding AI within your existing framework instead of adding it on top, you create experiences that feel cohesive and purposeful. Users interact with an intelligently enhanced version of your product instead of a chatbot that simply talks about your product. This distinction creates experiences that feel natural and integrated, preserving your product identity while expanding its capabilities.

通过将 AI 内嵌于你现有的技术框架之中（而非简单叠加其上），你所构建的用户体验将更具整体性与目的性。用户接触的是一个智能增强版的你的产品，而非一个仅就你的产品“侃侃而谈”的聊天机器人。这一本质区别，造就了自然、无缝的体验，在延续产品原有身份的同时，显著拓展其能力边界。

## **Real-world applications: v0**

## **真实场景应用：v0**

[v0](https://v0.dev/) exemplifies our approach to incorporating AI into our product line. by building on our expertise with JavaScript and modern web development, we created a powerful tool designed to turn natural language prompts into interactive web experiences.

[v0](https://v0.dev/) 是我们践行 AI 产品化理念的典范。依托我们在 JavaScript 与现代 Web 开发领域的深厚积累，我们打造了一款强大工具，可将自然语言提示词（prompt）直接转化为交互式网页体验。

Whether they're developers seeking to expand beyond their specialties, designers bringing Figma mockups to life, or product managers creating interactive prototypes without writing code, this tool supplements their expertise rather than replaces it—meeting users where they are. What matters isn't your technical background, but your vision.

无论你是希望突破专业边界的开发者、致力于将 Figma 设计稿变为现实的设计师，还是无需编写代码即可创建交互式原型的产品经理，这款工具都旨在赋能而非替代你的专长——它始终在你所在的位置与你同行。真正重要的是你的构想，而非你的技术背景。

By turning concepts into working prototypes in minutes instead of days, v0 represents our commitment to making the web more accessible to builders of all kinds. Everyone can cook.

v0 将构想转化为可运行原型的时间，从数天缩短至数分钟，这正体现了我们致力于让各类构建者（builders）更便捷地参与 Web 创造的承诺。“人人皆可下厨”（Everyone can cook）。

## **Finding your place in the AI world**

## **在 AI 世界中找到属于你的位置**

As AI capabilities advance, many developers worry about their role in this new landscape. The key insight is that AI tools like v0 are designed to get you started, not do your entire job. AI excels at certain tasks, but falls short in others. As developers, we need to find our areas of expertise where we can outperform AI. The technology is a tool to accelerate your work, not replace the thinking part.

随着 AI 能力不断进步，许多开发者担忧自己在这一新生态中的角色。关键洞见在于：v0 等 AI 工具旨在助你快速起步，而非包揽你的全部工作。AI 在某些任务上表现卓越，但在其他方面仍显不足。作为开发者，我们需要找准自身专长所在——那些我们能超越 AI 的领域。这项技术是加速你工作的工具，而非取代思考过程。

The AI revolution is here, and it's moving faster than any technological shift we've seen before. Rather than fearing this change, smart businesses are finding ways to integrate AI into their existing strengths.

AI 革命已然到来，其发展速度远超我们此前经历过的任何一次技术变革。与其畏惧这一变化，明智的企业正积极探索如何将 AI 深度融入自身既有优势之中。

The barrier to entry isn't a PhD anymore—it's the willingness to experiment, learn, and iterate. Start simple, ship early, and improve.

入行门槛已不再是博士学位——而是敢于尝试、乐于学习、勤于迭代的态度。从简单入手，尽早交付，持续优化。
