Mar 13, 2025

2025年3月13日

When I started Vercel, my vision was simple: make building for the web more accessible and more powerful. That belief has fueled Vercel’s growth, empowering developers to bring their biggest ideas to life.

当我创立 Vercel 时，我的愿景非常简单：让面向 Web 的开发变得更易用、更强大。这一信念推动了 Vercel 的持续成长，赋能开发者将最宏大的构想变为现实。

Today, we’re welcoming [Jeanne DeWitt Grosser](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jeannedewitt/), former Chief Business Officer at Stripe, as Vercel’s Chief Operating Officer to help further this mission. Vercel is building the foundation to power the next billion developers. Achieving this vision requires strong leadership and operational excellence. As COO, Jeanne will lead our go-to-market function.

今天，我们诚挚欢迎前 Stripe 首席业务官 [Jeanne DeWitt Grosser](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jeannedewitt/) 加入 Vercel，担任首席运营官（COO），助力我们进一步践行这一使命。Vercel 正在构建支撑未来十亿开发者的基础设施。实现这一愿景，离不开强有力的领导力与卓越的运营能力。作为 COO，Jeanne 将全面负责我们的市场进入（go-to-market）职能。

![](images/jeanne-dewitt-grosser-joins-vercel-as-coo-vercel/img_001.jpg)

## A leader who ships

## 一位专注交付的领导者

Vercel holds a high bar—not just for what we build, but for how we work together. We roll up our sleeves and operate on what I like to call " [recursive founder mode](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MQw_zncxk-E&t=895s)," moving fast and pushing boundaries. Jeanne embodies this approach.

Vercel 对自身要求极高——不仅体现在我们所构建的产品上，也体现在我们彼此协作的方式中。我们挽起袖子实干，以我称之为“[递归创始人模式（recursive founder mode）](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MQw_zncxk-E&t=895s)”的方式运作：行动迅速、勇于突破边界。Jeanne 完美诠释了这一精神。

Jeanne knows how to scale developer-first companies. At Stripe, she helped grow revenue from $100M to billions, pioneered usage-based go-to-market strategies, and built key partnerships. Before that, she shaped Google Workspace’s growth model before product-led growth was widely recognized.

Jeanne 深谙如何规模化发展以开发者为中心的企业。在 Stripe 任职期间，她助力公司营收从 1 亿美元增长至数十亿美元，开创了基于用量的市场进入策略，并建立了多项关键合作伙伴关系。在此前，她还在“产品驱动增长（product-led growth）”理念尚未被业界广泛认知之时，主导塑造了 Google Workspace 的增长模型。

To introduce Jeanne, I sat down with her for a conversation about her experience, leadership philosophy, and what’s ahead for Vercel.

为向大家介绍 Jeanne，我与她进行了一场深度对谈，话题涵盖她的职业经历、领导哲学，以及 Vercel 即将开启的新篇章。

### You’ve had an incredible career at companies like Stripe and Google. What drew you to Vercel?

### 您在 Stripe 和 Google 等公司拥有非凡的职业履历。是什么吸引您加入 Vercel？

**Jeanne**: I’m all about making big bets. When I think about the most exciting moments in my career, they’ve always been at companies that are changing how people build. That’s exactly what Vercel is doing for AI and the web.

**Jeanne**：我始终热衷于下大赌注。回顾我职业生涯中最激动人心的时刻，无一例外都发生在那些正在重塑人们构建方式的公司里。而 Vercel 正在 AI 与 Web 领域做着同样的事。

As an advisor to Vercel over the past 18 months, I’ve seen the team's talent and ambition firsthand. There’s an energy here—a belief that the future of the open web can be improved, and developers should have the best tools to build it. That’s what drew me in.

过去18个月来，作为 Vercel 的顾问，我亲身见证了团队的才华与雄心。这里洋溢着一种活力——一种信念：开放网络的未来可以被改善，而开发者理应拥有构建它的最佳工具。正是这一点吸引了我。

### You’ve helped shape go-to-market strategies at some of the world’s most influential companies. How do you see Vercel’s strategy evolving?

### 您曾协助全球最具影响力的一些企业制定市场进入策略。您如何看待 Vercel 战略的演进？

**Jeanne:** Vercel has a deeply loved product. The challenge is scaling in a way that serves indie developers to the world's largest enterprises, without losing what makes Vercel special.

**Jeanne：** Vercel 拥有一款深受用户喜爱的产品。真正的挑战在于如何规模化发展——既要服务独立开发者，也要满足全球最大型企业的需要，同时不丢失让 Vercel 独树一帜的核心特质。

The playbook for go-to-market strategies in today’s AI-driven, consumption-based businesses is still being written. I’m focused on pushing those boundaries, starting with Vercel’s platform as "customer zero" for products like v0, which is changing how developer-adjacent teams build for the web.

在当今以 AI 驱动、以使用量计费的商业环境中，市场进入策略的“操作手册”仍在撰写之中。我的工作重心是不断拓展这些边界——首先将 Vercel 自身平台作为 v0 等产品的“首位客户”（customer zero），而 v0 正在重塑与开发者紧密协作的团队构建 Web 应用的方式。

### AI has transformed how developers build. Where does Vercel fit with the future of development?

### AI 已彻底改变了开发者的工作方式。Vercel 将如何融入开发的未来图景？

**Jeanne:** Vercel is redefining how developers build with AI, by making AI more accessible and building a platform to meet the demands of modern web applications. Most companies focus on one or the other. Vercel does both, which unlocks new possibilities.

**Jeanne：** Vercel 正在重新定义开发者如何借助 AI 进行构建——一方面让 AI 更加触手可及，另一方面打造一个能应对现代 Web 应用需求的平台。大多数公司只聚焦于其中一点，而 Vercel 却二者兼备，从而释放出全新的可能性。

Great companies don't just follow industry shifts, they shape them. That’s exactly what Vercel is doing with AI.

伟大的公司从不只是追随行业变革，而是主动塑造变革。这正是 Vercel 在 AI 领域正在做的事。

### What excites you most about this next chapter?

### 对于 Vercel 的下一阶段，您最期待的是什么？

**Jeanne:** The team. Vercel is filled with people who are wildly ambitious, intensely customer-focused, and truly care about making the web better.

**Jeanne：** 是这支团队。Vercel 汇聚了一群抱负远大、极度以客户为中心、且真心致力于让 Web 变得更美好的人。

Scaling a company is not just about operational rigor. It’s about ensuring the culture, energy, and standards stay just as high as they were in the early days. I’m excited to help Vercel grow while staying true to what makes it great.

企业的规模化发展，绝不仅仅是运营层面的严谨性问题；它更关乎确保企业文化、团队活力与执行标准，始终如初创时期那般卓越。我很期待助力 Vercel 成长壮大，同时坚守使其真正伟大的核心价值。

## Looking ahead

## 展望未来

From [v0](https://v0.dev/), the [AI SDK](https://ai-sdk.dev/), to [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/), we’re building tools that power the next generation of AI-driven applications while staying committed to our open source roots.  

从 [v0](https://v0.dev/)、[AI SDK](https://ai-sdk.dev/) 到 [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/)，我们正构建一系列工具，以赋能下一代 AI 驱动的应用，同时始终坚守开源初心。

Jeanne’s leadership will strengthen our foundation, ensuring anyone—from solo creators to the world’s largest enterprises— [can just ship things](https://x.com/rauchg/status/1852834539464462799).  

珍妮的领导将夯实我们的根基，确保无论是个体创作者，还是全球最大的企业，都能“直接交付成果”——[can just ship things](https://x.com/rauchg/status/1852834539464462799)。
