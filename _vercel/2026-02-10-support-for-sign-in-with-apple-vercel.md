The login experience now supports Sign in with Apple, enabling faster access for users with Apple accounts.

登录体验现已支持“使用 Apple 账户登录”，让拥有 Apple 账户的用户能够更快速地访问。

If your Apple account uses an Apple email (@icloud.com, @mac.com, @me.com, etc.) that matches your Vercel account's email, you can use the Apple button from the login screen and your accounts will be automatically linked.

如果您的 Apple 账户所使用的 Apple 邮箱（例如 @icloud.com、@mac.com、@me.com 等）与您的 Vercel 账户邮箱一致，您可在登录界面直接点击 Apple 按钮，系统将自动关联两个账户。

If the emails don't match, you can manually connect your Apple account from your [account settings](https://vercel.com/account/settings/authentication) once logged in.

如果邮箱不匹配，您可在登录后，通过 [账户设置](https://vercel.com/account/settings/authentication) 手动关联您的 Apple 账户。
