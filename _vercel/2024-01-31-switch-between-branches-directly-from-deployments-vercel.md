2024 年 1 月 31 日

![](images/switch-between-branches-directly-from-deployments-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/switch-between-branches-directly-from-deployments-vercel/img_002.jpg)

You can now switch between branches directly from the [Vercel Toolbar](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/vercel-toolbar).

您现在可以直接从 [Vercel 工具栏](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/vercel-toolbar) 切换分支。

Access the command menu through the toolbar or `⌘K` (`Ctrl+K` on Windows) and select **branch switcher**. You’ll see your team’s branches sorted to highlight those with recent activity or unread comments. Then, select a branch to switch to that deployment.

通过工具栏或使用快捷键 `⌘K`（Windows 上为 `Ctrl+K`）打开命令菜单，然后选择 **分支切换器（branch switcher）**。您将看到团队的分支按最近活动或未读评论等维度排序，便于快速识别活跃分支。接着，选择目标分支即可切换至对应部署。

Learn more about the [command menu](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/vercel-toolbar#using-the-command-menu) and other features of the toolbar.

了解更多关于 [命令菜单](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/vercel-toolbar#using-the-command-menu) 及工具栏其他功能的信息。
