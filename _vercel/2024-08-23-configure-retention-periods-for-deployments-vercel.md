![](images/configure-retention-periods-for-deployments-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/configure-retention-periods-for-deployments-vercel/img_002.jpg)

You can now configure the retention period for deployments through the dashboard and CLI.

您现在可以通过 Vercel 控制台（Dashboard）和命令行界面（CLI）配置部署的保留期限。

For example, canceled and errored deployments might be set to 30 days retention, while production deployments might be set to 1 year. Recently deleted deployments are shown in your project settings and can be instantly restored within 30 days of deletion.

例如，已取消或出错的部署可设置为保留 30 天，而生产环境部署则可设置为保留 1 年。最近被删除的部署会显示在您的项目设置中，并可在删除后 30 天内即时恢复。

Learn more in our [documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/security/deployment-retention).

更多详情，请参阅我们的[文档](https://vercel.com/docs/security/deployment-retention)。
