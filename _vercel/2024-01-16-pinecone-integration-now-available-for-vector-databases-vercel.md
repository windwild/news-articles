Jan 16, 2024

2024 年 1 月 16 日

![](images/pinecone-integration-now-available-for-vector-databases-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/pinecone-integration-now-available-for-vector-databases-vercel/img_002.jpg)

You can now use the [Pinecone](https://vercel.com/integrations/pinecone) integration to create vector databases for your [AI applications](https://vercel.com/ai).  
您现在可以使用 [Pinecone](https://vercel.com/integrations/pinecone) 集成，为您的 [AI 应用](https://vercel.com/ai) 创建向量数据库。

[Vector databases](https://vercel.com/guides/vector-databases) enable augmenting LLMs with the ability to retrieve additional knowledge ( [RAG](https://vercel.com/guides/retrieval-augmented-generation)) from your provided sources.  
[向量数据库](https://vercel.com/guides/vector-databases) 能够增强大语言模型（LLM）的能力，使其可从您提供的数据源中检索额外知识（即 [检索增强生成，RAG](https://vercel.com/guides/retrieval-augmented-generation)）。

This integration is available for users on all plans.  
该集成面向所有订阅计划的用户开放。

[Check out the integration](https://vercel.com/integrations/pinecone) to get started.  
[立即查看该集成](https://vercel.com/integrations/pinecone)，开始使用。
