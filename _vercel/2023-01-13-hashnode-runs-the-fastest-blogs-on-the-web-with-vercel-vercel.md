[Hashnode](https://townhall.hashnode.com/powerful-and-superfast-hashnode-blogs-now-powered-by-nextjs-11-and-vercel), a blogging platform for the developer community built using Next.js, was born from the fundamental idea that developers should own the content they publish. A key component of that ownership is publishing articles on a custom domain—a feature the Hashnode team spent hours monitoring and maintaining themselves. That’s when they turned to Vercel.

[Hashnode](https://townhall.hashnode.com/powerful-and-superfast-hashnode-blogs-now-powered-by-nextjs-11-and-vercel) 是一个面向开发者社区的博客平台，基于 Next.js 构建。其诞生源于一个基本理念：开发者应当拥有自己所发布的内容。而这种“所有权”的关键一环，便是将文章发布在自定义域名下——这一功能曾由 Hashnode 团队耗费大量时间亲自监控与维护。也正是在此时，他们转向了 Vercel。

After evaluating alternative solutions like AWS Amplify, Hashnode ultimately chose Vercel because of the ability to manage custom domains at scale and the smooth and intuitive developer experience. The engineering team saves time by previewing deployments and testing changes in a production-like environment without configuring staging.

在评估了 AWS Amplify 等替代方案后，Hashnode 最终选择了 Vercel——不仅因其可大规模管理自定义域名的能力，更因其流畅直观的开发者体验。工程团队无需配置预发布环境（staging），即可在类生产环境中预览部署、测试变更，从而显著节省时间。

**“With Vercel, we spend less time managing our infrastructure and more time delivering value to our users.” — Sandeep Panda, Co-founder, Hashnode**

**“借助 Vercel，我们花在基础设施运维上的时间更少了，而用于为用户创造价值的时间则更多了。”—— Hashnode 联合创始人 Sandeep Panda**

Migrating to Vercel also enhanced how Hashnode monitors and optimizes speed, which is an essential selling point for its developer audience. [Vercel’s Edge Network](https://vercel.com/features/infrastructure) helps Hashnode guarantee a fast and delightful blogging experience that ensures posts rank higher in search results. And as for Lighthouse tests, lightning-fast load times and automatically optimized images mean every Hashnode-hosted blog gets perfect scores across the board.

迁移到 Vercel 还进一步提升了 Hashnode 对网站速度的监控与优化能力——而这恰恰是其开发者用户群体极为看重的核心卖点。[Vercel 边缘网络（Edge Network）](https://vercel.com/features/infrastructure) 助力 Hashnode 打造快速、愉悦的博客体验，从而提升文章在搜索引擎结果中的排名。至于 Lighthouse 测试，闪电般的加载速度与自动优化的图片意味着：所有托管于 Hashnode 的博客，各项指标均可获得满分成绩。
