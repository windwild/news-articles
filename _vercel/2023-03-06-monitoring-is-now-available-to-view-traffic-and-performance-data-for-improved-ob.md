![](images/monitoring-is-now-available-to-view-traffic-and-performance-data-for-improved-ob/img_001.jpg)![](images/monitoring-is-now-available-to-view-traffic-and-performance-data-for-improved-ob/img_002.jpg)

Monitoring helps you detect and diagnose issues in your web applications by surfacing errors, traffic, and performance data. You can leverage example queries like **Bandwidth by Project** or **Requests by Bot** or create your own to quickly resolve issues and optimize your projects.

监控功能可帮助您通过汇总错误、流量和性能数据，及时发现并诊断 Web 应用中的问题。您可以直接使用示例查询（例如 **按项目统计带宽** 或 **按机器人统计请求量**），也可自定义查询，从而快速定位并解决问题，并优化您的项目。

监控功能面向 Pro 和 Enterprise 计划用户开放：Pro 计划支持 30 天数据保留，Enterprise 计划支持 90 天数据保留。

[查看我们的文档](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/observability/monitoring) 以了解更多信息。
