2025 年 12 月 16 日

![](images/export-observability-query-results-to-csv-or-json-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/export-observability-query-results-to-csv-or-json-vercel/img_002.jpg)

You can now export the results from your Observability queries as CSV or JSON files. This allows you to analyze, share, and process your Vercel observability data outside of the Vercel dashboard.

您现在可以将可观测性（Observability）查询结果导出为 CSV 或 JSON 文件。这使您能够在 Vercel 控制台之外分析、共享和处理您的 Vercel 可观测性数据。

Click the download icon on any query to export your query results instantly.

点击任意查询旁的下载图标，即可立即导出该查询结果。

This feature is available for all teams with [Observability Plus](https://vercel.com/docs/observability/observability-plus).

此功能面向所有已启用 [Observability Plus](https://vercel.com/docs/observability/observability-plus) 的团队开放。

[Try it out](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%5Bproject%5D%2Fobservability%2Fquery) or learn more about [Query](https://vercel.com/docs/query).

[立即试用](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%5Bproject%5D%2Fobservability%2Fquery)，或深入了解 [Query](https://vercel.com/docs/query)。
