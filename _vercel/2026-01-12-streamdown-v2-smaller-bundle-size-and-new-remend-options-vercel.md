### Streamdown Plugins

### Streamdown 插件系统

The most requested feature since launch has been to reduce the bundle size. Streamdown v2 now ships with a bundle size much smaller than the previous version and uses a plugin-based architecture.

自发布以来，用户呼声最高的功能便是减小打包体积。Streamdown v2 现已大幅缩减打包体积，并采用基于插件的架构设计。

### Carets

### 插入符号（光标）指示器

Streamdown now includes [built-in caret](https://streamdown.ai/docs/carets) (cursor) indicators that display at the end of streaming content. Carets provide a visual cue to users that content is actively being generated, similar to a blinking cursor in a text editor.

Streamdown 现已内置 [插入符号（光标）指示器](https://streamdown.ai/docs/carets)，会在流式内容的末尾显示。这些插入符号为用户提供视觉提示，表明内容正在实时生成，其效果类似于文本编辑器中闪烁的光标。

### Configurable Remend

### 可配置的 Remend

Our underlying markdown-healing library Remend is now configurable. You can choose how much healing you would prefer during the markdown streaming.

我们底层的 Markdown 自动修复库 Remend 现已支持配置。您可在 Markdown 流式渲染过程中，自主选择所需的修复强度。
