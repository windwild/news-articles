Dec 12, 2024

2024 年 12 月 12 日

Monitoring pricing has been reduced to $1.20 per million events. This new pricing is effective immediately, with no action required.

监控服务的定价已下调至每百万事件 1.20 美元。此项新定价即日起生效，无需用户采取任何操作。

Monitoring is also now part of Observability Plus, which can be enabled in [Vercel Observability—now generally available](https://vercel.com/changelog/vercel-observability-is-now-generally-available). This enhanced suite builds on the Monitoring query engine, offering deeper insights into request handling, caching, compute, and build infrastructure.

监控服务现已整合进 Observability Plus 套件，您可在 [Vercel Observability——现已正式发布](https://vercel.com/changelog/vercel-observability-is-now-generally-available) 中启用该功能。这一增强型套件基于 Monitoring 查询引擎构建，可为您提供更深入的洞察，涵盖请求处理、缓存、计算资源及构建基础设施等方面。

To ensure uninterrupted workflows, both Monitoring and Observability remain visible in the dashboard for current Monitoring users.

为确保现有工作流不受影响，当前使用 Monitoring 的用户仍可在控制台中同时看到 Monitoring 和 Observability 相关功能。

Read more about [Monitoring for existing users](https://vercel.com/docs/observability#existing-monitoring-users), [Observability](https://vercel.com/docs/observability), and [Observability Plus](https://vercel.com/docs/observability#enabling-observability-plus).

了解更多：[面向现有用户的 Monitoring](https://vercel.com/docs/observability#existing-monitoring-users)、[Observability](https://vercel.com/docs/observability) 及 [Observability Plus](https://vercel.com/docs/observability#enabling-observability-plus)。
