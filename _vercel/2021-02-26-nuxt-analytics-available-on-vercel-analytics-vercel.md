Feb 26, 2021

2021 年 2 月 26 日

![](images/nuxt-analytics-available-on-vercel-analytics-vercel/img_001.jpg)

**Nuxt analytics** are now available for all Nuxt projects on Vercel as part of Vercel Analytics, with zero configuration. This allows developers to understand the [Real Experience Score](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics#real-experience-score?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=changelog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics) for Nuxt projects.

**Nuxt Analytics** 现已作为 Vercel Analytics 的一部分，面向所有部署在 Vercel 上的 Nuxt 项目正式开放，且无需任何配置。这使开发者能够了解其 Nuxt 项目的 [真实体验得分（Real Experience Score）](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics#real-experience-score?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=changelog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics)。

To enable, after [importing your Nuxt project](https://vercel.com/new?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=changelog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics):

启用步骤（在 [导入您的 Nuxt 项目](https://vercel.com/new?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=changelog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics) 后）：

1. **Open a Nuxt project in your** [**Vercel dashboard**](https://vercel.com/dashboard?analytics=1&utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=changelog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics) **.**

1. **在 [Vercel 控制台](https://vercel.com/dashboard?analytics=1&utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=changelog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics) 中打开一个 Nuxt 项目。**

2. **Select the "Analytics" tab and follow the flow.**

2. **点击顶部导航栏中的 “Analytics”（分析）标签页，并按引导完成设置。**

No code changes are required, and [options for self-hosted applications](https://vercel.com/contact/sales?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=changelog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics) are available for enterprises.

无需修改任何代码；企业用户还可选择 [自托管应用方案](https://vercel.com/contact/sales?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=changelog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics)。

Once deployed, your application will automatically report [Core Web Vitals](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics#web-vitals?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics) to Vercel.

部署完成后，您的应用将自动向 Vercel 上报 [核心网页指标（Core Web Vitals）](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics#web-vitals?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics)。

[Check out the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=changelog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics) as well.

也欢迎查阅 [完整文档](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=changelog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics)。
