Jul 29, 2025

2025 年 7 月 29 日

Since launch, we’ve seen a growing wave of people building with v0 and sharing what they’ve created, from full-stack apps to UI experiments.

自发布以来，我们欣喜地看到越来越多的开发者正使用 v0 构建各类项目，并积极分享成果——从全栈应用到 UI 实验，不一而足。

Now, we’re going a step further by sponsoring builders innovating and showcasing what’s possible with v0.

如今，我们更进一步：通过资助那些正在用 v0 进行创新并展示其潜力的开发者，来推动生态发展。

Today we’re launching the [v0 Ambassador Program](https://v0.dev/ambassador) as a way to recognize and enable members of our community who create, share, and inspire.

今天，我们正式推出 [v0 大使计划](https://v0.dev/ambassador)，旨在表彰并赋能那些持续创作、乐于分享、并激励他人的社区成员。

[Apply](https://v0ambassadorform.vercel.app/) to join the v0 Ambassador Program and help others discover the magic of what's possible with v0.

[立即申请](https://v0ambassadorform.vercel.app/) 加入 v0 大使计划，帮助更多人发现 v0 所能带来的无限可能。

## What is the Ambassador Program?

## 什么是大使计划？

The goal is simple: help more people discover what v0 can do, by highlighting those who are already building and sharing their v0 creations in public.

目标非常明确：通过重点展示那些已在公开场合构建并分享 v0 项目的开发者，帮助更多人了解 v0 的能力。

As an ambassador, you’ll get:

成为大使，您将获得：

- **v0 credits:** Use $100 in v0 credits per month to build, test, and grow

- **v0 积分：** 每月可获 $100 v0 积分，用于开发、测试与项目拓展

- **Early access:** Be the first to access new features

- **抢先体验权：** 第一时间试用新功能

- **v0 Swag:** Get limited-edition v0 ambassador swag  
- **v0 周边礼品：** 领取限量版 v0 大使专属周边  

- **Increased Visibility:** Have your work featured on v0's channels with an exclusive badge  
- **提升曝光度：** 您的作品将通过专属徽章，在 v0 官方渠道获得重点展示  

- **Referral Link:** Earn $10 for every Premium referral and $30 for every Team referral  
- **推荐链接：** 每成功推荐一位 Premium 用户可获 $10，每成功推荐一位 Team 用户可获 $30  

- **Micro Grants:** Apply for v0 credits when you post your public projects in the v0 Community  
- **微型资助计划：** 在 v0 社区发布您的公开项目后，即可申请 v0 积分资助  


## Ambassador Responsibilities  

## 大使职责  

We’re looking for people who love building, sharing, and giving feedback. Not just for the perks, but to help shape what v0 becomes.  
我们正在寻找热爱构建、乐于分享、积极反馈的伙伴——不仅为福利而来，更为共同塑造 v0 的未来。  

**Responsibilities:**  
**主要职责：**  

- Create and share content about v0 at least once per week  
- 每周至少创作并分享一次关于 v0 的内容  

  - This could be anything from videos, to blog posts, threads, live demos, and more  
  - 形式不限：视频、博客文章、社交媒体长帖、直播演示等均可  

  - Publish wherever your audience is: Reddit, LinkedIn, X, YouTube, or anywhere you think it’ll resonate  
  - 发布平台由你决定：Reddit、LinkedIn、X（原 Twitter）、YouTube，或任何你认为受众最活跃的平台  

- Add “@v0 Ambassador” to your social bio  
- 在你的社交平台个人简介中添加 “@v0 Ambassador” 标识

- Provide feedback on new features

  - You’ll be part of an early access group and help us improve with real input  
- 提供新功能的反馈  

  - 您将加入早期体验用户群组，并通过真实反馈帮助我们持续优化产品  
- Represent v0 positively in your community  

- 在您的社区中积极推广 v0  

## How to apply  

## 如何申请  

This is a beta version of the program, with limited spots available. We prioritize applicants who show a strong connection with the product and a proven ability to build and activate an audience.  

本计划目前处于测试阶段，名额有限。我们优先考虑与产品有深度联结、且已展现出成功构建并激活受众能力的申请人。  

To apply, fill out [this short form](https://v0ambassadorform.vercel.app/).  

请填写[这份简短申请表](https://v0ambassadorform.vercel.app/)完成申请。  

You’ll be asked to share:  

您需要提供以下信息：  

- Your name and location  

- 您的姓名和所在地  

- Links to your social profiles (X, Reddit, YouTube, TikTok, LinkedIn, etc.)  

- 您的社交平台主页链接（X、Reddit、YouTube、TikTok、LinkedIn 等）  

- Any content you’ve published about v0  

- 您已发布的关于 v0 的相关内容  

- Your v0 profile ( [example](https://v0.dev/user/estebansuarez))  

- 您的 v0 个人主页（[示例](https://v0.dev/user/estebansuarez)）

- A few thoughts on what you'd like to create or contribute as part of the program

- 关于您希望在本项目中创建或贡献的内容，简要谈谈您的想法

We’ll review applications on a rolling basis.

我们将持续审阅申请材料。

Let’s build the next generation of the web together and make sure it’s shared, forked, remixed, and out in the open.

让我们携手共建下一代网络，并确保它开放共享、可自由分叉、可重新混搭，且完全公开透明。
