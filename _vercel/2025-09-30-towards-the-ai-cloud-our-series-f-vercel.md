Sep 30, 2025

2025 年 9 月 30 日

Today, Vercel announced an important milestone: a Series F funding round valuing our company at $9.3 billion. The $300M investment is co-led by longtime partners at Accel and new investors at GIC, alongside other incredible supporters. We're also launching a ~$300M tender offer for certain early investors, employees, and former employees.

今天，Vercel 宣布了一项重要里程碑：完成 F 轮融资，公司估值达 93 亿美元。本轮融资规模为 3 亿美元，由长期合作伙伴 Accel 与新投资者新加坡政府投资公司（GIC）联合领投，并获得其他众多杰出支持者的参与。同时，我们还将启动一项约 3 亿美元的要约收购计划，面向部分早期投资者、在职员工及前员工。

To all the customers, investors, and Vercelians who have been on this journey with us: thank you.

向一路以来与我们同行的所有客户、投资者以及 Vercel 团队成员：衷心感谢！

## The web is evolving: From pixels to tokens

## 网络正在演进：从像素到 Token

We recently celebrated [our 10th anniversary,](https://x.com/rauchg/status/1962578038878548416) and this has been, without a doubt, the biggest year in Vercel's history. The web is undergoing a massive evolution. We're moving from the world of pixels to the world of tokens.

我们近期庆祝了[成立十周年](https://x.com/rauchg/status/1962578038878548416)，毫无疑问，这是 Vercel 历史上最具突破性的一年。网络正经历一场巨变——我们正从“像素时代”迈向“Token 时代”。

Our founding insight back in 2015 was simple: the world’s largest companies – Amazon, Google, Facebook – had internal infrastructure that allowed them to build and ship fast. They defined the protocols, the infrastructure, and the frameworks. The rest of the world didn't have access to that. Vercel was founded to democratize that advantage.

2015 年创立之初，我们的核心洞察十分简单：全球最大的公司——亚马逊、谷歌、脸书——都拥有内部基础设施，使其能够快速构建并交付产品；它们定义了协议、基础设施与开发框架。而世界其他地方却无法获得这种能力。Vercel 的使命，正是将这一优势普惠化。

Then the world changed. We used to build pages, and now we’re going to build agents.

随后，世界发生了改变：我们过去构建的是网页（pages），而如今，我们将构建智能体（agents）。

What makes this moment so unique is that not even the giants have figured it out yet. Everyone's being disrupted. It's a great time for startups, a great time for innovators, a great time for enterprises. But companies that don't iterate quickly will be left behind.

令此刻尤为独特的是：即便是科技巨头，也尚未真正掌握这一新范式。所有企业都在被重塑。这对初创公司而言是黄金时代，对创新者而言是黄金时代，对企业级用户而言亦是黄金时代。但若不能快速迭代，任何公司都将被时代抛下。

This transformation demanded we evolve with it. We've evolved as much in this last year as we did in the first nine. Because there has to be a cloud that's designed and purpose-built for AI applications. Vercel has built this from the ground up with the AI Cloud.

这场变革要求我们随之进化。过去一年中，我们的演进程度不亚于此前九年总和。因为必须有一朵专为 AI 应用而生、从底层设计并深度优化的云——Vercel 正是以此为使命，自底向上打造了 AI Cloud。

## The same mission, but for AI

## 同样的使命，面向 AI

When we raised our Series C four years ago, I [talked about three things](https://rauchg.com/2021/making-the-web-faster):

四年前，当我们完成 C 轮融资时，我曾[谈到三件事](https://rauchg.com/2021/making-the-web-faster)：

- Building the SDK for the web

- 为 Web 构建 SDK

- Lowering the barrier to entry

- 降低入门门槛

- Focusing on the end user experience

- 聚焦终端用户体验


Today, we're doing the same three things, but for AI.

今天，我们仍在做这三件事——只是对象已转向 AI。

**Building the SDK for AI.** The AI SDK might be one of the most consequential projects we've ever worked on. It provides access to [more than 60 models](https://ai-sdk.dev/providers/ai-sdk-providers) through a unified interface. Just like React brought accessibility to pages, the AI SDK is bringing accessibility to tokens to multiple sources of intelligence.

**构建面向 AI 的 SDK。** AI SDK 或许是我们迄今开展过的最具影响力项目之一。它通过统一接口，提供对[60 多种模型](https://ai-sdk.dev/providers/ai-sdk-providers)的访问能力。正如 React 让网页开发变得触手可及，AI SDK 正让“token”这一核心单元在多种智能源之间实现无障碍调用。

The numbers tell the story: the AI SDK has crossed [3 million downloads per week](https://x.com/lgrammel/status/1972565382314803492) (by far our fastest-growing open source project ever).

数据印证了一切：AI SDK 周下载量已突破[300 万次](https://x.com/lgrammel/status/1972565382314803492)（远超我们历史上增长最快的开源项目）。

**Lowering the barrier to entry.** With the rise in popularity of ChatGPT, developers we speak to every day want the power to build their own agents and deploy them safely in their own environments.

**降低入门门槛。** 随着 ChatGPT 日益普及，我们每天接触的开发者都渴望拥有自主构建智能体（agents）并将其安全部署至自有环境的能力。

We recently [open-sourced a ChatGPT Enterprise-like template](https://x.com/rauchg/status/1970877232441930026). We also shipped an open source [vibe coding platform with v0](https://x.com/rauchg/status/1964858646527197568). Our bet on open source has become a powerful flywheel, and we’re excited to continue democratizing agents and making them accessible to everyone.

我们近期[开源了一个类似 ChatGPT Enterprise 的模板](https://x.com/rauchg/status/1970877232441930026)；同时也推出了一个基于 v0 的开源[氛围感编程平台（vibe coding platform）](https://x.com/rauchg/status/1964858646527197568)。我们对开源的坚定投入已形成强大的飞轮效应；我们亦满怀热忱，持续推动智能体的民主化，让这项能力真正惠及每一个人。

**Focusing on the end user.** And don't forget about the pixels. Interfaces still matter. The biggest AI products, Grok, Claude, and Cursor, are using Next.js to power their frontends. In the last 12 months, Next.js has been downloaded over 500 million times, **exceeding the total downloads from 2016 to 2024**.

**聚焦终端用户。** 同时别忘了像素细节——界面体验依然至关重要。目前最主流的 AI 产品（Grok、Claude 和 Cursor）均采用 Next.js 构建其前端。过去 12 个月中，Next.js 下载量已突破 5 亿次，**超过 2016 至 2024 年全周期下载总量**。

## Building the future

## 构建未来

Agents will completely reshape the web, including how we work. Vercel is powering that shift. As we turn the corner into the last quarter of 2025, we're locking in.

智能体（Agents）将彻底重塑互联网生态，包括我们工作的方式。Vercel 正在驱动这一变革。随着我们迈入 2025 年最后一个季度，一切正在加速落地。

The AI Cloud isn’t just a foundation for developers building agents. It’s where we’re building our own. Earlier this month, we [introduced Vercel Agent](https://vercel.com/changelog/ai-code-reviews-by-vercel-agent-now-in-public-beta) in public beta with a new skill: AI-powered code reviews. These reviews go beyond the diff, scanning your entire codebase, generating and actually validating fixes (by running your app with the patch in Vercel Sandboxes), and surfacing them directly in your pull requests.

AI Cloud 不仅是开发者构建智能体的基础设施，更是我们打造自有智能体的平台。本月早些时候，我们[正式推出 Vercel Agent 公测版](https://vercel.com/changelog/ai-code-reviews-by-vercel-agent-now-in-public-beta)，新增一项核心能力：AI 驱动的代码评审。这些评审不再局限于代码差异（diff），而是全面扫描整个代码库，自动生成修复建议，并通过在 Vercel 沙箱中运行带补丁的应用程序来实际验证修复效果，最终直接呈现在你的 Pull Request 中。

What makes this powerful is that Agent isn’t static. It adapts to frameworks like Next.js and React, supports multiple languages from TypeScript to Go and Python, and validates every recommendation against real execution environments. It’s a glimpse of what happens when infrastructure is no longer just provisioned, but actively participates in making applications faster, safer, and more efficient.

这项能力的强大之处在于：Agent 并非静态工具——它能自动适配 Next.js、React 等主流框架，支持从 TypeScript、Go 到 Python 等多种语言，并且每一条建议均经真实执行环境验证。这预示着一种全新范式：基础设施不再只是被动配置，而是主动参与应用开发，让应用更快、更安全、更高效。

Coming up in the next few weeks: general availability of the [v0 iOS app](https://v0.app/ios). Currently in public beta, it's the manifestation of something profound we discovered about work: 70% of development happens outside traditional work hours. The best ideas don't wait for you to be at your desk.

接下来几周即将上线：[v0 iOS 应用](https://v0.app/ios)正式版。当前处于公测阶段，它源于我们对工作本质的一项深刻洞察：70% 的开发活动发生在传统办公时间之外；最棒的创意，从不等待你坐到工位前。

v0’s mobile app is the evolution of your Notes app, except your ideas get built for you in the background. Build at the speed of sound with voice prompts. Start a project on your computer, leave your desk, and get notified when your long-running agent tasks complete. With more than 10,000 people on the waitlist, v0 on mobile represents the collapse of the last barriers between imagination and creation.

v0 移动端应用，是笔记类 App 的进化形态——不同之处在于：你的灵感将在后台自动转化为可运行的项目。通过语音指令，以“声速”启动构建；在电脑上开启一个项目，离开工位后，系统将在智能体完成长时间任务时即时通知你。目前已有超 10,000 名用户加入等候名单，v0 移动端正标志着想象力与创造力之间最后一道壁垒的消融。

2025 was the year my lifelong belief in a dynamic web exceeded what I thought was possible: millions of developers (and agents) creating experiences we couldn't imagine just a year ago.

2025 年，我毕生信奉的“动态网络”愿景，已超越了我此前所能想象的边界：数百万开发者（以及智能体）正在创造出一年前我们根本无法预见的全新体验。

I'll see you there (and also at [Next.js Conf](https://nextjs.org/conf) and [Ship AI](https://vercel.com/ship/ai)), building the future on the free, open, and infinitely creative web.

期待在那里与你相见（也欢迎参加 [Next.js Conf](https://nextjs.org/conf) 和 [Ship AI](https://vercel.com/ship/ai)），共同在自由、开放、无限富有创造力的网络之上，构建未来。
