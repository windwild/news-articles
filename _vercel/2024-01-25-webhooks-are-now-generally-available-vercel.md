Jan 25, 2024

2024 年 1 月 25 日

![](images/webhooks-are-now-generally-available-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/webhooks-are-now-generally-available-vercel/img_002.jpg)

Webhooks allow you to get notified through a defined HTTP endpoint about certain deployment or project events that happened on the Vercel platform.

Webhook 允许您通过一个预定义的 HTTP 端点，接收有关 Vercel 平台上发生的特定部署或项目事件的通知。

Webhooks are now available for all Pro and Enterprise customers. You can create a maximum of 20 webhooks per account.

Webhook 现已面向所有 Pro 和 Enterprise 计划用户开放。每个账户最多可创建 20 个 webhook。

Check out [our documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/observability/webhooks-overview) to create your first webhook.

请查阅[我们的文档](https://vercel.com/docs/observability/webhooks-overview)，创建您的第一个 webhook。
