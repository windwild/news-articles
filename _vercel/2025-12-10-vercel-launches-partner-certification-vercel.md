## What certification requires and enables

## 认证要求与赋能内容

The certification program validates expertise across three areas: the Vercel platform (Developer Experience Platform, Managed Infrastructure, Observability & Analytics), Next.js development (best practices, advanced patterns), and AI Cloud ( [AI SDK](https://ai-sdk.dev/), [v0.app](https://v0.app/), [Workflow](https://useworkflow.dev/)). Partners must certify a minimum number of developers on their team, with recertification tied to major Next.js and Vercel releases to ensure teams stay current.

该认证计划从三大领域验证合作伙伴的专业能力：Vercel 平台（开发者体验平台、托管基础设施、可观测性与分析）、Next.js 开发（最佳实践与高级模式），以及 AI Cloud（[AI SDK](https://ai-sdk.dev/)、[v0.app](https://v0.app/)、[Workflow](https://useworkflow.dev/)）。合作伙伴需确保其团队中至少有规定数量的开发者通过认证；且须在每次 Next.js 和 Vercel 的重大版本发布后完成再认证，以确保团队技术能力持续保持前沿。

Certification creates a strong connection between partners, customers, and Vercel. Certified solution partners:

认证在合作伙伴、客户与 Vercel 之间建立起强有力的纽带。获得认证的解决方案合作伙伴可享有以下权益：

- Prominently featured as "Certified" in Vercel's [partner directory,](https://vercel.com/partners/solution-partners) helping customers find qualified teams

- 在 Vercel [合作伙伴目录](https://vercel.com/partners/solution-partners) 中显著标注为“已认证”，助力客户快速识别并选择具备资质的交付团队

- Work more closely with Vercel Professional Services and go-to-market teams to match customers to the right delivery teams

- 与 Vercel 专业服务团队及市场拓展（Go-to-Market）团队开展更紧密协作，为客户精准匹配最合适的交付团队

- Participate in recurring enablement and recertification to stay current on Next.js and Vercel best practices

- 定期参与赋能培训与再认证，持续掌握 Next.js 与 Vercel 的最新最佳实践

This certification complements the work we’re rolling out in the Partner Program and Professional Services, from partner enablement and partner funds to scaling high-quality delivery across the Vercel ecosystem.

此项认证是对我们当前合作伙伴计划（Partner Program）与专业服务（Professional Services）系列举措的重要补充——涵盖合作伙伴赋能、合作伙伴资金支持，以及在整个 Vercel 生态系统中规模化交付高质量解决方案。
