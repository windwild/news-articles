Introducing partner fund, technical excellence track, and celebrating partner success.

推出合作伙伴基金、技术卓越计划，并庆祝合作伙伴成功。

At Vercel, we believe in the power of partnership and collaboration to drive innovation and mutual success. One in two sales and project delivery is done in collaboration with our partners. Last month, [over 35 partners](https://nextjs.org/conf#sponsors-section) sponsored and supported [Next.js Conf](http://nextjs.org/conf)—our annual open-source conference—where over 1,000 people gathered in San Francisco and tens of thousands online from around the world. From championing an open web, [supporting industry alliances](https://machalliance.org/newsroom/mach-alliance-strengthens-position-as-a-technology-leader), to developing joint features that enhance customer and user experiences, we're achieving more together.

在 Vercel，我们坚信合作与协同的力量能够推动创新并实现共赢。目前，半数以上的销售与项目交付均通过与合作伙伴协作完成。上个月，[超过35家合作伙伴](https://nextjs.org/conf#sponsors-section)赞助并支持了我们的年度开源大会——[Next.js Conf](http://nextjs.org/conf)，逾千名参会者齐聚旧金山，另有数万人在线参与，覆盖全球各地。从倡导开放网络、[支持行业联盟](https://machalliance.org/newsroom/mach-alliance-strengthens-position-as-a-technology-leader)，到联合开发提升客户与用户体验的新功能，我们正携手创造更大价值。

## **Empowering partners through collaboration and enablement**

## **通过协作与赋能赋能合作伙伴**

The Vercel Partner Program offers trainings, sales, and marketing opportunities. Today, we're announcing additional partner benefits:

Vercel 合作伙伴计划为合作伙伴提供培训、销售及市场营销机会。今天，我们宣布新增以下合作伙伴权益：

- **Partner Fund:** Our Partner Fund program—traditionally known as Market Development Fund (MDF)—offers partners a way to compound and invest their referral earnings into technical expertise and demand generation initiatives. Led by Vercel experts, these initiatives aim to increase project success rates, enhance your brand visibility, and create demand for your services and solutions.

- **合作伙伴基金（Partner Fund）**：本计划原名为市场发展基金（Market Development Fund, MDF），旨在帮助合作伙伴将推荐返佣进行复利式再投资，用于提升技术能力与市场需求培育。该计划由 Vercel 专家主导，致力于提高项目成功率、增强合作伙伴品牌曝光度，并为其服务与解决方案创造市场需求。

- **Partner enablement**: Our Partner Learning Portal boosts your team's understanding of the Frontend Cloud ecosystem and co-selling with Vercel. It offers battle cards, sales decks, and technical guides. Additionally, partners are invited to monthly Technical Excellence sessions. These sessions—led by Vercel's Partner Solutions Engineering team—cover crucial product updates and discussions.

- **合作伙伴赋能（Partner Enablement）**：我们的合作伙伴学习门户（Partner Learning Portal）可帮助您的团队深入理解前端云（Frontend Cloud）生态体系，并掌握与 Vercel 联合销售（co-selling）的关键能力。门户内提供销售战卡（battle cards）、销售演示文稿（sales decks）及技术指南等资源。此外，合作伙伴还可受邀参加每月一次的“技术卓越”系列研讨会（Technical Excellence sessions）。该系列研讨会由 Vercel 合作伙伴解决方案工程团队（Partner Solutions Engineering team）主持，聚焦核心产品更新与深度技术探讨。

- **Partner tiers with dedicated GTM support:** We understand each partner is at a different stage with unique needs. With the new partner tiers, engaged partners will have the opportunity to plan ahead on GTM and Product initiatives, working directly with their dedicated Partner Managers. Meanwhile, new partners get an excellent onboarding experience to kick off our partnership.

- **分层级合作伙伴体系与专属上市支持（GTM Support）**：我们深知每位合作伙伴所处发展阶段不同、需求各异。依托全新设立的合作伙伴层级体系，活跃合作伙伴将能与专属合作伙伴经理（Partner Manager）紧密协作，提前规划上市策略（GTM）与产品相关举措；而新加入的合作伙伴则将获得优质的入职体验（onboarding experience），为双方合作顺利启航奠定坚实基础。

## Celebrating solution partner and customer success

## 庆祝解决方案合作伙伴与客户成功

Solution partners have been instrumental in helping customers achieve ambitious digital innovation and business goals.

解决方案合作伙伴在助力客户实现宏大的数字化创新与业务目标方面发挥了关键作用。

- [**Work & Co**](https://vercel.com/experts/work-and-co)—隶属于埃森哲歌（Accenture Song）—主导了[**BNP 巴黎银行公开赛**](https://vercel.com/blog/bnp-paribas-open-serving-up-scores-and-experiences-in-real-time-with-work)全新数字体验的战略规划与交付工作。仅用数月时间，Work & Co 即上线了全新的网站与移动应用体验，为全球数百万球迷打造了卓越的赛事观赛体验。

- [**Code and Theory**](https://vercel.com/experts/code-and-theory) 实现了逾 200 个新闻编辑部的数字化转型。凭借其在 Next.js 与 Vercel 方面的专业能力，Code & Theory 主导了[**明尼苏达星报（Minnesota Star Tribune）**](https://vercel.com/blog/break-the-news-not-the-site)基础设施的升级工作，为 2024 年美国大选做好充分准备；最终实现选举季平稳运行，并显著提升转化率，核心网页指标（Core Web Vitals）稳定在 80–90 分区间。

- [**Americaneagle.com**](https://vercel.com/experts/americaneagle-com) 作为 Vercel 的长期合作伙伴，助力[**Mecum 拍卖公司**](https://vercel.com/blog/managing-275-thousand-pages-and-8-million-assets-with-isr)全面重塑其数字形象。双方共同构建了一套高效协作工作流，使开发人员与营销人员均可协同交付业界领先的拍卖体验，支撑年均超 1.2 亿次页面浏览量。

## 与技术合作伙伴携手创新

我们正通过与技术合作伙伴联合开发新功能、打造无缝集成，助力客户更轻松地取得成功。

- [**Optimizely 功能实验模板（Optimizely Feature Experimentation Template）**](https://vercel.com/templates/next.js/optimizely-feature-experimentation)：一款开箱即用的 Next.js 模板，帮助开发者快速搭建演示型电商 storefront，集成 Vercel 功能开关（Feature Flags）与 Optimizely 功能实验（Feature Experimentation）能力。

- [**Sanity 实时 API + Next.js 15**](http://www.sanity.io/live)：在 [Next.js Conf 2024](https://vercel.com/blog/recap-next-js-conf-2024) 大会上，Sanity [正式发布其实时 API（Live API）](https://nextjs.org/conf/session/live-by-default)，支持无需刷新页面即可实现内容的即时、实时更新。结合 Next.js App Router 与 Vercel 数据缓存（Data Cache），您的应用现在可向所有用户实时推送内容更新，真正赋能动态化 Web 体验。

- [**Contentful 通过 Vercel 内容链接（Content Link）获得原生支持**](https://vercel.com/changelog/content-link-can-now-be-used-with-contentful)：该功能使用户能够将 Vercel 部署与其 CMS 紧密集成，赋予内容编辑者直接在网页上进行实时编辑的能力。用户可通过 [Contentful 应用市场（Contentful Marketplace）](https://www.contentful.com/marketplace/vercel/) 提供的集成插件，便捷连接 Vercel 与 Contentful。

- [**BigCommerce Catalyst**](https://vercel.com/templates/next.js/catalyst-by-bigcommerce): 一款面向可组合式 storefront 的加速器，融合 BigCommerce、Next.js 与 Vercel 的强大能力。采用 Catalyst 的零售商普遍反映，其产品上线周期显著缩短——从原本以“年”为单位大幅压缩至仅需“数月”。

- [**Supabase 借力 Vercel Marketplace 实现增长跃升**](https://vercel.com/blog/how-supabase-increased-signups-through-the-vercel-marketplace)：Vercel Marketplace 上线仅一个月，便为 Vercel 用户提供了无缝集成与快速上手体验，助力其高效采纳新解决方案；这一全新渠道亦为我们的合作伙伴开辟了高转化率的增长通路。
