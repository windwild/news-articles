2022 年 12 月 2 日

![](images/instant-rollback-public-beta-now-available-in-the-cli-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/instant-rollback-public-beta-now-available-in-the-cli-vercel/img_002.jpg)

You can now use [Instant Rollback](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/instant-rollback) on the CLI to quickly revert to a previous production deployment, to prevent regressions with your site’s availability. Now available in Beta for all plans.

您现在可在 CLI 中使用 [Instant Rollback（即时回滚）](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/instant-rollback)，快速回退到此前的生产环境部署，从而避免网站可用性出现回归问题。该功能现已面向所有套餐开放 Beta 测试。

[Check out the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/cli/rollback) to learn more.

[查看文档](https://vercel.com/docs/cli/rollback) 以了解更多信息。
