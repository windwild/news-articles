Sep 9, 2025

2025 年 9 月 9 日

We’re updating Vercel’s Pro plan to better align with how modern teams collaborate, how applications consume infrastructure, and how workloads are evolving with AI. Concretely, we’re making the following changes:

我们正在更新 Vercel 的 Pro 计划，使其更契合现代团队的协作方式、应用对基础设施的使用模式，以及 AI 驱动下工作负载的演进趋势。具体而言，我们将做出以下调整：

- **A flexible spending model:** Instead of discrete included allotments across 20+ infra products, we’re transitioning to a flexible usage balance.

- **灵活的支出模型：** 不再为 20 多种基础设施产品分别设定固定的免费配额，而是转向统一、可灵活使用的余额机制。

- **Free Viewer seats:** Teammates can access previews, analytics, and more without needing to pay for a seat.

- **免费 Viewer 帐户：** 团队成员可免费访问预览环境、分析数据等功能，无需为其单独付费。

- **Self-serve Enterprise features:** SAML SSO, HIPAA BAAs, and more are now available to everyone on the Pro plan. No need to contact sales.

- **自助式企业级功能：** SAML 单点登录（SSO）、HIPAA 商业合作协议（BAAs）等高级功能现已向所有 Pro 计划用户开放，无需联系销售团队。

- **Better Spend Management:** Spend Management is now enabled by default, to provide peace of mind against rare runaway usage.

- **更完善的支出管理：** 支出管理（Spend Management）现默认启用，帮助您安心应对极少数情况下可能出现的异常高用量。

We’ve been iterating on this new Pro plan for a while now, based on feedback from thousands of customers, to better support the way modern teams build.

我们已基于数千名客户的反馈，持续迭代这一新版 Pro 计划，旨在更好地支持现代团队的开发实践。

With this transition, around 7% of teams with diverse usage across Vercel products will likely see their bills go up, the majority of which will see only a modest increase. The remainder of paying teams, over 100,000, will see either a decrease or no change in their bill.

此次调整后，约 7% 的团队（其在 Vercel 各类产品上的使用场景较为多样化）账单可能上涨，其中绝大多数仅会面临小幅增长；其余超过 10 万个付费团队的账单则将下降或保持不变。

Either way, [we’re here to help](https://community.vercel.com/t/updated-pro-plan/18608) with recommendations and optimizations to ensure you’re happy with **the value you get from Vercel for the price you pay**.

无论您的账单如何变化，[我们随时为您提供支持](https://community.vercel.com/t/updated-pro-plan/18608)，包括个性化建议与优化方案，确保您为所支付的价格获得 **与之相匹配的 Vercel 价值**。

### A flexible spending model

### 灵活的支出模型

You shouldn’t have to worry about keeping track of allocations and on-demand rates for 20+ different usage metrics while you’re building.

在开发过程中，您本不应为追踪 20 多种不同用量指标的配额和按需计费而操心。

Pro now uses a simpler credit-based usage model. Each month starts with $20 in flexible credit that can be used across your team’s projects in any configuration. Additionally, the new Pro plan includes $150+ dedicated to Fast Data Transfer, and $20+ for Edge Requests.

Pro 现已采用更简洁的“积分制”用量模型。每月初始即提供 **20 美元灵活积分**，可在您团队的所有项目中按任意方式自由分配使用。此外，新版 Pro 套餐还额外包含 **150 美元以上专属额度用于快速数据传输（Fast Data Transfer）**，以及 **20 美元以上额度用于边缘请求（Edge Requests）**。

### Free Viewer seats

### 免费查看者席位（Free Viewer Seats）

Teams on Vercel come in all shapes and sizes and are made up of both developers and non-developers. One piece of recurring feedback we’ve heard is that you want to be able to collaborate with teammates, friends, and clients who do not deploy code, and that it feels unfair to have to pay for seats for them.

Vercel 上的团队形态各异、规模不一，成员既包括开发者，也包括非技术人员。我们反复收到的一项反馈是：您希望与那些不部署代码的同事、朋友或客户协作，但为他们付费购买席位又显得不太公平。

Pro now offers free Viewer seats for those who need access to your team’s projects, previews, and dashboards, but who do not need to build, deploy, or update settings.

Pro 现已为仅需访问您团队项目、预览环境及仪表板（但无需构建、部署或修改设置）的人员，提供**免费的查看者席位（Viewer seats）**。

### Self-serve Enterprise features

### 自助式企业级功能（Self-Serve Enterprise Features）

We've also heard overwhelming feedback that teams don’t want to have to contact sales or sign a contract in order to access the features they need.

我们还收到大量反馈：团队不希望仅为启用所需功能，就不得不联系销售团队或签署合同。

Pro now offers the ability to purchase features that have historically only been available on our Enterprise plan including SAML SSO and access to HIPAA BAAs. In the coming months, we’ll be making additional features available including PrivateLink, Static IPs, and more.

Pro 现已支持自助购买部分此前仅面向 Enterprise（企业版）客户开放的功能，例如 **SAML 单点登录（SSO）** 和 **HIPAA BAA 协议访问权限**。未来几个月内，我们将陆续上线更多功能，包括 **PrivateLink、静态 IP（Static IPs）** 等。

### Better Spend Management, enabled by default

### 更优的支出管理（默认启用）

You shouldn’t have to monitor your costs every day to avoid surprises, or worry about runaway bills on Vercel, ever.

您本不该为了规避意外支出，而每天紧盯费用；也完全不必担心在 Vercel 上产生失控账单。

Pro now includes improved Spend Management that’s enabled by default for all projects. Two sets of alerts will be configured automatically for all teams:

Pro 计划现已包含升级版的支出管理（Spend Management）功能，并默认为所有项目启用。所有团队将自动配置两组支出提醒：

- 如果您在上一个计费周期内未产生按需用量（on-demand usage），则系统将在您即将产生按需用量时触发提醒。

- 当您的按需支出接近默认限额时，将触发额外提醒；新团队的初始默认限额设为 200 美元。

You can customize your spend limits and optionally configure Spend Management to pause deployments automatically when the limit you set is reached.

您可自定义支出限额，并可选择配置支出管理功能，在达到所设限额时自动暂停部署。

### And more, available now for all teams

### 更多新功能，即日起面向所有团队开放

We’ve also enabled our latest improvements to the platform, like [on-demand concurrent builds](https://vercel.com/changelog/no-build-queues-on-demand-concurrent-builds-now-on-by-default) and our new [transform-based Image Optimization pricing](https://vercel.com/changelog/faster-transformations-and-reduced-pricing-for-image-optimization) by default on the Pro plan.

我们还已在 Pro 计划中默认启用平台最新优化功能，例如 [按需并发构建（on-demand concurrent builds）](https://vercel.com/changelog/no-build-queues-on-demand-concurrent-builds-now-on-by-default)，以及全新的 [基于图像变换（transform-based）的图片优化定价方案](https://vercel.com/changelog/faster-transformations-and-reduced-pricing-for-image-optimization)。

Whether you’re scaling production apps or getting started with your first AI app, these updates are designed to support the way modern teams build. We’ll be rolling them out to all users in the coming months, but you can also opt into them starting today.

无论您正在扩展生产级应用，还是刚刚启动首个 AI 应用，这些更新均旨在支持现代团队的开发方式。我们将在未来数月内逐步向所有用户推出这些功能；但您从今天起即可主动启用。

These changes lay the foundation for a platform that grows with your team, whether you’re just starting out or operating at a global scale.

这些变更奠定了平台演进的基础——无论您处于初创阶段，还是已实现全球化运营，平台都将随您的团队共同成长。
