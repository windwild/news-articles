Nov 11, 2021

2021 年 11 月 11 日

Today, we're excited to share [Rich Harris](https://twitter.com/rich_harris), the creator of Svelte, has joined Vercel to help us in our mission to make the Web. Faster.

今天，我们非常高兴地宣布：Svelte 的创始人 [Rich Harris](https://twitter.com/rich_harris) 已加入 Vercel，助力我们共同践行“让 Web 更快”的使命。

Both [Svelte](https://svelte.dev/) and Vercel want to make building for the Web both enjoyable _and_ fast. Svelte has been voted the [most loved Web framework](https://insights.stackoverflow.com/survey/2021#section-most-loved-dreaded-and-wanted-web-frameworks) with the [most satisfied developers](https://2020.stateofjs.com/en-US/technologies/front-end-frameworks/) and has a thriving community. Its usage has doubled in the past year, with over 200,000 weekly downloads and over 50,000 stars on GitHub.

[Svelte](https://svelte.dev/) 与 Vercel 的愿景高度一致——让 Web 开发既愉悦又高效。Svelte 连续多年被评选为 [最受开发者喜爱的 Web 框架](https://insights.stackoverflow.com/survey/2021#section-most-loved-dreaded-and-wanted-web-frameworks)，并拥有 [满意度最高的开发者群体](https://2020.stateofjs.com/en-US/technologies/front-end-frameworks/)，社区活跃而蓬勃。过去一年中，Svelte 的使用量翻倍增长，每周下载量超 20 万次，GitHub 星标数逾 5 万。

Joining Vercel enables Rich to work on Svelte full-time, giving the project its first dedicated contributor. The governance of Svelte does not and will not change – it's still the same independent, open-source project and community. With Vercel's backing, Svelte can get even more ambitious.

加入 Vercel 使 Rich 能够全职投入 Svelte 的开发工作，这也是该项目迎来的首位专职贡献者。Svelte 的治理模式不会发生任何改变——它仍将是那个独立、开源的项目，拥有同样开放、自主的社区。在 Vercel 的支持下，Svelte 将能迈向更宏大的目标。

The future of Svelte is [Edge-first](https://vercel.com/features/edge-functions). By taking the best parts of traditional apps, combined with the best parts of single-page apps, we can build " [transitional apps](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=860d8usGC0o)" that deliver a delightful end-user experience _and_ a great developer experience.

Svelte 的未来是“以边缘计算为先”（[Edge-first](https://vercel.com/features/edge-functions)）。通过融合传统应用与单页应用（SPA）各自的最佳特性，我们得以构建“[过渡型应用](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=860d8usGC0o)”，既为终端用户提供愉悦的体验，也为开发者带来卓越的开发体验。

Vercel is an open platform, committed to developing and supporting tools that are a joy to use and enable developers to build incredible experiences for end-users. By sponsoring frameworks like Svelte, Nuxt, Astro, and more – as well as community-built libraries we believe in and use – we're helping create a better Web ecosystem.

Vercel 是一个开放平台，致力于开发与支持那些令人愉悦、易用且能赋能开发者为终端用户打造非凡体验的工具。通过赞助 Svelte、Nuxt、Astro 等框架，以及我们认可并实际采用的各类社区共建库，我们正携手共建更健康、更繁荣的 Web 生态系统。

We're excited about the future of Svelte and creating templates for other frameworks in the open ecosystem to follow. See why developers love Svelte and try [Svelte on Vercel](https://vercel.com/new/clone?s=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fvercel%2Fvercel%2Ftree%2Fmain%2Fexamples%2Fsveltekit&template=sveltekit&id=67753070&b=main&from=templates) today. We look forward to building the future of Svelte with you.

我们对 Svelte 的未来充满期待，并将持续为开放生态中的其他框架创建可复用的模板，供业界参考与借鉴。立即了解开发者为何钟爱 Svelte，并马上试用 [Svelte on Vercel](https://vercel.com/new/clone?s=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fvercel%2Fvercel%2Ftree%2Fmain%2Fexamples%2Fsveltekit&template=sveltekit&id=67753070&b=main&from=templates)。我们期待与您携手，共同塑造 Svelte 的未来。
