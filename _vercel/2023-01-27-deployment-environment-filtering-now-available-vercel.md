2023 年 1 月 27 日

![](images/deployment-environment-filtering-now-available-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/deployment-environment-filtering-now-available-vercel/img_002.jpg)

You can now filter your Deployments on Vercel by their environment type in addition to [filtering by branches](https://vercel.com/changelog/deployment-filters-for-project-dashboard), making it easier to navigate to your production and/or preview deployments.

现在，您除了可以按分支筛选 Vercel 上的部署（Deployments）外，还可按环境类型（environment type）进行筛选，从而更便捷地定位您的生产环境（production）和/或预览环境（preview）部署。

[Check out the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/managing-deployments#filter-deployments) to learn more.

[查看相关文档](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/managing-deployments#filter-deployments)，了解更多信息。
