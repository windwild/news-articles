With Instant Rollback you can quickly revert to a previous production deployment, making it easier to fix breaking changes. Now available in Beta for everyone.

借助“即时回滚（Instant Rollback）”，您可以快速回退到之前的生产环境部署，从而更轻松地修复造成故障的变更。该功能现已面向所有用户开放公测（Beta）。

[Check out the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/instant-rollback) to learn more.
