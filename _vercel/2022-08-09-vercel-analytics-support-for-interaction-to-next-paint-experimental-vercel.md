2022 年 8 月 9 日

![](images/vercel-analytics-support-for-interaction-to-next-paint-experimental-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/vercel-analytics-support-for-interaction-to-next-paint-experimental-vercel/img_002.jpg)

Vercel Analytics now supports measuring [Interaction to Next Paint (INP)](https://web.dev/inp/).

Vercel Analytics 现已支持测量 [交互到下一次绘制（Interaction to Next Paint，INP）](https://web.dev/inp/)。

INP measures your site’s responsiveness to user interactions on the page. The faster your page responds to user input – the better. INP is an experimental metric to develop a better way of measuring responsiveness than [First Input Delay (FID)](https://vercel.com/blog/core-web-vitals#first-input-delay).

INP 用于衡量网站对页面上用户交互行为的响应速度——页面响应用户输入越快，体验越好。INP 是一项实验性指标，旨在提供一种比 [首次输入延迟（First Input Delay，FID）](https://vercel.com/blog/core-web-vitals#first-input-delay) 更优的响应性衡量方式。

[Try Vercel Analytics today](https://vercel.com/analytics) to start measuring your performance.

[立即试用 Vercel Analytics](https://vercel.com/analytics)，开始监测您的网站性能。
