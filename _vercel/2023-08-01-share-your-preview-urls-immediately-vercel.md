Aug 1, 2023

2023 年 8 月 1 日

Preview URLs are now shareable at the beginning of the build process, instead of after the build finishes.

预览 URL 现在可在构建过程**开始时**即被分享，而不再需要等到构建完成之后。

The [Preview URL](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/preview-deployments#preview-urls) in the video above is surfaced both in the dashboard and in the Vercel Bot comments attached to the pull request. Customers on all plans will now see this functionality automatically on all new builds.

上方视频中提到的 [Preview URL](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/preview-deployments#preview-urls) 将同时显示在 Vercel 控制台（Dashboard）以及关联至 Pull Request 的 Vercel Bot 评论中。所有套餐（包括免费版）的客户，现在均可在所有新构建中自动使用此功能。

[Check out the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/preview-deployments#preview-urls) to learn more.

[查阅文档](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/preview-deployments#preview-urls) 了解更多信息。
