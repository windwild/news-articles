Dec 15, 2022

2022 年 12 月 15 日

![](images/edge-functions-are-now-generally-available-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/edge-functions-are-now-generally-available-vercel/img_002.jpg)

During the private beta, we’ve made Edge Functions **faster**, more **flexible**, and **more compatible** with Node.js:

在私有测试阶段，我们让 Edge Functions 变得**更快**、**更灵活**，且与 Node.js 的**兼容性更强**：

- Major infrastructure optimizations for improved performance  
- 针对性能提升的重大基础设施优化  

- Support for `cache-control` for function responses  
- 支持为函数响应设置 `cache-control`  

- Added ability to express [region preference](https://vercel.com/blog/edge-functions-generally-available#regional-edge-functions)  
- 新增了指定[区域偏好](https://vercel.com/blog/edge-functions-generally-available#regional-edge-functions)的能力

- Added support for

- 新增支持

[WebAssembly workloads](https://vercel.com/blog/edge-functions-generally-available#support-for-webassembly)

[WebAssembly 工作负载](https://vercel.com/blog/edge-functions-generally-available#support-for-webassembly)

- [Increased function size limits](https://vercel.com/blog/edge-functions-generally-available#increased-workload-size-and-improved-infrastructure)

- [提升函数大小限制](https://vercel.com/blog/edge-functions-generally-available#increased-workload-size-and-improved-infrastructure)

for Enterprise and Pro teams

面向 Enterprise（企业）和 Pro（专业）团队

We’re excited to announce that beginning today, Edge Functions are now generally available so you can start executing more efficiently with instant “cold starts” to your functions for a better end-user experience.

我们很高兴地宣布，即日起 Edge Functions 正式全面开放（Generally Available），您现在即可开始更高效地执行函数——函数启动将实现即时“冷启动”，从而为终端用户提供更佳体验。

[Check out the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/functions/edge-functions) to learn more.

[查阅文档](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/functions/edge-functions) 以了解更多信息。
