2023 年 1 月 27 日

![](images/create-and-view-support-cases-on-the-vercel-dashboard-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/create-and-view-support-cases-on-the-vercel-dashboard-vercel/img_002.jpg)

Enterprise customers can now submit support cases using the Vercel Support Center. The Support Center allows you to create and view all support cases, their statuses, and any messages from our Customer Success team in your dashboard. All cases are securely stored to safeguard your data.

企业客户现在可通过 Vercel 支持中心（Vercel Support Center）提交支持工单。支持中心允许您在控制台中创建和查看所有支持工单、其当前状态，以及客户成功团队（Customer Success team）发送的任何消息。所有工单均经过安全存储，以保障您的数据安全。

Check out the documentation on [Support Center](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/dashboard-features/support-center) to learn more.

请查阅 [支持中心](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/dashboard-features/support-center) 文档以了解更多信息。
