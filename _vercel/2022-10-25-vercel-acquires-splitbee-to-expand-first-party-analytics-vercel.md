Oct 25, 2022

2022 年 10 月 25 日

The future of web analytics is real-time and privacy-first. Today, we're excited to announce our acquisition of [Splitbee](https://splitbee.io/)—bringing more analytics capabilities to all Vercel customers.

网络分析的未来是实时化与隐私优先。今天，我们很高兴地宣布收购 [Splitbee](https://splitbee.io/) —— 为所有 Vercel 用户带来更多分析能力。

Along with the acquisition of Splitbee, we're adding top pages, top referring sites, and demographics to [Vercel Analytics](https://vercel.com/analytics)—available now. With Analytics, you can go beyond performance tracking and experience the same journey as your users with powerful insights tied to real metrics.

在收购 Splitbee 的同时，我们已将“热门页面”、“主要引荐网站”和“用户人口统计信息”等功能添加至 [Vercel Analytics](https://vercel.com/analytics) —— 即日起即可使用。借助 Analytics，您不仅能追踪性能指标，更能通过与真实数据深度绑定的有力洞察，亲身体验用户所经历的完整旅程。

## First-party analytics for the Web

## 面向 Web 的第一方分析工具

Splitbee is an all-in-one analytics and conversion tool that helps you understand the user's experience on your site so that you can take immediate action to optimize it. With features like user-triggered events and simplified A/B testing, you'll gain key insights on user behavior.

Splitbee 是一款集分析与转化功能于一体的工具，助您深入理解用户在您网站上的体验，从而即时采取行动进行优化。借助用户触发事件、简化的 A/B 测试等功能，您将获得关于用户行为的关键洞察。

Founders of Splitbee, Tobias and Timo Lins, will join Vercel to help bring their analytics tools to Vercel users.

Splitbee 的联合创始人 Tobias 和 Timo Lins 将加入 Vercel 团队，协助将他们的分析工具带给 Vercel 用户。

### **Privacy-friendly**

### **注重隐私保护**

Data is a foundational part of a great user experience. However, gathering user data often comes at the expense of additional third-party scripts and cookies, ultimately degrading the customer experience and site performance we're trying to improve. We want to rid the Web of unnecessary consent banners and move towards a privacy-first model with Analytics—GDPR-compliant with the addition of page view and traffic metrics.

数据是打造卓越用户体验的基础要素。然而，收集用户数据往往需要引入额外的第三方脚本和 Cookie，最终反而损害了我们本意要提升的客户体验与网站性能。我们希望借助 Analytics 消除网络中不必要的同意提示横幅，转向以隐私为先的分析模式——在支持页面浏览量与流量指标的同时，确保符合 GDPR 合规要求。

### **Real-time matters**

### **实时性至关重要**

Analytics will track visitor count in real-time and display historical data, so it's simple and accessible to understand trends over time. Teams will see real-time metrics to get insights immediately after shipping an update to their project. Since this data is collected at the edge, **no third-party scripts or cookie tracking** is required.

Analytics 将实时追踪访客数量，并展示历史数据，使团队能够轻松、直观地把握随时间变化的趋势。团队在项目更新上线后，即可立即获得实时指标与洞察。由于所有数据均在边缘（edge）采集，因此**无需任何第三方脚本或 Cookie 追踪技术**。

### **Accessible to everyone**

### **人人皆可使用**

We believe teams ship better work when everyone can contribute to the development process. As we continue to build out its capabilities, Analytics will blend the boundaries of traffic, application, and Web Vitals data, making insights accessible to entire teams and helping organizations adopt data-driven deployment methods.

我们坚信：当开发流程中的每个成员都能参与贡献时，团队才能交付更优质的工作成果。随着 Analytics 功能持续完善，它将打通流量数据、应用数据与 Web Vitals 数据之间的边界，让全团队成员均可便捷获取洞察，并助力组织采用数据驱动的部署方式。

Today, you can [enable](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/analytics) these new insights in your Vercel dashboard for free during the beta. If you’re already using Analytics to track Web Vitals, you can update your project to start seeing page views and visitor events.

目前，在 Beta 测试期间，您可免费在 Vercel 控制台中[启用](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/analytics)这些全新洞察功能。若您已在使用 Analytics 追踪 Web Vitals，则只需更新项目配置，即可开始查看页面浏览量与访客事件数据。

[Start using Vercel Analytics today.](https://vercel.com/analytics)
