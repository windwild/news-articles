Mar 22, 2022

2022年3月22日

![](images/filters-are-persisted-for-vercel-analytics-vercel/img_001.jpg)

You can now share and bookmark a specific filter state of Vercel Analytics, which are now persisted as URL parameters.

现在，您可以分享并收藏 Vercel Analytics 的特定筛选状态，该状态现已作为 URL 参数持久化保存。

Check out the [documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/analytics) as well.

也请查阅[文档](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/analytics)。
