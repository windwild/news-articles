Improved logs are now in public beta for all Enterprise accounts. This improvement allows you to search, inspect, and share your organization's runtime logs, either at a project or team level.

改进版日志现已面向所有企业版账户开放**公开测试（Public Beta）**。此次升级使您能够按项目或团队级别搜索、查看和共享组织的运行时日志。

The new UI consolidates and streamlines error handling and debugging. Enterprise users can now search runtime logs from all your deployments directly from the Vercel dashboard. Vercel will retain log data for 10 days and continue increasing our retention policy throughout the beta period. For longer log storage, you can use [Log Drains](https://vercel.com/integrations#logging).

全新用户界面整合并简化了错误处理与调试流程。企业用户现可直接通过 Vercel 控制台，搜索所有部署的运行时日志。Vercel 将在公开测试期间默认保留日志数据 10 天，并持续延长日志保留期限。如需更长的日志存储时间，您可使用 [Log Drains（日志导出）](https://vercel.com/integrations#logging)。

Read the [documentation](https://www.vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/runtime-logs) to learn more.

请查阅[相关文档](https://www.vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/runtime-logs)，了解更多信息。
