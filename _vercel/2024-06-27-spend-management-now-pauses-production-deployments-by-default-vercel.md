2024 年 6 月 27 日

Based on your feedback, Spend Management now **pauses production deployments by default** when your set amount is reached.

根据您的反馈，Spend Management 现在在达到您设定的消费额度时，**默认自动暂停生产环境部署**。

Spend Management allows you to receive notifications, trigger a webhook, and pause projects when [metered usage](https://vercel.com/docs/limits/overview#additional-resources) exceeds the set amount within the current billing cycle. This stops you incurring further cost from the production deployments.

Spend Management 可在当前计费周期内，当[按量计费用量](https://vercel.com/docs/limits/overview#additional-resources)超出您设定的额度时，向您发送通知、触发 Webhook，并暂停相关项目——从而避免因生产环境部署而产生额外费用。

- You'll receive realtime notifications when your spending approaches and exceeds the set amount. For further control, you can continue to use a webhook in addition to automatic project pausing

- 当您的消费额接近或超出设定额度时，您将收到实时通知。如需更精细的控制，您可在自动暂停项目的同时，继续使用 Webhook 功能。

- This includes **Web** and **Email** notifications at 50%, 75%, and 100%. You can also receive **SMS** notifications when your spending reaches 100%

- 通知方式包括在消费达 **50%、75% 和 100%** 时通过 **网页界面（Web）和电子邮件（Email）** 发送；当消费达 **100%** 时，您还可额外收到 **短信（SMS）** 通知。

- Hobby customers will have their projects automatically paused when reaching the included free tier limits and do not need Spend Management

- Hobby 用户在达到所含免费额度上限时，其项目将被自动暂停，无需启用 Spend Management 功能。

[Check out our documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/accounts/spend-management) to learn more.

[查阅我们的文档](https://vercel.com/docs/accounts/spend-management)，了解更多信息。
