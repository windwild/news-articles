2026年1月13日

It's a thrilling time to work in Sales at Vercel. The web is transitioning from pages to agents, and Vercel is building the self-driving infrastructure to power it. We've assembled a Sales organization that equally understands the continually shifting technical landscape and pressing business needs to stay flexible, move fast, and be secure in the AI era.

在 Vercel 从事销售工作，正逢激动人心的时代。互联网正从静态网页时代迈向智能体（agent）时代，而 Vercel 正在构建驱动这一变革的“自动驾驶式”基础设施。我们已组建起一支销售团队——它既深刻理解持续演进的技术格局，也敏锐把握企业在 AI 时代对敏捷性、安全性和响应速度的迫切需求。

We're rethinking how Sales operates, and we're building the most AI-forward go-to-market organization in the industry. To lead this charge, we're welcoming **Nick Bogaty** as our Chief Revenue Officer.

我们正在重新构想销售的运作方式，并致力于打造业内最具 AI 前瞻性的市场进入（Go-to-Market, GTM）组织。为引领这一变革，我们诚挚欢迎 **Nick Bogaty** 加入 Vercel，担任首席营收官（Chief Revenue Officer, CRO）。

## Built for every stage of growth

## 适配企业全生命周期的增长

Nick brings more than two decades of experience building and scaling enterprise GTM organizations, and he'll lead our global Sales team as we deepen relationships with the world's largest enterprises and fastest growing startups alike.

Nick 拥有逾二十年构建与规模化企业级市场进入（GTM）组织的丰富经验。他将带领 Vercel 全球销售团队，进一步深化与全球顶尖大型企业和增长最快初创公司的合作关系。

Nick has a diverse background. He has built teams from scratch, scaled them through hypergrowth, and run large global organizations. He joins Vercel from Adobe, where he started and scaled the enterprise GTM for Adobe Express, competing with Canva in the enterprise. Before that, he served as CRO at AppDynamics, growing the business from $100M to $700M through its acquisition by Cisco and leading a 1,250-person team spanning sales and post-sales globally. He also served as CRO at Instabug, a Series B startup.

Nick 的职业履历极为多元：他曾从零开始组建团队，带领团队穿越高速增长期，并执掌过大型全球化组织。他此前任职于 Adobe，主导创建并规模化了 Adobe Express 的企业级 GTM 体系，在企业市场中与 Canva 展开直接竞争。更早之前，他曾担任 AppDynamics 首席营收官（CRO），推动公司营收从 1 亿美元增长至 7 亿美元，并最终被思科（Cisco）收购；期间，他领导一支覆盖全球销售及售后职能、规模达 1,250 人的庞大团队。他还曾担任 Series B 阶段初创公司 Instabug 的首席营收官。

Like many at Vercel, Nick is a founder. Nick co-founded Adobe Digital Publishing Suite, the largest homegrown Adobe business since Acrobat at the time. He also developed the EPUB standard for electronic books while serving as Executive Director of the International Digital Publishing Forum (IDPF), a format still used today by Apple and Amazon. He even holds a patent for an electronic book distribution system.

与 Vercel 许多同事一样，Nick 也是一位连续创业者。他曾联合创立 Adobe Digital Publishing Suite（Adobe 数字出版套件）——这是自 Acrobat 以来 Adobe 历史上首个完全自主孵化、规模最大的内部业务。在担任国际数字出版论坛（International Digital Publishing Forum, IDPF）执行董事期间，他还主导制定了电子书通用格式 EPUB 标准；该标准至今仍被 Apple 和 Amazon 广泛采用。此外，他本人还拥有一项电子书分发系统的发明专利。

## Rethinking the CRO role

## 重新思考首席营收官（CRO）的角色

Every day, our GTM Engineering team applies AI to go-to-market challenges, dogfooding the Vercel AI Cloud to build internal agents that give our sales and marketing teams an edge. (We've open-sourced some of this work, including a [Lead Agent template](https://vercel.com/templates/ai/lead-processing-agent).)

每天，我们的 GTM 工程团队都将人工智能应用于市场落地（Go-to-Market）挑战中，通过“以己产品为先”（dogfooding）的方式使用 Vercel AI Cloud，构建内部智能体（agents），从而为销售与营销团队赋予竞争优势。（我们已将部分工作开源，包括一个 [潜在客户处理智能体模板（Lead Agent template）](https://vercel.com/templates/ai/lead-processing-agent)。）

Nick brings a clear point of view on how enterprise sales is evolving, and I knew early that he was a strong fit for an organization using our own products to transform how we engage with customers.

尼克对大型企业销售模式的演进有着清晰而独到的见解；早在初期，我就确信他非常契合这样一家组织——即正依托自身产品重塑客户互动方式的公司。
