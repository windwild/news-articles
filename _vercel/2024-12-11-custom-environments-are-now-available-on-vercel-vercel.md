Dec 11, 2024

2024年12月11日

![](images/custom-environments-are-now-available-on-vercel-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/custom-environments-are-now-available-on-vercel-vercel/img_002.jpg)

[Custom Environments](https://vercel.com/docs/deployments/custom-environments) are now available on Vercel.

[自定义环境](https://vercel.com/docs/deployments/custom-environments) 现已在 Vercel 上线。

With this feature, you can define an additional pre-production environment, such as staging or QA, directly within the Vercel dashboard, without relying on external workarounds or multiple projects.

借助此功能，您可直接在 Vercel 控制台中定义一个额外的预发布环境（例如 Staging 或 QA 环境），无需依赖外部变通方案或创建多个项目。

This functionality allows you to reshape your release workflow by separating it from code management. Environments can now operate independently of branches, offering greater flexibility for specific organizational workflows, targeted deployments, and managing multiple development environments across teams.

该功能支持您将发布流程与代码管理解耦，从而重构发布工作流。现在，环境可独立于分支运行，为组织特定的工作流、定向部署以及跨团队管理多个开发环境提供了更高灵活性。

Customers on the Pro plan have the ability to configure one Custom Environment and customers on the Enterprise plan can configure up to 12 from the dashboard.

Pro 计划用户可在控制台中配置 1 个自定义环境；Enterprise 计划用户最多可配置 12 个。

[Learn more about Custom Environments](https://vercel.com/docs/deployments/custom-environments).

[了解更多关于自定义环境的信息](https://vercel.com/docs/deployments/custom-environments)。
