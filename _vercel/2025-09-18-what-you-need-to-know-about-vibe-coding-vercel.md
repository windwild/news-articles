Sep 18, 2025

2025 年 9 月 18 日

Vibe coding is revolutionizing how we work. Here's what you need to know from our State of Vibe Coding report.

“氛围编程”（vibe coding）正在彻底改变我们的工作方式。以下内容摘自我们最新发布的《氛围编程现状报告》（State of Vibe Coding），为您提炼关键要点。

In February 2025, Andrej Karpathy [introduced](https://x.com/karpathy/status/1886192184808149383?lang=en) the term _vibe coding:_ a new way of coding with AI, “\[where\] you fully give in to the vibes, embrace exponentials, and forget that the code even exists.”

2025 年 2 月，Andrej Karpathy 在社交媒体上[首次提出](https://x.com/karpathy/status/1886192184808149383?lang=en)“_vibe coding_”（氛围编程）这一概念：这是一种借助 AI 编程的全新范式——“\[你\]彻底沉浸于‘氛围’之中，欣然拥抱指数级增长，并完全忘却代码本身的存在。”

Just months later, vibe coding is completely reshaping how developers and non-developers work. [Over 90%](https://github.blog/news-insights/research/survey-reveals-ais-impact-on-the-developer-experience/) of U.S. developers use AI coding tools, adoption is accelerating for other roles, and English has become the fastest growing programming language in the world.

短短数月之后，“氛围编程”已全面重塑开发者与非开发者的工作方式。[超过 90%](https://github.blog/news-insights/research/survey-reveals-ais-impact-on-the-developer-experience/) 的美国开发者正在使用 AI 编程工具；该趋势正加速向其他岗位蔓延；而英语，也已成为全球增长最快的“编程语言”。

We explore this shift in detail in our new [State of Vibe Coding](https://v0.app/vibecoding). Here are a few of the key takeaways.

我们在全新的《[氛围编程现状报告](https://v0.app/vibecoding)》中深入剖析了这一转变。以下是几项核心发现。

## No-code to vibe code

## 从无代码到氛围编程

Fifteen years ago, no-code tools let people build by dragging and dropping components. This marked the start of “WYSIWYG” (what you see is what you get)  software tools, where users could edit content in a visual interface that mirrored their final product.

十五年前，无代码（no-code）工具让人们通过拖放组件即可构建应用，由此开启了“所见即所得”（WYSIWYG，What You See Is What You Get）软件工具时代——用户可在视觉化界面中直接编辑内容，界面所呈现的效果即为其最终成果。

Now, vibe coding tools like v0, Lovable, or [Replit](https://v0.app/docs/compare/v0-vs-replit) make the process even simpler with “WYSIWYG 2.0”: what you _say_ is what you _get_. Anyone can generate apps, websites, or workflows instantly. No experience required. It’s transforming how individuals and teams work in organizations of all sizes, shifting how projects are staffed and how individuals contribute.

如今，v0、Lovable 或 [Replit](https://v0.app/docs/compare/v0-vs-replit) 等“氛围编程”工具进一步简化了这一过程，实现了“WYSIWYG 2.0”——即“你说即所得”（What You *Say* Is What You *Get*）。任何人都能即时生成应用程序、网站或工作流，无需任何技术经验。它正在重塑各类规模组织中个人与团队的工作方式，深刻影响着项目人员配置模式及个体贡献形式。

## Vibe coding’s key benefits

## 氛围编程的核心优势

Large companies have now been more public with their adoption of vibe coding. Amazon launched [Kiro](https://kiro.dev/blog/introducing-kiro/), its own vibe coding tool, and [more than 30%](https://abc.xyz/assets/66/ae/c94682fc4137b5fb90a5d709ac4b/2025-q1-earnings-transcript.pdf) of Google’s new code is AI-generated. But the impact isn’t limited to tech giants.

大型企业如今已更公开地采用“氛围编程”（vibe coding）。亚马逊推出了其自研的氛围编程工具 [Kiro](https://kiro.dev/blog/introducing-kiro/)，而谷歌新编写的代码中，[超过 30%](https://abc.xyz/assets/66/ae/c94682fc4137b5fb90a5d709ac4b/2025-q1-earnings-transcript.pdf) 由 AI 生成。但这一影响并不仅限于科技巨头。

Teams of any size can now complete projects in days instead of months. In fact, in March of 2025, [Garry Tan said](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/03/15/y-combinator-startups-are-fastest-growing-in-fund-history-because-of-ai.html) AI-powered teams of 10 people now do what once took 100 engineers. Vibe coding also shifts the unit of productivity from the team to the individual. It only takes one person to create a prototype or build a product.

无论团队规模大小，如今均可在数天内完成过去需耗时数月的项目。事实上，2025 年 3 月，[Garry Tan 表示](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/03/15/y-combinator-startups-are-fastest-growing-in-fund-history-because-of-ai.html)，一支仅 10 人的 AI 赋能团队，如今已能完成过去需 100 名工程师才能完成的工作。氛围编程还将生产力的基本单位，从“团队”转向了“个人”——仅需一人，即可构建原型或打造完整产品。

Faster iteration, cost savings, and fewer gaps between idea and execution are within clear reach. The only real barrier to entry is being able to clearly describe your idea.

更快的迭代周期、成本节约，以及构想与落地之间更短的差距，均已清晰可及。真正唯一的入门门槛，是能够清晰地描述你的想法。

## Vibe coding is for everyone

## 氛围编程面向所有人

Vibe coding has redefined who can code, and what they’re able to create, reshaping what being a developer truly means. Traditional developers rely on programming languages, a deep understanding of code, and years of experience. Vibe coders only need English, a basic understanding of how technology works, and an idea.

氛围编程重新定义了“谁可以写代码”以及“他们能创造什么”，彻底重塑了“开发者”的真实内涵。传统开发者依赖编程语言、对代码的深入理解以及多年实践经验；而氛围编程者仅需掌握英语、具备基础的技术运作常识，并拥有一个想法。

In fact, most vibe coders aren’t developers at all. About 63% of users exploring vibe tools are non-developers, using tools like v0 or Cursor to streamline tasks and create custom solutions to their problems.

事实上，大多数氛围编程者根本不是开发者。约 63% 探索氛围编程工具的用户为非开发者，他们借助 v0 或 Cursor 等工具优化日常任务，并为其具体问题定制解决方案。

This accessibility opens new opportunities but also raises new risks, because many users don’t know how to secure what they’re creating.

这种低门槛带来了全新机遇，同时也催生了新的风险——因为许多用户并不了解如何保障自己所创建内容的安全性。

## Building the guardrails of vibe coding

## 构建氛围编程的防护机制

As more people adopt vibe coding, security has to be built into the tools themselves. Since not every user can be trained to manage these, the responsibility falls to the platforms.

随着越来越多的人采用“氛围编程”（vibe coding），安全性必须内置于工具本身之中。由于并非每位用户都能接受相关培训以妥善管理安全风险，这一责任自然落在了平台方身上。

Vibe coding tools must be created to handle the security considerations that users can’t be expected to master. This is especially necessary given the sheer volume of people putting their trust (and sensitive data) into these products.

“氛围编程”工具必须被设计为能够主动应对那些用户无法、也不应被要求掌握的安全问题。尤其在大量用户正将信任乃至敏感数据托付给这些产品的情况下，这一点尤为关键。

To us, that looks like automated intervention systems that scan for and block vulnerabilities in real-time, or kill-switch capabilities to protect users from insecure deployments in real-time.

在我们看来，这体现为：能实时扫描并拦截漏洞的自动化干预系统，或具备“紧急关停”（kill-switch）能力——在检测到不安全部署时，即时切断风险，保护用户。

The most successful vibe coding platforms will be the ones building security directly into their interfaces.

最成功的“氛围编程”平台，将是那些将安全性直接融入其用户界面与交互流程之中的平台。

## The future of vibe coding

## “氛围编程”的未来

Interest in vibe coding is only growing.

人们对“氛围编程”的兴趣持续升温。

Businesses can either adopt vibe coding and create leaner teams that can build faster, more efficiently, and without traditional experience. Or, they can ignore vibe coding, sticking to traditional coding for complex, important projects and only using AI where security and safety aren’t at risk.

企业面临两种选择：其一，采纳“氛围编程”，组建更精简的团队，实现更快、更高效、且无需传统开发经验的构建；其二，忽视“氛围编程”，对复杂而关键的项目坚持使用传统编码方式，仅在安全与可靠性无虞的场景中有限引入AI。

无论选择哪条路径，这场变革已然开启。请查阅我们的[《“氛围编程”现状全景报告》全文](https://v0.app/vibecoding)，确保您已做好准备。
