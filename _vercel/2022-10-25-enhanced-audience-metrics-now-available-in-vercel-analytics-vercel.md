With the [acquisition of Splitbee](https://vercel.com/blog/vercel-acquires-splitbee), Vercel Analytics now has privacy-friendly, first-party audience analytics.

随着 [收购 Splitbee](https://vercel.com/blog/vercel-acquires-splitbee)，Vercel Analytics 现已支持注重隐私保护、基于第一方数据的受众分析功能。

Measure page views and understand your audience breakdown, including referrers and demographics—available now in Beta.

可统计页面浏览量，并深入洞察您的受众构成，包括流量来源（referrers）与人口统计学特征（demographics）——该功能现已开放 Beta 测试。

Check out the [documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/analytics/audiences) to get started.

请查阅 [文档](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/analytics/audiences) 开始使用。
