Oct 2, 2023

2023 年 10 月 2 日

![](images/command-menu-now-available-in-deployments-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/command-menu-now-available-in-deployments-vercel/img_002.jpg)

You can now use `⌘K` (or `Ctrl+K` on Windows) to open the Command Menu on any deployment where the Vercel Toolbar is enabled, including [production and localhost](https://vercel.com/changelog/vercel-toolbar-now-available-to-use-collaboration-features-in-production).

现在，您可在任何已启用 Vercel 工具栏（Vercel Toolbar）的部署页面上使用 `⌘K`（Windows 系统为 `Ctrl+K`）快捷键打开命令菜单（Command Menu），包括[生产环境和本地开发环境（localhost）](https://vercel.com/changelog/vercel-toolbar-now-available-to-use-collaboration-features-in-production)。

You can use `Cmd + Shift + K` if you're viewing a deployment of a website that has its own `⌘K` menu.

若您正在查看一个自身已实现 `⌘K` 菜单的网站的部署页面，则可改用 `Cmd + Shift + K` 快捷键。

Users can now navigate between a deployment and other Vercel pages relevant to the project directly through the menu.

用户现可通过该菜单直接在当前部署页面与该项目相关的其他 Vercel 页面之间进行导航。

[Check out our documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/deployments/preview-deployments#cmd+k-navigation) to learn more.

[查阅我们的文档](https://vercel.com/docs/deployments/preview-deployments#cmd+k-navigation) 以了解更多信息。
