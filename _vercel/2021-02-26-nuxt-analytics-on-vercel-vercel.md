2021年2月26日

Monitor your page speeds over time with reporting from real user experiences.

通过真实用户访问体验生成的性能报告，持续监测网页加载速度。

Since the last Next.js Conf, we have expanded Vercel analytics offerings to include [Next.js](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics#next.js-(v10+)) and [Gatsby](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics#gatsby-(v2+)). Today, we expand that offering to include **Nuxt analytics**, providing developers with their [Real Experience Score](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics#real-experience-score) through data from actual visitors.

自上届 Next.js Conf 以来，Vercel 的分析服务已扩展支持 [Next.js](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics#next.js-(v10+)) 和 [Gatsby](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics#gatsby-(v2+))。今天，我们进一步将该服务拓展至支持 **Nuxt 分析（Nuxt analytics）**，使开发者能够基于真实访客数据获取其 [真实体验评分（Real Experience Score）](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics#real-experience-score)。

## Get Started

## 快速开始

Nuxt analytics for projects deployed to Vercel are available with zero configuration:

部署至 Vercel 的 Nuxt 项目可开箱即用地使用 Nuxt 分析功能，无需任何配置：

1. **Open a Nuxt project in your** [**Vercel dashboard**](https://vercel.com/dashboard?analytics=1&utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics)

1. **在您的** [**Vercel 控制台**](https://vercel.com/dashboard?analytics=1&utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics) **中打开一个 Nuxt 项目**

2. **Select the "Analytics" tab and follow the flow**

2. **点击“Analytics（分析）”标签页，并按引导操作**

No code changes are required ( [docs](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics)), and [options for self-hosted applications](https://vercel.com/contact/sales?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics) are available on the [Enterprise plan](https://vercel.com/contact/sales).

无需修改任何代码（[文档](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics)），且 [自托管应用的相关方案](https://vercel.com/contact/sales?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics) 可通过 [企业版（Enterprise plan）](https://vercel.com/contact/sales) 获取。

Once deployed, your application will automatically report [Core Web Vitals](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics#web-vitals?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics) to Vercel.

部署完成后，您的应用将自动向 Vercel 上报 [核心网页指标（Core Web Vitals）](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics#web-vitals?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics)。

![An example of the Analytics tab on the project view.](images/nuxt-analytics-on-vercel-vercel/img_001.jpg)  
项目视图中“Analytics（分析）”标签页的示例界面。

Next.js, Gatsby, and Nuxt are the first three [frameworks supported](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics#supported-frameworks?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics).  
Next.js、Gatsby 和 Nuxt 是首批[受支持的框架](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics#supported-frameworks?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics)。

[Let us know on Twitter](https://twitter.com/vercel) which frameworks you'd like to see next.  
欢迎在 [Twitter 上告诉我们](https://twitter.com/vercel)，您希望后续支持哪些框架。

Next.js, Gatsby, and Nuxt analytics are new essentials for ensuring your sites continue to perform beyond initial launch testing.  
Next.js、Gatsby 和 Nuxt 的分析功能，是保障网站在初始上线测试之后仍能持续保持优异性能的新利器。

Not yet on Vercel? [Import your Nuxt project today](https://vercel.com/import/git?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics).  
尚未使用 Vercel？[立即导入您的 Nuxt 项目](https://vercel.com/import/git?utm_source=vercel&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nuxtanalytics)。
