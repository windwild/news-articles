Mar 6, 2023

2023年3月6日

![](images/activity-date-filtering-now-available-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/activity-date-filtering-now-available-vercel/img_002.jpg)

You are now able to filter the Activity of your team on Vercel based on a specific date. This makes it easier to find the actions your team has taken.

您现在可以根据特定日期筛选团队在 Vercel 上的活动记录，从而更便捷地查找团队执行的操作。

[Check out the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/observability/activity-log) to learn more.

[查看文档](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/observability/activity-log) 了解更多信息。
