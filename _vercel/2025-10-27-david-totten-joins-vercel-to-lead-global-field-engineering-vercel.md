Oct 27, 2025

2025年10月27日

The next era of enterprise technology will be defined by shortening the distance between idea and impact. At Vercel we help companies move faster, turning velocity into measurable business outcomes by anticipating customer needs and owning results end-to-end.

企业技术的下一个时代，将由“从创意到实际影响”之间距离的不断缩短所定义。在 Vercel，我们助力企业加速前行——通过前瞻性地洞察客户需求，并端到端地承担成果责任，将执行速度切实转化为可衡量的业务成果。

To help us lead in this new era, **David Totten** is joining Vercel as VP of Global Field Engineering. David brings two decades of experience building and scaling technical teams, and he'll lead a unified organization that includes Sales Engineering, Developer Success, Professional Services, and Customer Support Engineering.

为引领这一新时代，**David Totten** 正式加入 Vercel，担任全球现场工程副总裁（VP of Global Field Engineering）。David 拥有二十年构建与规模化技术团队的深厚经验，他将领导一支高度整合的组织，涵盖销售工程（Sales Engineering）、开发者成功（Developer Success）、专业服务（Professional Services）以及客户支持工程（Customer Support Engineering）。

## Owning the complete technical journey

## 全程主导技术旅程

This organizational change reflects a broader philosophy at Vercel: we build go-to-market like a product. Just as we obsess over developer experience in our platform, we're equally committed to crafting a world-class experience for every customer interaction.

此次组织架构调整，体现了 Vercel 更深层的理念：我们将“上市策略”（go-to-market）当作一款产品来打造。正如我们在平台中极致关注开发者体验，我们也同样致力于为每一次客户互动打造世界级体验。

By bringing our customer-facing technical teams together, we're creating a seamless technical journey, whether you're a prospect evaluating Vercel or a five-year customer scaling to hundreds of millions of users.

通过整合所有面向客户的工程技术团队，我们正在为客户构建一条无缝衔接的技术旅程——无论您是初次评估 Vercel 的潜在客户，还是已使用五年、正扩展至数亿用户规模的长期客户。

Too often in our industry, context is lost as customers move from evaluation to onboarding to growth. Sales hands off to post-sales, and relationships restart from scratch.

在当前行业中，客户从评估、上线到规模化增长的过程中，上下文信息常常丢失：销售团队交接给售后团队，客户关系不得不从零开始重建。

We're closing those gaps. David's organization will ensure that every customer has a consistent, contextual relationship with our technical teams from day zero and beyond.

我们正全力弥合这些断点。David 所领导的组织将确保每一位客户——自合作首日起乃至更久——都能与我们的技术团队保持一贯、连贯且富有上下文的协作关系。

## Built for what's next

## 为未来而生

David joins Vercel from Databricks, where he helped define and scale customer engagement during a critical period of growth. As AI evolved rapidly, he worked with organizations to unlock actionable intelligence from their data while building repeatable go-to-market and customer success strategies internally.

> “What drew me to Vercel was seeing a company powering the forefront of AI, powered by AI itself.”  
> “吸引我加入 Vercel 的，是看到一家公司正站在 AI 发展的最前沿——而驱动这家公司的，正是 AI 本身。”

Before Databricks, David spent nearly two decades at a Fortune 5 company helping thousands of organizations modernize their tech stacks and transform their workforces. As a field CTO, he guided customers on how to drive efficiency and leverage emerging cloud solutions.

“是什么吸引我加入 Vercel？是看到一家公司正站在 AI 发展的最前沿——而驱动这家公司的，正是 AI 本身。” David 表示，“因为 Vercel 并不只是在帮助开发者构建 AI 应用；我们正在运用 AI，规模化地交付更优的客户成果。”

我们早已成为自身 AI Cloud 产品的‘首位客户’（customer zero）：

- 我们的智能客服代理处理了超过 75% 的客户案例，即便这些案例技术复杂度极高，从而让我们团队得以专注于高价值的客户互动。

- 我们构建了多个智能代理，将销售开发代表（SDR）的人力投入减少了 90%，同时维持原有转化率并显著提升运营效率：每位潜在客户的平均触达次数从 8 次降至 4 次。

- 我们正大力投入“前移式部署工程”（Forward Deployed Engineering），助力企业将 AI 深度融入其内部运营及面向客户的各类产品中。

> “Using the power and scale of AI to create actionable customer experiences is how every business aspires to lead.”  
> “借助 AI 的强大能力与规模化优势，打造可落地、可衡量的客户体验——这正是每一家企业渴望引领行业的方式。”

依托我们的 GTM Engineering（上市工程）理念，我们正在打造业内最具 AI 前瞻性的技术型销售组织。这为 David 的团队带来了独特优势：他们拥有第一手经验，将 AI 应用于内部运营优化与客户产品升级。因此，当我们协助客户规模化部署 AI 时，所分享的并非纸上谈兵的理论，而是经过实践验证的有效方法。

"I've seen how platforms and data access are accelerating, but also becoming commoditized," David shared. "The real value companies will bring is in how they can serve real-time insights and opportunities to their customers directly in a frictionless, responsive way.”

“我亲眼见证了平台和数据访问能力的加速发展，但同时也正日益走向商品化，”David 分享道，“企业真正能带来的价值，在于如何以无缝、敏捷的方式，直接向客户实时提供洞察与机遇。”

As AI becomes embedded in every application and agentic experiences become standard, the leaders will be those who treat technical enablement as a competitive advantage, not a support function.

随着 AI 深度融入每一款应用，以及智能体（agentic）体验成为行业标配，真正的领导者将是那些将技术赋能视为核心竞争优势、而非辅助职能的企业。
