## Support open-source projects

## 支持开源项目

As a company with deep roots in open-source technology, we believe in the power of communities to create a faster Web. We've seen this first-hand with Next.js, a collaboration of more than 1,800 developers with now over 2 million weekly installs.

作为一家深深植根于开源技术的公司，我们坚信社区的力量能够共同打造更快的 Web。Next.js 就是最好的例证——这一由 1800 多名开发者协作共建的项目，目前每周安装量已突破 200 万次。

In addition to creating and maintaining [Next.js](http://nextjs.org/) and other [open-source projects](https://vercel.com/oss), we sponsor popular libraries and frameworks like Nuxt, Astro, webpack, Babel, NextAuth, Parcel, and Unified. We work with hundreds of communities across the Web to donate free Vercel accounts to support their projects. Most recently, we [welcomed Rich Harris](https://vercel.com/blog/vercel-welcomes-rich-harris-creator-of-svelte) to Vercel to work full-time on Svelte, the powerful open-source framework.

除创建并持续维护 [Next.js](http://nextjs.org/) 及其他 [开源项目](https://vercel.com/oss) 外，我们还资助了 Nuxt、Astro、webpack、Babel、NextAuth、Parcel 和 Unified 等广受欢迎的库与框架。我们与 Web 上数百个开源社区合作，向他们无偿提供 Vercel 账户，以支持其项目发展。最近，我们正式邀请 Svelte（一款功能强大的开源框架）的创始人 Rich Harris 加入 Vercel，全职投入 Svelte 的开发工作。

## Make the Web Edge-first

## 让 Web 以边缘计算为先

We believe the modern Web is at the Edge and embraces the open Web platform.

我们坚信，现代 Web 生于边缘（Edge），并拥抱开放的 Web 平台。

Our customers are already embracing this new paradigm of Web development rooted in Edge-aware capabilities. Since our Series A in April 2020, traffic on the Vercel Edge Network has grown over 700%. Large ecommerce organizations are realizing agile and tailored Web experiences are the future of buying online. Startups are building their products with an Edge-first mentality from the beginning because they know that's where the Web is going and should be.

我们的客户已积极采纳这一以“边缘感知能力”为根基的全新 Web 开发范式。自 2020 年 4 月完成 A 轮融资以来，Vercel 边缘网络（Vercel Edge Network）的流量增长逾 700%。大型电商企业正逐步认识到：敏捷、个性化的 Web 体验，正是在线购物的未来；初创公司则从产品构建之初便秉持“以边缘为先”的理念，因为他们深知——这就是 Web 的演进方向，也理应如此。

Last month we released [Edge Functions](https://vercel.com/features/edge-functions), which unlock the ability to run normally-complex personalization without performance implications or client-side burden. But this is just the beginning of what's possible when your code and infrastructure are deeply integrated.

上个月，我们推出了 [Edge Functions（边缘函数）](https://vercel.com/features/edge-functions)，它使原本复杂度较高的个性化逻辑得以在无性能损耗、无需客户端负担的前提下运行。然而，这仅仅是代码与基础设施深度集成后所释放潜力的起点。

[**Geolocation** Use Edge Functions to determine a user's location](https://github.com/vercel/examples/tree/main/edge-functions/geolocation) [**Simple A/B Testing** Run A/B tests from the server-side](https://github.com/vercel/examples/tree/main/edge-functions/ab-testing-simple) [**Basic Auth** Password protection for your page at the Edge](https://github.com/vercel/examples/tree/main/edge-functions/basic-auth-password)

[**地理位置识别（Geolocation）**：利用 Edge Functions 判定用户所在位置](https://github.com/vercel/examples/tree/main/edge-functions/geolocation)  
[**简易 A/B 测试（Simple A/B Testing）**：在服务端运行 A/B 测试](https://github.com/vercel/examples/tree/main/edge-functions/ab-testing-simple)  
[**基础认证（Basic Auth）**：在边缘节点为您的页面提供密码保护](https://github.com/vercel/examples/tree/main/edge-functions/basic-auth-password)

## Build the end-to-end development platform

## 构建端到端的开发平台

This round represents a renewed commitment by our team and our investors to execute on our vision for the end-to-end platform for the modern Web.

本轮融资标志着我们团队及投资方再度承诺：全力推进我们关于“面向现代 Web 的端到端开发平台”的愿景落地。

We're prioritizing infrastructure that enables seamless [integration with any tool,](https://vercel.com/integrations) brought into a single workflow. From ideation to iteration, we want to build the best developer experience for every part of the software lifecycle, no matter what you choose to build with.

我们正优先建设可实现无缝 [与任意工具集成](https://vercel.com/integrations) 的基础设施，并将其统一纳入单一工作流。从创意构想到持续迭代，无论您选用何种技术栈进行开发，我们都致力于为软件全生命周期中的每一环节打造最佳的开发者体验。

To build great Web experiences you need code _and_ collaboration. We're expanding capabilities of features like [Preview Deployments](https://vercel.com/features/previews) and [Next.js Live](https://vercel.com/live) to unlock the potential for _everyone_ to join in on the development process.

要打造卓越的 Web 体验，既需要代码，也需要协作。我们正在增强诸如 [预览部署（Preview Deployments）](https://vercel.com/features/previews) 和 [Next.js Live](https://vercel.com/live) 等功能的能力，以释放潜能，让 _每一位开发者_ 都能参与开发流程。

We're committed to building the end-to-end platform for the entire development lifecycle. Our work is just beginning. [Join us](https://www.vercel.com/careers).

我们致力于构建覆盖整个开发生命周期的一站式端到端平台。我们的征程才刚刚开始。[加入我们](https://www.vercel.com/careers)。

### Details of Vercel valuation and Series D funding

Vercel 新增融资 1.5 亿美元，公司估值超过 25 亿美元。至此，Vercel 累计融资总额已达 3.13 亿美元。本轮融资由 [GGV Capital](https://www.ggvc.com/) 领投，现有投资方 [Accel](https://accel.com/)、[Bedrock Capital](http://www.bedrockcap.com/)、[CRV](https://crv.com/)、[Geodesic Capital](https://www.geodesiccap.com/)、[Greenoaks Capital](http://www.greenoakscap.com/)、[GV](https://www.gv.com/)、[8VC](https://8vc.com/)、[Flex Capital](https://www.flexcapital.com/)、[Latacora](https://www.latacora.com/)、[Salesforce Ventures](https://www.salesforce.com/company/ventures/)、[SV Angel](https://www.svangel.com/) 和 [Tiger Global](https://www.tigerglobal.com/) 也参与了本轮投资。
