Aug 2, 2024

2024年8月2日

![](images/improved-user-experience-for-account-settings-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/improved-user-experience-for-account-settings-vercel/img_002.jpg)

We've revamped the Account Settings with a new, intuitive navigation structure by breaking down into three different sections - Overview, Activity, and Settings.

我们已全面重构账户设置页面，采用全新、直观的导航结构，并将其划分为三个独立板块：概览（Overview）、活动记录（Activity）和设置（Settings）。

The [Overview page](https://vercel.com/account) now offers a quick snapshot of your teams and domains, including the option to request access to teams you're not part of.

[概览页面](https://vercel.com/account) 现在可快速展示您所属的团队与绑定的域名，并支持向尚未加入的团队提交访问申请。

The [Activity page](https://vercel.com/docs/observability/activity-log#using-the-activity-log) presents a chronological list of events for the last 12 months.

[活动记录页面](https://vercel.com/docs/observability/activity-log#using-the-activity-log) 以时间顺序列出过去12个月内的所有操作事件。

The [Settings page](https://vercel.com/docs/dashboard-features#settings) consolidates all user-specific options, including authentication, billing, and access tokens.

[设置页面](https://vercel.com/docs/dashboard-features#settings) 汇总了全部用户专属配置项，涵盖身份认证、账单管理及访问令牌等。

This streamlined layout aims to enhance clarity and simplify account management for all users.

这一精简优化的布局旨在提升信息清晰度，简化所有用户的账户管理流程。
