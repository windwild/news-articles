2021年7月16日

![](images/removing-domains-is-now-much-easier-vercel/img_001.jpg)

Starting today, removing Domains in the Project Settings now also optionally removes them globally, for all other Projects on your Personal Account or Team.

从今天开始，在项目设置中删除域名时，现在可选择同时将其全局删除——即从您的个人账户或团队下的所有其他项目中一并移除。

Before, this required navigating to the global Domains page on your Personal Account or Team.

此前，您需要手动前往个人账户或团队的全局“Domains”页面才能执行此操作。

Check out the [documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/custom-domains?query=remove%20domain#removing-a-domain) as well.

也欢迎查阅[相关文档](https://vercel.com/docs/custom-domains?query=remove%20domain#removing-a-domain)。
