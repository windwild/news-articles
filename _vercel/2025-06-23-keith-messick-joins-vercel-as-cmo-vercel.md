Jun 23, 2025

2025年6月23日

Vercel is evolving to meet the expanding potential of AI while staying grounded in the principles that brought us here. We're extending from frontend to full stack, deepening our enterprise capabilities, and powering the next generation of AI applications, including integrating AI into our own developer tools.

Vercel 正在持续演进，以充分释放人工智能日益拓展的潜力，同时始终坚守引领我们走到今天的初心与原则。我们的技术边界正从前端延伸至全栈，企业级能力不断深化，并全力赋能下一代 AI 应用——包括将 AI 深度集成至我们自研的开发者工具中。

Today, we’re welcoming [Keith Messick](https://www.linkedin.com/in/keithmessick/) as our first Chief Marketing Officer to support this growth and (as always) amplify the voice of the developer.

今天，我们诚挚欢迎 [Keith Messick](https://www.linkedin.com/in/keithmessick/) 加入 Vercel，担任公司首位首席营销官（CMO），以支持这一战略增长，并一如既往地为开发者群体发声。

![](images/keith-messick-joins-vercel-as-cmo-vercel/img_001.jpg)

## A rich background in marketing to developers

## 面向开发者的深厚营销积淀

Keith is a longtime enterprise CMO and comes to Vercel from Redis. There, he oversaw a 100-person team across brand, demand generation, product marketing, technical marketing, and marketing operations. He previously helped create the Feature Management category at LaunchDarkly, and led marketing at DialPad and Lucidworks.

Keith 是一位资深的企业级首席营销官，此前任职于 Redis。在 Redis 期间，他领导一支涵盖品牌、获客、产品营销、技术营销及营销运营等职能的百人团队。他早年曾参与在 LaunchDarkly 共同开创“功能管理（Feature Management）”这一全新品类，并曾担任 DialPad 与 Lucidworks 的市场营销负责人。

Keith blends commercial insight, creative vision, analytical mindset, and technical empathy, shaped by his experience as both a founder and a developer. He jokingly calls his early career persona “the world’s worst self-taught C developer with a marketing degree.” I’m pleased to report Keith’s development skills have vastly improved with the help of [v0](https://v0.dev/).

Keith 融合了商业洞察力、创意视野、数据分析思维与技术共情力——这源于他既作为创始人又作为开发者的双重经历。他笑称自己职业生涯早期的形象是“拥有市场营销学位、自学 C 语言却堪称全球最差的开发者”。值得高兴的是，在 [v0](https://v0.dev/) 的助力下，Keith 的开发能力已大幅提升。

## **A look ahead at Vercel marketing**

## **展望 Vercel 的市场营销方向**

Whether it’s an exchange on X with Guillermo or a one-sentence billboard on the 101, we value direct engagement with our community. It’s an ethos that will continue to inspire Keith as we deepen Vercel’s role at the crossroads of creativity and technical innovation.

无论是与 Guillermo 在 X 平台上的即兴对话，还是高速公路上 101 号公路旁一句精炼的广告牌标语，我们都珍视与社区成员的直接互动。这种精神将持续激励 Keith，推动 Vercel 在创意表达与技术创新交汇处扮演更深入、更关键的角色。

Keith joins a Marketing team that are deeply native to the web. This team has recently spearheaded launches like [AI Gateway](https://vercel.com/blog/ai-gateway), [v0 composite models](https://vercel.com/blog/v0-composite-model-family), [Vercel Blob GA](https://vercel.com/blog/vercel-blob-now-generally-available), [Flags Explorer GA](https://vercel.com/blog/introducing-the-flags-explorer-first-party-integrations-and-updates), [Chat SDK](https://vercel.com/blog/introducing-chat-sdk), and [Fluid compute](https://vercel.com/blog/introducing-fluid-compute).

Keith 加入的是一支真正“生于网络、长于网络”的市场营销团队。该团队近期主导推出了多项重要产品发布，包括：[AI Gateway](https://vercel.com/blog/ai-gateway)、[v0 复合模型系列](https://vercel.com/blog/v0-composite-model-family)、[Vercel Blob 正式版（GA）](https://vercel.com/blog/vercel-blob-now-generally-available)、[Flags Explorer 正式版（GA）](https://vercel.com/blog/introducing-the-flags-explorer-first-party-integrations-and-updates)、[Chat SDK](https://vercel.com/blog/introducing-chat-sdk)，以及 [Fluid Compute（弹性计算）](https://vercel.com/blog/introducing-fluid-compute)。

The team is growing, with open roles for [Content Engineers](https://vercel.com/careers/content-engineer-us-5234837004), [Customer Marketers](https://vercel.com/careers/customer-marketer-5543027004), [Field Marketers](https://vercel.com/careers/field-marketing-manager-emea-5469272004), and [more](https://vercel.com/careers?function=Marketing). If you want to help shape Vercel’s story and how people experience AI and the web, let's talk.

团队正在不断壮大，目前开放招聘[内容工程师](https://vercel.com/careers/content-engineer-us-5234837004)、[客户营销专员](https://vercel.com/careers/customer-marketer-5543027004)、[现场营销经理（EMEA 地区）](https://vercel.com/careers/field-marketing-manager-emea-5469272004)，以及[更多职位](https://vercel.com/careers?function=Marketing)。如果你希望参与塑造 Vercel 的品牌故事，并影响人们体验 AI 与网络的方式，欢迎与我们联系！

Please join me in welcoming Keith to Vercel.

诚邀大家一同欢迎 Keith 加入 Vercel！
