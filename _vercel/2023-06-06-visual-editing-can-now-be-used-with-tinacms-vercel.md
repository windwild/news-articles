[Visual Editing](https://vercel.com/blog/visual-editing)  
[可视化编辑](https://vercel.com/blog/visual-editing)

allows you to click-to-edit content on your Vercel site, with a direct link to exactly where your content lives in your CMS.  

允许您直接点击编辑 Vercel 网站上的内容，并一键跳转至该内容在 CMS 中的确切存储位置。

This functionality is now possible for Enterprise customers using [Tina](https://tina.io/) as their CMS. TinaCMS is now the third CMS to adopt content source-mapping technology that enables Visual Editing from a headless CMS with zero code changes to your website.  

此项功能现已面向使用 [Tina](https://tina.io/) 作为其 CMS 的企业客户开放。TinaCMS 已成为第三家采用“内容源映射（content source-mapping）”技术的 CMS，该技术可在**无需修改网站任何代码**的前提下，实现从无头 CMS（headless CMS）发起的可视化编辑。

[Check out the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/visual-editing#get-started) to learn more or [contact us for access](https://vercel.com/contact/sales).  

[查阅文档](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/visual-editing#get-started) 了解详情，或 [联系我们申请访问权限](https://vercel.com/contact/sales)。
