[Vercel Firewall](https://vercel.com/docs/security/vercel-firewall) protects your application from DDoS attacks and unauthorized access.

[Vercel 防火墙（Vercel Firewall）](https://vercel.com/docs/security/vercel-firewall) 可保护您的应用免受 DDoS 攻击和未授权访问。

Enterprise teams now have increased security with the ability to control traffic and restrict access through static IP addresses or entire network CIDR ranges.

企业团队现在可获得更高安全性：通过静态 IP 地址或整个网络的 CIDR 地址段，灵活控制流量并限制访问。

Stay secure by blocking entire subnets, or restricting access from untrusted networks, to prevent attacks against your applications.

通过屏蔽整个子网，或限制来自不可信网络的访问，保障您的应用安全，防范各类攻击。

[在我们的文档中了解更多信息](https://vercel.com/docs/security/vercel-waf/ip-blocking)，或 [联系我们的销售团队](https://vercel.com/contact/sales) 升级至企业版。
