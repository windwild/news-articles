May 30, 2025

2025年5月30日

![](images/faster-login-flow-and-new-google-sign-in-support-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/faster-login-flow-and-new-google-sign-in-support-vercel/img_002.jpg)

The login experience has been redesigned for faster access and now includes full support for Google Sign-in, including Google One Tap.

登录体验已全面重构，以实现更快的访问速度，并现已完整支持 Google 登录（Google Sign-in），包括 Google One Tap 功能。

If your existing Vercel account's email matches your Google email, you can use the Google button from the login screen and your accounts will be automatically linked.

若您现有的 Vercel 账户邮箱与您的 Google 邮箱一致，则可在登录界面直接点击 Google 按钮，系统将自动关联两个账户。

If the emails don’t match, you can manually connect your Google account from [your account settings](https://vercel.com/account/settings/authentication) once logged in.

若邮箱不一致，您可在登录后，前往[账户设置](https://vercel.com/account/settings/authentication)手动绑定您的 Google 账户。
