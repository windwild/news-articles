Nov 21, 2025

2025 年 11 月 21 日

In April, we launched the [Vercel Open Source Program](https://vercel.com/open-source-program) to give maintainers the resources, credits, and support they need to ship faster and scale confidently. The first group joined through our [spring 2025 cohort](https://vercel.com/blog/spring25-oss-program).

今年 4 月，我们推出了 [Vercel 开源计划](https://vercel.com/open-source-program)，旨在为开源项目维护者提供所需资源、积分与支持，助力他们更快速地交付产品，并更有信心地实现规模化发展。首批入选者来自我们的 [2025 年春季批次](https://vercel.com/blog/spring25-oss-program)。

Today we are welcoming the fall 2025 cohort.

今天，我们诚挚欢迎 2025 年秋季批次的加入。

From AI-native apps and developer infrastructure to design systems and creative tooling, open-source builders continue to amaze us. Meet the creators and explore their projects.

从原生 AI 应用、开发者基础设施，到设计系统与创意工具链，开源构建者们持续带给我们惊喜。认识这些创作者，并深入了解他们的项目。
