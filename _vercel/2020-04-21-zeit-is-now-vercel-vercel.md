Apr 21, 2020

2020年4月21日

Today, we have some very special news regarding the evolution of our company.

今天，我们有一则关于公司发展历程的特别消息。

**ZEIT is now Vercel**.

**ZEIT 现已更名为 Vercel**。

This new identity aligns with our new focus — to provide the ultimate workflow for developing, previewing, and shipping Jamstack sites.

这一全新品牌标识与我们的新使命高度契合——为 Jamstack 网站的开发、预览与发布提供终极工作流。

Towards that goal, we are thrilled to also announce that we secured **$21M in Series A funding**. [Accel](https://www.accel.com/) and [CRV](https://www.crv.com/) are our [lead investors](https://vercel.com/about#investors), alongside [Naval Ravikant](https://twitter.com/naval), [Nat Friedman](https://twitter.com/natfriedman), [Jordan Walke](https://twitter.com/jordwalke), and others.

为此目标，我们非常激动地宣布：公司已成功完成 **2100 万美元 A 轮融资**。[Accel](https://www.accel.com/) 与 [CRV](https://www.crv.com/) 担任本轮融资的联合领投方，[Naval Ravikant](https://twitter.com/naval)、[Nat Friedman](https://twitter.com/natfriedman)、[Jordan Walke](https://twitter.com/jordwalke) 等知名投资人也参与了本轮投资。

## Our Origins

## 公司起源

In 2016, we started with a simple goal: empowering solo developers to effortlessly deploy their apps.

2016 年，我们怀揣一个朴素的目标启程：赋能独立开发者，使其能够轻松部署自己的应用。

There is an overwhelming layer of technology and terminology in web development: domain setup with DNS and SSL, networking with TCP and HTTP, caching with CDN and Edge Networks... it's a huge list. It creates much complexity.

现代 Web 开发中充斥着大量技术与术语：DNS 与 SSL 域名配置、TCP 与 HTTP 网络通信、CDN 与边缘网络缓存……这个清单冗长而繁杂，带来了巨大的复杂性。

Our first product, `now`, focused on simplifying deployments:

我们的首款产品 `now`，核心使命便是简化部署流程：

The original tweet announcing \`now\`. With a single command, we helped developers get their apps and services live, inclusive of SSL and built-in routing.

这是最初发布 \`now\` 的推文。仅需一条命令，我们便帮助开发者将应用与服务一键上线——全程自动配置 SSL 证书，并内置智能路由功能。

Developers loved `now`. Tired of boilerplate, they appreciated the ability to trivially deploy, share, and scale their apps. The positive response was motivating — we were stoked!

开发者们深深爱上了 `now`。厌倦了重复繁琐的模板代码后，他们格外珍视这种“一键部署、即时分享、弹性伸缩”的极简体验。用户的积极反馈令我们备受鼓舞——我们兴奋不已！

Since then, we have invested all our energy into building products that simplify developer experience. Our open-source React framework [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) is the most notable of them. At organizations big and small, it has become _the_ framework to build React apps due to its simple learning curve, versatility, and performance.

自那时起，我们将全部精力投入于打造简化开发者体验的产品。其中最引人注目的，便是我们开源的 React 框架 [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/)。凭借其平缓的学习曲线、高度的灵活性与卓越的性能，Next.js 已成为大小企业构建 React 应用程序的**首选框架**。

Today, Next.js powers over **35,000 sites** at companies like Uber, Nike, and Starbucks.

如今，Next.js 为 Uber、Nike 和 Starbucks 等公司旗下超过 **35,000 个网站**提供支持。

![A selection of companies using Next.js.](images/zeit-is-now-vercel-vercel/img_001.jpg)![A selection of companies using Next.js.](images/zeit-is-now-vercel-vercel/img_002.jpg)A selection of companies using Next.js.

![使用 Next.js 的部分公司。](images/zeit-is-now-vercel-vercel/img_001.jpg)![使用 Next.js 的部分公司。](images/zeit-is-now-vercel-vercel/img_002.jpg)使用 Next.js 的部分公司。

In these 4 years, ZEIT has gone from being a simple idea to a thriving community. 300,000+ frontend engineers and designers are using our technology to bring their ideas to life — whether company websites, SaaS products, or personal blogs.

过去四年间，ZEIT 从一个简单的构想，成长为一个蓬勃发展的社区。已有**逾 30 万名前端工程师与设计师**借助我们的技术将创意变为现实——无论是企业官网、SaaS 产品，还是个人博客。

## Our Renewed Focus: Develop. Preview. Ship

## 我们重新聚焦的核心理念：开发 · 预览 · 发布

From observing our community, we know that **frontend engineers** are more productive than ever. Frameworks like [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) eliminate boilerplate, and serverless APIs give them **full-stack powers**.

通过观察社区实践，我们深知：**前端工程师**的生产力正达到前所未有的高度。诸如 [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) 这样的框架消除了大量样板代码，而无服务器（serverless）API 则赋予他们**全栈开发能力**。

Projects that once took months to develop, are getting completed by lean teams of frontend engineers and designers in days. These teams want to iterate rapidly, and collaboratively. They want to develop, preview, and ship at the speed of thought. They don't want to leave their browsers, and other tools they're already working with. That's where we come in.

过去需耗时数月才能完成的项目，如今仅靠精干的前端工程师与设计师团队，几天内即可交付。这些团队追求快速、协同的迭代节奏；他们希望以“思维的速度”完成**开发、预览与发布**；他们不愿离开浏览器，也不愿切换早已熟悉的其他工具——这正是我们存在的意义。

We enable teams to **develop**, **preview**, and **ship** their apps faster, and with confidence.

我们助力团队更快速、更自信地完成应用的**开发、预览与发布**。

For teams, Vercel closes the gap between development, feedback and release phase. Anyone can set up Vercel in a couple of clicks — no DevOps knowledge necessary.

对团队而言，Vercel 消弭了开发、反馈与发布各阶段之间的鸿沟。任何人只需几次点击即可完成 Vercel 的配置——无需任何 DevOps 知识。

We use Vercel to build Vercel. We are constantly examining every interaction in own team workflow, and are obsessed about removing every little source of friction.

我们用 Vercel 构建 Vercel。我们持续审视自身团队工作流中的每一次交互，并执着于消除每一处细微的摩擦点。

We have already introduced multiple updates focused on teams. Most recently:

我们已经推出了多项面向团队的更新，最近的包括：

- [Simpler Pricing](https://vercel.com/blog/simpler-pricing)

- [更简化的定价方案](https://vercel.com/blog/simpler-pricing)

- [Environment Variables UI](https://vercel.com/blog/environment-variables-ui)

- [环境变量 UI 界面](https://vercel.com/blog/environment-variables-ui)

- [Cancelable Deployments](https://vercel.com/blog/canceling-ongoing-deployments)

- [可取消的部署](https://vercel.com/blog/canceling-ongoing-deployments)

We look forward to adding even more to our team features in the weeks to come.

我们期待在未来几周内为团队功能持续增添更多新特性。

## Our New Brand: Vercel

## 我们的新品牌：Vercel

As we expand our outlook, we felt this new focus merited a new identity.

随着我们视野的不断拓展，我们认为这一全新战略重心值得启用一个全新的品牌形象。

As of today, we are re-establishing our brand identity as **Vercel**.

即日起，我们将正式以 **Vercel** 作为我们的全新品牌标识。

The new Vercel logo.

全新的 Vercel 标志。

_Vercel_ strongly connects to the words _versatile_, _accelerate_, and _excel_. It aligns with the benefits our platform offers: **faster and better product collaboration**, across every team in your organization.

“Vercel”一词与 _versatile_（多功能的）、_accelerate_（加速）和 _excel_（卓越）高度契合。它精准体现了我们平台所赋予的价值：助力贵组织内**每一支团队实现更快、更优的产品协作**。

We are introducing a new logotype for the brand, which we will use across our product and marketing. We decided to retain our signature triangle logo, although we have simplified it.

我们为品牌推出了全新的标志（logotype），该标志将统一应用于我们的产品与市场营销中。我们决定保留标志性的三角形 Logo，但对其进行了简化。

## Our Series A Funding

## 我们的 A 轮融资

Our mission is to provide [11M JavaScript developers](https://www.theinternetofthings.eu/sites/default/files/docs/SoN_17_report.pdf) _the_ workflow to work with Jamstack sites.

我们的使命是为 [1100 万 JavaScript 开发者](https://www.theinternetofthings.eu/sites/default/files/docs/SoN_17_report.pdf) 提供构建 Jamstack 网站的**首选工作流**。

To accelerate us in our mission, we have raised $21M in Series A funding. Our investors have led early design at Facebook and Dropbox, created frameworks like React, and are running companies like GitHub, Sentry, and Auth0. We are thrilled to count them as partners in our mission.

为加速实现这一使命，我们已成功完成 2100 万美元的 A 轮融资。本轮融资的投资人曾主导 Facebook 和 Dropbox 的早期产品设计，创建了 React 等主流开发框架，并执掌 GitHub、Sentry 和 Auth0 等知名科技公司。我们非常荣幸能与他们携手，共同推进这一使命。

![Our experienced investors and angel investors.](images/zeit-is-now-vercel-vercel/img_003.jpg)  
我们的资深投资人与天使投资人。

This new funding ensures an ever improving Vercel product experience and allows us to continue investing in our open-source projects like Next.js.

本轮融资将助力 Vercel 持续优化产品体验，并支持我们持续投入 Next.js 等开源项目。

## The Path Ahead for Vercel

## Vercel 的未来发展之路

**Vercel is the easiest way to deploy any Jamstack site in 2020.**

**2020 年，Vercel 是部署任意 Jamstack 网站最简单的方式。**

If you're new to our community, welcome! We encourage you to deploy with us — it takes 15 seconds to get started with [**zero-config deployments**](https://vercel.com/import), and our unlimited usage Hobby plan is free.

如果您是社区的新成员，欢迎加入！我们诚挚邀请您使用 Vercel 进行部署——只需 15 秒，即可通过 [**零配置部署**](https://vercel.com/import) 快速上手；且我们的“Hobby”免费计划不限使用量。

If you're an existing customer — you'll find that none of your existing workflows, pricing plans, or projects have changed. We're more focused than ever on helping you succeed.

如果您已是现有客户——您会发现，所有既有的工作流、定价方案及项目均保持不变。我们比以往更加专注于助您取得成功。

Over the next few weeks, we will begin to update our branding across all of our products, communication channels, and open-source projects. We have compiled a [comprehensive FAQ](https://www.notion.so/zeithq/FAQ-c726facaf7cc41c095ba49aeea90219e) that offers details about our full migration plan.

在未来几周内，我们将陆续更新所有产品、沟通渠道及开源项目中的品牌标识。我们已整理了一份[详尽的常见问题解答（FAQ）](https://www.notion.so/zeithq/FAQ-c726facaf7cc41c095ba49aeea90219e)，其中详细说明了本次全面品牌迁移计划。

### Conclusion

### 结语

ZEIT is rebranding to Vercel.

ZEIT 正式更名为 Vercel。

It's the same passionate team, with the same attention to detail.

团队依旧是我们这支充满热忱的原班人马，对细节的关注也一如既往。

With a new focus on the **develop**, **preview**, **ship** workflow and a **$21M Series A**, we're excited to take developer productivity to the next level. In his [blog](https://rauchg.com/2020/vercel), our CEO Guillermo Rauch has written about the industry trends surrounding Jamstack, and the thought process behind our new focus.

依托对 **开发（develop）**、**预览（preview）**、**发布（ship）** 工作流的全新聚焦，以及 **2100 万美元 A 轮融资**，我们满怀期待地将开发者生产力推向全新高度。我们的首席执行官 Guillermo Rauch 在其[博客文章](https://rauchg.com/2020/vercel)中深入探讨了 Jamstack 相关的行业趋势，以及我们此次战略聚焦背后的思考过程。

As always, we are incredibly grateful for your never-ending support. There are many more milestones to come and we invite you to join us on this journey.

一如既往，我们由衷感谢您始终如一的支持。前方还有诸多重要里程碑等待达成，诚邀您与我们携手同行，共赴这场旅程。
