With the [Vercel Provider](https://registry.terraform.io/providers/vercel/vercel/latest), now verified in the Terraform Registry, you can configure and deploy Vercel projects alongside your back-end services from Terraform’s infrastructure as code (IaC) software tool. By codifying cloud infrastructure and frontend deployment into a single workflow, your team can provision, preview, and ship applications faster.

借助已在 Terraform Registry 中完成认证的 [Vercel Provider](https://registry.terraform.io/providers/vercel/vercel/latest)，您现在可以通过 Terraform 的“基础设施即代码”（Infrastructure as Code，IaC）软件工具，统一配置并部署 Vercel 项目与后端服务。通过将云基础设施和前端部署纳入单一工作流，您的团队能够更快地完成应用的资源供给、预览与发布。

[Check out our guide](https://vercel.com/guides/integrating-terraform-with-vercel) to get started.

[查看我们的入门指南](https://vercel.com/guides/integrating-terraform-with-vercel) 开始使用。
