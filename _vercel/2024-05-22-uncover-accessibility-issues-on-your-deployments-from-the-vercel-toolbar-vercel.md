Accessibility Audit now runs in the background for you everywhere you have the Vercel Toolbar.  
无障碍审计（Accessibility Audit）现已在您启用 Vercel 工具栏（Vercel Toolbar）的所有位置后台自动运行。

You can view the compliance of [Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.0](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG20/) level A and AA rules for the page you are on from the toolbar menu.  
您可通过工具栏菜单，查看当前页面对 [《Web 内容无障碍指南 2.0》（WCAG 2.0）](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG20/) A 级与 AA 级规范的符合情况。

The rules will show grouped by impact as defined by [deque axe](https://github.com/dequelabs/axe-core/blob/develop/doc/rule-descriptions.md#wcag-21-level-a--aa-rules).  
各项规则将依据 [Deque axe](https://github.com/dequelabs/axe-core/blob/develop/doc/rule-descriptions.md#wcag-21-level-a--aa-rules) 所定义的影响程度（impact）进行分组展示。

You can also turn on recording to keep track of issues that turn up as you move around a page. This feature is available to all Vercel users.  
您还可开启“记录”功能，持续追踪在页面中浏览时新出现的问题。该功能面向所有 Vercel 用户开放。

See the [Accessibility Audit documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/accessibility-audit-tool) to learn more.  
请参阅 [无障碍审计工具文档](https://vercel.com/docs/workflow-collaboration/accessibility-audit-tool)，了解更多信息。
