Aug 28, 2024

2024 年 8 月 28 日

![](images/integrated-billing-for-supabase-redis-and-edgedb-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/integrated-billing-for-supabase-redis-and-edgedb-vercel/img_002.jpg)

The Vercel KV product has been sunset. You can now deploy alternative KV stores and other storage solutions through the [Vercel Marketplace Storage](https://vercel.com/marketplace/category/storage?category=storage&search=redis), with automatic account provisioning and unified billing. [Learn more](https://vercel.com/blog/introducing-the-vercel-marketplace).

Vercel KV 服务已正式停用。您现在可通过 [Vercel Marketplace 存储分类](https://vercel.com/marketplace/category/storage?category=storage&search=redis) 部署其他键值存储（KV）服务及各类存储解决方案，享受自动账户开通与统一账单功能。[了解更多](https://vercel.com/blog/introducing-the-vercel-marketplace)。

Vercel now has native integrations with Supabase, Redis, and EdgeDB.

Vercel 现已原生集成 Supabase、Redis 和 EdgeDB。

Start for free or purchase storage at the same price as going direct. Our new [storage add-ons](https://vercel.com/blog/introducing-the-vercel-marketplace) include integrated billing, direct access to provider consoles, and more.

可免费开始使用，或以与直接向服务商购买完全相同的价格采购存储服务。我们全新推出的 [存储附加组件](https://vercel.com/blog/introducing-the-vercel-marketplace) 支持统一计费、直连服务商控制台等功能。

In the coming months, we will begin a zero-downtime migration for Vercel Postgres and KV to our new marketplace. Postgres will transition to our Neon integration, and KV will transition to our Upstash integration. No action is required on your part.

在接下来的几个月中，我们将启动 Vercel Postgres 和 KV 向新 Marketplace 的零停机迁移：Postgres 将迁移至 Neon 集成，KV 将迁移至 Upstash 集成。您无需执行任何操作。

Get started with the [Vercel Marketplace](https://vercel.com/marketplace), available to customers on all plans.

立即通过 [Vercel Marketplace](https://vercel.com/marketplace) 开始使用——所有付费计划用户均可访问。
