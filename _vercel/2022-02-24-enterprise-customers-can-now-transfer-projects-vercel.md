2022 年 2 月 24 日

![](images/enterprise-customers-can-now-transfer-projects-vercel/img_001.jpg)

Enterprise customers can now [transfer projects](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/projects/overview#transferring-a-project) to other Vercel accounts.

企业客户现在可以将项目[转移至其他 Vercel 账户](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/projects/overview#transferring-a-project)。

This makes it easier for Enterprise teams to move projects between different accounts when ownership changes.

当项目所有权发生变更时，此举可帮助企业团队更轻松地在不同账户之间迁移项目。

Check out [the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/projects/overview#transferring-a-project) as well.

也请参阅[相关文档](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/projects/overview#transferring-a-project)。
