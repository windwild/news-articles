Composing the modern digital experience.

Today, we've announced our strategic partnership with WPP, a world leader in communications, experience, commerce, and technology.

今天，我们宣布与WPP达成战略合作伙伴关系。WPP是全球领先的传播、体验、商业与科技服务集团。

Through the years, brands have entrusted Vercel and [WPP’s global network of agencies](https://www.wpp.com/) to help them modernize their digital experience with the best creative _and_ the best technologies. Together, we serve leading organizations like **The International Olympic Committee, James Hardie, Fluor, and Country Road Group.**

多年来，众多品牌一直信赖Vercel与[WPP遍布全球的代理机构网络](https://www.wpp.com/)，借助顶尖创意与前沿技术，共同推动其数字体验的现代化升级。目前，我们携手服务的领先组织包括：**国际奥林匹克委员会、James Hardie、福陆（Fluor）以及Country Road集团**。

Building on the foundation we’ve had, Vercel and WPP will be further investing resources into:

在既有合作基础之上，Vercel与WPP将进一步投入资源，聚焦以下方向：

- Co-developing an AI-first approach to the development and delivery of digital experiences  
- 共同开发以AI为先的数字体验研发与交付方法论  

- Facilitating quarterly global technology leader forums  
- 每季度举办全球技术领导者论坛  

- Optimizing project delivery efficiency through a shared and standardized development process for Next.js and Vercel  
- 通过统一、标准化的Next.js与Vercel开发流程，提升项目交付效率  

- Providing early product roadmap access to WPP and its selected customers  
- 向WPP及其精选客户优先开放产品路线图

Hydrow 联合 [VML（前身为 Wunderman Thompson 商务与技术事业部）](https://vercel.com/customers/how-vercel-enables-wunderman-thompson-to-launch-global-brands)，致力于为其内部团队及终端用户打造升级版数字体验，涵盖实时内容更新、高效协同工作流，以及行业领先的用户界面。
