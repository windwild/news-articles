2022 年 9 月 15 日

![](images/search-domains-on-the-vercel-dashboard-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/search-domains-on-the-vercel-dashboard-vercel/img_002.jpg)

You can now search your list of domains in the Domains tab on the Vercel dashboard to instantly find what you're looking for.

您现在可以在 Vercel 控制台的「Domains（域名）」标签页中搜索您的域名列表，从而即时找到目标域名。

The search bar improves discoverability for teams working with multiple domains that often have long lists of domains to parse through.

该搜索栏提升了团队的查找效率——尤其适用于管理大量域名的团队，他们常常需要在冗长的域名列表中逐一筛选。

Check out the [documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/projects/custom-domains) to learn more.

请查阅[文档](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/projects/custom-domains)，了解更多信息。
