You can now more easily add custom nameservers to your Vercel hosted domain, allowing for delegation to other DNS providers. Add up to four nameservers at once, and revert to your previous settings if necessary.

现在，您可以更便捷地为托管在 Vercel 上的域名添加自定义 DNS 服务器，从而将域名解析委托给其他 DNS 服务商。您可一次性添加最多四个 DNS 服务器，并在必要时恢复至之前的设置。

Check out [the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/projects/custom-domains#nameservers) as well.

也欢迎查阅[相关文档](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/projects/custom-domains#nameservers)。
