The [Integrations Marketplace](https://vercel.com/integrations) has been upgraded. With an improved design, it's now easier than ever to discover and install Integrations in one place. All listed Integrations have been reworked to maximize usability and deliver a stellar user experience.

[集成市场](https://vercel.com/integrations) 已完成升级。凭借优化后的界面设计，您现在可以更轻松地在一个地方发现并安装各类集成（Integrations）。所有已上架的集成均经过全面重构，以最大限度提升易用性，并为用户提供卓越的使用体验。
