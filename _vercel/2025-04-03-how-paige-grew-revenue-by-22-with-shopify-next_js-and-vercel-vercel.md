PAIGE, a leading denim and apparel retailer, faced significant technical complexity due to their existing ecommerce architecture. Seeking a faster and more reliable online experience, they reimagined their ecommerce strategy by adopting a simpler headless tech stack—one powered by Shopify, Next.js, and Vercel—that ultimately boosted their Black Friday revenue by 22% and increased conversion rates by 76%.

PAIGE 是一家领先的牛仔及服饰零售商，其现有电商架构带来了显著的技术复杂性。为打造更快速、更可靠的线上购物体验，PAIGE 重新构想了其电商战略，转而采用一套更简洁的无头技术栈——以 Shopify、Next.js 和 Vercel 为技术底座。该转型最终助力其“黑色星期五”销售额提升 22%，转化率提升 76%。

## Overcoming inherited complexity

## 应对历史遗留的复杂性

When Director of Engineering Michael La Colla joined PAIGE, he inherited a overly complicated and nuanced system built on Commercetools, Angular, and Bloomreach.

工程总监 Michael La Colla 加入 PAIGE 时，接手了一套基于 Commercetools、Angular 和 Bloomreach 构建的过度复杂且高度定制化的系统。

Despite this being a headless approach, integration and data syncing issues plagued the company’s technical capabilities and omnichannel goals. These limitations slowed innovation, delayed development, and created operational challenges across departments. PAIGE’s priority became clear: simplify their technology stack.

尽管采用了无头架构（headless approach），集成与数据同步问题仍严重制约了该公司的技术能力及全渠道（omnichannel）战略目标。这些局限性拖慢了创新节奏、延迟了开发进度，并在各部门间引发了运营挑战。PAIGE 的首要任务由此变得明确：精简其技术栈。

### Simplifying through complete re-architecture

### 通过全面重构实现简化

Michael and the team at PAIGE chose to re-architect their ecommerce platform instead of simply redesigning the site. They worked across departments to surface key issues such as outdated Angular code, operational friction, and complex vendor integrations, using that alignment to streamline their tooling decisions and remove unnecessary dependencies.

PAIGE 公司的 Michael 及其团队选择对电商平台进行整体架构重构，而非仅限于网站界面的重新设计。他们跨部门协作，深入识别出若干关键问题，例如过时的 Angular 代码、运营流程中的摩擦点，以及复杂的第三方供应商集成；并以此共识为基础，优化工具选型决策，剔除冗余依赖。

Their new architecture resolved existing limitations across departments and enabled sustainable growth:

新架构有效消除了各部门此前面临的技术瓶颈，为可持续增长奠定了坚实基础：

- **Shopify:** Integrated smoothly across ecommerce, retail, fulfillment, customer service, and marketing–enhancing omnichannel capabilities  
- **Shopify**：无缝集成至电商、零售、履约、客户服务及市场营销等各业务环节，显著增强全渠道能力  

- **Next.js:** Offered [Server-side Rendering (SSR)](https://nextjs.org/docs/app/building-your-application/rendering/server-components), resolved SEO issues previously encountered with Angular, aligned PAIGE with current industry standards, and provided flexibility without vendor lock-in  
- **Next.js**：支持[服务端渲染（SSR）](https://nextjs.org/docs/app/building-your-application/rendering/server-components)，解决了此前 Angular 架构下长期存在的 SEO 问题，使 PAIGE 技术栈与当前行业标准保持一致，同时在避免厂商锁定（vendor lock-in）的前提下提供了高度灵活性  

- **Vercel:** Ensured reliable user experiences even during peak traffic periods with automated deployments, [global CDN](https://vercel.com/docs/edge-network), and simplified developer experience through features like [preview deployments](https://vercel.com/products/previews) and [instant rollbacks](https://vercel.com/docs/instant-rollback), while also providing continuous access to Next.js and React expertise  
- **Vercel**：依托自动化部署、[全球内容分发网络（CDN）](https://vercel.com/docs/edge-network)，以及[预发布环境（preview deployments）](https://vercel.com/products/previews)、[即时回滚（instant rollbacks）](https://vercel.com/docs/instant-rollback)等开发者友好功能，在流量高峰期依然保障稳定可靠的用户体验；同时持续提供 Next.js 与 React 领域的专业技术支持

## **Expanding omnichannel capabilities**  
## **拓展全渠道能力**

PAIGE’s simplified tech stack is driving continued innovation, enabling rapid expansion of multi-channel features, including buy-online, fulfill-from-store, and the introduction of a new loyalty program. Improved UX/UI design and faster site performance have also led to sustained conversion increases and enhanced customer engagement.  
PAIGE 简化的技术栈正持续推动创新，使其能够快速拓展多渠道功能，包括“线上下单、门店履约”（buy-online, fulfill-from-store），以及全新会员忠诚计划的上线。优化后的用户体验（UX）与用户界面（UI）设计，叠加网站性能的显著提升，共同带来了持续的转化率增长和更强的客户参与度。

With a stable and flexible foundation, PAIGE can now quickly implement new features and respond promptly to business requests, staying ahead of customer expectations and market trends. PAIGE is well-positioned for future peak seasons and beyond.  
依托稳定而灵活的技术底座，PAIGE 现在能够快速上线新功能，并及时响应业务需求，在满足客户期待与把握市场趋势方面始终领先一步。PAIGE 已为未来各场大促高峰及更长远的发展做好充分准备。
