Jan 29, 2025

2025 年 1 月 29 日

![](images/filter-for-your-own-requests-in-logs-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/filter-for-your-own-requests-in-logs-vercel/img_002.jpg)

You can now filter logs to display only requests made from your browser. This simplifies debugging by isolating your requests in high-traffic environments. It matches your IP address and User Agent to incoming requests.

现在，您可对日志进行筛选，仅显示来自您浏览器的请求。该功能通过在高流量环境中隔离您的请求，从而简化调试过程；其原理是将您的 IP 地址和 User Agent 与传入请求进行匹配。

Visit [your project's Logs tab](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%5Bproject%5D%2Flogs&title=Open+Logs) and toggle the user filter to get started or learn more about [runtime logs](https://vercel.com/docs/observability/runtime-logs).

请访问 [您项目的 Logs（日志）标签页](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%5Bproject%5D%2Flogs&title=Open+Logs)，开启用户筛选器以立即开始使用；或进一步了解 [运行时日志（runtime logs）](https://vercel.com/docs/observability/runtime-logs)。
