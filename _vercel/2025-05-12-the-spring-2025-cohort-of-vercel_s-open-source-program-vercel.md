May 12, 2025

2025 年 5 月 12 日

Open source runs the world. The frameworks, libraries, and tools we rely on are strengthened by communities that share ideas, review code, and build in the open.

开源驱动世界运转。我们所依赖的各类框架、库和工具，正因那些乐于分享创意、审阅代码、公开协作的社区而日益强大。

At Vercel, we want to help those communities thrive. That’s why we launched the [**Vercel Open Source Program**](https://vercel.com/docs/open-source-program): a developer initiative that gives maintainers the resources, credits, and support they need to ship faster and scale confidently.

在 Vercel，我们致力于助力这些社区蓬勃发展。因此，我们推出了 [**Vercel 开源计划**](https://vercel.com/docs/open-source-program)：一项面向开发者的倡议，为项目维护者提供所需资源、额度支持与专业协助，助其更快速交付产品，并更有信心地实现规模化发展。

Four times a year, we’ll welcome a new cohort of projects into the program. Members receive $3,600 in Vercel credits, perks from partners, and a dedicated Slack space to learn from one another.

每年四次，我们将迎来一批新项目加入该计划。入选成员可获得价值 3,600 美元的 Vercel 积分、合作伙伴提供的专属权益，以及一个专属 Slack 讨论区，用于彼此交流学习。

Today we are announcing this spring's cohort.

今天，我们正式公布 2025 年春季批次入选项目。

### Frontpage（首页）

A federated link aggregator that has since been adopted as the defacto link-aggregation website for the Bluesky community. It serves as a front page to the internet, allowing users to post, vote, and comment on links and discussions from across the web.

一个去中心化的链接聚合平台，目前已成为 Bluesky 社区事实上的链接聚合网站。它充当互联网的“首页”，允许用户发布、投票和评论来自全网的链接与讨论。
