Jun 16, 2025

2025 年 6 月 16 日

![](images/introducing-the-dubai-vercel-region-dxb1-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/introducing-the-dubai-vercel-region-dxb1-vercel/img_002.jpg)

Dubai (`dxb1`) is now part of Vercel’s delivery network, extending our global CDN's caching and compute to reduce latency for users in the Middle East, Africa, and Central Asia without requiring any changes.

迪拜（`dxb1`）现已加入 Vercel 的交付网络，将我们全球 CDN 的缓存与计算能力延伸至中东、非洲及中亚地区，从而降低终端用户的访问延迟，且无需任何配置变更。

The new Dubai region serves as the first stop for end-users based on proximity and network conditions. It's generally available and serving billions of requests.

该全新迪拜区域将根据地理位置与网络状况，作为终端用户的首个接入节点。目前该区域已全面开放，并正为数十亿次请求提供服务。

Teams can configure Dubai as an execution region for Vercel Functions, which supports [Fluid compute](https://vercel.com/fluid) to increase resource and cost efficiency, minimize cold starts, and scale dynamically with demand.

团队可将迪拜配置为 Vercel Functions 的执行区域；该区域支持 [Fluid compute（弹性计算）](https://vercel.com/fluid)，以提升资源与成本效率、最大限度减少冷启动，并随需求动态扩缩容。

Learn more about [Vercel Regions](https://vercel.com/docs/edge-network/regions) and [Dubai's regional pricing](https://vercel.com/docs/pricing/regional-pricing/dxb1).

了解更多关于 [Vercel Regions（Vercel 区域）](https://vercel.com/docs/edge-network/regions) 和 [迪拜区域定价](https://vercel.com/docs/pricing/regional-pricing/dxb1) 的信息。
