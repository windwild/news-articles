Oct 11, 2023

2023 年 10 月 11 日

What will you ship?

你将交付什么？

A few weeks ago, we introduced [v0](https://v0.dev/): a product that makes website creation as simple as describing your ideas. We call it Generative UI—combining the best practices of frontend development with the potential of generative AI.

几周前，我们推出了 [v0](https://v0.dev/)：一款让网站构建变得如同描述想法一样简单的工具。我们将它称为“生成式 UI”（Generative UI）——融合前端开发的最佳实践与生成式 AI 的强大潜力。

The interest in v0 has been incredible, with 100,000 people registering for the waitlist in just three weeks. Today, we’re transitioning v0 from Alpha to Beta, rolling out access to 5,000 additional users, and introducing subscription plans for those who want to unlock the full v0 feature set.

v0 引发了空前的关注——仅三周内就有 10 万人加入等候名单。今天，我们正式将 v0 从 Alpha 版本升级为 Beta 版本，向额外 5,000 名用户开放访问权限，并推出订阅计划，供希望解锁 v0 全功能的用户选用。

## **About v0**

## **关于 v0**

Our goal is to help developers build the first iteration of their product. Like [Ingo](https://twitter.com/ingokpp/status/1708899085481115700), who built the first version of their [Resume Builder](https://v0.dev/t/g8Rb2LSYMDP) product with v0. Or [Braintrust](https://twitter.com/braintrustdata), a new AI platform that built its [pricing page](https://twitter.com/alanaagoyal/status/1707434643602255929) using v0.

我们的目标是帮助开发者快速构建其产品的首个可用版本。例如 [Ingo](https://twitter.com/ingokpp/status/1708899085481115700)，他借助 v0 快速搭建了其 [简历生成器（Resume Builder）](https://v0.dev/t/g8Rb2LSYMDP) 产品的初版；又如新兴 AI 平台 [Braintrust](https://twitter.com/braintrustdata)，也使用 v0 构建了其 [定价页面（pricing page）](https://twitter.com/alanaagoyal/status/1707434643602255929)。

Here’s how it works:

其工作流程如下：

- Describe the interface you want to build  
- 描述你希望构建的用户界面  

- v0 produces code using open-source tools like React, Tailwind CSS, and [Shadcn UI](https://ui.shadcn.com/)  
- v0 基于 React、Tailwind CSS 和 [Shadcn UI](https://ui.shadcn.com/) 等开源技术自动生成代码  

- Select an iteration and keep editing in v0  
- 选择一个迭代版本，并继续在 v0 中编辑优化

- When you're ready, copy and paste that code into your app and develop from there

- 准备就绪后，请将该代码复制并粘贴到您的应用中，然后在此基础上进行开发。


[Learn more about how v0 works](https://v0.dev/faq).

[了解 v0 的工作原理](https://v0.dev/faq)。

## **v0 Subscription Plans**

## **v0 订阅方案**

v0 offers a free plan and three paid plans. If you are on a paid plan, you’ll also have the option to purchase more credits on-demand as needed. A “credit” is a method of payment tied to generations. Each generation costs ten credits, except for the first generation, which costs 30 credits.

v0 提供一项免费方案及三项付费方案。若您订阅的是付费方案，还可根据需要按需购买额外积分（credits）。“积分”是一种与生成次数（generations）挂钩的计费单位：除首次生成需消耗 30 积分外，后续每次生成均消耗 10 积分。

- **Free**: $0 per month includes 200 credits

- **免费版**：每月 $0，含 200 积分

- **Basic**: $10/month includes 1,500 credits

- **基础版**：每月 $10，含 1,500 积分

- **Standard**: $30/month includes 5,000 credits

- **标准版**：每月 $30，含 5,000 积分

- **Premium**: $50/month includes 10,000 credits

- **高级版**：每月 $50，含 10,000 积分


[Read more about v0 pricing](https://v0.dev/pricing).

[详细了解 v0 定价信息](https://v0.dev/pricing)。

## **What's Ahead**

## **未来展望**

v0 is the starting point for the next generation of user interfaces.

v0 是下一代用户界面的起点。

As we continue to build, we’ll also be adding new features like support for custom design systems, theming, transforming images to code, and further security and access controls.

在持续开发过程中，我们还将陆续推出新功能，例如对自定义设计系统（custom design systems）的支持、主题化（theming）、图像转代码（transforming images to code），以及更完善的的安全机制与访问控制（security and access controls）。

What will you ship? [Get started today](https://v0.dev/).

你将交付什么？[立即开始](https://v0.dev/)。
