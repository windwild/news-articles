Jan 28, 2021

2021 年 1 月 28 日

There is a new way to [transfer projects on Vercel](https://vercel.com/docs/platform/projects#transferring-a-project) from Hobby accounts to Team plans or between two different Teams with zero downtime. This means **Teams no longer need to redeploy projects that were deployed under a Hobby plan or a different Team.**

Vercel 推出了一种全新方式，支持将项目从 Hobby 账户[迁移至 Vercel Team 套餐](https://vercel.com/docs/platform/projects#transferring-a-project)，或在两个不同的 Vercel Team 之间迁移，全程零停机。这意味着：**Team 不再需要对原本部署在 Hobby 套餐下或另一 Team 下的项目重新部署。**

Vercel’s newest update allows a team member to transfer a project on a Hobby plan to a Vercel Team plan or between two Vercel Teams. This is done without having to reconfigure the site’s deployment settings. All of the project’s dependencies will be moved or copied over to the new Vercel Team namespace with absolutely no downtime.

Vercel 最新更新允许团队成员将 Hobby 套餐下的项目迁移至 Vercel Team 套餐，或在两个 Vercel Team 之间迁移。整个过程无需重新配置网站的部署设置；项目的所有依赖项将被迁移或复制到新的 Vercel Team 命名空间中，全程零停机。

Seamlessly upgrade projects from the Hobby plan to a Vercel Team plan to give full access to collaborative features, multiple Concurrent Builds, and more powerful Serverless Functions. In addition, transfers can happen between Vercel Teams.

可无缝将项目从 Hobby 套餐升级至 Vercel Team 套餐，从而全面启用协作功能、多并发构建（Concurrent Builds）以及更强大的 Serverless Functions。此外，项目亦可在不同 Vercel Team 之间自由迁移。

This feature is automatically enabled for all Hobby and Vercel Team plans. To make use of it, navigate to the Advanced section of your Project settings. There you will find an option to transfer your project to a new Team.

该功能已对所有 Hobby 套餐及 Vercel Team 套餐自动启用。如需使用，请进入项目设置中的「高级（Advanced）」选项卡，即可找到将项目迁移至新 Team 的选项。

With just a few clicks, you can upgrade or transfer your Project to a new Vercel Team plan while your Project settings and history remain intact.

仅需数次点击，即可将您的项目升级或迁移至新的 Vercel Team 套餐，且项目设置与历史记录均完整保留。
