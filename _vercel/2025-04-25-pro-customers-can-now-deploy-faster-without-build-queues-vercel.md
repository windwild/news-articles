Apr 25, 2025

2025 年 4 月 25 日

When multiple team members deploy to Vercel at once, builds by default have a queue. Now, you can remove these queues, enabling your builds to start immediately.

当多名团队成员同时向 Vercel 部署时，构建任务默认会进入队列。现在，您可以移除这些队列，使构建任务立即开始执行。

This is available to both Pro and Enterprise customers with [new per-minute pricing](https://vercel.com/changelog/pricing-for-on-demand-concurrent-builds-reduced-by-over-50-percent) and can be applied in the following ways:

该功能面向 Pro 和 Enterprise 计划用户开放，并采用[全新的按分钟计费模式](https://vercel.com/changelog/pricing-for-on-demand-concurrent-builds-reduced-by-over-50-percent)，可通过以下两种方式启用：

- **Manually,** per deployment, for urgent builds

- **手动启用**：针对单次部署，在有紧急构建需求时使用；

- **Automatically,** at the project level, to avoid queues by default

- **自动启用**：在项目级别全局开启，使构建默认跳过队列；

Concurrent build slots remain available for teams with steady, high-volume workloads.

对于具有稳定、高吞吐量工作负载的团队，仍可继续使用并发构建配额。

Learn about [on-demand concurrent builds](https://vercel.com/docs/builds/managing-builds#on-demand-concurrent-builds) and [enable them on your project](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%5Bproject%5D%2Fsettings%2Fbuild-and-deployment&title=Enable%20on-demand%20concurrent%20builds).

了解更多关于[按需并发构建（on-demand concurrent builds）](https://vercel.com/docs/builds/managing-builds#on-demand-concurrent-builds)的信息，并[在您的项目中启用该功能](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%5Bproject%5D%2Fsettings%2Fbuild-and-deployment&title=Enable%20on-demand%20concurrent%20builds)。
