Oct 7, 2025

2025 年 10 月 7 日

Enterprise customers with [Observability Plus](https://vercel.com/docs/observability/observability-plus) can now receive anomaly alerts by **email** or in-app notifications, in addition to existing delivery options: webhooks, Slack, and the dedicated alerts dashboard.

已订阅 [Observability Plus](https://vercel.com/docs/observability/observability-plus) 的企业客户，现可在现有通知渠道（包括 Webhook、Slack 和专属告警仪表板）基础上，新增通过 **电子邮件** 或应用内通知接收异常告警。

Currently, two types of anomaly alerts are available:

目前支持两类异常告警：

- **Usage anomalies**: Detects unusual spikes in key billable metrics

- **用量异常**：检测关键计费指标的异常激增

- **Error anomalies**: Detects sudden increases in 5XX responses on a specific route or path

- **错误异常**：检测特定路由或路径上 5XX 响应的突然增加

Anomaly alerts are available in limited beta for Enterprise customers with [Observability Plus](https://vercel.com/docs/observability/observability-plus).

异常告警目前正面向已订阅 [Observability Plus](https://vercel.com/docs/observability/observability-plus) 的企业客户进行限量公测。

[Try it out](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%7E%2Fobservability%2Falerts) or learn more about [Alerts](https://vercel.com/docs/alerts).

[立即试用](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%7E%2Fobservability%2Falerts)，或深入了解 [告警功能](https://vercel.com/docs/alerts)。
