May 29, 2023

2023 年 5 月 29 日

Today marks a significant milestone for frontend development.

今天标志着前端开发领域的一个重要里程碑。

May 29th is the 10th anniversary of [React](https://react.dev/), a project that has transformed the web industry and reshaped the way we build digital experiences.

5 月 29 日是 [React](https://react.dev/) 问世十周年纪念日。这一项目深刻变革了整个 Web 行业，重塑了我们构建数字体验的方式。

A huge congratulations and thank you to the team at Meta, who through their stewardship and relentless innovation, have created and maintained one of the [most successful](https://twitter.com/github_tracker/status/1626397466135343104) open source projects of all time.

向 Meta 团队致以最热烈的祝贺与诚挚感谢！正是凭借他们的悉心维护与不懈创新，React 才得以成长为有史以来[最成功](https://twitter.com/github_tracker/status/1626397466135343104)的开源项目之一。

## A decade of innovation

## 十年创新之路

React's inception a decade ago set in motion a cascade of innovation that continues to shape the future of web and [native](https://reactnative.dev/) user interfaces.

十年前 React 的诞生，开启了一波又一波创新浪潮，持续塑造着 Web 及 [原生](https://reactnative.dev/) 用户界面的未来。

The true strength of React lies in its versatility. React is more than just a library; it's a platform that powers countless pieces of software across the web and beyond. It’s helped raise the bar for user interface development for the entire industry.

React 的真正优势在于其卓越的通用性。它远不止是一个前端库，更是一个平台——驱动着 Web 及更广阔生态中无数软件的运行。它整体提升了整个行业在用户界面开发领域的标准。

While Vercel supports developers on their journey to build better web experiences, regardless of the tech they use, React holds a special place in our heart. It has shaped our understanding of [UI](https://rauchg.com/2015/pure-ui), inspired the [creation of Next.js](https://vercel.com/blog/next), and even today, continues to inspire us to push boundaries and create better web experiences.

尽管 Vercel 始终支持开发者使用任何技术栈打造更优质的 Web 体验，但 React 在我们心中始终占据着特殊地位。它塑造了我们对 [UI](https://rauchg.com/2015/pure-ui) 的理解，启发了 [Next.js 的诞生](https://vercel.com/blog/next)，直至今日，依然激励着我们不断突破边界、创造更出色的 Web 体验。

## <ThankYou />

## <致谢 />

As we celebrate this milestone, we'd like to extend a special thank you to the [individuals](https://react.dev/community/team) who have been integral to React's journey, and countless others [who have contributed](https://react.dev/community/acknowledgements) to React's growth and success over the years, we express our heartfelt gratitude.

值此里程碑时刻，我们谨向 [React 核心团队](https://react.dev/community/team) 中一路相伴、不可或缺的各位同仁，以及多年来为 React 的成长与成功倾注心血的[无数贡献者](https://react.dev/community/acknowledgements)，致以最诚挚的感谢。

Here's to [a decade of React](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8pDqJVdNa44) \- a decade of innovation, learning, and transformation. As we look forward to what the next decade brings, we do so with excitement and anticipation for the next generation of innovation.

致敬 [React 的十年历程](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8pDqJVdNa44)——这是一段充满创新、学习与变革的十年。展望未来十年，我们满怀兴奋与期待，迎接下一代创新的到来。

To celebrate React's 10th anniversary, watch Delba de Oliveira (Senior Developer Advocate at Vercel) host a roundtable discussion on React, Server Components, and more with Andrew Clark and Sebastian Markbåge from the React core team.

为庆祝 React 十周年，敬请观看 Vercel 高级开发者倡导者 Delba de Oliveira 主持的圆桌讨论——她将携手 React 核心团队成员 Andrew Clark 与 Sebastian Markbåge，深入探讨 React、服务端组件（Server Components）等前沿话题。
