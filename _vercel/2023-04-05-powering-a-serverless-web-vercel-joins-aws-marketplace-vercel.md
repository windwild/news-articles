## **A historic relationship, realized**

## 历史性合作关系，今朝落地

Vercel has a deep, historic relationship with AWS. We’ve [leveraged AWS Lambda](https://vercel.com/blog/aws-and-vercel-accelerating-innovation-with-serverless-computing) since our inception and have been a frequent design partner as they explore new products. Our [CEO Guillermo Rauch shared the stage](https://youtu.be/mZsGPCSg7Vg) with Holly Mesrobian, AWS VP of Serverless Compute at last year’s re:Invent.

Vercel 与 AWS 拥有深厚而悠久的合作关系。自创立之初，我们便已开始[采用 AWS Lambda](https://vercel.com/blog/aws-and-vercel-accelerating-innovation-with-serverless-computing)，并长期作为其新产品探索过程中的重要设计合作伙伴。去年 re:Invent 大会上，我们的[首席执行官 Guillermo Rauch 与 AWS 无服务器计算副总裁 Holly Mesrobian 同台演讲](https://youtu.be/mZsGPCSg7Vg)。

We've implemented [framework-defined infrastructure](https://vercel.com/blog/framework-defined-infrastructure) for more predictable, lower cost, and lower risk DevOps through truly serverless—and, for developers, infrastructureless—architecture. This has helped [brands like The Washington Post, Under Armour, and others](https://vercel.com/customers) scale their applications seamlessly and securely.

我们已落地实践[框架定义的基础设施（Framework-Defined Infrastructure）](https://vercel.com/blog/framework-defined-infrastructure)，依托真正意义上的无服务器架构——对开发者而言，更是“零基础设施”架构——实现更可预测、更低成本、更低风险的 DevOps。这一方案助力[《华盛顿邮报》（The Washington Post）、安德玛（Under Armour）等品牌客户](https://vercel.com/customers)无缝、安全地扩展其应用规模。

## **Fueling and streamlining your business**

## **赋能并优化您的业务运营**

Vercel’s AWS Marketplace integration creates value for our customers through:

Vercel 与 AWS Marketplace 的集成，为我们的客户带来以下核心价值：

- **Speed and governance** from a streamlined procurement process, including one-click deployments.

- **高效采购与合规治理**：流程精简，支持一键部署。

- **Extensive security validation**, so you can rest easy with [ironclad security practices](https://aws.amazon.com/partners/foundational-technical-review/) by default.

- **全面的安全验证**：默认启用[坚如磐石的安全实践](https://aws.amazon.com/partners/foundational-technical-review/)，让您高枕无忧。

- **Simplified billing** with bill consolidation, cost analysis, and subscription management.

- **简化的账单管理**：支持账单合并、成本分析及订阅管理。

- **Discounts and flexible pricing** for agreements as unique as your business needs.

- **专属折扣与灵活定价**：协议条款量身定制，精准匹配您独特的业务需求。
