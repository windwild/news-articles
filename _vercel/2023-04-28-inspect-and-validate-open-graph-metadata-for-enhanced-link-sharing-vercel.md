2023年4月28日

![](images/inspect-and-validate-open-graph-metadata-for-enhanced-link-sharing-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/inspect-and-validate-open-graph-metadata-for-enhanced-link-sharing-vercel/img_002.jpg)

You can now inspect and validate Open Graph (OG) images and metadata for your Vercel deployments directly from the dashboard, without needing to use third-party tools.

您现在可直接在 Vercel 控制台中检查和验证部署项目的 Open Graph（OG）图片及元数据，无需借助第三方工具。

View link previews for social platforms like Twitter, Slack, Facebook, and LinkedIn, and optimize your site for sharing on the web. The OG inspector also provides context-aware suggestions for routes in your application based on the deployment output. Protected routes using [Deployment Protection](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/deployment-protection) can also be inspected.

查看 Twitter、Slack、Facebook 和 LinkedIn 等社交平台上的链接预览效果，并优化您的网站以提升网页分享体验。OG 检查器还会根据部署输出，为应用中的路由提供上下文感知的优化建议。使用 [Deployment Protection（部署保护）](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/deployment-protection) 保护的路由同样支持检查。

Try it now by visiting the Open Graph tab on a [Vercel Deployment](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/overview).

立即尝试：访问任意 [Vercel 部署页面](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/deployments/overview)，点击其中的 “Open Graph”（Open Graph）标签页。
