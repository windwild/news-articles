2023 年 9 月 18 日

![](images/new-project-access-controls-for-enterprise-customers-vercel/img_001.jpg)![](images/new-project-access-controls-for-enterprise-customers-vercel/img_002.jpg)

Today, we’re introducing more ways for Enterprise customers to have control over which members of their Vercel team have access to certain projects for increased security.

今天，我们为 Vercel 企业版客户推出更多管控方式，使其能够更精细地控制团队成员对特定项目的访问权限，从而提升安全性。

The new team level role **Contributor**, has restricted access to make changes at the project level, and only has access to the projects to which they’ve been assigned. This role can be useful for agencies and contractors working on a limited project basis.

全新团队级角色 **Contributor（贡献者）** 对项目级操作拥有受限访问权限，仅能访问已被明确分配给他们的项目。该角色特别适用于以限定项目范围开展工作的代理机构或外包服务商。

Additionally, we’ve introduced new [Project level roles](https://vercel.com/docs/accounts/team-members-and-roles/access-roles/project-level-roles): **Project Admin**, **Project Developer**, and **Project Viewer**. Project level roles are assigned to a team member on a project level and are only valid for the project they are assigned to.

此外，我们新增了以下[项目级角色](https://vercel.com/docs/accounts/team-members-and-roles/access-roles/project-level-roles)：**Project Admin（项目管理员）**、**Project Developer（项目开发者）** 和 **Project Viewer（项目查看者）**。项目级角色按项目为单位分配给团队成员，且仅在所分配的项目内有效。

[Check out the documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/accounts/team-members-and-roles/access-roles) to learn more.

[查阅相关文档](https://vercel.com/docs/accounts/team-members-and-roles/access-roles) 以了解更多信息。
