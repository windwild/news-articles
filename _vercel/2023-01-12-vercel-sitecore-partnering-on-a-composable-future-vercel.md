Jan 12, 2023

2023 年 1 月 12 日

Today, we've announced a strategic partnership with Sitecore, a leading Digital Experience Platform (DXP) and Content Hub, to deliver an end-to-end composable solution for building and deploying dynamic web experiences.

今天，我们宣布与领先的数字体验平台（DXP）和内容中心 Sitecore 建立战略合作伙伴关系，共同提供一套端到端可组合式解决方案，用于构建和部署动态网页体验。

Combining customer data and AI to deliver personalized experiences and offering a powerful CMS to create and manage content across channels and devices, Sitecore is an ideal solution for today’s connected, omnichannel digital experience.

Sitecore 将客户数据与人工智能相结合，以交付个性化体验；同时提供功能强大的内容管理系统（CMS），支持跨渠道、跨设备创建与管理内容——这使其成为当今互联化、全渠道数字体验的理想解决方案。

### Modernizing the frontend

### 前端现代化

As users expect a fast, continuous experience across all devices and channels, Sitecore needed to bring innovative development approaches to their suite of composable solutions and provide a better experience for users and developers.

随着用户期望在所有设备与渠道上获得快速、无缝的体验，Sitecore 需要为其一系列可组合式解决方案引入创新的开发方法，从而为终端用户和开发者提供更优体验。

They looked to partner with a frontend platform that:

他们希望与一个前端平台合作，该平台需满足以下要求：

- **Offers a more developer-centric stack**: Frontend developers needed more flexibility to leverage the best frameworks and tools with Sitecore.

- **提供更以开发者为中心的技术栈**：前端开发者需要更高灵活性，以便结合 Sitecore 自由选用最佳框架与工具。

- **Improves the performance of their DXP solutions:** Users demanded better performance than what a monolithic .NET codebase could deliver.

- **提升其 DXP 解决方案的性能**：用户对性能的要求已超出单体式 .NET 代码库所能提供的水平。

- **Enables omnichannel:** Headless APIs and app architecture were required to unify the customer experience across different channels.

- **支持全渠道能力**：需通过无头 API 与应用架构，在不同渠道间统一客户体验。

- **Supports digital portfolios:** Brands needed an easier way to manage multiple sites with tens or hundreds of domains from a single codebase.

- **支持数字资产组合管理**：品牌方亟需一种更简便的方式，借助单一代码库管理数十乃至数百个域名的多个网站。

As a global leader in digital experience software, our partnership with Vercel extends the Sitecore Composable DXP product portfolio. This provides our customers with an end-to-end solution for all their digital needs.”  
>  
> “在 Sitecore，我们的使命是通过令客户难忘的数字化连接，助力领先品牌实现跃升。作为数字体验软件领域的全球领导者，我们与 Vercel 的合作进一步拓展了 Sitecore 可组合式 DXP（Composable DXP）产品组合，为客户提供覆盖全场景的端到端数字化解决方案。”

Sitecore chose Vercel as the frontend for their DXP for the developer experience with Vercel's frontend framework, [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/), build system with [Turborepo](https://turbo.build/repo), and interactive preview capabilities, as well as optimal user experience with a global edge network and serverless compute for speed and scale.  
Sitecore 选择 Vercel 作为其 DXP 的前端平台，原因在于 Vercel 提供卓越的开发者体验：依托其前端框架 [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/)、构建系统 [Turborepo](https://turbo.build/repo)，以及交互式预览能力；同时，凭借覆盖全球的边缘网络与无服务器计算能力，为终端用户带来极致的速度与可扩展性体验。

### **Into the future: a fully composable stack**  
### **迈向未来：一套完全可组合的技术栈**

Together, Sitecore and Vercel offer an end-to-end composable solution for building and deploying dynamic applications at the Edge with a developer and content creator workflow optimized for iteration.  
Sitecore 与 Vercel 携手，提供一套端到端的可组合式解决方案，支持在边缘（Edge）高效构建与部署动态应用，并为开发者与内容创作者打造高度迭代优化的工作流。

### The Vercel + Sitecore stack creates:  
### Vercel + Sitecore 技术栈带来以下核心优势：

- **Optimized performance:** Applications on the Sitecore + Vercel stack are optimally fast and scale automatically. Boost site performance and increase conversion rate by delivering your storefront, publication, or application [at the edge](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/edge-network/overview). With [Vercel Edge Middleware](https://vercel.com/features/edge-functions) and [Sitecore](https://www.sitecore.com/products.), you can dynamically render your pages and personalize without sacrificing speed.  
  **极致性能表现：** 基于 Sitecore + Vercel 构建的应用具备最优响应速度，并可自动弹性伸缩。通过将您的线上商店、出版物或应用程序[部署至边缘网络](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/edge-network/overview)，显著提升网站性能并提高转化率。借助 [Vercel 边缘中间件（Edge Middleware）](https://vercel.com/features/edge-functions) 与 [Sitecore](https://www.sitecore.com/products.)，您可在不牺牲加载速度的前提下，实现页面的动态渲染与个性化呈现。

- **Collaborative content editing:** With [Figma-like commenting on auto-generated previews](https://vercel.com/blog/introducing-commenting-on-preview-deployments), Vercel makes development a team sport. Developers, designers, and content creators collaborate instantly, streamlining the review process and launching sites faster. For web updates that don't require developers, [Vercel integrates with Sitecore’s XM Cloud](https://vercel.com/partners/sitecore) so content editors can make changes and publish pages directly.  
  **协同式内容编辑：** Vercel 提供[类似 Figma 的自动预览评论功能](https://vercel.com/blog/introducing-commenting-on-preview-deployments)，让开发真正成为团队协作项目——开发者、设计师与内容创作者可即时协同，大幅简化审核流程，加速网站上线。对于无需开发者介入的网页更新，[Vercel 已深度集成 Sitecore XM Cloud](https://vercel.com/partners/sitecore)，使内容编辑人员可直接修改并发布页面。

- **Lower infrastructure costs:** Vercel's serverless, edge-first infrastructure delivers world-class performance at a fraction of the overhead cost to maintain and operate a litany of CDNs, cloud providers, security tools, CI/CD platforms, monitoring and observability vendors, and more.  
  **更低的基础设施成本：** Vercel 以无服务器（serverless）、边缘优先（edge-first）架构，在仅需传统运维开销一小部分的前提下，交付世界级性能——无需再自行维护和运营大量 CDN、云服务提供商、安全工具、CI/CD 平台、监控与可观测性服务商等复杂基础设施。

- **Omnichannel compatibility:** The headless nature of the platform ensures a unified presence across different channels without re-architecting the entire design.  
  **全渠道兼容性：** 平台采用无头（headless）架构，确保品牌在各类渠道（如 Web、移动 App、IoT 设备、语音助手等）中保持一致体验，且无需对整体设计架构进行重构。

- **Better developer talent and productivity:** Because we integrate with the latest developer tools built by leaders in the ecosystem, developers love to collaborate and ship on Vercel, and the integration between Sitecore’s DXP and [OrderCloud](https://vercel.com/integrations/ordercloud) makes full-stack development faster and easier.

- **更优秀的开发者人才与更高开发效率：** 由于我们深度集成生态系统中领军企业打造的最新开发者工具，开发者乐于在 Vercel 上协作并快速交付产品；而 Sitecore 数字体验平台（DXP）与 [OrderCloud](https://vercel.com/integrations/ordercloud) 的集成，则显著加快并简化了全栈开发流程。

We’ve already seen amazing results from customers like Avid Technology and Focus Brands, who are using Sitecore and Vercel to develop and host the Schlotzky's and Moe’s online platforms with an emphasis on speed and throughput during peak ordering hours.

我们已从 Avid Technology 和 Focus Brands 等客户处见证了卓越成效：他们正借助 Sitecore 与 Vercel 共同开发并托管 Schlotzky’s 和 Moe’s 的线上平台，在订单高峰期尤为注重响应速度与系统吞吐能力。

### **为您的无头架构转型提供世界级支持**

不仅 Vercel 和 Sitecore 对此次合作所蕴含的巨大潜力充满期待。

Our incredible implementation partners not only see the rewards of moving to composable application stacks, but the tight integration between Next.js, Vercel, and Sitecore streamlines implementation to reduce the cost, risk, and length of migrations.

我们杰出的实施合作伙伴不仅深刻认识到转向可组合式应用架构所带来的收益，而且 Next.js、Vercel 与 Sitecore 之间紧密的集成，更大幅优化了实施流程，从而有效降低迁移成本、风险及周期。
